Two Six Technologies is looking to add a Lead Forensic Analyst to our team. This role will be responsible for performing critical tasks in the information response and media analysis cycles used by our clients.  You will use your in depth knowledge of drive forensics to develop and enable mitigations for network exploitation attempts within our client.
Job Responsibilities  Duties:

Perform forensic analysis on computer systems as part of network exploitation and abuse investigations
Identify items of interest and properly document results of the forensic analysis in accordance with client established procedures
Perform triage and analysis of digital storage media to identify and recover items of analytical interest

Minimum Qualifications:

Five years of experience in performance of computer forensics, vulnerability analysis, systems engineering, or design of information systems
Five years of experience supporting the DOD or other government agencies
Bachelors degree in Network Engineering, Systems Engineering, Information Technology or related field

Nice to haves:

Experience in the use of commercial forensic toolkits for both desktop and mobile applications
Demonstrable knowledge in the performance of virtual machine forensic and data capture techniques
Understanding of techniques for performing forensic timelining of events based on evaluated data sources

Clearance Requirements:

This position requires a TSSCI with polygraph
