Mission Summary
The Autonomy Metrics team is a group of software engineers who accelerate the progress of Motionals autonomous vehicles by developing high-quality, data-driven key performance indicators organization-wide. Specifically, the AV system and subsystem metrics are used to guide training and evaluation of our neural networks, our dashboards are used to guide strategic decisions across all levels including senior leadership, and our infrastructure provides always-available critical tools to research scientists and developers. 
The Motional Pittsburgh office is located in the new Hazelwood Green development, a culmination of the citys goal of restoring an economic driver to the neighborhood in a thoughtful, inclusive, and sustainable way. With views along the Monongahela river, the Pittsburgh office is in the heart of this new state of the art development.
What youll be doing:

As either an infrastructure generalist or specialist based on your interest and background, helping us build the infrastructure necessary to understand our system
Set up AWS infrastructure to process and analyze data at scale
Develop metrics and data pipelines supporting those metrics, in Python
Build complex software systems and collaborate with cross-functional machine learning and robotics teams to influence design for scalability, testability, flexibility and cost efficiency
Work with motivated teammates in a fast-paced, collaborative environment
The teams core project focuses on on-road performance analysis, and autonomy regression detection and prevention
