About the job
This position is a hands-on role to support the management of our data center footprint across the US and EU as well as our AWS cloud infrastructure. Successful candidates join at a time where we are executing on a hybrid strategy that will optimize our on-premise data center infrastructure while leveraging operational efficiencies in AWS. 
Responsibilities
In this role, youll:

Build automation to support the existing systems and processes for data center management, hardware lifecycle, server provisioning, and configuration management.
Be part of the team that is leading our cloud migration strategy while maintaining a resilient on-premise platform.
Drive efficiencies in both cloud and on-prem footprints through a data-driven approach to design decisions. 
Demonstrate effective communication by authoring and reviewing design documents, runbooks, and other service documentation.
Become part of the on call rotation for the team.
Foster an inclusive environment and imports team members to succeed and grow.

Required:

Proficiency with one or more programming languages: Ruby and Go preferred.
Experience with running one or more public clouds at scale (AWS preferred), using automation to support deployment, scaling, monitoring, and management (Terraform  Pulumi etc.).
Deep familiarity with Linux (CentOSRHEL or Ubuntu preferred), bash scripting, service configuration and management.
Comfortable with supporting and configuring physical hardware (servers, switches, power distribution, etc.) and virtual machine environments
Strong understanding of networking concepts and internet application fundamentals (TCPIP, DNS, SSL, load balancing, etc)
A passion for automation and DevOps  blending operational excellence with development best practices.
An understanding of computer science fundamentals and practical experience developing distributed systems.
Proven ability to work across teams to address complex engineering challenges.   

Desired:

Experience running large-scale critical production infrastructure supporting 247 business needs
Experience automating management of physical infrastructure in a hybrid cloud environment.
Experience with configuring and administering load balancing.
Familiarity and practical experience deploying applications to Kubernetes.
Past development projects involving interacting with, or creating, structured APIs.

Location 
This role will be based in Dublin or remotely in Ireland.
