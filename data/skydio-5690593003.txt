Skydio is the leading US drone company and the world leader in autonomous flight, the key technology for the future of drones and aerial transportation. The Skydio team combines deep expertise in artificial intelligence, best-in-class hardware and software product development, and operational excellence to empower a broader, more diverse audience of drone users - from first responders to insurance claims adjusters, utilities, and more!About the role:
By combining best-in-class autonomy and cloud connected real-time visibility, drones will become critical and ubiquitous infrastructure that can be deployed and monitored in real time to solve a variety of problems faster, cheaper, and safer than ever before. As one of the first security software engineers hired at Skydio, you will play a foundational role in defining, architecting, and developing best-in-class security infrastructure and processes at the forefront of the drone industry. You will be responsible for:

Conducting code reviews and managing our DevSecOps security controls
Designing and implementing defensive security controls for our products, cloud, and corporate environments 
Deploying identity and trust management on our cloud and corporate environments

How youll make an impact:

Design and implement security mechanisms to protect data, authorize users, monitor systems, etc.
Develop a deep understanding of Skydios technology, customers, and workflows to ensure that our products meet a wide range of security profiles (Fortune 500, government, DoD)
Assess security of existing systems, identify technological risk, propose remediation initiatives, and plan ahead for security at scale
Evaluate security impacts of software designs and make recommendations on infrastructure improvements necessary to uphold a high security standard
Leverage your experience and best practices to not only uphold but improve Skydios engineering standards

What makes you a good fit:

5-10 years of Python experience
You love having fun building great products and enjoy working on systems that involves hardware and software
You are customer focused with hands-on software development experience around writing and shipping code to solve customer problems
You have experience building and delivering scalable and secure cloud based systems with a high proficiency in modern cloud technologies
Direct experience with our stack (Typescript, React, Python, Terraform, C, AWS)
You are a security generalist with deep experience and passion for securing complex systems at enterprise scale
Demonstrated ability to collaborate with cross-functional engineering teams (experience working with hardware development teams is a significant plus)
Knowledge of relevant security and compliance frameworks, standards and regulations (such as SOC2, cloud security, GDPR, FedRAMP, ISO27001, etc.) a bonus
