The Curation team at Pinterest is seeking a Sr Product Manager to drive experiences that allow Pinners to meaningfully engage with their favorite content to help navigate the inspiration to realization journey. This role will drive some of the most critical investments on Pinterest and will get to work on some of the most loved features on Pinterest that are used by 10s of millions of Piners everyday. The experiences you envision and build with a world class team of engineers, designers, and researchers will directly drive engagement and shopping on Pinterest. Come join the team thats at the heart of the Pinterest experience, and make your mark on how Pinners enrich their lives and make their dreams a reality!
 
What youll do:

Work on product strategy, development, and execution for curation experiences at Pinterest, such as the Boards surface
Deliver an engaging, cohesive, and frictionless experience for hundreds of millions of Pinners who are looking to get more out of their saved content
Partner closely with engineering, design, research and analytics to define, measure, and drive execution: understand diverse Pinner needs to create experiences that match our wide breadth of audiences and business objectives
Collaborate across product teams to drive alignment and resolve dependencies, creating a unified vision for the Boards experience and how teams come together to create that experience

What were looking for:

Demonstrated success in product management or related experience at fast-growing companies
Experience developing full-stack consumer-facing experiences at scale and advocating for your vision of these experiences
Proven ability to work cross-functionally and cross-teamorg to deliver great user experiences on time in a highly collaborative environment, influencing, escalating, and establishing working models where needed to move quickly across teams
Ability to move quickly and make appropriate trade-offs based on data analysis and qualitative feedback from Pinners
