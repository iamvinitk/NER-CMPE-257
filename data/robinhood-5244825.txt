Robinhood is looking for a Senior Tax Operations Analyst to join our team! This is a pure project management role, and we are seeking driven individuals who thrive in a fast-paced environment, and who can juggle multiple high priority initiatives. If youre a self-starter, who can work independently, but also are excited to drive and collaborate on Tax projects - this is a great opportunity for you! 
This role will be reporting to the Senior Manager, Tax Ops and will work closely with our Tax, Product, and Engineering teams to prioritize all projects that impact Robinhoods Tax Operations. This role will manage and help develop the Tax Operations teams strategic roadmap, including tax involvement in company-wide projects and launches, vendor strategy, and tax regulatory project management.
It is preferred that this role is located in one of the office locations listed on this job description which will align with our in-office working environment. This position is only eligible for remote work in limited geographies within the US where we do not have physical office locations. Please connect with your recruiter for more information regarding our in-office philosophy and expectations.
What youll do

Develop and manage the Tax Ops roadmap for key projects that impact tax operations, and communicate requirements to senior leadership and key stakeholders in cross-functional teams
Manage all tax related communications and training materials to ensure customers and other stakeholders are aware of key deadlines, including the tax season calendar
Strategically lead various tax-related projects to launch, improve, and scale Robinhood products and systems (i.e. Securities, Crypto, and Cash-related products) both domestically and internationally
Understand and prioritize regulatory projects and deadlines, including tax season preparation and coordination in partnership with Product Managers andor Engineering teams across all lines of business to secure vital resources and ensure timely delivery
Monitor and track new product launches, use metrics and KPIs to evaluate team progress, and hold vendors accountable to service level agreements and delivery timelines
Collaborate with various business partners on tax projects designed to enhance customer experience by offering new reports or functionality and by improving communications and help content

What you bring 

Bachelors Degree or higher
5 years experience in successful project delivery; including 2 years of project management experience for financial services, securities, or crypto companies - project management certifications are a plus! 
High attention to detail and ability to drive projects to completion in a deadline-driven and highly regulated environment
Strong analytical and communication skills
Experience leading large-scale projects impacting a retail customer base
Knowledge of tax information reporting and withholding rules for US and non-US customers, including 1099 and 1042-S reporting and withholding, is a plus!
