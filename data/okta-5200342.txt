We are seeking an experienced, self-motivated Finance Analyst who will be a key player in, and is truly excited about, driving and effectively scaling Oktas growth. This person will report to the Manager, Corporate FPA and will partner with FPA peers as well as with Business Technology (BT) and Finance Transformation (FT) teams to enable the improvement of FPA processes and reporting. This role is a fantastic opportunity for a candidate who wants to be part of a dynamic FPA organization that is operating at an accelerated pace and scaling for the future.
The ideal candidate has strong analytical skills, excellent attention to detail without losing the big picture, and the ability to work both cross functionally and independently. They are highly organized, and are truly excited about creating an impact to enhance reporting capabilities and systems improvements.
 
Job Duties and Responsibilities:

Play a key role in driving reporting initiatives between FPA, Finance Transformation, and Business Technology (including Anaplan and Data  Insights teams)
Partner with FPA and Data  Insights to design, structure and standardize reporting needs for FPA
Understand FPA analysis to assist in supporting Anaplan and Data  Reporting needs and at times assist in performing those analyses
Support ad hoc systems related activities such as communication with Business Technology for month close activities, model management work, user access and other ad-hoc activities as needed
Assist with and manage frequent ad-hoc projects to support management and business decisions
Engage in training and enablement of reporting activities for FPA
