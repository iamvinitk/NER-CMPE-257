The art market is the last major consumer marketplace to come online and, with over 30B in commercial inventory, Artsy is the first company to aggregate the lions share of it onto a single marketplace platform. Our Product team is central to unlocking the full potential of Artsys marketplace to not only connect existing buyers and sellers, but to create a liquid secondary market and expand the art market overall.
Product at Artsy comprises of three product areas; Collector, Core Marketplace and Platform. In our Collector team, Product Managers (PMs) are responsible for collector experience (B2C and C2C), mobile, how collectors discover new artworks, and growth. In Core Marketplace, PMs are responsible for our partner experience (B2B), transactions  purchasing, and auctions. PMs in our Platform team are technical PMs, focused on data, infrastructure, security, etc.

Artsy Values
Artsy has five core values that will inform your experience at Artsy.
For the Love of Art: We have relentless curiosity, empathy, and passion for collectors, artists, and the art market. We are here to make the art world more fair and welcoming.
Own Our Outcomes: We are all individual owners of Artsys shared success. We are resourceful and resilient, and to get the job done, we each take responsibility for our outcomes.
