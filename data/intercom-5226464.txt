The Intercom Creative Studio is looking for a Staff-Level Designer with experience building design systems based in thoughtful creative strategy. At an exciting moment in time for Intercom, youll be responsible for applying cutting-edge creative thinking to all of the brands most impactful touchpoints. Our small but growing in-house team is home to a diverse group of creatives with a broad range of experience, backgrounds and talents. Were constantly looking for ways to make our day-to-day work feel fresh and meaningful, and we deeply value the health and wellness of each member of the team.

Own the rollout of new brand design systems
Develop sub-brands
Audit brand surfaces for inconsistencies and own new creative solutions

Create bespoke campaigns that deliver on our brand and business strategy
