About the team
The Revenue and Finance Automation (RFA)  Platforms  Ecosystem Partnerships team is part of our Global Partnerships organization, and supports new and existing SaaS and platform products for Stripe. This includes defining new and managing existing partnership opportunities, strategies, and segments for our SaaS products (some examples include: Stripe Tax, Stripe Billing, Stripe Revenue Recognition) and expanding our ecosystem through platforms such as Stripe Apps.
What youll do
This role will be responsible for driving strategies and partnerships for existing and new products, with a focus on growing the Stripe Apps ecosystem requiring both partner recruitment and ongoing relationship management. As part of these efforts, you will collaborate closely with cross-functional stakeholders (e.g. product, eng, go to market) across the company and with external partners as you develop and execute new partnerships (and related strategies) and manage the existing partner ecosystem. You will drive mission critical projects to expand the Stripe Apps ecosystem into new partner types (e.g. plug-ins), drive adoption of new Stripe platforms and surfaces, and increase attach of Stripes SaaS products.
