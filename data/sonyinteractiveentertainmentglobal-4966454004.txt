Join the award-winning team that created God of War
Santa Monica Studio is seeking a Senior Character Rigger for the development of a new unannounced title!
Animator and programmer, or programmer and character artist  which are you? No matter; you bridge tech and character. You understand the creative process, you have an artistic sensibility, and an eye for animation. Above all you collaborate with character artists and animators to deliver the highest quality vision of the game.
Talent, initiative, collaboration  bring them all. We are a large team that fundamentally works together across all disciplines. Sound interesting? Give us a shout!
Responsibilities

Create and maintain animation rigs and related tools
Research, define, extend, and maintain production pipelines, processes, and workflows
Collaborate with character artists and animators
Identify problems; solve them by inventing or building robust technical implementations

Requirements

Minimum five years of professional experience in computer-generated art
Intimate knowledge of Maya for rigging and animation
Ability to research, develop, and drive new rig systems and workflows including but not limited to facial rigging, hair and cloth simulations, and non-traditional approaches to rigs
Ability to pioneer, document, and mentor others on rig theory, systems, and production-oriented workflows
Well-versed in human and animal anatomy
Working knowledge of shaders, specifically as they relate to animation
Solid understanding of 3D math
Proficient with polygon modeling and topology adjustments related to character deformations
Strong working knowledge of Python
Deep knowledge of animator workflows
Openness to share knowledge and mentor others
Excellent spoken and written communication
Able and motivated to work with other team members
