The opportunity
Unity is hiring an HRIS Workday Tech Analyst to supervise HRIS functions such as system configuration, updates, security roles, reports, development, and training. This role will support and enhance global Workday projects to optimize their performance and growth.
The HRIS Tech Analyst role is responsible for supporting HRIS technologies used by the IT Team. This role will act as an IT technical representative for all projects within the Business Systems team. As a technical solution owner, you will be expected to provide domain expertise for projects requiring technical development and integration. In addition, the role will provide support to other administrative functions of the HRIS Landscape including access, configuration changes, and issue resolution.
What youll be doing 

Work with third-party vendors, internal resources, and business owners to effectively implement global solutions of varying complexities; support Data Migration efforts from legacy applications during implementation processes
Provide technical expertise across HRIS applications with the ability to understand and define technical solutions agnostic of a platform; develop detailed technical requirements and establish traceability to the business vision
Work closely with business analysts and partners to understand and develop technical solutions to meet captured requirements and Unity standards
Lead and implement smoke, unit, and integration testing in required situations
Work with integration teams to ensure ongoing efficiency  effectiveness in communication between critical applications

What were looking for 

Experience creating, updating, and maintaining Workday inboundoutbound integrations using EIBs, Core Connectors, Packaged Integrations, and Workday Studio
Experience migrating historical and current data sets into HRISHCM applications; familiar with sophisticated Workday reporting using all report types including SQL or other querying languages
Experience creating and maintaining complex calculated fields and custom objects
Global Platform Production Support  Issue Resolution experience
Familiar with common system administration tasks including annual release cycles, general access controls

You might also have

Global, Enterprise HRISHCM Implementation Experience preferably with Workday Certifications in related disciplines
Broader development experience in a variety of languages andor custom applications
