About the Team
DoorDash Drive is one of the fastest growing segments within DoorDash. Cost and efficiency play a massive role in our ability to drive profitability and scale the business.
About the Role
You will report into the Senior Manager of our Non-Restaurant Cost  Efficiency Team, and will play a pivotal role in driving company growth by optimizing cost  efficiency for our largest partners. Specifically, you will be responsible for developing and implementing strategies alongside cross-functional partners to reduce operating cost and increase profitability for the Drive Non-Restaurants business.
Your responsibilities will include:
Developing  executing cost reduction strategy,  

Identify opportunities to improve dasher efficiency and execute operational tests to identify the most effective levers 
Analyze the dasher experience and identify opportunities to reduce support and refund related costs
Create internal reporting to track progress against cost reduction goals
Size, scope, and prioritize product and analytics asks necessary to deliver on cost reduction strategy

Driving merchant acquisition and retention by ensuring we are providing accurate and competitive pricing throughout the deal negotiation process:

Proactively identify opportunities to reduce merchant fees
Collaborate with Business Development and General Managers to ensure deal models have accurate cost assumptions
Partner with Finance to buildmaintain accurate pricing models to streamline deal process
Build modelstooling for Business Development and General Managers to size cost reduction opportunities
