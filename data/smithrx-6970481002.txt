Job summary:
The Revenue Operations team focuses on our most critical and strategic cross-company projects. This ranges from data-driven deep dives into our pricing strategy to key account reviews to getting in the weeds streamlining our business systems and processes. 
We are looking to add a Revenue Operations Analyst to the team who will be responsible for a wide breadth of commercial projects. This includes partnering with Sales and Clinical to manage our deal review process, partnering with Legal and Finance to streamline our contracting processes, and working with Account Management and Implementations to improve our business processes and systems. 
The successful candidate will be a commercially-minded generalist who evidences a passion for improving business efficiency through creative solutions and will be comfortable generating consensus decisions from multiple stakeholders.
What you will do:

Shepherd deals through the sales cycle and internal approval processes while partnering with the appropriate cross-functional teams
Analyze deals from a financial and key terms perspective while partnering with the Clinical team to ensure a thorough drug-level evaluation of deals
Ensure opportunities are properly tracked in Salesforce, validating our systems against the signed contract
Design and implement complex business and operational systems and processes
Drive analysis and research to help inform our overall strategy and make the right growth decisions for SmithRx
Contribute to operational process improvements to increase the effectiveness of SmithRxs teams
Partner directly with senior leaders and cross functional teams to accelerate progress, solve hard scaling initiatives, and incubate new approaches
Effectively prioritize and manage additional ad-hoc requests and responsibilities
