Learning and Developments mission is to ensure employees are well equipped and knowledgeable by building role-specific skills to run a highly effective business. We create programming for strong skill development, provide accurate and easily accessible reference information and process documentation for the business to have what they need to be confident, competent, and excited about delivering an exceptional customer experience.
As a Senior Instructional Designer, you will be responsible for the design and delivery of learning and development programs that build world-class experiences for our customers. Specifically, you will be accountable for creating training and educational programs related to skills training, new hire onboarding, product launches, and new process or policy implementations, among other areas for our associates and registered representatives using a variety of instructional methods.
You will also be responsible for strategizing and developing new learning methodologies to meet our learner needs, project managing large-scale projects and lead team initiatives. The ideal candidate will provide resolutions to a diverse range of complex business problems and will assess curriculum improvement opportunities, revise training collateral and make recommendations as necessary.
The role is located in the office location(s) listed on this job description which will align with our in-office working environment. Please connect with your recruiter for more information regarding our in-office philosophy and expectations.
