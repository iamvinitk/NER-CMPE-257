Supernal is at the forefront of creating emerging mobility solutions that will foster the development of human-centered cities. We are designing a completely new electric vertical take-off and landing (eVTOL) aircraft tailored to the mobility needs of future cities. This allows passengers a seamless intermodal journey that safely transports them to their final destination. We fuse research in autonomy, robotics, aviation and services to define a new category of mobility for the worlds communities. We believe in creative thinking and collaboration to help build a better mobility experience for everyone, improving peoples ability to move  whether for work or play.
What we do:
As the Lead Process Improvement Engineer, you will play a pivotal role in optimizing and streamlining our internal business processes for our rapidly growing and evolving company in our new Change Management team on Digital Operations (DigOps). Your focus will be to elevate efficiency, productivity, and effectiveness through design thinking and Lean Six Sigma methods. You will work closely with business stakeholders to identify bottlenecks, increase efficiencies, and align processes across departments demonstrating digital solutions to enable human users. You will have the opportunity to lead and inspire others to adopt design thinking and lean six sigma methods and foster a collaborative and innovative environment where ideas are nurtured. You will also work closely to develop and execute the process improvement roadmap, contributing to Supernals strategic objectives and long-term success. Your guidance wont just elevate corporate processes; it will elevate our culture.
This position will be required to work on-site 5 days a week.
What you can do:

Process Analysis and Improvement: Lead assessment and analysis of existing processes within the organization identified by key leaders or other Supers (Supernal employees) as high priority for change, collaborating with appropriate teams across the business
Design Thinking: Apply design thinking principles and methods to demonstrate a user centered approach to delivering effective internal operations solutions that drive positive outcomes
Lean Six Sigma Implementation: Apply Lean Six Sigma principles and tools to streamline processes, reduce variability, and eliminate non-value-added steps, ultimately driving continuous improvement and operational excellence
Multi-functional Collaboration: Facilitate workshops and meetings with multi-functional teams in implementing process improvements
Data-Driven Approach: Leverage data analytics and key performance indicators to measure the effectiveness of process improvements, identify trends, and make data-backed decisions to drive efficiency
Change Management: Partner with change management to ensure successful adoption of process improvements, fostering a culture of continuous improvement and excellence
Documentation and Training: Create comprehensive documentation of improved processes, standard operating procedures (SOPs), and training materials to ensure widespread understanding and adoption
Stakeholder Engagement: Collaborate with leadership and key partners to understand their needs and expectations, align process improvement initiatives with strategic goals and business units, and gather feedback for ongoing enhancements
Mentorship and Training: Offer coaching and mentorship to team members and partners in process improvement methodologies, fostering a culture of empowerment and skill development
