Databricks is experiencing tremendous growth and we are looking for a Senior Technical Enablement Program Manager to join our Technical Field Enablement Team. You will report to the Director of Technical Enablement for Field Engineering.
An ideal candidate possesses expertise in instructional design and adult learning principles and has a genuine passion for Data and AI technologies. The candidate should demonstrate accountability, exceptional organizational skills, and the capability to lead cross-functional projects effectively. Furthermore, they should exhibit a strategic mindset and the ability to adapt to evolving priorities, ultimately driving performance, productivity, and results through the cultivation of strong relationships.
The mission of the Technical Enablement team is to empower the technical field team and all customer-facing technical roles with essential technical, business, leadership, and customer engagement skills through exceptional enablement programs throughout their tenure at Databricks. In this position, you will assess and enhance the skills necessary to elevate the overall learner effectiveness in the field.
 
The impact you will have:

Collaborate closely with senior leadership and key stakeholders to validate, prioritize, and execute innovative technical enablement initiatives
Map and own the technical leadership programs and initiatives in alignment with the strategic priorities of the company and OKRs of the Field Engineering organization
Contribute to the technical enablement strategy by creating scalable and engaging field leadership programs, encompassing program design, development, implementation, and the oversight of reporting processes
Work cross-functionally to keep up-to-date with a fast-evolving platform, product, messaging, product capabilities, and new processes
Create, curate, and utilize content, while fostering Communities of Practice through cross-functional connections and harnessing the expertise of subject matter experts (SMEs) to further enrich our enablement programs and content.
Continually seek opportunities for enhancement that yield measurable results, all while maintaining a commitment to refining and optimizing the strategy for enablement solutions at Databricks
Coordinate and facilitate leadership sessions, workshops, and other enablement launches
Monitor programs for improvement that lead to measurable outcomes, always looking to evolve the enablement and training solutions at Databricks
Establish effective assessment methods and metrics to gauge the effectiveness and impact of leadership programs.
Collaborate with external partners and vendors to enrich program content and improve delivery by leveraging industry insights and expertise

What we look for:

Minimum 12 years experience in enablement, and education at a SaaS company
Can translate datainsights into strategies and provide data-driven solutions
Solid experience running large-scale enablement programs, and how to evangelize and roll out the programs effectively across the organization.
Results-driven with deep project management skills and customer focus
Excellent communication and storytelling skills along with polished presentation skills
Ability to work well in a fast-paced dynamic environment, able to manage competing priorities, and stay flexible through growth and change.
Strong executive presence, communication and influence skills, comfortable presenting in front of large audiences
