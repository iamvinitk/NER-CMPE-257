Senior Privacy Engineer
 
Responsibilities 

Partner closely with cross-functional partners across Engineering, Legal, Data and Product to embed sound technical privacy practices in everything we ship and help uncover risks before they become issues;
Contribute towards development of new privacy features, tools, and scalable solutions to optimize the work of the Privacy Engineering team and enable us to deliver privacy-centric solutions for our internal and external stakeholders
Foster a forum of data stewards to operate as an extended support team for privacy engineering initiatives. 
Collaborate with Privacy Engineering leadership for annual planning, prioritizations and progress management

 
Required Qualifications

B.S. or M.S. Computer Science or related field, or equivalent experience
Possess foundational knowledge, including the ability to write SQL queries, manage databases, and perform basic data analysis.
Privacy domain knowledge including an ability to identify common hurdles to and solutions for effective data privacy and security
Coding and code review experience in one or more general purpose language, including the ability to create simple programs and scripts along with diagnostic and troubleshooting skills
Ability to build relationships, collaborate, and influence stakeholders across multiple disciplines (engineering, legal, compliance, and data)
Ability to challenge the status quo while remaining pragmatic and championing trust with partners
Ability to evaluate new tools and processes to enhance data privacy capabilities
Ability to articulate complex technical concepts to both technical and non-technical audiences

 
Bonus Qualifications

Experience in privacy with one or more of the following privacy domains or technologies:

Privacy impact assessments, data inventory, user data protection, loss prevention, encryption, data anonymization, pseudonymization, 

Experience with data pipelines for large scale systems
Experience implementing data privacy in cloud environments (AWS, Azure) 
Experience implementing data privacy in large scale data system (Snowflake)
Experience implementing data privacy in NoSQL data stores including MongoDB
Knowledge of GDPR, CCPA  CPRA, or other privacy regulations
IAPP privacy certifications (e.g., CIPP, CIPM, CIPT)
