The Security Architect will be responsible for defining and advising on security requirements for solutions. This position is a security subject matter expert applying broad technical skills and security-focused expertise in solution architecture, technical architecture and softwaresystems engineering. This individual will lead cross-functional efforts and ensure solutions meet Security standards and industry best practice.
Job Responsibilities

Review solution design for compliance to Information Security Standards.
Provide security requirements and recommendations on solution design to technical and business teams
Provide oversight and leadership for automation
Review architecture diagrams, data flow diagrams, and network diagrams and advise on non-compliance issues.
Lead security architecture direction for solutions as well as Influence peers, cross functional partners, and IT leadership
Effectively communicate security risk to various audience levels
Assist in setting the strategic security direction and approach for utilizing new technologies
Utilize security expertise to influence approaches to solving strategic business needs
Understand security trends and direction technology must take to support the business

QualificationsExperience

Bachelors degree in Information Technology (or a related discipline) or relevant work experience
5 years of experience in information security, including engineering and implementation of complex security systems
5  years of experience with OSI model, creation and maintenance of flow diagrams, network diagrams, communicating and collaboration in company environments
Familiarity with industry benchmarks such as NIST-CSF, ISO 27001 etc.
Analytical and problem-solving skills
Solution development and thought leadership
Must have working knowledge of standard IT technologies and Cloud services
Demonstrated effectiveness working across multiple business units to achieve results
Solid understanding of Threat and Vulnerability Management, SIEM, EDR, DMARC, DKIM, DLP, and PKI
Proven Experience applying security architecture framework to IT solutions
Experience with cybersecurity tools, process, methods, and fundamentals
Significant technical expertise in cloud security, network security, application security
Programming or system administration experience are a plus
CISSP or equivalent security certification preferred
Experience with SentinelOne is preferred
