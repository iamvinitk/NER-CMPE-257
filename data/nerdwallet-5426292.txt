Position: DevOps Engineer
Location: Norwich, UK
Employment type: FTE
Department: Engineering
DevOps Engineers within NerdWallet are the driving force behind the technical progression of our cloud infrastructure and supporting systems. In this role you will be responsible for the day-to-day running, maintenance, and enhancements of those systems.
Working as part of a small and creative team supporting NerdWallet International, you will flex your technology muscles to help the team deliver on long term strategies alongside responding to short-term change to adapt to market conditions.
We are seeking a candidate who is passionate about enabling engineering peers to deliver software with minimal constraints and is motivated to take the lead in technology choices to support our SOA plans for 2024 and beyond.
If you were here 12 months ago, here are some things you might have worked on:

Launching multiple new product channels
Influencing large scale migrations and technical implementations
Growing the engineering influence across NerdWallet International and crafting our approach for future architecture

Where you can make an impact:

Design and own the development of high-performance, secure, scalable consumer-first experiences that empowers customers to make informed financial choices
Build out the infrastructure architecture that allows your peers to optimise frontend stack to seamlessly talk to our identity services, data layer or business partner APIs
Architect, develop and drive improvements to our evolving tech stack and be a key driver in back-end  data development - using the tech for the task, not letting technology dictate the solution
Implement product features that have a direct impact on user engagement and monetization
Build strong relationships with the wider NerdWallet DevOps  SecOps network to share learnings and best practices

You are:

Creative problem solver: Able to think around constraints
Communication and teamwork: You are able to work effectively with technical and non-technical partners such as engineers, UX, BI  data, commercial  compliance, and business development, to build features and high quality products
High-energy, company first, and possess a positive attitude: Being part of the Nerdy team we are committed to delivering fast, high quality code with the flexibility to adapt to change

Your experience:
We recognize not everyone will meet all of the criteria. If you meet most of the criteria below and youre excited about the opportunity and willing to learn, wed love to hear from you.

Strong expertise in AWS services including Lambda, ECS, CloudFront, EC2, Event Bridge, SQS, CloudWatch, SNS, and Systems Manager
Experience working with containerisation technologies such as Docker
Proficient in Python, especially within AWS Lambda Functions and systems development (CLI tooling)
Experience with Infrastructure as Code (IaC) tools such as Terraform, CloudFormation, CDK, and Serverless
Effectively work across team boundaries to establish overarching architecture, and provide guidance to individual teams
Familiarity with CICD pipelines and tools like GitLabs, GitHub
Additional experience which could make you a stand-out candidate:

Working with 3rd party managed hosting providers
Experience of migrating services across different infrastructure stacks
Proficiency in programming languages such as Go, JavaScript, and TypeScript
