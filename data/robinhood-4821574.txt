We are looking for a creative and detail oriented Workplace Operations leader who is passionate about creating safe and productive workspaces that not only reflect company culture, but energizes it! In this position, you will oversee operational strategy, implementation, and experience of multiple offices and operations teams. The Workplace Operations team represents the companys value system by creating new and maintaining existing office locations. You will be responsible for establishing a highly functional, organized, and fun workplace that meets the needs of a diverse employee population. The Workplace Operations team is dedicated to excellence through an execution mindset, attention to detail and a profound sense of pride in our work.
The role is located in the office location(s) listed on this job description which will align with our in-office working environment. Please connect with your recruiter for more information regarding our in-office philosophy and expectations.
What youll do

Managing, mentoring, and inspiring Workplace Operations Managers across multiple offices Globally
Identifying gaps and driving solutions to maintain, improve, and scale standard office operating procedures company-wide
Leading cross-functional projects by facilitating communication, coordination, planning, and execution across HR, Marketing, Real Estate, Physical Security, IT, etc.
Leading and supporting various ad hoc projects within the facilities and operation teams
Identifying and driving the development of tools, templates and processes to improve efficiency and effectiveness across the organization
Utilizing metrics to advise standards development through both quantitative and qualitative analysis
Developing and implementing sustainability programs in accordance with applicable state regions
Supporting and guiding business leaders and related functional groups on the best way to roll out initiatives based on workplace planning
Maintain high performance from our 3rd party suppliers through rigorous vendor selection, contract negotiation, controlling costs, reducing vendor-related risks and ensuring service delivery.

Bachelors Degree or equivalent, relevant experience
8 years of experience in Workplace Operations
5 years of experience managing a team of direct reports
Experience building and scaling operations in a high-growth, rapidly adapting environment
Experience managing, coaching, and retaining talent
Demonstrated change management, administration, and vendor management experience
Excellent written and verbal communication skills
Outstanding organizational and time management skills, with strong attention to detail and a proven ability to take ownership of multiple projects

Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously, work autonomously, and navigate ambiguity with ease.
