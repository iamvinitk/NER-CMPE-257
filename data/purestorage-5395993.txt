Act as quarterback across Product, Engineering, GTM and finance to develop and drive our public cloud storage-as-a-service business.

Build and know core customer journey metrics to ensure adoption of new products and features that drive customers to renew, buy or add on services.

Work closely with the executive team to establish the scope and goals of growth initiatives and provide leadership for the program teams.
