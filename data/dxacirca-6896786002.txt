 
Learning  Development Director 
Reports to: Senior Vice President (SVP), People Development 
SUMMARY 
The Learning and Development Director helps to assess agency-wide development needs to drive training initiatives. This person will identify, develop, and arrange suitable training solutions for employees. In this position you will actively search, creatively design, and implement effective methods to educate our teams and enhance performance. Additionally, you will evaluate the effectiveness of programs and ensure alignment with strategic business objectives while maintaining high quality standards in instructional design and development for all methods of program delivery. 
SPECIFIC JOB DUTIES 

Partner with SVP, People Development and members of the Global People team to help identify, develop and implement key learning programs, locally or regionally as needed
Contribute to the development and continuous improvement of learning curriculums ensuring strategic alignment of training with business goals and ensure recommended solutions are based on performanceskill and knowledge gaps and will help drive high performing teams 
Facilitate learning sessions for various audiences via multiple channels (remote, online, in-person); support train the trainer initiatives for our People team
Help leverage and scale local training initiatives more effectively across the network; identify opportunities to create synergies 
Collaborate with People team members and business leaders to understand their needs related to training and development by proactively interfacing with key stakeholders to understand, recommend and deploy effective solutions that align with our values and business priorities
Help design and develop programs and curriculum for particular audience segments, partner with internal subject matter experts andor external vendors to achieve defined training, learning and development objectives
Contribute to the development of a comprehensive communication strategy for training and development programs
Establish and maintain individual and organizational performance evaluation methods for training content, delivery, activities, engagement and outcomes. Utilize metrics to validate knowledge transfer and return on investment
Optimize learning management system (LMS) content to increase utilization; manage content library
Participate andor lead key work streams as related to systems implementation and roll out, with particular focus on learning and development modules
Develop competency-based playlists from LinkedIn Learning, create and manage communications to drive increased utilization and ROI 
Partner with NA People leads on other learning and development initiatives as identified (Steer Your Career, Healthcare Academy, systems training, etc.)
Manage knowledge library of curriculum and programming

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS 

8 years of learning and development experience including developing, implementing, and delivering training, inclusive of needs assessment and instructional design experience; minimum 2 years of experience acting as advisor to leaders on learning  talent development needs
Strong consultative approach and ability to provide counsel while also developing and delivering content that is inclusive and engaging 
Current knowledge of effective adult learning and development methods 
Strong project management skills
Effective communication skills, both written and verbal; candidate must be able to effectively write and present content that engages participants, while flexing style depending on situation and audience
Familiarity with e-learning platforms and practices
Proficient in MS Office and Learning Management Systems (LMS)
Experience in measuring and evaluating impact of learning solutions
Experience working with LMS systems and strong technology skills
Occasional travel within North America and ability to flex across time zones for meetings 
2-3 days in office required

PREFERRRED QUALIFICATIONS 

Bachelors degree, preferably in Learning Management, Instructional Design, Human Resource Development or a related field
Certification a strong plus (APTD, CPTD, CPLP, etc.)
eLearning design experience and demonstrated expertise in the technical development, implementation and troubleshooting of web-based training modules
Experience working in client servicebillable environment preferred; agency experience a strong plus
