We are looking for a Senior Data Analyst to join our Product Analytics team reporting into the Manager of Product Analytics. In this role, you will have the opportunity to work with exciting featuresproducts in the mobile AdTech space and partner with senior leadership across the business. We are looking for candidates who are quick learners, enjoy working in a horizontal fast paced environment, have strong problem-solving skills, and are passionate about using their technical prowess to build processes and reports that monitor and automate our advertising business and provide technical support for our advertising partners. This candidate should be self-motivated, results-oriented and sharp. 
Responsibilities:

Partner with internal business leads and external advertising customers to solve complex technical problems. Being a strategic critical thinker who is excited about tackling challenging open-ended problems and enjoys coming up with innovative solutions is key to this role. 
Analyze  debug data discrepancies between us and our partners. Taking end-to-end ownership of customers technical issues, including initial troubleshooting, identification of root cause and issue resolution and external communication is a key part of this role. 
Automate internal processes and script reports and alerts to monitor performance metrics that are key to the business. 
Write clear, detailed, and high quality project specs. Proactively manage timelines and priorities and carry an execution oriented mindset to work through tasks efficiently. 
Work cross functionally with engineering, business operations and business development to tackle various requests and manage the entire project from solution ideation, development, testing, deployment, measurement and refinementiterations.
Contribute to the development of support resources such as internal knowledge base articles and troubleshooting documentation to enhance the teams efficiency.

Basic Qualifications:

BABS degree in computer science, engineering, math, economics, finance, statistics or a related technical discipline OR previous experience writing code. 
4 years of experience in a technical or analytical problem solving role ideally working with a software product (doesnt have to be a job).
Have a deep and technical understanding of the Ad Tech ecosystem, clients, partners, and marketing technology
Proficient with Python and writing SQL queries
Exceptional analytical literacy and quantitative problem-solving skills. Has the ability to make data-driven decisions but can also look beyond the data and holistically assess situations.
Experience collaborating with highly technical engineering teams and driving complex projects from ideation to go live.
Excellent organizational and project management skills. An ideal candidate will be able to scope projects, estimate timelines, and coordinate with stakeholders to get the job done.
Able to be self sufficient, proactive and execute quickly when given autonomy. This is a small team that sits at the cross section of many other teams. Candidates need to be driven, independent, comfortable researching, building, and debugging solutions on their own.

Preferred Qualifications:

Previous experience in the mobile or AdTech space, ideally at a mobile measurement partner in a technicalsupport role
Prior experience in a startup environment
Experience with Salesforce
Experience with REST APIs.
