The Duolingo Thrive Program is an immersive 10-week summer training program that provides second-year students from underrepresented communities the opportunity to develop their illustration skills and experience what its like to work at Duolingo. 
As part of the Thrive Program, youll spend the summer learning Duolingos approach to illustration. Youll work with our Studio Artists and other trainees to complete projects that will sharpen your art and problem solving skills while receiving hands-on technical training, mentorship, and professional development. Well provide you with support and guidance to advance your skillset as you Illustrate Duolingo World Characters.
