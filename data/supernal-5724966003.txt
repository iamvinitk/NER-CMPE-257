What we do: 
The Battery Lab Safety  Test Technician I works to install, build, and test equipment in a functioning battery lab. The technician uses knowledge of wiring and circuitry, in order to properly set up, test and troubleshoot lab equipment. The role collaborates closely with the Energy Storage Systems team as well as other engineering departments.
This position will be required to work on-site 5 days a week.
What you can do:

Maintain operation of battery safety lab by creating and maintaining lab usage schedule aligned with project priorities
Create and implement battery safety lab SOPs
Ensure all safety procedures are followed and lab is operated in a safe manner
Identify key operational risks and work across teams to develop solutions, escalating where appropriate
Evaluate and ensure wiring and installation of custom testing equipment
Fabricate and modify testing fixtures and equipment
Install lab test chambers
Partner with sourcing to ensure pipeline of lab consumables
Collaborate with engineering for scheduling, test setup, BOM execution
Create, maintain, and communicate test schedules that align with milestones
May require up to 10 of domestic and international travel
Other duties as assigned

What you can contribute:

Associates degree in electronics, equivalent military electronics training or related field required
One (1) year experience working in a battery lab (an equivalent combination of education and experience may be considered)
Experience with safety requirements in a lab or machining environment
Exposure to and ability to meet safety requirements in a lab or machining environment
Ability to troubleshoot machinery
Ability to keep machinerylab operational
Excellent verbal and written communication skills
Sense of urgency, proactive delivery of communication and follow up
Excellent organizational skills and attention to detail
Must have the ability to independently prioritize and accomplish work within time constraints

You may also be able to contribute:

Experience in a startup lab preferred
Experience in battery environmental, safety  abuse testing and verification preferred
