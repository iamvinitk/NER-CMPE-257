Mission Summary
Motional is actively looking for a Hardware Design Engineer to join the team to focus on our next generation high performance computing platforms. Its an exciting time in the autonomous space and were looking to expand our engineering team onsite in the Silicon Valley office.
What youll be doing:

Working within a group of experienced Engineers, deliver High Performance Automotive grade ElectricalElectronic Hardware Platforms to support Motionals business objectives.
DevelopExecute Hardware Design documentation, Schematics, Code reviews and Test Plans.
Work collaboratively with partner organizations, to support PCB Layout, SignalPower Integrity, EMC, Test Engineering, Mechanical and Thermal Engineering.
Partner with HW Test Team to develop Test scripts, Checklists and support in all bring updebug activities
Support Manufacturing, Quality and Reliability teams to enable successful prototyping, NPI and Sustaining.
Some international travel may be required during critical project phases.

What were looking for: 

BSMS (MS Preferred) in ElectricalElectronics Engineering or equivalent professional experience 
2 - 5 years of experience with HW development of High Performance ComputeNetworking.
Understanding of high speed board design, simulation and validation techniques.
Effective documentation, presentation and collaboration skills.
Ability to follow tasksinstructions from Senior EngineersManagers.
Aptitude for learning in a fast paced environment.
Familiarity of hardware engineering principles including requirements gathering, architecturedesign, source control management, build processes, reviews, testing methods. 
Hands-on experience using system communication protocols such as PCIe, I2C, UART, SPI and Ethernet.
Knowledge of computing architectures and technologies such as FPGAs, GPUs, MCUs, SoCs and their associated hardware computing platforms.
Comfortable working within Google Docs, Atlassian and Safety frameworks.
Experience with JIRA, and Quality Management Systems.

Bonus points:
