Your mission
Wise has already pioneered new ways for people to transfer money across borders and currencies. Our customers can also manage their hard-earned money with the Worlds first platform to offer true international multi-currency accounts. Your mission is to work with our Treasury team to ensure that we remain compliant with our safeguarding and custody regulatory requirements globally, whilst enabling us to continue launching exciting new products and features. 

Heres how youll be contributing 
Enable Product Launches, acting as a subject matter expert on safeguarding regulations

Work with Regulatory Product Compliance regional team members and Product teams to identify key regulatory blockers, issues andor interpretation points regarding new product developments and deliver solutions
Develop an escalation framework for product teams to escalate queries and receive timely input and feedback
Work as part of a global team, building good relationships with regional Compliance Officers and supporting them with regulatory notifications  external reporting in relation to Safeguarding

Incident management

Assess the materiality and risk related to specific incidents and assist product teams in responding with the requisite urgency 

Remediation  Improvement projects

Work with Treasury Product to drive remediation projects in response to periodic audits and testing
Identify key regulatory priorities given regional risk exposure and use this to motivate and prioritise the most important fixes
Advising on regulatory risks by mapping our control environment to regional regulatory obligations


This role will give you the opportunity to

Grow your hands-on Fintech knowledge at full speed
Choose your path to impact  we believe people are most empowered when they can act autonomously. So rather than telling you what to do, youll work with your team to create a vision of your own. Of course, you can always gather feedback from smart, curious people across Wise but youll have the freedom to make your own calls

A bit about you

You have Financial Regulation advisory experience in a ComplianceLegal department, Risk and Controls function, Finance function,  or in a consultancylaw firm environment  
You have deep knowledge of safeguarding regulatory obligations for payments firms as articulated in the revised Payments Services Directive (PSD2) and the Payment Services Regulations 2017 and Electronic Money Regulations 2011
You are customer obsessed, meaning you go deep into understanding them, identifying where to alleviate pain points and deliver empathy 
Youre always up for the challenge of analysing interpreting complex regulatory frameworks and apply them effectively
Youre able to work effectively with, and unblock Product Managers and Engineers and provide practical, actionable, and value added recommendations
You can roll up your sleeves to solve complex problems through using regulations together with data to drive business decisions 

Some extra, but not mandatory experience
You have a good understanding of Product Management principlesYou have experience working in Fintech and interpreting regulatory requirements in multiple jurisdictionsKnowledge of safeguarding obligations in non-UKEU jurisdictions is also plus
