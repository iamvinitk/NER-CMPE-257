Insights from data power most decisions at Robinhood. Data Scientist here works with other data scientists and engineers to collect, organize, and analyze data from all parts of our business to power these decisions.
Every decision made at Robinhood is backed by data. Our company trajectory is defined by the systems, tools, and analytics powered by our exceptional team. We integrate multifaceted data streams such as rapidly changing market data, user data based on app activity, and brokerage operations data to perfect our processes and workflows.
We are looking for an experienced data scientist to join our experimentation platform team to lead our experimentation strategy and excellence. This role requires deep domain knowledge on experimental design and statistical methodology. Prior experience in developing  experimentation tooling capabilities is preferred. 
The role is located in the office location(s) listed on this job description which will align with our in-office working environment. Please connect with your recruiter for more information regarding our in-office philosophy and expectations.
What youll do

Shape the roadmap and experimentation best practices for teams across Robinhood
Evaluate current experimentation practices across the organization and identify opportunities for improvement
Improving the statistical methodology backing our experimentation platform
Enhance our experimentation quality and velocity
Develop best practices for experimentation capabilities across the organization
Partner with engineering team to develop platform capabilities to support a culture of experimentation and data-driven decision making
Consult with data science and engineering teams to provide guidance on statistically valid approaches to problems

What you bring

PhD or masters degree in a quantitative field such as mathematics, statistics, engineering or natural sciences.
Extensive hands-on experience with experimentation and causal inference
Solid understanding of statistical analysis and machine learning algorithms
Excellent programming skills, including expert-level familiarity with either Python or R programming languages
At least 6 years of experience in statistics, design of experiments, AB testing, causal inference
Demonstrates strong communication, proactiveness, creativity, and prioritization skills.
