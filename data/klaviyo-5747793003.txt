In this role, you will report to the Senior Manager, OPS  Analytics, and partner closely with Customer Success and Go-To-Market leadership to further develop upon and deliver robust revenue forecasting and trend insights and analysis for the Global Customer Success org (post-sales  retention). This role will be instrumental in driving greater insight and measurement into trends and opportunities within our existing customer base, improving accuracy and visibility into forecasting and growth expectations, and identifying and sizing strategies that represent greater retention and expansion outcomes for Klaviyo. 
The ideal candidate is intellectually curious, strategically and analytically minded, proactive in seeking solutions to challenging problems, and highly focused on creating solutions for our customers.
How you will make a difference:

Collaborate with senior leaders across the global customer success (post-sales) and Go-to-Market (GTM) organization, and own forecasting and revenue reporting and analysis to support strategic initiatives that shape the growth trajectory of Klaviyo
Develop reporting and analytics resources and forecasting models, in partnership with stakeholders in BI and Data Science; creating improved processes and tools that provide increased visibility into the drivers of customer success, and tell data driven stories about the health of Klaviyos customers
Plan and scope end-to-end projects, and create and drive forward roadmaps, by identifying business problems and selecting appropriate investigative testing and data solutions, and determining appropriate key performance indicators (KPIs) for the audience
Dissect trends, quantify opportunities, and undertake customer and driver based analytics and modeling to support business planning  strategic decision making
Analyze customer engagements and transactions across the post sales customer journey and systems to drive actionable insights, recommendations, and optimizations
Partner with peers in Go-to-Market Sales and Marketing Analytics to create comprehensive end to end customer acquisition and retention forecasting and CLV models and processes for improved forecasting accuracy
Leverage data to conduct deep dive analysis across different areas of the business (e.g. customer segments  regions  product and service types) to develop an understanding of key value drivers, and identify improvement opportunities
Implement processes to promote operational efficiency in a rapidly growing environment
Collaborate with cross-functional teams across disciplines such as CS Systems, Sales Operations, Marketing Operations, Product, Business Intelligence, Data Science and Finance

Who you are:

You have 8 years of professional experience, with direct forecasting and revenue analytics experience, from a role in business planning  finance  consulting; prior experience with SaaS or Subscription based business and retention modeling a plus
You possess well developed Excel, GSheet and financial modeling and reporting experience; experience with SQL and data visualization software skills (e.g. Tableau, PowerBI, etc) and experience with advanced analytics techniques is an advantage
You have well developed communication and organizational skills, a demonstrated ability working cross functionally to partner with Senior Business leaders, experience developing and actioning against a roadmap with clear milestones, and experience preparing materials for an executive audience
You balance attention to detail with swift execution, taking ownership and delivering work product in a timely manner to drive outcomes 
You are comfortable operating autonomously with a bias towards action, learning and constant iteration
