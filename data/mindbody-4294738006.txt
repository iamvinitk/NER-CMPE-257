We are looking for a Lifecycle Program Manager to manage our global referral program at ClassPass. Because of the virality of our product, referrals is one of the largest acquisition channels at ClassPass. This role will sit on the Lifecycle team, however they will also work closely with the acquisition team on reporting and forecasts.  
The Lifecycle team oversees initiatives like referrals, email leads and winback acquisitions, increasing user retention, average revenue per user and user communication through lifecycle channels. The person in this role will be responsible for execution, campaign set-up, and strategy of all elements of referrals while employing strong analytical skills and cross functional work to deliver growth in one of our most important channels. You should have an understanding of growth and how referrals deliver towards the broader business objectives.  
About the role

Execute on emails, modals, in-app messages within Braze and campaign launches globally to grow the referral channel 
Manage the experimentation roadmap for referrals to optimize performance through messaging, incentives, placements and other referral levers 
Understand the technicalities of a Content Management System to QA referral messaging shown in product around the referral invite, share and redemption flow 
Analyze referral test results and the referral funnel to identify actionable opportunities (external and internal levers) to improve performance and drive scale  
Leverage data to back proposals and recommendations based off of learnings 
Partner with the in-house creative team, BI team, and product team to innovate on referral capabilities and programs 
Present senior stakeholders on how referrals are tracking against forecasts and company goals 
Work closely with the acquisition team on forecasting and reporting  
Support and execute on other lifecycle functions and campaigns
