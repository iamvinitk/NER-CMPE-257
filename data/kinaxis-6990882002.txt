
About Kinaxis  
Kinaxis is the global leader in modern supply chain management. We serve supply chains and the people who manage them in service of humanity. Our software is trusted by renowned global brands to provide the agility and predictability needed to navigate todays volatility and disruption. We combine our patented concurrency technique with a human-centered approach to AI to empower businesses of all sizes to orchestrate their end-to-end supply chain network, from multi-year strategic planning through down-to-the-second execution and last-mile delivery. The Opportunity:
We are looking for a talented and ambitious leader to join our team.  As a manager on our RD team, youll work alongside a great group of people.  Youll have a choice of working in our spectacular new office in Ottawa (preferred) or working remotely from elsewhere in Canada in Easter Standard Time (EST).    
The core of our product is our in-memory database.  Kinaxis has its own proprietary database, optimized for Supply Chain.  With growing volumes of data and increasing adoption of our product, we have the opportunity to scale out our database and increase throughput.  We are in an era of fascinating and challenging work as we build the next-generation database architecture. 
Youll guide your team to solve complex problems, implement challenging and important new features, optimize our process, apply new and emerging technologies. 
If youre looking to do your best work, lead a talented team and work on an innovative product, this is the right opportunity for you.
