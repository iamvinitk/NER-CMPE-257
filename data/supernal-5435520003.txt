Supernal is at the forefront of creating emerging mobility solutions that will foster the development of human-centered cities. We are designing a completely new electric vertical take-off and landing (eVTOL) aircraft tailored to the mobility needs of future cities. This allows passengers a seamless intermodal journey that safely transports them to their final destination. We fuse research in autonomy, robotics, aviation and services to define a new category of mobility for the worlds communities. We believe in creative thinking and collaboration to help build a better mobility experience for everyone, improving peoples ability to move  whether for work or play.
What we do: 
The Software Architect is responsible for the aircraft embedded avionics computer application architecture. The role collaborates with other engineering colleagues, teams, customers, and contractors to test and evaluate aircraft technologies. The embedded applications for the Supernal avionics will be based on automatic code generation from models, both for the logic and the displays. The Software Architect will help with the design of the high-level structure of the applications and the interfaces with the software infrastructure.
This position will be required to work on-site 5 days a week.
What you can do:

Design and implement guidelinesprocedures for the development of embedded safety-critical avionics application functions
Manage the technical implementation of the avionics application architecture by the software team
Develop requirements for the embedded applications and application architecture
Develop some of the embedded avionics applications
Assist in building a lab for the testing and integration of the avionics applications and systems
Work directly with simulated, laboratory, and field test data to debug the system
Produce documentation in support of avionics systems certification to required standards
May require up to 10 of domestic and international travel
Other duties as assigned
