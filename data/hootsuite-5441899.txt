Were looking for a Global Sales Operations Business Partner to work directly with our global Sales leaders to identify, prioritize, and lead key projects and initiatives. As a trusted partner to our Sales teams you will develop a deep understanding of business opportunities and challenges, and collaborate with internal stakeholder teams in order to execute and deliver results that maximize business impact and improve the overall efficiency and execution of the global Sales organization. While working in a hybrid work arrangement; a blend of virtual (in accordance with Hootsuites distributed workforce strategy), and in Hootsuites Vancouver office, you will report to the Senior Manager, Sales Strategy  Analytics for a 6 month contract. 
 
WHAT YOULL DO:

Be a trusted advisor and consultant to the Sales Leadership Team by understanding global business and operational needs. You will be responsible for consolidating insights and recommendations in order to influence strategic plans while ensuring alignment across all regions.
Execute on prioritized projects and initiatives for the global Sales leaders by identifying opportunities for high business impact; documenting business requirements and current state operational processes; validating and refining potential solutions through data analysis and stakeholder feedback; and delivering on the appropriate course of action.
Utilize deep sales operations expertise in order to partner on key focus areas such as Go-To-Market, business and sales planning, territory and coverage optimization, demand management, pipeline and forecasting, and performance management.
Lead collaborative, cross-functional projects and project teams with the broader sales leadership team to negotiate stakeholder buy-in, keep initiatives on track, and report on status to leadership. Hold and be accountable to ensure deadlines and quality of deliverables meetexceed expectations.
Lead in the development and design of sales supporting processes to align to business needs as well as to sales operations process standards; champion the adoption and roll-out of sales enablement activities.
Work closely with internal teams to prioritize technology requests from the Sales organization and drive successful adoption of existing and new tools.
Influence and partner with the wider global Sales Operations team by sharing feedback, requirements and best practices to ensure efficient operation of the Sales organization.

8-10 years experience working in a Sales Operations role within a B2B sales organization, preferably Enterprise Software, SaaS, or Technology.
