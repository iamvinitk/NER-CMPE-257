Were building a world where Identity belongs to you.We are looking for a compliance analyst to join our GRC team and help us achieve our compliance measures. In this role you will be responsible for facilitating SOX and operational control activities, and for documentation across all SOX domains as well as for other control compliance requirements, as needed.  You will be a subject matter expert on best practices for BT controls.
Responsibilities:

Coordinate with system owners and own process of quarterly access reviews, change reviews and annual profile reviews
Function as a liaison between process owners, and internal and external auditors 
Ensure appropriate documentation of internal controls to comply with SOX requirements and internal policies
Collect, review and analyze data from information systems
Manage BT SOX internal control and process risk assessments to include planning, walkthrough and testing for BT controls
Collaborate with the system owners, and internal, and external audit teams to lead, plan, and execute SOX compliance
Identify any gaps in BT controls, establish a process to communicate gaps to relevant parties, and track remediation to closure
Design and implement BT Controls, Policies and Procedures
Perform risk assessments, monitor control deficiencies, and support management in the development of remediation plans to address deficiencies in a timely manner
Research, recommend and implement best practices in SOX compliance for continuous improvement of the program

Technical Bachelors Degree 
3 Years SOX program management experience
Detailed knowledge of internal control and compliance frameworks
Experience and thorough understanding of SOX review requirements and coordination (User Access Reviews, Change Reviews, Profile Reviews, etc.)

Ability to comfortably work in and move between both Excel and Sheets
