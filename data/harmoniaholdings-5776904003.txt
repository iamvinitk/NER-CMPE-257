Harmonia Holdings Group, LLC is an award-winning minority and female owned federal government contractor committed to providing innovative, high-performing solutions to our government clients and focused on fostering a workplace that encourages growth, initiative, creativity, and employee satisfaction.  
Position Summary:  
Harmonia Holdings Group LLC is currently looking for a smart and self-motivated Solutions Software Architect with the ability to take on and overcome challenges in a dynamic and collaborative team environment.  To perform this job successfully, an individual must possess the knowledge, skills, and abilities listed; meet the education and work experience required; and must be able to perform each essential duty and responsibility satisfactorily. Other duties, in addition to the below, may be assigned as necessary to meet business needs.  
Essential Job Functions:  

Plan, Design, and build multiple complex software applications.
Examine proposed business requirements, existing code, and the current database design to provide solutions and recommendations for system architecture.
Lead overall design, architecture, and data migration for new project modules in Microsoft Azure.
Collect code metrics and refactor solutions in the legacy system to reduce architecture and structural decay.
Prepare comprehensive System design and development specification documents for the detailed Design Phase of a project.
Direct and mentor other Software Engineers on various technologies including but not limited to C and .NET.NET Core, MVC, Entity framework, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, AJAX, Telerik and SQL Server.
Experience in application vulnerability and remediation process. 
Maintain a high-level roadmap that outlines the overall direction for the technologies and architecture of the system.
Produce presentation materials and conduct design presentations for the project stakeholders.

Minimum Required Qualifications:  

Bachelors or masters degree in computer science, Architecture or related degree preferred.  
At least 10 years of extensive work experience in various aspects of computer programming, including architecture, analysis, design, development, reengineering, support, and test.
At least 3 years of experience in Azure architecture and implementation.
Must have Microsoft Azure Solutions Architect Expert Certification.
Must be able to obtain and sustain a Public Trust clearance after employment.  

Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:  

Ability to design and develop robust, fast, scalable, cross-platform and cloud-based solutions with modernized User Interface (UI) on Azure.
Excellent oral and written communication skills. 
Strong customer serviceclient facing skills. 
Strong Analytical and problem-solving skills.
Comfortable with ambiguity and time spent outside of comfort zone acquiring new skillscertifications.
Proficient understanding of code versioning tools - such as Azure DevOps, TFS, Git. 
Ability to work under indirect supervision.
Work in tandem with highly collaborative multi-discipline design and development agile teams.

Other:  

Work Location: McLean, VA Office. This role is a hybrid position and needs someone who can be onsite 6 to 8 days per month as needed
