The Partner  Project Success Manager Opportunity
Reporting to the Head of Professional Services APJ, this role will be responsible for leading and managing complex identity access management (IAM) projects, as well as developing and supporting our partners to successfully deliver Okta services to our valued customers. 
 
What youll be doing 

Lead and manage delivery of complex identity access management (IAM) projects within tight project deadlines, budget, and demonstrated value of services
Initiate, plan, schedule, forecast, and provide oversight to the work of assigned professional services (PS) team members.
Think strategically across multidisciplinary teams establishing value-based relationships with our customers, relevant project stakeholders and partners
Build and convey compelling value propositions that will encourage partners (both emerging and strategic partners) to build Okta specialists and partner with Okta PS to co-sell and co-deliver customer solutions
Establish a vision for identifying delivery partners, increasing certifications, and preparing our service delivery partners to build and grow their identity management practice in partnership with Okta Professional Services
Formulate Oktas Partner business strategy in alignment with Okta stakeholders, Regional and Global PSM team
In collaboration with PS Sales and our network of delivery partners, identify customer opportunities and ensure accuracy of scoping and Statement of Work

 
What youll bring to the role

Deep understanding of IAM technologies, software delivery lifecycle, and SaaS
Strong project management background with excellent communication and coordination skills
Equipped with business focus, delivery mindset and sales knowledge that enables engagement at the executive level
Energy, flexibility and drive to create real and measurable business results
Ability to context switch and manage up to 20 engagements each week
Proficient business analytical skills to deliver insights feeding into services delivery and partner strategy
Excellent writtenverbal communication skills both in Japanese and English

Project Management Professional certification or other relevant accreditation
