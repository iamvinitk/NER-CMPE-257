As a Solution Architect, youll be responsible for leading the design, development, and implementation of innovative ServiceNow solutions that address complex business challenges with the goal of making an impact across the federal government.  You will collaborate closely with cross-functional teams, clients, and stakeholders to translate business requirements into effective technical solutions on the ServiceNow platform.

Because we know our future relies on our deep understanding and relentless focus on our people, we live by our mission: A passion for our people. Value for our customers. 
If you think you can see yourself delivering our mission and pursuing our goals with us, then check out the job description below! 
What youll do: 

Collaborate with business stakeholders to gather and analyze requirements and translate them into actionable technical specifications on the ServiceNow platform.
Lead solution design and architecture discussions, guiding the development team to implement best-in-class solutions that align with industry best practices and client goals.
Design and oversee the development of customized applications, integrations, workflows, and automation within the ServiceNow environment.
Provide expertise in configuring and customizing ServiceNow modules, including Incident Management, Problem Management, Change Management, Service Catalog, and more.
Define and document technical requirements, system architecture, and implementation plans for ServiceNow projects.
Drive technical feasibility studies and proof-of-concept efforts to validate solutions before implementation.
Collaborate with project managers to define project scope, timeline, and resource requirements.
Serve as a subject matter expert on ServiceNow best practices and architecture for both internal teams and external clients.
Conduct regular reviews of existing implementations to identify areas for optimization and enhancement.
Provide mentorship and guidance to junior developers and team members.

What youll need to succeed: 

At least 12 years of experience designing and implementing solutions on the ServiceNow platform, including experience as a Solution Architect.
In-depth understanding of ServiceNow capabilities, modules, and features, with relevant certifications such as ServiceNow Certified System Administrator and Certified Implementation Specialist.
Proven track record of successfully delivering complex ServiceNow projects, showcasing your architectural expertise.
Strong knowledge of IT Service Management (ITSM) processes and practices.
Proficiency in scripting languages like JavaScript, AngularJS, and HTMLCSS for customizations and integrations.
Experience with ServiceNow orchestration, automation, and integration with third-party systems using APIs, SOAP, REST, and other integration methods.
Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to articulate complex technical concepts to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.
Demonstrated leadership abilities and the ability to collaborate effectively in a team-oriented environment.
An active TSSCI clearance with CI poly preferred or Active TSSCI clearance with ability to pass a CI poly
Experience with Agile and DevOps methodologies is a plus.
