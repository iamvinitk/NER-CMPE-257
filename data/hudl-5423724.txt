Your Role
Were looking for an Engineering Manager to lead our talented team of engineers focused on developing Hudls human performance product family (centered on WIMU). These products consist of wearable trackers, smart docking stations and local positioning system antennas, which work together seamlessly to capture holistic athlete performance and positioning data. Youll be responsible for coaching a high performance team who develops the hardware, firmware and algorithms for human performance, providing both technical and non-technical mentorship while ensuring best practices are followed and team processes, tools, and methodologies are continuously improved. Collaborating with other teams and stakeholders to integrate WIMU with existing Hudl products and services is also a huge part of your role. This will include some global travel to other Hudl offices, suppliers, development and manufacturing partners.For this role, were currently considering candidates who live within a commuting distance of our offices in Den Bosch. But with our flexible work policy, there arent any current requirements for the number of days you come to the office.
Must-Haves

Experienced in managing engineering teams. You have a proven track record of leading successful cross-functional teams in complex projects.
A high level of technical proficiency. You have a strong technical background in embedded systems, high level system design and complex algorithms. Familiarity with peripherals such as Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, GPS, batteries and other key components is also a plus!
A user-centric mindset. Youre excited to help real people solve real problems.

Nice-to-Haves

A computer science, electrical engineering or other STEM degree. You have a solid academic foundation that you can use to inform your work.
