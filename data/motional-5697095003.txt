On our Perception and Prediction team, you have the opportunity to work with world-class ML engineers and research scientists, whose mission is to make self-driving vehicles a reality and to create positive social impact. Our team works on the tech stack responsible for perceiving the dynamic scenarios and predicting the future behavior of agents around our robo-taxi. We are looking for engineers who are passionate about Level 5 autonomous driving, excited by intellectual challenges,  and interested in pursuing career growth with a fast growing company.What Youll Do

Develop clean and extensible C software for the perception and prediction modules that sit at the core of autonomous driving systems and interface with all other key modules such as sensors, localization, and planning
Collaborate with deep learning experts in perception and prediction to port  Python model implementations to run in the production C stack.
Optimize the utilization of GPU resources and sharing of GPU access across multiple programs running on the same system
Apply deep software development expertise to teach others better software practices and principles

 What Were Looking For

Bachelors degree or higher in Computer Science or relevant field.
4 years of software development experience
Proficiency in C (i.e. C11 and beyond) 
Experience in TensorRT and porting deep learning algorithms from Python to C
Experience in software performance benchmarking, profiling, and optimization
Experience with multi-threaded programming
Strong problem-solving skills and desire to learn
Strong communication and interpersonal skills

Bonus Points

Experience in designing, developing, and optimizing CUDA code for deployment to GPU architecture
Experience with Pytorch
Experience with advanced TensorRT topics, such as low-precision inference, quantization, and custom plugins
