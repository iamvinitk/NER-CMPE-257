The Role
The Receptionist position is a valued team member of the Physical Security  Safety department and plays a critical role for Pure Storage. Customer First is one of the companys core values and we care deeply about creating a positive experience for all our customers - whether theyre true customers, partners, candidates, or employees helping make Pure the best storage company in the world. Our receptionists are often the first face of Pure Storage that our customers, partners, and candidates see. If youre someone who cares about creating a great first impression and is passionate about superior customer service, this might be the role for you!
The successful candidate will be flexible, organized, proactive and collaborative. Someone who can prioritize, multi-task, and operate in a dynamic and fast-paced environment.

What You Will Be Doing
Greet  help guests connect with their intended host at Pure Storage
Problem-solve all issues that arise at the front desk
Ergonomics program supportticket response, ordering, task management
Walk-up requests from employees including badging, parking validations, deliveries
Supports the Executive Briefing Center and events
Answer operational questions such as how tos, etc.
Sort mail for distribution and pick up by employees
General administrative support activities





WHAT YOULL NEED TO BRING TO THIS ROLE...





Office Experience of 2-3 years
Strong verbal and written communication skills, excellent interpersonal skills
Must be detail oriented
Friendly, energetic and a positive attitude
Must be able to multitask and can work well independently with little supervision
Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment, managing changing priorities with constant
interruptions
Demonstrates flexibility, organization, and self-motivation
Proficient with the latest version of Google Workspace (sheets, docs, slides, calendar, gmail)
