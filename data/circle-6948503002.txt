Circle is looking for a Product Manager to lead the build out of our liquidity infrastructure that will power the seamless movement of money between fiat and crypto around the world. The Liquidity team delivers the infrastructure that allows all of our products including stablecoin issuance and redemption to access global banking and payment rails. You will work closely with customers, engineering, as well as a broad set of internal teams such as treasury, compliance and legal to build global fiat on and off ramps for our stablecoins that are used by thousands of businesses and impact millions of end users around the world.
