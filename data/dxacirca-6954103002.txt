
VP Director, Social and Digital 
Los Angeles 
 
Golin LA is looking for a VP, Social and Digital Experience to join (and grow) our social and digital team. You will play a vital role as mentor for a team of digital and social media strategists and tacticians, while delivering creative and results-driven solutions for our clients.
 
This all-encompassing digital leadership role will have you identifying and providing POVs on real-time opportunities, issues and trends, as well as creating and executing large and small-scale marketing campaigns for clients. Your role will include fostering integration among IATsocialPR teams, driving channel and social strategy for a range of clients, and driving and overseeing content creative to run across a variety of digital platforms (including organic and paid) to drive optimal results.
 
Golin is a progressive public relations agency with PR, digital and content at its core. Inclusion is a key value for Golin, and we are focused on establishing a culture that is intentionally inclusive, potently diverse and uncompromisingly equitable. Our expertise ranges from brand-building and cutting-edge digital content to corporate reputation, and measurement. Golin aligns earned-first, data-driven creative with the customer journey through our collaborative G4 Model to deliver maximum impact for clients. Our roster includes many of the largest direct-to-consumer companies in the world.
 
What Youll Do:

Serve as the social and digital client relationship leader (provide senior counsel, create and manage client budgets, lead the team and our daily work in digital)
Own the digital strategy process; proactively identify needs, drive trend research, lead the brainstorms, and develop, pitch and secure award-winning work
Regularly share trends and perspective with Golin team and our clients
Work directly with account, influencer and analytics teams to develop inspirational multi-channel strategies to help elevate our clients brands 
Provide strategic counsel and advise on digitalsocial campaigns and tactics across the ecosystem of paid, earned, shared and owned media
Play a leadership role across the agency
Oversee work of junior employees across social media strategy, content creation, community management, analytics and more
Work with the team to create ongoing compelling content calendars, creative and copywriting
When necessary, roll up your sleeves and get into execution mode as both player and coach
Take a proactive approach to leading and building business, strategy, and platforms
Identify client objectives and learn how to integrate digital and traditional communication strategies
Lead and mentor junior to mid-level team members to build a culture of high performance
Collaborate in the practices management and growth
Bring analytics and insights to the table through your own experience, as well as through the broader Golin team

What You Have:

Bachelors degree in marketing, communications or related field with 10 years of experience driving and executing digital  social media  influencer marketing campaigns
Impeccable client relationship skills and experience
Experience managing large and always on digitalsocial programs from start to finish
Ability to work with vendors and manage small and large scale budgets
Ability to galvanize internal teams to approach communications with a digital-first mindset
Comfort operating in ambiguous situations and navigating challenging situations
Strong creative and strategic thinking with the ability to deliver innovative programs and approaches
Passion for client service and an ability to juggle multiple projects and changing priorities
Experience managing and leading an account team and working with large fully integrated teams
Excellent verbal, written, presentation and problem-solving skills
Ability to execute and measure multifaceted campaigns and day-to-day communications initiatives
A strong understanding of traditional media and content marketing strategies, ability to integrate campaign strategies and tactics across earned, owned and paid media, and apply analytics to measure results and provide insights
Poise and calm under pressure and a desire to immerse yourself in a clients business to become a true partner
Confidence when presenting no matter the size of the group or situation
A solutions-oriented mindset
A self-starter mentality  you dont wait for someone to tell you what to do next
