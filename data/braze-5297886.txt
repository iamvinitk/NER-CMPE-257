Were looking for a world class Senior Recruiter to help build and scale our Sales team. This person should be excited to design and execute customized candidate recruitment plans in partnership with recruiters and hiring managers.
WHAT YOULL DO

Source, recruit, and hire top talent
Manage the end-to-end recruitment lifecycle from sourcing to offer acceptance
Partner with hiring managers on their needs and priorities to implement best-in-class talent acquisition strategies.
Screen relevant candidates from outreach, inbound applications and referrals. 
Facilitate debrief sessions to identify the best talent for each role
Leverage data in our applicant tracking system (Greenhouse ATS) to monitor the effectiveness of all recruiting efforts, bringing strategic insights and proposed solutions to our business leaders.
Utilize broader trends and data in the business and industry to continuously improve the companys brand from a hiring perspective, scale recruiting processes and systems, and contribute to the broader talent acquisition strategic planning process
Mentor and provide guidance to other members of the team as needed
Contribute to TA team OKRs and projects as needed
Foster and maintain a productive relationship with local People Business Partner teams to ensure hiring strategy alignment with departmental objectives and workforce planning

WHO YOU ARE

6 years of experience in a recruitment role, either as a recruiter, sourcer, or recruitment coordinator either internally or at a 3rd party staffing agency
Experience with Sales candidates in the SaaSCloud industry
Minimum 3 years of experience in an in-house full cycle recruitment role, preferably at a startup or technology company
Track record of successfully filling challenging senior level positions, including up to the VP-level
Ability to forge strong partnerships with senior leaders across the business and thoughtfully consult with senior stakeholders as needed
Contribute to process improvements in partnership with Talent Management and TA Operations teams, playing an active role in formulating recruiting strategies for the teams that you are supporting 
Leverage data to proactively diagnose potential issues and make recommendations to improve the recruitment process
Proven organizational skills and attention to detail
Ability to manage projects and simultaneously prioritize the talent acquisition flow in an environment with competing demands
Prior experience managing difficult searches, ranging from senior individual contributor roles to senior leadership
Proficient with LinkedIn Recruiter and other sourcing tools
Prior experience partnering with internal stakeholders to identify new sources of candidates with the goal of diversifying our candidate pools
Experience with a modern applicant tracking system, preferably Greenhouse ATS
