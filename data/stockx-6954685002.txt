The Director of Operations is the leader of our facility in West Caldwell, NJ and a key member of the leadership team responsible for overseeing all aspects of the operation including but not limited to facility PL, order fulfillment, labor management, quality assurance, inventory accuracy, safety, and materials procurement. The Director, Operations will work cross-functionally with the business teams as they develop short- and long-term growth strategies. The Director of Operations will also be responsible for additional projects to support the growth, efficiency, and development of the global network.
What you will do

Holistically oversee the success of the operation, maintaining cross-functional relationships with all key internal stakeholders within the building(s) and throughout the company to achieve planned positive results
Overall site management with ultimate accountability for production, staffing, inventory, budgets, long-term planning, maintenance, supplies, security, and safety
Guide, coach, develop management and support teams to establish a culture of honesty, integrity, and respect consistent with StockXs goal for inclusion and positive work environment
Establish and maintain a safe and secure work environment, working with Environmental, Health, and Safety team as necessary
Ensure that Managers, Operations and Team Leaders responsibilities are driven to meet the daily business demands and productivity targets while maintaining highly engaged teams
Ensure accurate, expected quality, and on-time fulfilment within established SLAs for all customer orders

Proactively identify, lead and support process improvement initiatives to optimize throughput, quality, and cost for a healthy growth environment
