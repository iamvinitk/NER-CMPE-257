Client Products  Emerging Tech
The Client Products  Emerging Tech team is a newly established team that builds impactful product features for Airbnbs Guests and Hosts with new and existing technologies, as well as horizontal components such as navigation that aim to provide a cohesive user experience. The Senior Android UX Engineer joining this team will play a critical role in the end-to-end development of new product areas that leverage emerging technologies. They will collaborate closely with Design partners to rapidly ideate, prototype, develop, and ship innovative product experiences.
The team is part of the Client Foundation  Products organization, which is responsible for Airbnbs product infrastructure including Design Language System (DLS), UI frameworks, media frontend, and data access across different client platforms (Android, iOS, and Web). It plays a central role in Airbnbs overall client strategy, contributes to libraries such as Lottie and Epoxy, and has more recently enabled Airbnbs transition to Jetpack Compose.
Learn more about Mobile Engineering at Airbnb: Making Airbnbs Android App More Accessible, Introducing Showkase for Jetpack Compose, and Page Performance Score on Android. Also, meet our Client Foundation  Products leader, Jordanna Kwok!
