NerdWallet is growing its CRM initiatives and is in search of a CRM Marketer to lead the development, implementation, and optimization of email and push campaigns. These campaigns will use dynamic data and event triggers to onboard, activate, cross-sell, and retain our valued members. Specifically, you will craft and execute strategies to engage our product active members, utilizing data-driven insights to deliver highly personalized communications to drive conversion.
If you were here 6 months ago, here are some things you might have worked on: 

Channel expansion - Exploring strategies to maximize user engagement and retention by leveraging high-touch communication channels.
Lifecycle optimization - Crafting approaches to capture users attention within the first 30 days of registration, and finding new ways to win users back through personalized and relevant content. 
Targeted cross-sell - Leveraging data to drive revenue conversion by effectively cross-selling users on their next best financial product.

Where you can make an impact: 

Develop and launch campaigns: Youll own the strategy and execution for your verticals for CRM marketing strategy across email, push, in-app, and SMS.
Segment audience effectively: Identify and target the right audience by leveraging data-driven insights.
Leverage high-impact data: Identify opportunities to harness high-impact data for personalization.
Execute lifecycle strategies: Strategically map out user journeys, designing user flows that foster lasting retention and loyalty.
Work cross-functionally: Youll drive alignment with internal cross-functional and external solution partners including Product, Engineering, Analytics, Creative, Sales and Program Management.
Test and Optimize: Continuously optimize campaigns through rapid AB testing, measuring impact, and iterating for improved results.
Measure campaign performance: Monitor and analyze campaign performance data to inform and recommend strategic initiatives.

You are:

A results-driven marketer with a proven track record of delivering the right message to the right audience.
Highly analytical, able to synthesize insights from various campaigns to shape strategy.
Skilled in balancing strategic thinking with hands-on execution.
Adaptable and able to thrive in a dynamic environment.
A natural leader and team-player who excels in fostering effective collaboration.

Your experience:
We recognize not everyone will meet all of the criteria. If you meet most of the criteria below and youre excited about the opportunity and willing to learn, wed love to hear from you.

2 years of marketing experience (lifecycle marketing experience is a plus).
2 years of experience developing marketing campaigns using an email campaign management system (e.g., Iterable, Braze, Pardot, Marketo, Responsys, ExactTargetSalesForce Marketing Cloud, etc.).
Experience collaborating with multiple stakeholders in a matrixed organization.
Proven track record of successfully ideating and executing marketing campaigns independently.
