Robinhood is hiring an Associate Creative Director for our Creative team. As a key member of the Creative team, you will be involved with, and have responsibility for, all levels of the design process - from defining strategic project goals, to establishing conceptual and stylistic direction for work. Having a deep understanding of production processes is also required.
This person in this role should be highly creative, passionate about the industry, have strong communication skills, exhibit phenomenal attention to detail, easily build rapport with others and excel at managing and motivating team members.
This role is hands-on and would suit a creative who still enjoys daily design work. Youll work hand in hand with peers to develop, review, and guide art direction, copywriting, tone of voice, look and feel, motion graphics, illustrations, video, and more.
The role is located in the office location(s) listed on this job description which will align with our in-office working environment. Please connect with your recruiter for more information regarding our in-office philosophy and expectations.
What youll do

Works to provide clarity, communication, and leadership that moves creative work forward
Collaborate and present to agency partners, cross-functional leaders, and senior executives
Contribute to culture of creativity and provide functional, technical, and creative leadership
Guide and direct projects to ensure creative work is to the standard and completed before necessary deadlines

What you bring 

Strong narrative building skills and understands how to develop a brands voice
3-5 years of experience in a lead level in either a creative-focused agency or in-house at a leading brand
Past experience in crafting work in the mediums of social media, editorial, conceptual campaigns, photography, video, and product launches
A strong knowledge of the fundamentals -- interaction design, brand development, digital marketing, and the creative process
