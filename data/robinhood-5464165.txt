We are looking for an experienced and innovative attorney to be responsible for a range of legal matters arising from Robinhoods marketing and communications initiatives and programs. You will work closely and collaboratively with Robinhoods marketing and communications organizations. This is an exciting opportunity for a highly adaptable and collaborative team player to join a growing company and contribute to Robinhoods mission to democratize finance for all.
It is preferred that this role is located in one of the office locations listed on this job description which will align with our in-office working environment. This position is only eligible for remote work in limited geographies within the US where we do not have physical office locations. Please connect with your recruiter for more information regarding our in-office philosophy and expectations.
What youll do

Work collaboratively with marketing and communications teams in support of initiatives and campaigns, including reviewing claims and collateral
Conduct legal reviews of marketing communications, in particular business and product marketing communications to ensure compliance with relevant laws and regulations, including those related to consumer protection, advertising standards, including FTC, FINRA and SEC marketing regulations. 
Identify legal risks associated with our marketing efforts and provide pragmatic advice to help our marketing and communications teams manage legal requirements while achieving their business goals
Work cross functionally across the enterprise with stakeholders in marketing, compliance, product, business operations and other subject matter experts as well as executive leadership. 
Train clients and colleagues on legal and regulatory issues related to marketing and communications and changes in the regulatory landscape.
Serve as a subject matter expert within the company for advertisingmarketing law issues, and staying up-to-date on marketing-related and communications-related legal issues including recent enforcement actions.  
Advise on marketing and communications strategy

What you bring 

J.D. degree from an ABA accredited law school (or foreign equivalent)
Membership in at least 1 U.S. State Bar, with good standing
4 years of legal practice, including support of marketing and communications initiatives and programs, ideally with both a law firm and as in-house counsel within a fast-paced financial services, fintech andor payments company
The ability to provide clear, concise legal advice, work effectively cross-functionally to drive to results
Extraordinary interpersonal and communication skills; phenomenal ability to communicate information effectively internally and externally, including to non-legal audiences, and to drive cross-functional alignment and action
Execution-oriented mindset with the humility, drive, and scrappiness to get things done
Experience with publicly traded companies and advising PR and communications teams regarding relevant issueslawsregulations
Deep background in the financial services, fintech or payments industry
Knowledge of online marketing channels, social media platforms and recent trends.
