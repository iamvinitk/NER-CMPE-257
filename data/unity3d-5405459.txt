The opportunity
Unity is a leading business platform enabling mobile content creators to prosper in the app economy. Our mission is to help app developers turn their apps into scalable and sustainable businesses, with the most robust, comprehensive business platform available. Ultimately, we help customers focus on what they do best  creating great apps and user experiences  while we enable their business expansion in the app economy.
The Offerwall is an in-app marketplace that provides a value exchange between app publishers, advertisers, and consumers. Publishers integrate the Offerwall to make more ad revenue within their apps, advertisers promote their products to the publishers user base to acquire quality traffic, and consumers engage with the ads within the Offerwall in return for in-app rewards to continue their user journey.
We are looking for an experienced product manager to join our team to lead our publisher dashboard, to ensure were providing an efficient and innovative experience for the app developers who monetize via our platform.
Our publisher dashboard is a key part of our platform, enabling app developers to configure their Offerwall set up, measure the Offerwalls performance, and optimize their integration to drive higher revenue.
What youll be doing 

You will own the roadmap for our publisher dashboard, working with internal and external business stakeholders to ensure we are prioritizing and delivering the right features and projects.
Youll play a key role in managing documentation that clearly explains how our customers can get the most value from our feature set.
This is a customer facing role, and youll be required to join quarterly business reviews with our customers to both present new feature releases, and gather feedback on future initiatives.

What were looking for 

A validated experience as a product manager for B2B and B2C  experience working with within mobile ad tech at a previous role
Experience in handling simultaneous projects, priorities and proven team leadership abilities in a fast-paced environment
Ability to define a compelling product, path to market, and then evangelize that vision internally and externally while taking full ownership of the projectproduct
Ability to collaborate and work efficiently with teams in various locations worldwide
Strong product management, organizational and execution skills.

Life at Unity
