You will be a part of the Integrated Operations Program Management Office to facilitate the end-to-end lifecycle of GTM interrelated projects within a program.
KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:

Manage the end-to-end lifecycle of interrelated projects within a program, and ensure projects are delivered on time and within scope: define project objective, scope, and resource requirements; establish timelines and milestones with gantt charts; track and minimize risks; identify and assist in the resolution of conflicts within and between projects; manage project budgets and resource allocation; and develop methods to monitor project or area progress
Build, drive,  enforce business centric project management governance, processes, project plans, schedules, budgets, and templates for cross-functional, transformational projects supporting various organizations that include but are not limited to: IT, Sales, Channel, Marketing, Product BUs, and more
Manage and lead 2 or more overarching programs at the same time 
Take meeting minutes and communicate project updates to project team members, stakeholders, steering committee members, and C-level executives 
Independently research, analyze, and recommend solutions and best practices while being comfortable challenging status quo
Think strategically about issues leading to recommendations and maximization of ROI
Work with business stakeholders to identify OKRs, KPIs, and key metrics of success to measure projectprogram progress 
Review and analyze business requirement documents and shepherd the process of clarifying business outcomes with ITs solutioning plans 
Proven ability to communicate technical concepts and business impact to team members and leadership in a manner that is readily understandable and actionable 
Manage and solve scope creep, change management, and interventions to ensure project goals are achieved
Identify and effectively manage risks. Drive escalations up to C-level executives to resolution
Document and lead retrospectives at the end of each project or program launch and incorporate feedback into Lessons Learned 
Partner with IT, Finance, Product, Sales Operations, and Manufacturing Operations teams from a resourcing and SME stand point, as well as bridge the gaps and silos for cross-functional workstreams 
Effectively guide and mentor supporting project manager  project coordinator on large transformational projects
Partner with PMO leadership and peers to scale PMO Center of Excellence; implement org-wide project management best practices, frameworks, processes, and templates
