Harmonia Holdings Group, LLC is an award-winning minority and female owned federal government contractor committed to providing innovative, high-performing solutions to our government clients and focused on fostering a workplace that encourages growth, initiative, creativity, and employee satisfaction.  Harmonia Holdings is seeking multiple exceptional Business Systems Analyst to join our rapidly growing team. The Senior Business Analysts primary responsibility will be to gather, analyze, evaluate, document, validate, and refine the needs of all business stakeholders and effectively communicate requirements to the software development team, applying Agile principles and practices throughout the project duration. The ideal candidate will be experienced in interacting with product owners to gather requirements and will be an excellent communicator who can simplify complexity and bring teams together around ideas. A Business Analyst at Harmonia must have a strong customer focus and enjoy working in a fast-paced environment.
Essential Job Functions, Skills and Abilities:

Gather, analyze, evaluate, document, and refine requirements for software systems
Communicate effectively with business, demonstrate an understanding of their priorities and processes, and lead scaled agile planning for the team
Support team in design, development, testing, and maintenance of software applications
Persist in solving complex business and technical challenges; troubleshoot issues
Collaborate with team members; serve as Scrum Master and support team in meeting commitments
Document business process flows, critical business information, and reports
Achieve quality measures and metrics through agile processes and practices
Coordinate requirements, schedules, and activities; contribute to team meetings; support troubleshooting efforts across multiple environments and operating platforms
Grow the teams knowledge base by contributing to the team wikis
Understanding of change control processes
Business and technical requirements analysis
Ability to develop stories in JIRA, to work with Developers for time estimation for work.
Ability to Manage the Kanban boards and run DSUs and Grooming Sessions
Ability to multi-task efficiently and effectively with large remote team
Excellent technical writingdocumentation skills
Excellent communications skills
Must be a self-starter, self-learner, and take leadership of responsibilities
Must be able to work in a fast-paced environment

 Mandatory Requirements:

10 years of experience working on agile software development projects
U.S. Citizen
Public Trust clearance
Ability to travel 1-4 times a year
