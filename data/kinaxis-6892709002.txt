About the role
As the Vice President,Cloud Services, you will lead the team that designs and implements automated cloud services to provide a secure and highly available environment for Kinaxis Inc. RapidResponse Software as a Service offering.
What you will do

Responsible for the Kinaxis Inc. Cloud Engineering offering delivery and management of operational planning, strategic efficiency measures, infrastructure availability, and security policies implementation.
Collaborate with senior executives across Kinaxis Inc. to develop and drive the long-term cloud services strategy to accomplish the organizations business objectives.
Uses internal system and user data to drive new business case opportunities for efficiency for operation effectiveness of the Kinaxis Inc. SaaS platform.
Takes the initiative to develop strategies that will anticipate trends and drive business decisions for new measurements of success.
Acquire a thorough understanding of the industry, business, and its clients, including specific challenges, anticipating industry trends, risks, and potential impacts, and using information to identify strategic opportunities for new services or manage organizational risk.
Select appropriate Cloud Services to design and deploy an application based on given requirements for Kinaxis Inc. software.
Manage and orchestrate the migration of enterprise products from data centers to public cloud providers and guide the development of infrastructure automation tooling.
Ensure high availability of the platform through thoughtful monitoring, release practices, and an efficient incident management system.
Define annual and quarterly priorities of projects for the department; ensure follow-up and successful execution of projects within the defined timeframes.
Deliver operational efficiency and transparency through metric-driven strategies and reporting.

What we are looking for

10 years of experience in progressive cloud services experience.
5 years at the senior leadership level.
Extensive experience with private and public cloud solutions.
Ability to communicate complex, technical concepts to executive staff, business sponsors, and technical resources concisely.
Extensive experience in presenting and narrating business outcomes to senior executives through complex data sets.
Experience collaborating with technology RD teams and leaders to develop infrastructure and operational solutions, including deployment and automation for new products and services.
Proven experience migrating  implementing both cloud and container technologies.
Experience managing Cloud Services data centers in multiple countries.
Strong problem-solving and vendor management skills; highly organized, detail-oriented, and able to work effectively in a fast-paced, dynamic environment.
Successful track record of balancing internal and external customer needs.
Interpersonal and leadership skills, collaboration, facilitation, and negotiation skills.
A passionate leader with expertise in people and leadership development, with a demonstrated history of driving strategic talent and skills programs that enable team growth.
