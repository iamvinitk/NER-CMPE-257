About the job
Twilio is seeking a technical leader for our Solution Advisors on the Digital Sales team in our Twilio Communications business. Twilio makes it easy for developers and businesses of all-sizes to sign up and get started using our APIs. The Digital Sales team is responsible for scaled acceleration of our self-service funnel, growth plays for existing customer cohorts, and technical advisory strategies to help self-service customers launch projects faster.
In this role, you will work closely with Sales, Solution Engineering, Marketing, and Support leaders to set the strategy and direction of our technical engagement in the customer onboarding cycle and growth stages of existing self-service accounts. You will manage a global team of Solution Advisors and be accountable for their development, performance, and operating model within Digital Sales. 
Responsibilities
In this role, youll:

BE AN OWNER: Partner with the Digital Sales leadership team to set our strategy for finding, closing and supporting opportunities in our sales funnel and in our existing customer base.
BE INCLUSIVE: Work across functions to ensure collaboration for shared goals and appropriate capacity for your team.
DRAW THE OWL: Build and roll out programs that enable the Solution Advisors role to scalably  accelerate our self-service funnel.
EMPOWER OTHERS: Attract, develop, and motivate a team of hard-working Solutions Advisors, continually educating your team and seeking innovative solutions for improving your team. Ensure that your team has the support that they need to be successful as well as providing direct mentoring and guidance on a day to day basis.
WRITE IT DOWN: Lead the technical strategy, determine what role Solutions Advisors should play in accelerating new sign-ups, and how to deploy the team most effectively. Measure and communicate results  use data to communicate results and make decisions.
RUTHLESSLY PRIORITIZE: Set clear expectations for your team, remove obstacles where possible, and actively communicate tradeoffs to stakeholders when deciding where your team needs to focus.
WEAR THE CUSTOMERS SHOES: Engage with Product, Support, Sales, Operations, Legal, Security, Engineering, and every single other department as necessary to make your customers successful on our platform.
