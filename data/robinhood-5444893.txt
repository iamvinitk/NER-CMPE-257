The Internal Communications teams mission is to inform and inspire Robinhoodies through communications that connect them to our business and culture.
As an Internal Comms Manager, you will own the communication strategy and messaging for programs, changes, and news coming from our PeopleX (HR) function (i.e. compensation, benefits, and other employee programs). You also will lead manager-facing communication efforts, and serve as the comms point for other GA functions such as ethics, compliance, learning, legal, community affairs, finance, and more.
The role is located in the office location(s) listed on this job description which will align with our in-office working environment. Please connect with your recruiter for more information regarding our in-office philosophy and expectations.
What youll do

Develop strategy and messaging to communicate HR news to the company
Improve manager-facing communications channels and cadence
Own communications originating in our administrativeservice functions
Manage internal information and collaboration tools, such as our editorial calendar and wiki page
Partner with HR teams for story mining and amplification, including IEB, recruiting, talent brand, and more
Support corporate communications efforts, such as deals, earnings, and other announcements

What you bring 

4 years of experience in Internal Communications, including partnering with internal HR teams to communicate changes and updates to the broader org
Exceptional writing, editing, and presentation skills
Experience managing programs, events, and organizational change
Eagerness and aptitude to learn and explain broad subject matter quickly
Expertise with information and collaboration apps
Track record of cultivating community and culture
