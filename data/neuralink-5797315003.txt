Company Description:
We are creating the future of brain-computer interfaces: building devices now that have the potential to help people with paralysis regain mobility and independence and invent new technologies that could expand our abilities, our community, and our world.Team Description:
The facilities team is responsible for the maintenance, operation, and management of our buildings and facilities. Our team comprises a range of professionals with different areas of expertise, such as maintenance technicians, electricians, plumbers, HVAC technicians, groundskeepers, security personnel, and administrative staff. Our main goal is to ensure that our facilities operate efficiently, effectively, and safely while meeting the needs of the people who use them. Members of the team must be able to work collaboratively, prioritize tasks, and adapt to changing situations. 
Job Responsibilities: 

Perform plumbing, electrical, HVAC, and carpentry work
Construction, maintenance, repair, remodeling, and alteration of building structures and facilities according to work orders or verbal instructions
Install finished hardware such as door locks, hinges, and door checks
Install floor coverings such as linoleum tile, construct minor buildings or small additions to existing structures, and make minor roofing repairs
Install plumbing fixtures
Assist and support various contractors and engineers on job sites
Safely operate machinery such as boom lifts, scissor lifts, forklifts, etc

Key Qualifications:

Experience working with multiple trades during construction
Strong verbal and written communication skills
Ability to read construction drawings and interpret specification sheets
Working knowledge of industry software such as Microsoft products
Ability to multitask, prioritize, and work in a fast-paced environment

Preferred Qualifications:

Evidence of exceptional ability
Strong desire to be a part of a world-class team focused on achieving amazing results
