About the job
Twilio is looking for a skilled Technical Program Manager to join the Communications BU, who can facilitate our teams to reach our aggressive product delivery and engineering scale goals. Acting as the hub in a world of spokes, with technical aptitude and a product mindset. 
Responsibilities
In this role, youll:

Lead teams collaboration
Deftly identify dependencies and risks
Resolve blockers 
Structure and lead complex cross-functional programs for product and cross-company initiatives
Drive towards continuous improvement through analyzing data and inspect  adapt practices to work with agile teams to improve project delivery
Track and report progress
Leverage best practices to ensure predictable and timely delivery of our programs
You will be partnering and collaborating with product managers, engineering counterparts, and stakeholders across BUs, to craft and deliver on the vision, strategy, and roadmap for Communication Products.
Support the transparency and communication of the health and progress of an overall program that you are coordinating.
