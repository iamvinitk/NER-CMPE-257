Would you like to work on a new IP and new genre at Bungie that combines amazing action feel with lighthearted and whimsical characters? Are you drawn to games with stylized art direction? As a Senior Graphics Engineer at Bungie, you will help create a joyful graphical experience for millions of players. You will work closely with artists to bring to life a buoyant, playful visual style that champions gameplay readability. Most importantly, you will work with a fun, dedicated, cross-discipline team united around our mission to create a positive impact in peoples lives as we bring a new Bungie world to life.
RESPONSIBILITIES

Design, build, extend, document, and maintain graphics features and associated tools and workflows in Unreal Engine, with minimal oversight.
Collaborate daily with a cross-disciplinary team to realize the artistic vision for the game.
Provide strategic input for the scalability and performance of proposed game features across multiple platforms.

REQUIRED SKILLS

Ability to write, debug, optimize, and maintain production-quality C and HLSL code.
Proficiency in 3D math, data structures, and algorithms
Familiarity with industry-standard graphics techniques
Ability to communicate with customers of different technical and non-technical backgrounds to help them navigate technology and achieve their goals.

NICE-TO-HAVE SKILLS

Experience with the Unreal Engine graphics
Experience with mobile graphics development
Experience leading technical projects
