Job Description:

End to end oversee and comprehensively manage quarterly reserve study
Evaluate reserving methodologies and improve accuracy of reserves by utilizing both conventional and new, more sophisticated techniques
Support Chief Actuary,  external consultants, FPA, Accounting and auditors in providing timely and accurate analysis, including preparation of executive facing quarterly reports and decks.
Provide comprehensive financial support to cross functional stakeholders in accounting and FPA on key insurance reserve line items 
Responsible for team growth and workflow management
Vision-setting for cross-functional stakeholder collaboration
