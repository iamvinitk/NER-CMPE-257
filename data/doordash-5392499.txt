About the Team
At DoorDash, our customers are at the center of our product strategy and we believe that an amazing customer experience is the key to our success.
The DashPass and Affordability teams are responsible for thinking through the lifecycle of our customers, taking people from using DoorDash occasionally to helping them habituate on DoorDash. DashPass is on a mission to offer unbeatable benefits and affordability to our users. You will play a critical role in helping to define the future of DashPass as well as other efforts to help make DoorDash more affordable to everyone.
About the Role
As a Staff Product Designer on the team, you will be responsible for thinking about the overarching Consumer experience for key customer cohorts for DoorDash. Youll work on vision and system architecture projects thinking about the long-term product experience for the app. You will help deliver strategic initiatives, set product vision, and jump in to help teams execute. This is an opportunity to have impact across our entire consumer app.
You will report directly to the Head of Engagement on our Consumer Design team. The role can be based in the San Francisco Bay Area, Seattle, or New York City. This is a hybrid role, with some time in-office and some time remote.
