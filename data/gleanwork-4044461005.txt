

About Glean

Were on a mission to make knowledge work faster and more humane. We believe that AI will fundamentally transform how people work. In the future, everyone will work in tandem with expert AI assistants who find knowledge, create and synthesize information, and execute work. These assistants will free people up to focus on the higher-level, creative aspects of their work.
Were building a system of intelligence for every company in the world. On the surface, you can think of it as Google  ChatGPT for the enterprise. Under the hood, our platform is the connective tissue between AI and knowledge. It brings all of a companys knowledge together, understands it at a deep level, provides industry-leading search relevance over it, and connects it to generative AI agents and applications.
Glean was founded by a seasoned team of former Google search and Facebook engineers who saw a need in the enterprise space for their technical depth and passion for AI. Were a diverse team of curious and creative people who want to help each other get big things done—so we can help other teams do the same. 
Were backed by some of the Valleys leading venture capitalists—including Sequoia, Kleiner Perkins, Lightspeed, and General Catalyst—and have assembled a world-class team with senior leadership experience at Google, Slack, Facebook, Dropbox, Rubrik, Uber, Intercom, Pinterest, Palantir, and others.
 

Role
We are seeking a highly organized and efficient Executive Assistant to provide comprehensive support to several executives within our organization. The ideal candidate will have excellent communication skills, strong attention to detail, and the ability to manage multiple tasks and priorities effectively.


Who you are

BABS Degree
Proven experience as an Executive Assistant or similar role, supporting multiple executives.
Excellent organizational and time management skills.
Strong written and verbal communication skills.
Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint) and Google Workspace (Docs, Sheets, Slides).
Ability to handle sensitive and confidential information with discretion.
Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
Flexibility to adapt to changing priorities and demands.



Key Knowledge  Skills

Calendar Management:

Coordinate and manage schedules for multiple executives, ensuring efficient use of their time.
Schedule and confirm appointments, meetings, and conference calls.
Resolve scheduling conflicts and prioritize commitments as needed.


Communication Support:

Act as a liaison between executives and internalexternal stakeholders.
Manage and prioritize incoming correspondence, including emails, phone calls, and messages.
Draft and edit correspondence, reports, and presentations on behalf of the executives.


Meeting and Event Coordination:

Organize and coordinate meetings, including preparing agendas, materials, and logistics.
Take meeting minutes and distribute them to relevant parties.
Assist in planning and executing events, such as conferences and team offsites.


Travel Arrangements:

Coordinate travel arrangements for executives, including flights, accommodations, and transportation.
Prepare and organize travel itineraries and necessary documentation.


Administrative Support:

Maintain and update executive contact lists and organizational charts.
Assist with expense reporting and budget tracking for executives.
Perform ad-hoc administrative tasks as needed to support the executives.
Must be in-office at HQ in Palo Alto 5 days a week.
