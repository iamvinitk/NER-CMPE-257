The Opportunity
Unitys Documentation team is building the future of modern documentation platforms for distributed, federated and configurable documentation authoring and web publication at enterprise scale. We are seeking a versatile Senior Full Stack Developer to help craft innovative, user-facing experiences for documentation consumers and internal documentation authors, in direct collaboration with backend devs, designers and product leaders. You are passionate about flexible web-based tools and visually stunning web applications on robust, decoupled backend systems? Join our team at Unity with a strong web developer culture, and phenomenal benefits! We put users first, and collaborate boldly to deliver delightful end-user experiences. Apply with us today, this hybrid on-siteremote role may be the perfect fit for your next career adventure!
What youll be doing

Own the collaborative crafting of React components using a MaterialUI-backed Design System
Implement and leverage modern workflows: from Figma, to Storybook, to NextJS, with LinearApp and Vercel deployments.
Maintaining a secure codebase in a micro-service architecture
Collaborating with Engineering Managers, Technical Program Managers, Product Owners, and Senior Developers to grow your software development experience and drive for impact across related teams in Muse Chat (AI), Community, and Web Platform.
Mentoring and empowering scalable execution through a team of more junior developers.

What were looking for

JAMStack Proficiency: Git, Typescript, React, Storybook, NextJS, plus RESTful APIs and GraphQL
SSO and OAuth for user login management.
Experience with decoupled backend systems for content management

You might also have

Experience with PostgreSQL andor MongoDB
Documentation publication tools like Docusaurus, Tina.io, Forestry.io, or any others.
Containerization tools like Docker, experience with building and deploying microservices on GCP (Google Cloud) and familiarity with DocFX, Doxygen

Life at Unity
Unity (NYSE: U) is the worlds leading platform for creating and operating real-time 3D (RT3D) content. Creators, ranging from game developers to artists, architects, automotive designers, filmmakers, and others, use Unity to make their imaginations come to life. Unity is the foundation upon which the worlds most powerful digital content is created. Specifically, Unitys platform provides a comprehensive set of software solutions to create, run and monetize interactive, real-time 2D and 3D content for mobile phones, tablets, PCs, consoles, and augmented and virtual reality devices.
In the fourth quarter of 2022, more than 70 of the top 1000 mobile games were made with Unity as derived from a blended number of the top 1000 games in the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. In 2022, Made with Unity Applications had more than 4 billion downloads per month. For more information, please visit www.unity.com.
