Klaviyo is looking for a Real Time Analyst to join our growing Customer Success Operations team. In this role, you will report to the Manager of Customer Success Operations and partner closely with Support management and leadership to identify and execute on areas of opportunity to improve operational efficiency and deliver outstanding customer experiences. The ideal candidate is detail oriented, collaborative, intellectually curious, and proactive in seeking solutions to problems they identify.
About the role:
As a Real Time Analyst you will work closely with other members of the team to ensure daily and weekly performance of our support teams are meeting expectations both in terms of operational efficiency and our service levels. As the team continues to grow you will partner with other planning analysts to incorporate historical business performance into our forecast and headcount models to improve accuracy as well as develop workflows to automate ongoing monitoring of business performance.
To note: This job requires you to work from 7am to 3pm UK time with an hour for lunch. This helps with our global coverage model for clients. 
How youll have an impact:

Develop, implement, document and train on repeatable processes to track and monitor the performance of the business against model assumptions and business goals
Identify and take action in partnership with Support leadership to drive alignment of daily operations to established business rules and processes
Make strategic recommendations to adjust business plans to best meet customer need given real time information and real world constraints
Identify periods that our teams are likely to be over or under staffed and deliver plans and recommendations to address these gaps proactively
Document and train on implemented standards and processes to other planning and functional analysts that support relevant business groups
Partner closely with Customer Success  Support leadership on identification, design, implementation, and testing of processes to improve business and customer outcomes
Collaborate with functional counterparts and cross-functional teams across disciplines such as Sales Operations, Product, Business Intelligence, Finance and People Ops

What were looking for:

Strong Excel skills and strong mathematical background
Individual that thrives given well defined objectives and processes
Is excited to approach new problems and does so with an analytical mindset
Candidate that demonstrates strong communication skills in individual and group settings
Individual accountability and organizational skills are critical for this role
Desire and interest to learn both SQL and Python
2-3 Years of experience working in or supporting Contact or Support Center
Eligibility to work in the UK without restriction

Nice to have:

Familiarity with SQL for querying of data
