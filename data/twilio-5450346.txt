About the job
If you are someone who likes doing the same type of work every day, then this role is not for you. The Twilio Compliance team plays the critical role of bridging the Carrier Relations team with a broad set of internal teams and stakeholders. We are responsible for disseminating the wireless carriers policies and rules, as well as the industry Best Practices to the broader Twilio organization. We are a results driven organization, who is looking for a dynamic, thoughtful communicator who enjoys solving rules based problems. 
This role is a critical part of Twilios compliance first approach to our messaging business. Having a dedicated  compliance leader responsible for addressing the day-to-day compliance needs of Twilios Account Executives, Solution Engineers, and Support team provides customers with the expertise they need to develop and grow compliant messaging businesses in North America. 
 The right candidate for this role will be able to collaborate effectively across internal teams, including Operations, Customer Support, SalesGo-to-Market, Product, Legal, and others, to provide customers the information and support needed to maintain compliance in a dynamic and nuanced policy environment.
Responsibilities
In this role, youll:

Partner with Carrier Relations to support the implementation of carrier Policies and Best Practices across Twilios varied businesses
Collaborate with the Carrier Relations team to advocate for customers with North American Carriers
Build trusted relationships within Twilio to support compliance initiatives across the organization
Develop processes to connect varied internal Twilio teams with Carrier Relations to engage with the North American carriers about policies and best practices
Partner with the Operations team to support compliance enforcement and advise on complex compliance matters
Own the process to update and maintain the North American carrier compliance policy documentation utilized by internal Twilio teams
Work closely with Compliance Operations to support uniform compliance enforcement policies and practices 
Act as primary point of contact for customer compliance escalations, be the voice of the carriers with the front line
Participate in all North American Carrier Relations meetings with carriers
Support the development of internal and external compliance materials including: interactive educational trainings, customer reference guides, customer trainings, and specific materials for high value customers 
Develop processes and documentation to address customer compliance questions about campaign rejections (Short Code, 10DLC, Toll-Free)
Support executive compliance escalations through to resolution
Support ongoing Compliance initiatives across Twilio in partnership with Carrier Relations, Operations, Product, Support, Legal, Regulatory Affairs, and Sales
Document compliance issue trends to support Carrier Relations QBR with the North American Carriers
