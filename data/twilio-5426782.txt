About the job
This position is needed for a Staff Accountant II to be a key contributor on our International Accounting team. Reporting to the Senior Manager, International Accounting in Dublin, Ireland, this position will ensure our fixed assets, prepayments, accruals, OPEX  and other GL transactions are complete and accurate both in US GAAP and Local GAAP. It will also be a crucial role in ensuring that the company is compliant in regulatory filings and returns to the authorities. This role will partner internally with the Tax, AP  Global Accounting teams to understand what improvements can be made to our processes and anticipate accounting impacts.  We are looking for an individual excited to bring their passion for process improvement to a collaborative, team-based working environment in a fast-growing company.
Responsibilities
In this role, youll:

live the Twilio Magic values while owning the GL function of current acquired International entities
ensure our fixed assets, prepayments, accruals, OPEX  and other GL transactions are complete and accurate. 
partner internally with the Finance  Accounting teams to understand what improvements can be made to our processes and anticipate accounting impacts.  
bring your passion for process improvement to a collaborative team-based working environment in a fast-growing company.
