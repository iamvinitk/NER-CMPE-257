About Us

At Cloudflare, we have our eyes set on an ambitious goal: to help build a better Internet. Today the company runs one of the worlds largest networks that powers approximately 25 million Internet properties, for customers ranging from individual bloggers to SMBs to Fortune 500 companies. Cloudflare protects and accelerates any Internet application online without adding hardware, installing software, or changing a line of code. Internet properties powered by Cloudflare all have web traffic routed through its intelligent global network, which gets smarter with every request. As a result, they see significant improvement in performance and a decrease in spam and other attacks. About the Role:  
As a Strategic Customer Solutions Engineer (CSE), your role is pivotal in nurturing and sustaining strong relationships with our Enterprise customers. Your primary responsibility is to serve as their trusted technical advisor across every stage of their journey with Cloudflare. This entails being intimately familiar with Cloudflares suite of products and services, allowing you to provide expert guidance tailored to each customers unique needs and challenges.
Your role extends beyond mere technical support; you are the conduit through which our customers gain a comprehensive understanding of the Cloudflare platform. This involves not only resolving technical issues but also proactively identifying opportunities for customers to harness the full potential of our solutions. You will collaborate closely with Sales, Customer Success, and Product teams to create tailored strategies that align with customer objectives, whether thats enhancing web performance, bolstering cybersecurity, or optimizing their online presence.
Your overarching goal as a CSE is to empower our Enterprise customers to succeed by unlocking the maximum value from their Cloudflare investment. This entails providing insightful recommendations, assisting with platform implementation, and offering ongoing support and education. By ensuring our customers thrive in their digital endeavors, you play a pivotal role in fortifying their loyalty and fostering long-term partnerships with Cloudflare. Your role is instrumental in driving customer satisfaction and contributing to the overall growth and success of our organization.
Responsibilities: 

Identify Savings  Expansion Opportunities: You will proactively identify opportunities for cost savings and expansion within the customers technical landscape. This involves a deep understanding of their infrastructure and Cloudflares capabilities to optimize their usage effectively. 
Modernize Future-Ready Applications: Collaborating closely with customers, you will guide them in modernizing their applications to align with Cloudflares cutting-edge solutions, ensuring they are well-prepared for the future technological landscape. 
Consulting Services: Offer strategic consulting services, leveraging your expertise to design tailored solutions that align customers business priorities with Cloudflares capabilities. 
Customize Service Features: Tailor Cloudflare service features to meet the unique needs of each customer, ensuring that the platform is optimized for their specific use cases. 
Innovative Solution Building: Employ innovative approaches to crafting customer solutions that align business goals with Cloudflares technical capabilities, ensuring differentiation in the market. 
Educate and Engage: Foster strong relationships with customers through education, engagement, and problem-solving. Utilize data-driven insights and customer journey mapping to make informed decisions and ensure customer satisfaction. 
Resolve Adoption and Usage Issues: Address adoption and usage issues promptly, applying best practices and your in-depth understanding of customers environments to facilitate seamless integration. 
Time to Value Acceleration: Work closely with senior-level customer stakeholders to expedite the realization of project success, ensuring adherence to best practices, and delivering tangible value swiftly. 
Key Imperatives Achievement: Collaborate with customers to help them achieve one or all of the three key imperatives: Reducing Risk, Generating Revenue, and Cost Savings, employing workshop-based engagements to drive these initiatives.

What Makes Cloudflare Special?
Were not just a highly ambitious, large-scale technology company. Were a highly ambitious, large-scale technology company with a soul. Fundamental to our mission to help build a better Internet is protecting the free and open Internet.
Project Galileo: We equip politically and artistically important organizations and journalists with powerful tools to defend themselves against attacks that would otherwise censor their work, technology already used by Cloudflares enterprise customers--at no cost.
Athenian Project: We created Athenian Project to ensure that state and local governments have the highest level of protection and reliability for free, so that their constituents have access to election information and voter registration.
Path Forward Partnership: Since 2016, we have partnered with Path Forward, a nonprofit organization, to create 16-week positions for mid-career professionals who want to get back to the workplace after taking time off to care for a child, parent, or loved one.
1.1.1.1: We released 1.1.1.1 to help fix the foundation of the Internet by building a faster, more secure and privacy-centric public DNS resolver.
