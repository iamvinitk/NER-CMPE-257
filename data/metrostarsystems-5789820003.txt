What youll do: 

Lead efforts to develop and maintain a project-level Integrated Master schedule.
Develop reports and displays.
Perform andor assist in critical path analysis and other ad-hoc forms of schedule-related analysis.

What youll need to succeed: 

Six  years of scheduling experience.
Understand the development of Integrated Master Schedules (IMS) in Microsoft Project
A bachelors degree or equivalent.
An active SECRET clearance.
Ability to commute to Fort Belvoir, VA.
