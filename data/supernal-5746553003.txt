Supernal is at the forefront of creating emerging mobility solutions that will foster the development of human-centered cities. We are designing a completely new electric vertical take-off and landing (eVTOL) aircraft tailored to the mobility needs of future cities. This allows passengers a seamless intermodal journey that safely transports them to their final destination. We fuse research in autonomy, robotics, aviation and services to define a new category of mobility for the worlds communities. We believe in creative thinking and collaboration to help build a better mobility experience for everyone, improving peoples ability to move  whether for work or play.
What we do:
The IT Senior Infrastructure Architect is responsible for designing and implementing innovative IT solutions to support our organizations core operations. Drawing from a robust background in infrastructure, data center management, multi-cloud environments, and support across office locations, the Senior Infrastructure Architect will refine and improve our existing infrastructure, ensuring scalability, performance, and world-class security.
This position will be required to work on-site 5 days a week.
What you can do:

Design and Implement Solutions: Develop and introduce IT solutions that are robust, scalable, and secure with a focus on hybrid infrastructure, multi-cloud, and multi-office settings
Strategy and Planning: Collaborate with IT leadership and key partners to define the strategic direction for our IT infrastructure, ensuring it aligns with business objectives
Data Center Management: Supervise multiple data centers, ensuring peak performance, robust duplication, and disaster recovery capabilities
Hybrid Infrastructure Management: Lead and optimize the blend of on-premises and cloud-based infrastructure, ensuring smooth integration
Multi-cloud Management: Design, deploy, and lead all aspects of solutions across various cloud platforms, integrating them seamlessly with on-premises systems
Support Multiple Offices: Ensure consistent IT operations across different office locations, promoting efficient communication and data accessibility
Documentation: Produce and maintain architectural documentation to ensure clarity for all stakeholders
Vendor Relations: Engage with vendors to ensure product and service alignment with organizational needs
Continuous Learning: Stay updated with new technologies and industry standard methodologies
May require up to 10 of domestic and international travel
Other duties as assigned

Bachelors degree in computer science, Information Systems, Engineering, or equivalent experience preferred

Hybrid Infrastructure Expertise: Strong capability in designing, deploying, and leading interconnected on-premises and cloud systems
Data Center Knowledge: Proficiency in leading data center environments, understanding redundancy and disaster recovery aspects
