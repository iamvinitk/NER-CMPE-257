Reporting to the Associate General Counsel, your role will involve actively supporting Coinbases Trust and Risk function. This includes conducting diverse research tasks related to the companys thought leadership initiatives, ongoing corporate and litigation matters, and regulatory concerns associated with the companys product suite. We seek a proactive, responsible, team-focused individual capable of handling multiple concurrent tasks and enthusiastic about joining a nimble team that addresses a range of innovative and intricate legal challenges.
What youll be doing (ie. job duties):

Providing support to Coinbases Legal operations, risk management efforts and strategic business objectives and priorities.
Assisting Attorneys on reviews of institutional and consumer product development initiatives, the review and intake of consumer litigation matters and support on entity management and corporate governance.
Support drafting and negotiating of different contracts across a broad range of areas including marketing, sales, procurement, IP licensing and general commercial engagements with third parties.
Research tasks related to a range of general corporate, product and litigation matters including capital deployment, Asset listing, Regulatory, MA, and pending litigation cases with courts and tribunals. 
General support on projects related to the cryptocurrency industry and the developing regulatory environment for the leader in this cutting edge space.

Strong interest in and common understanding of crypto-assets and crypto-related products andor stockstrading as well as a good working knowledge of traditional financial products.
