About the Role
DoorDash is hiring an exceptional Litigation Paralegal to oversee our personal injury litigation docket at an exciting time for our rapidly growing business. You will be a part of the Legal teams Insurance Litigation group and responsible for owning an important docket of litigation. You will work closely with other in-house counsel and paralegals and serve as a key cross-functional partner for our claims and safety teams. 
The role is based in the Greater Chicago area and will be expected to work from DoorDashs Chicago office on a hybrid schedule. 
Youre excited about this opportunity because you will

Be responsible for owning and maintaining the integrity of DoorDashs personal injury litigation docket, from case inception to completion
Assist with retrieving documents via service of process and distributing them to the appropriate stakeholders in a timely manner
Support an integral part of the discovery process, including but not limited to, issuing legal holds, responding to subpoenas, and organizing case files
Improve workflows that directly impact DoorDashs quarterly financial reporting requirements
Partner with our in-house legal team, outside counsel, insurance carriers, and cross-functional teams across the company

Were excited about you because

Youre self-motivated, thrive working autonomously, and exercise sound judgment in a fast-paced environment
You have excellent organization skills and excel at prioritizing multiple tasks at once, following through, and meeting important deadlines 
You maintain a positive attitude and have a high EQ. No job is too big or too small! 
You are committed to fostering a diverse, equitable, and inclusive work environment
You have 1-3 years of experience managing a large docket of cases at a top law firm or in-house legal department, or similar experience
You have a bachelors degree from an accredited college or university
