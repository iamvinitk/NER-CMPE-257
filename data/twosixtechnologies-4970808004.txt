Two Six Technologies is looking for a Lead Pricing Analyst to support our growing business. In this role, you will leverage your strong background in Pricing as well as your excellent communication and organizational skills to support all aspects of proposal pricing activities. You will serve as liaison between Project Management, Business Development, Finance, Contracts, Engineering teams and other relevant functional areas to appropriately align the pricing strategy. A successful candidate will need to be able to multi-task, adhere to proposal schedules, and work well with multiple teams to complete costprice models in an environment with competing priorities. While this is a remote position, preference will be given to candidates on the East Coast.
What you will do:

Proposal Pricing of all types of Contracts (Firm Fixed Price, Cost Plus Fixed Fee, Award Fee, Time and Material, etc.)
Support a diverse and cross-functional team in the development of compliant, responsive and competitive proposals
Develop proposal pricing for efforts in accordance with RFP, statement of work, and company requirements
Determine estimating methodology; develop and document estimate assumptions
Research and analyze historical data, develop costfinancial models to support proposal efforts
Support audits, fact-finding, proposal updates, and negotiations
Coordinate proposal pricing activities among various departments including Program Management, Contracts, Business Development and Purchasing
Provide risk assessment of terms and conditions of potential contract award to ensure compatibility
Price planning and contract proposals with current forward pricing rates
Maintain auditable files for proposals and cooperates with government audits and requests
Maintain current knowledge base of federal acquisition regulations and customer requirements
Ensure compliance with federal, state and other related pricing laws regulations and guidelines to minimize legal and financial exposure
Ensures compliance with internal policies and procedures, as well as FAR, DFAR, SBA, DOL, and other government regulations.
Evaluation of Request for Proposals (RFP) andor Requests for Quotes (RFQ); determine functional inputs that will be required for a successful proposal
Provide counsel and advice to proposal bid teams related to pricing strategy and other financial issues related to possible execution
Maintain Pricing Files including documenting proposal assumptions and sources of data used as basis of estimates

What you will need:

8 years of pricing experience in the government contracting industry
Bachelors degree or additional experience and related certifications
Experience pricing of all types of contracts (Firm Fixed Price, Cost Plus Fixed Fee, Award Fee, Time and Material, etc.)
Knowledge of contract laws and regulations including FAR, TINA, CAS
Solid Excel experience

What we would like:

Active Top Secret clearance
Experience with Unanet accounting system

Clearance needed:

Candidate must be willing and eligible to receive a government security clearance
