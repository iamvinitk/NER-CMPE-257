About the job
Twilio is seeking a Product Designer to join our Design Systems team. In this role, you will be empowered to create a best-in-class and inclusive-by-default UX platform, Paste, that helps designers and engineers express and build sophisticated user experiences. Your work will involve defining and designing robust, easy-to-use, and reusable components, patterns, and flows. Youll think platform-first, and prioritize high-quality communication and great documentation. Youll be part of creating a UX platform to power the next generation of the Twilio customer engagement platform, used by internal Twilions and Twilio customers.
Responsibilities
In this role, youll:

Be curious, not judgmental. You will truly feel at home in this team if you approach your work with humility and curiosity, collaborate with fellow product designers and researchers, and are able to state your point of view with clarity.
Youll create and maintain high-quality components and systems that are resilient and scalable so that all Twilions can deliver world-class customer experiences. Youll frequently evaluate the barriers to entry and implementation challenges that our product teams face, and find ways to make them better. Follow through on commitments, speak up when you see a problem, and offer to help when a team goal is at risk. 
Conduct user research to improve and evaluate solutions for customers. Articulate customer-centric reasoning for design decisions. Youll write documentation to make the goals for your work clear, and to guide our product teams toward best practices.
 Empathize with stakeholder needs and equip them with the tools required to succeed. Our culture is highly collaborative, and youll work closely with engineering, product design, and product management, among others, to create new user experiences and improve existing ones. Advocate for the needs of each user (not the average user) and commit to UI and systems that are accessible and approachable by all.

Required:

Designs and ships high-quality user interfaces, components, and patterns in a systematic, detail-oriented way. Ensures the coded implementations are consistent with designs.
Is proficient in maintaining component and style libraries in Figma.
Has a solid understanding of visual design, typography, color, and hierarchy.
Has a strong interest in creating beautiful, accessible experiences, and has an eagerness to learn and apply WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines.
Uses research and evidence to improve and evaluate solutions for customers, developers, and designers.

Desired:

Has 4 years of industry experience and a related degree or equivalent experience in software interface design andor development. Bachelors or Masters in the field of Human Computer Interaction or Design is a nice to have.
Experience at a SaaS software company serving B2B customers.

Location 
This role will be remote and based in Colombia.
