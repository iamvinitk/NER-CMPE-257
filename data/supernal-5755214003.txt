Supernal is at the forefront of creating emerging mobility solutions that will foster the development of human-centered cities. We are designing a completely new electric vertical take-off and landing (eVTOL) aircraft tailored to the mobility needs of future cities. This allows passengers a seamless intermodal journey that safely transports them to their final destination. We fuse research in autonomy, robotics, aviation and services to define a new category of mobility for the worlds communities. We believe in creative thinking and collaboration to help build a better mobility experience for everyone, improving peoples ability to move  whether for work or play.
What we do:
The Sr. Internal Auditor is responsible for implementing a variety of assurance and consulting engagements focused on assessing the efficiency and effectiveness of Supernals business processes and their related controls. This includes responsibilities for understanding and documenting processes, testing controls, working with business leaders to develop responses to findings, and preparing reports.
This position will be required to work on-site 5 days a week.
What you can do:

Planning and implementing operational audits and consulting engagements
Analyzing and documenting complex and varied business processes
Identifying and assessing risks, as well as helping business leaders develop risk responses
Testing the design, implementation, and operating effectiveness of controls
Meeting with management and stakeholders to discuss findings and outcomes
Optimizing and maximizing the efficiency of audit activities
Coordinating with members of Compliance, Internal Controls, and Cybersecurity teams to ensure alignment and reduce duplicative efforts
May require up to 25 of domestic and international travel
Other duties as assigned

What you can contribute:

Planning and implementing operational audits and consulting engagements
