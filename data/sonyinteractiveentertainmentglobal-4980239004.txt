As a part of Sony Interactive Entertainment, the Future Technology Group (FTG) team is leading the cloud gaming revolution, putting console-quality video games on any device!
As a Data Engineer on the Future Technology Data Engineering team you will play a key role in the design and development of server-side applications for shaping, managing and transforming data on a large geographically distributed infrastructure for Sony Interactive Entertainment (SIE)s Future Technology Group (FTG).
You bring passion and expertise to continuously improve the value that our data platform provides to data producers and consumers across the organization, providing tooling, standard methodologies and state-of-the art technology to empower data-driven decisions to the company.
