About the team
The Data Analyst, as a proficient-level professional with well-defined experiences, will be a leader in analyzing, implementing, and improving data solutions to drive and support Kinaxis global business by enabling them to make intelligent business decisions. As a technical expert, you will be key in developing a Center of Excellence and mentoring other technical and business resources. The successful candidate will collaborate with skilled and enthusiastic team members to develop a world class data environment. In addition, identification and assisting in Data Governance, Data Quality solutions is critical part of the role.
The Data Analyst proactively partners with stakeholders to understand business objectives and identifies areas where efficiency and productivity could be improved using business process improvements and technology solutions.
What you will do

Partner closely with leadership and business stakeholders as a trusted and influential evangelist to identify and answer important questions, define key metrics, cultivating a data driven decision making culture.
Deliver data, reporting, analysis and dashboarding solutions using KANBAN and agile processes.
Proactively identify opportunities for upgrades and advise the business on value and accelerate Data Governance, Data Quality processes, tools, and concepts.
Contribute and establish and Grow the Analytics  BI CoE (Center of Excellence), User Self Service, Train the Trainer concepts.
Enhance Analytics  BI deliverables by enabling predictive capabilities.
Participate in ML  AI initiatives to further support a data driven culture.
Take ownership of complex or time critical support issues, improve response times, establish a deputy concept within the Data Team.
Participate in an IT environment that is focused on security by design, education, and communication.

What we are looking for

Bachelor of Science degree or equivalent in Information Systems, Computer Science, or related field, and working towards certification on specific application(s) andor platform(s).
4 years of functionalprofessional experience in data analytics supporting sales operations, finance and data management in a high tech, global environment.
Analytical, system, and design thinking skills with an inventive approach to work through deep, ambiguous, and progressively complex problems.
Highly adaptable and able to pivot based on prioritization and needs of the business; proactively solicits feedback to ensure alignment.
Agile and resilient in managing multiple projects with multiple sources of information.
Ability to communicate complex, technical concepts to executive staff, business sponsors and technical resources in a clear, concise, and professional language

Role Specific Skills and Qualifications

Proven ability to translate high-level business requirements in to functional and system requirements, and to build solutions accordingly.
Highly skilled in Power BI (DAX, Power Query and dashboarding techniques), SQL and Excel  
Hands-on experience with cloud-based analytics, streaming and batch data processing.
Experience querying data to perform ad hoc analysis and deliver meaningful results.
Deep knowledge of data modelling, data governance, data quality processes,tools and concepts.
Experience working with DOMO, Snowflake, cloud-based CRM  ERP, ML and AI.
Intermediate to advanced skill using Office tools, flow diagraming, collaboration and documentation tools.
Experience prioritizing competing demands in a fast-paced environment.
Intermediate to advanced knowledge of industry standard related toolstechnology.
