Our vision for the next shift between humans and computing requires innovation across multiple emerging technologies in hardware and software, from devices to cloud services.About The Role
Humane is rapidly growing and looking for an Accounting Manager to join our Finance team. You will report directly to our Corporate Controller to support accurate and timely financial reports and statements. In this role you will assist with various aspects of the accounting function (General ledger, Monthly Close, Sales Tax Filings, Equity Accounting, Revenue Recognition, NetSuite ERP improvements and related system implementations for e-commerce) and will work with leaders within all corporate functions to ensure adherence to appropriate internal control procedures.
What You Might Do

Perform month and quarter end close activities, including preparation of monthly journal entries, balance sheet reconciliations, accruals and analysis to supporting financial reporting
Act as a key member for an ERP improvement by creating process workflows and training company personnel
Prepare, review and analyze financial statements to ensure accuracy and completeness
Review and preparation of quarterly sales and use tax filings
Manage and oversee the daily operations of the accounting department
Establish and enforce proper accounting methods, policies and principles
Prepare schedules and analysis to support external audits
Assess current practices and procedures and make recommendations for improvements to internal controls, policies, and procedures
Provide responses and support for departmental inquiries and ad-hoc analysis
Support accounting teams commitment to timely and accurate processing of accounting transactions in accordance with US GAAP and the Companys internal controls
Proactively identify issues and implement process improvements to streamline processes
Ability to interpret accounting rules and regulations and apply to Company processes
