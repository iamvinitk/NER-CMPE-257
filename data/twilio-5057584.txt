About the job
This position is needed to configure and manage Workday modules on the People team at Twilio.
Twilio is seeking a Workday SME with knowledge of multiple modules in Workday to be a key contributor on the HR Technology team. The person in this role will primarily focus on running Workday projects with varying levels of complexity and evolving our HR support model. In addition, this individual will help with Workday support. Reporting to the Manager of HR Product Management, this position is critical in supporting Twilios rapid growth.
 
Responsibilities
In this role, youll:

Work hands on in Workday configuration of several modules among Advance Compensation, Benefits, Time Tracking, Payroll, Absence, Talent  Performance, Core HCM.
Research, design, advise and deploy best practices and how to effectively translate requirements into scalable realistic business processes.  
Works in a highly agile environment where processes are constantly developed out of ambiguity or redeveloped for scale. Research new ways to solve the unknown problems affecting the business through keeping current on new Workday releases, enhancements. 
Work on and own People projects, end to end, with varying levels of complexity. Gather, document and evaluate end-user requirements by working with cross functional teams. Prototype and configure based on project requirements. Manage testing, identify test scenariosuse cases, help develop test scripts and execute UAT with the project team. Ensure solution designs are in line with security, scalability and best practices. Strong quality assurance skills to minimize risk through use of audits, checklists, repeatable processes and attention to detail.  
Be the partner to the functional teams and proactively support the business by providing the best end user experience, advising on enhancements, and best practices. Create related system documentation and end user training resources. Communicate and provide training to enable end users on necessary functions.
Maintain and support ad-hoc reporting needs and data loads using EIB. Assist with production support.
Support SOX compliance efforts by ensuring process and data integrity.
