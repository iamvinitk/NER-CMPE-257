What we do: 
The Sr. Actuation Engineer is responsible for the development of the actuation system for the Supernal eVTOL flight control system. The role is heavily involved in developing efficient electro-mechanical actuation solution for aircraft flight control surfaces from conceptualizing and design to prototyping and manufacturing, and through production support. The role works closely with an outside supplier and internal technical teams including Flight Controls, Electrical  Wiring, Flight Technology, Airframe, and Test  Evaluation.
This position will be required to work on-site 5 days a week.
What you can do:

Participate in Actuation system lifecycle development activities including requirements definition, design reviews, procurementmanufacturing, and qualificationcertification
Define and mature the actuation system  actuator LRU requirements (Functional, Performance Safety, Environmental) and mechanicalelectrical interfaces
Provide technical guidance and oversight to actuator supplier through development lifecycle
Analyze actuation performance including mechanism kinematics, loadstress, and clearance
Support integrating actuation components 3D models into aircraft digital mockup model
Document system function and performance, including specifications  compliance reports
Support aircraft installation and integration, including writing and releasing engineering orders and functional test procedures required for flight test and production aircraft
Prepare and communicate design and technical material to management and stakeholders
May require up to 10 of domestic and international travel
Other duties as assigned
