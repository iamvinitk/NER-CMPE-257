Were looking for an experienced product lead to head our Inclusive Product team. This is a senior individual contributor role, responsible for groundbreaking work that is redefining what representation means online. By pairing inclusive, equitable AI with intuitive user experiences, the Inclusive Product team ensures that the entire user journey on Pinterest is diverse by default. Youll drive a cross-functional team building industry-leading full stack solutions like our recently-launched body type technology. Inclusivity is core to our company mission  values, and this role is highly visible - both internally and externally.
