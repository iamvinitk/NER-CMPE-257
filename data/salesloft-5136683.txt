WHAT WERE LOOKING FOR:
We are seeking individuals who can accomplish any task assigned with no additional direction, participates in cross-team planning and estimation, significantly influential in their team, fully understands the goals of the team and department and effectively influences to achieve them. Covers the Product Surfaces supported by their team and related areas. Demonstrates exceptional levels of ownership of the teams development efforts focusing on how the components within the teams surface area work together. The architect carries the vision for their surface area. The architect understands the underlying infrastructure of their surface area and takes ownership in the monitoring of the application. In the event of a incident involving the architect responsible for the affected product surface should take a lead role in addressing the issue. Architects are responsible for working with their teams to develop Feature Development Plans (FDP) and reviewing the plans submitted by other teams. Contributes to and executes off of the Architecture Backlog. A Software Architect is expected to focus 20 of their time on Architecture initiatives, the remaining 80 would be for team activities.
On a day-to-day basis, you will be responsible for contributing in planning and grooming sessions, coding, reviewing team members code, and providing ongoing support for the production product.
If youre passionate about sales technology, providing a world-class customer experience, and thrive in a fast-paced, hyper-growth startup environment, then becoming a Software Architect is the career path for you!

WITHIN ONE MONTH, YOULL:

Attend SalesLofts New Hire Orientation, where you will learn our SalesLoft story and understand what makes our Lofters unique 
Attend the half day introduction to Salesloft Engineering, where you will get a high level introduction to the architecture of the platform and learn the basic processes of the Engineering Team.
Begin 1:1s with your manager, understand your 30-6-90 plan, and be assigned to a development team.
Set your OKRs (Objectives and Key Results) with your manager and develop an action plan to achieve them
Regularly commit production code

WITHIN THREE MONTHS, YOULL:

Be very knowledgeable of your team(s) surface area.
Be able to take significant ownership for developing a new feature 
Understands the technical implications of the business requirements and work with your team(s) to develop Feature Development Plans (FDP).

WITHIN SIX MONTHS, YOULL:

Cover the product surfaces supported by two or more teams and demonstrate exceptional level of expertise in the systems that make up your coverage areas.
Be a major contributor to team planning and execution
Guide each teams development efforts focusing on how the components within the teams area work together. Work closely with those teams Software Architect on long term technical strategy.
Evaluate the tools and techniques that are best suited for any given project and provide recommendations to architecture leadership.
Be mentoring support engineers on engineering practices and coding standards
Achieve the results defined in your first set of OKRs
