 CUSTOMS ANALYST - PHILIPPINES
 As the Global Trade Compliance Customs Analyst, you will help the business understand and navigate applicable trade laws and regulations in Asia Pacific.
 RESPONSIBILITIES: 

Manage all import brokerage operations within the region, including but not limited to, import permits, authorizations to ship, signatures and audits
Communicate with customs authorities in region to support import operations
Work with other departments, including Finance, Logistics, and business teams to devise and implement strategic plans to optimize import activities in region
Retain applicable import records according to local requirements
Support the selection and management of customs brokers in the AP region
Assist with onboarding new entities to import into the region
Obtain import data from providers and the government to support import tax reclamations and duty mitigation opportunities
Monitor import compliance changes in the region and communicate relevant changes to appropriate teams
Participate in internal compliance audits for the region

BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:

Collegeuniversity degree
5 years of experience with international trade compliance
Fluency in English and another Asian language
Current residency and work authorization in the Philippines

PREFERRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE:

Comprehensive and up-to-date knowledge of customs laws and requirements in more than one country in the Asia Pacific region
Experience with customs clearance in the Philippines
Prior international assignments or projects
Knowledge of the aerospace andor telecommunications industry
Proven ability to work independently in a fast-paced environment
Creative and proactive problem solver
Proficiency in Tagalog or Malay

 ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS:

Willingness to travel approximately 50 of the time
