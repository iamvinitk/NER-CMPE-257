About the Role
DoorDash is looking for a Senior Associate to build and execute strategic initiatives to grow the Drive Restaurant business. The DoorDash Australia Drive team is responsible for the operations, development and growth of the Drive product suite, our white-label solution that empowers Merchants to offer their own customers on-demand delivery through their own channels, utilizing DoorDashs logistics network.Given the rapid growth of our Drive business, this role is critical to continue supporting our Restaurant partners grow their platforms. As a member of the Restaurant verticals Drive Team, youll support delivery of the growth strategy through autonomous ownership of key strategic initiatives, have responsibility for operational metrics, build and scale processes to support fast onboarding of merchants, and empower sales teams to effectively sell Drive products and manage merchants.
