Position Overview:
We are looking for a highly skilled Customer Operations Manager to join our dynamic team. This role will serve as the linchpin for customer operations, working cross-functionally across Customer Success, Account Management, Product, Product Marketing, and Professional Services. The ideal candidate will have 5-7 years of experience in a SaaS or comparable environment, be highly organized, analytical, and entrepreneurial.
 
Responsibilities:

Collaborate with Customer Success and Account Management teams to streamline customer onboarding, retention, and upselling efforts.
Act as a liaison between Product and Customer Success to ensure client requirements are incorporated into the product roadmap.
Coordinate with Product Marketing to develop and implement customer communication strategies.
Work with Professional Services to improve the customer implementation experience and customer satisfaction scores.
Use analytics to identify patterns, anticipate customer needs, and propose solutions for operational efficiency.
Develop and implement churn mitigation strategies, including early warning systems and customer health metrics.
Forecast churn rates and work closely with the Customer Success team to address at-risk accounts proactively.
Drive the maximal adoption of our products through effective collaboration with Product and Professional Services teams.
Provide weekly reports and insights to senior leadership on customer metrics, including NPS, Customer Lifetime Value, retention rates, churn rates, and product adoption.
Develop and implement standard operating procedures (SOPs) for the customer operations team.

Qualifications:

Bachelors degree in Business, Operations, or a related field. Masters degree is a plus.
5-7 years of experience in SaaS or a comparable environment.
Proven track record of managing cross-functional projects and teams.
Highly organized, with exceptional attention to detail.
Strong analytical skills, with a focus on data-driven decision-making.
Entrepreneurial mindset, with a high level of energy, dedication, and an unrelenting drive to succeed and win.
Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
