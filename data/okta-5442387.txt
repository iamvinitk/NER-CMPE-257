You will get to work with engineers throughout the organization and what you build will be a foundational piece of the infrastructure that allows Okta to scale for years to come.
We are looking for a Senior Software Engineer, Platform Services (Customer Identity) who is passionate about distributed systems, availability, and delivering customer value. Because we build and support tools used by services throughout Okta, the ideal candidate is someone who is excited about collaborating to solve problems that affect the entire ecosystem of services.
Location - Due to the current team distribution our preference is an Eastern Time Zone location in Canada
You will:

Contribute to the development of new services, tools and automation that address cross-cutting concerns such as caching, message-passing, rate-limiting, and feature-flagging.
Work with engineers across the organization to integrate tooling into the Okta CIC service ecosystem.
Discover and advocate for new technologies to accelerate improvements in the reliability and scalability of the Okta CIC platform.
Carry cross-team initiatives from end to end: code reviews, design reviews, operational robustness, security hygiene, etc.
Participate in the teams on-call rotation.

You might be a good fit if you:

Have 3 years of software development experience.
