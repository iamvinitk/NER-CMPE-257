The Senior Technical Recruiter plays an integral role in growing Mindbodys pipeline of talent. The selected individual will identify and work with internal and external candidates through the entire recruitment lifecycle, including sourcing, screening, interview setup,  offers,  negotiations,  and  closing.  The Senior Technical Recruiter acts as a subject matter expert by staying current with recruiting best practices, changing trends and strategic sourcing methodologies. 
This team member will need to be fluent in both written and spoken english. 
About the role

Full lifecycle recruitment for external candidates with a special focus on strategic hiring for high impact software engineering and technical roles.     
Engages with candidates as single point of contact and screens candidates for full understanding of  availability, interest levels, salary range, relocation needs, visa status and basic qualifications.  
Acts as a recruiting  subject  matter  expert  providing  mentorship,  sharing  and  demonstrating  use  of industry best practices to other recruiters and team members across the company.   
Assists leadership in developing high level strategy that contributes to meeting companywide hiring goals. 
Manages stakeholder expectations by presenting data the helps demonstrate a deep understanding of the available talent pool for hard to fill positions. 
Evaluates employment factors such as job experience, education, skills, training, fit with organization, and future potential.
Builds and maintains a strong recruiting funnel by proactively identifying and building relationships with hard-to-find talent.
Provides candidates with information regarding company culture, compensation, benefits, policies and procedures.
Builds strong relationships with hiring managers to ensure hiring requirements are understood, anticipated and met.
Maintains applicant tracking system for recruiting statistical analysis and maintains thorough candidate documentation.
Maintains knowledge of the changing environment of the workforce in the marketplace.
Maintains current knowledge of applicable laws, regulations, and trends in recruitment.
Ensures compliance with established procedures and applicable laws.
Partners effectively with People  Culture Business Partners.
Provides regular reports on the status of positions and presents data and solutions, as needed.
Assists with Talent Acquisition related project work as needed. 
Effectively handles challenging and sensitive personnel situations, while maintaining  confidentiality and exhibiting sound logic and judgment.
