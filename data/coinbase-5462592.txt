To strengthen our growing Enterprise Applications and Architecture (EAA) team, we are looking for an Integration Engineer Intern, passionate about transforming technology into critical business value. You will synthesize a product vision into key technical decisions and partner with Business Units and engineering teams to buy, build, launch, and scale platforms. You will report to Senior Manager (Enterprise Integrations), and be part of the larger Coinbase Enterprise Applications and Architecture Organization.
What youll be doing (ie. job duties):

Design and build integrations for Coinbase Enterprise Apps using approved integration patterns and design principles.
Collaborate with integration engineering and product managers to understand functional requirements and create technical solutions.
Improve integration build solutions to address architectural or security gaps as well as address technical debt.
Drive and manage creation of standards, integration best-practices and methodologies for the team
Participate in major cross-functional efforts end to end, working with multiple business units to deliver best in class integration solutions.
Check in and deploy code and configurations; participate in release management
DeliverExecute with a team of solution architects, program managers and engineers.
Produce documentation and tutorials that enable other teams and participate in bi-weekly technical demo sessions.
Collaborate with external vendors for integration using APIs and File Based systems.
Be on rotation duty for product support, troubleshoot, mitigate and triage prod incidents as they occur.

What we look for in you (ie. job requirements):

Good knowledge of building web services using REST APIs, or other similar protocols like GRPC or SOAP.
Experience working with ETL tools such as Apache Airflow (or similar) for synchronizing huge data volumes between systems, connecting APIs and databases
Good knowledge of CICD processes with experience using Maven, MUnits, GitHub and deployments on K8s and docker containers run on AWS EC2 Instances.
Good communication skills and ability to grasp requirements and work independently in an agile working environment.
Experience delivering API integration solutions with internal or external systems, e.g. Salesforce, NetSuite, Workday and other legacy and cloud-based solutions.
