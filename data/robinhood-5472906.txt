Robinhood continues to build out its Brand team. As the Brand Campaign Manager, youll help elevate and amplify the Robinhood brand and our products to audiences key to our business growth through compelling, provocative, and impactful marketing campaigns.
We are looking for a customer- and culture-obsessed marketer who can develop campaign strategy, then execute on it by leading a cross-functional team. As a champion of the audience and the brand, youll recommend powerful consumer and cultural insights rooted in data that will be the springboard for work that cuts through the category noise to our target audience, inspiring action and driving business impact.
Youll work on our most innovative and meaningful products alongside cross-functional teams from Product Marketing, Media, Operations, Creative, Data Science, Comms, Research, Legal and more.
The role is located in the office location(s) listed on this job description which will align with our in-office working environment. Please connect with your recruiter for more information regarding our in-office philosophy and expectations.
What youll do

Independently orchestrate end-to-end 360 campaign development from insight mining to ideation to execution as the strategic and operational backbone of a cross-functional team, across digital, social and IRL channels
Leverage data and research to inform cut-through channel and activation strategies to reach our target audiences in an bold, impactful and efficient way resulting in outsized impact beyond that of the planned media spend.
Identify opportunities to test and learn, pushing beyond the expected while ensuring that we have appropriate measurement set up for each learning agenda.
Standardize and improve campaign development processes that empower yourself and others to work more efficiently
Provide thought leadership and best practices recommendations grounded in a pulse of the category, understanding of our competitor landscape, best in class marketing, and past learnings first-hand.

What you bring 

4-6 years of professional work experience in a Brand Campaign or Integrated Marketing capacity
Naturally keeps a pulse on the marketing andor financial services industries and applies learnings to their day-to-day.
Strong relationship building capabilities and ability to work cross-functionally
Experience managing creative, media, andor research agency partners
Experience in tech companies where customer insight is paramount
