About the Role
As a member of the Sourcing and Procurement team at DoorDash, this role provides a great opportunity to learn, grow and be challenged. 
Attributes that will help you succeed include strong negotiation, analytical and problem-solving skills. If you have a passion for the personal gratification that comes from a career with a high-growth, high-impact company like DoorDash, we want to talk to you. Were looking for someone with 2 years of relevant experience, eager to learn new business and a take-charge personality having the ability to independently get work done.
What Youll Do

Demonstrate an understanding of business requirements and category strategyownership
Collaborate with eng, finance, sourcing, security, legal and other cross functional stakeholders
Manage new and existing supply base through sourcing, contracting, renewal cycles etc
Demonstrate an understanding of hardware and software sourcing and budget impacts 
Execute category sourcing strategies, including:

Develop and execute RFIs and RFPs
Document bid events
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop selection criteria and drive supplier selection  

Conduct supplier negotiations

Gain alignment from stakeholders
Lead or follow cross-functional, category-based negotiation teams
Implement appropriate supplier risk analysis

Determine KPI measurements for hardware and cloud adoption, usage and sentiment, and deliver reporting to stakeholders across the organization.
Monitor hardware and hardware accessories across the organization for optimal cost-effectiveness, including the elimination of application or department redundancy.
Manage hardware life cycles including the creation of a refresh renewal calendar; build a documented, collaborative evaluation and certification process; and develop action plans for upcoming refresh  renewal dates.
Use influence to coach teams to independently evaluate with best practices and adopt a value-driven mindset when sourcing and procuring new hardware and accessories.
Optimize purchasing agreements 
Develop an overall cost reduction plan, managing category performance versus corporate savings goals and financial models
Implement supplier management programs including metrics, performance goals, and improvement initiatives:

Maintain supplier score cards to measure performance against agreed-upon key performance indicators (KPIs)
Benchmark, track, and analyze annual performance metrics and sourcing trends against commitments on a quarterly basis
Report results to Sourcing team management and members of senior management

Serve as internal point of contact for supplier management and complaint resolution
Actively seek ways to streamline business processes, finding growth or change opportunities within the supply base
Research and share industry category trends and best practices

Experience collaborating with resources
Direct to OEM experience

Experience managing projects and working with cross-functional teams

Demonstrated ability to manage projects requiring frequent communication, organizationtime management and problem-solving skills
Intermediate-level skill in Google Suite, CongaSalesforce, Coupa, inventory management tools and the ability to learn and leverage various tools and systems
