About the job
This position on the billing team is needed to  maximize the  value for customers, sales and finance. We play a critical role in providing SaaS billing needs for new offerings, upselling of new features, and distributing information to other engineering services about what feature  usage limits customers are entitled to in their assigned workspaces. Our team vision is to provide a billing and monetization system that can efficiently scale and deliver new billing packages, automate end-to-end provisioning to help scale financial operations, feature usages, experimentation and pricing capabilities leading to better insights, transparency and control into what customers are billed for.
If youre passionate about leading and solving crucial business problems and enjoy working on them in a small, data-driven team, this may be for you!
Responsibilities
In this role, youll:

Lead design  implementation of complex backend billing, provisioning and usage systems in collaboration with team members
Work with cross functional teams to build solutions in order to address pain points faced by Revenue, Accounting, Sales and Support teams to ensure excellent customer experience
Operates at a team level, helping team work expeditiously through blockers and orchestrating resolutions.
Mentoring junior engineers and building strong mentor relationship attributing to mentees career growth
Evaluate and analyze new technology for feasibility, impact and viability
Actively participate in design, code review, backlog grooming. Works with peers to meet teams commitments.
