What youll do: 

Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate security practices into the entire software development lifecycle, from design and coding to testing and deployment.
Implement and maintain automated security checks, continuous integration, continuous delivery (CICD) pipelines, and infrastructure-as-code (IaC) configurations to ensure consistent security posture.
Perform vulnerability assessments, penetration testing, and security code reviews to identify and mitigate potential security risks in software applications and systems.
Develop and maintain security policies, standards, and procedures in alignment with DoD and industry best practices.
Research and implement advanced security tools and technologies to enhance the organizations cybersecurity capabilities.
Monitor and respond to security incidents and breaches, conducting post-incident analysis and recommending corrective actions.
Collaborate with system administrators, developers, and other stakeholders to ensure secure configuration management and system hardening.
Provide expertise in securing cloud environments, containerization, and microservices architecture.
Participate in security audits, compliance assessments, and reviews to meet DoD and regulatory requirements.

What youll need to succeed: 

Bachelors degree in Computer Science, Information Security, or a related field; advanced degree preferred.
Minimum of 8 years of hands-on experience in DevSecOps, cybersecurity, or related roles.
Strong understanding of software development methodologies, CICD practices, and version control systems (e.g., Git).
In-depth knowledge of security principles, protocols, and technologies, including OWASP Top Ten, encryption, authentication, and access control.
Experience with security tools such as static and dynamic analysis tools, vulnerability scanners, and intrusion detectionprevention systems.
Expertise in cloud security (e.g., AWS, Azure), containerization (e.g., Docker, Kubernetes), and infrastructure automation.
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to assess complex systems and identify security gaps.
Experience working on an Agile development team and the ability to obtain privileged user access.
An active TSSCI clearance with CI Poly
Experience with Kubernetes, including Elastic Kubernetes Service (EKS) and Azure Kubernetes Service (AKS) a plus.
Experience building andor deploying a data protection as-a-service (DPAAS) solution a plus.
Relevant certifications such as CISSP, Certified DevSecOps Professional, AWS Certified Security  Specialty, or equivalent a plus.
