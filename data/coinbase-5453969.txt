What youll be doing (ie. job duties):

Assist in the development, execution, and scaling of integration programs that position Base as a preferred network amongst both developers and users.
Cultivate and nurture strategic partnerships with builders, creators, infrastructure providers, and other relevant industry players.
Negotiate partnership agreements and service contracts, ensuring mutually beneficial terms.
Maintain productive relationships with partners, establishing shared KPIs and resolving business, policy and technical issues.

An unrelenting passion for crypto and a robust onchain resume.
