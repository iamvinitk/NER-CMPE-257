



At Lyft, community is what we are and its what we do. Its what makes us different. To create the best ride for all, we start in our own community by creating an open, inclusive, and diverse organization where all team members are recognized for what they bring.
Lyft is looking for an Inclusion  Diversity (ID) professional to own the development and execution of communications strategy for ID.   This individual will collaborate with stakeholders across the organization inclusive of internal and external communications, the people team, business partners and Employee Resource Groups (ERGs). They will be responsible for building a ID narrative that aligns to our business objective and furthers our mission, vision and values.  
In addition to ID communications, this individual will also be responsible for supporting our International Regions.  As Lyft continues to build its ID footprint globally, we are looking for an individual who can focus on localization strategies that meet the needs of our international markets.  To be successful in this role, this individual will allocate their efforts as follows:

25 strategy building an marcomms roadmap that signals our commitment to ID internally and externally 
40 execution of marcomms strategy tactics
10 unexpected asks rapid response and change management
25 focus on International ID support 

The ideal candidate is someone who is a self-starter and can own and operationalize strategies while working through ambiguity.  Most importantly, Lyft is about fostering an open, inclusive and diverse community where all team members are recognized for what they bring, and we want you to be you.
The role will report to The Head of Inclusion  Diversity and will be part of our People organization.
