Pinterest is seeking an experienced Staff Security Engineer to drive corporate security initiatives and keep our employees and infrastructure safe from real-world threats. You will have the opportunity to solve challenging problems and provide a meaningful impact on our overall security posture. We are looking for a candidate with a passion for innovative solutions which achieve security while minimizing employee friction and maximizing productivity. 
What youll do:

Perform security assessments and enhance the posture of core corporate infrastructure and services
Develop or implement tooling to detect and mitigate threats 
Work in conjunction with IT architectural and infrastructure groups to coordinate and implement roadmaps for future scalability, growth, and capacity
Harden our endpoints, services, and infrastructure against abuse and nefarious activity 
Interface with other teams, and take a leadership role in driving internal security initiatives
Participate in on-call rotation for major security events or outages in tools we support

Who you are:

7 years of experience in Information Security covering network, systems andor application security 
Proficient in developing and building tools in Python, or a similar scripting language
Knowledge of operating system internals (macOS, Linux), network, and cloud fundamentals
Exposure to digital forensics and incident response 
Enthusiasm and passion for security and staying abreast of new attacks and adversary TTPs

This position is not eligible for relocation assistance.
