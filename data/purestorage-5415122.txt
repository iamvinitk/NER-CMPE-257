As the torchbearer of the Pure Storage Corporate IT Technology operations and infrastructure team, you wont just lead; youll redefine whats possible. Heres what youll be doing:

Network Operations: Ensure seamless connectivity, robust security, and effortless remote access across all corporate networks
System Operations: Manage and integrate pivotal back office systems like Okta, Slack, Splunk, and Google G Suite
ITSM: Champion the enhancement and governance of our IT Service Management processes
Service Desk: Lead our service desk operations with unwavering focus on customer satisfaction
