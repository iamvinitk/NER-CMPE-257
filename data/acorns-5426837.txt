Senior Counsel  Acorns
Acorns is seeking an enthusiastic self-starter to add to our Legal team.  We are a company populated with creative and solution-driven individuals, and the Legal team is no exception.  The Senior Counsel will report directly to the General Counsel  Corporate Secretary and collaborate closely with teams throughout the organization, as well as engage with the leadership team and our broker-dealer, registered investment advisor and European subsidiaries.
This role has the option to be hybrid or based in our New York, NY offices. 
Job Responsibilities:

Draft, negotiate, execute and create processes and playbooks related to a wide range of commercial contracts for Acorns, GoHenry and PixPay, including MSAs, marketing agreements, influencer agreements, licensing agreements and softwaresoftware-as-a-service agreements.
Provide legal guidance regarding the development, marketing and use of the organizations global suite of products, including advising on regulatory, data privacy and intellectual property matters. 
Participate in the creation of company policies and procedures to ensure ongoing compliance with applicable US, UK  EU laws, rules and regulations, company practices and standards, and best practices in an evolving regulatory environment.
Coordinate with subject matter experts within the Legal and Compliance teams and outside advisors to ensure comprehensive and consistent legal risk mitigation strategies, including those related to the laws and regulations concerning the delivery of financial services to Acorns subscribers.

What you will bring to Acorns:

5-8 years of experience at a top-tier law firm and corporate legal department.
JD with strong academic credentials from accredited U.S. law school
Admission in good standing with at least one state bar, preferably California or New York.
A bias for action, clear and concise communication skills and an owners approach to lawyering.
Ability to independently manage complex and competing projects and priorities.
Experience with or deep interest in investment management and broker-dealers.
Knowledge of global data protection frameworks is a plus.
In-house counsel experience with a fintech or financial services company is a plus.
A strong passion for the Acorns mission.
Thirst for delivering game-changing products.
A belief that your work is tied to your lifes mission.
Optimistic about the potential of societal change.
