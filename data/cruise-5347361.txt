This team is responsible for development of new vehicle introduction programs, ensuring the integration of the vehicle, autonomous driving system, and commercial operations come together and integrate seamlessly. As a Systems Engineer on the Safety and Systems team, you will collaborate closely with Product Managers, Program Managers, Hardware and Software Engineers, Systems Engineers, as well as Operations, Legal and Security teams to drive success towards the major initiatives. For new vehicle introduction, you will own the highest level of systems engineering and will lead requirements, analysis, and rolling lessons from previous programs forward to new programs. You will also navigate uncharted territory in how to evaluate and test fail-operation absent a human controlling a vehicle and how best to quantify success.
 
WHAT YOULL BE DOING:


Decompose top-level system functions, behaviors and features into a set of requirements that specify the expected end-to-end system and driving behavior.


Ensure that the respective Requirement Documents are necessary and sufficient to fulfill the goal of a safe, legal and responsible autonomous product


Communicate early and often with partner teams, document the requirements and decisions, hold reviews


Develop and maintain clear development pipelines, workflows and dashboards for these functions, and partner closely with the development teams on the most effective way to engineer driving behaviors


Define a verification plan and test methods for the function or behavior, at the integrated system level, that verifies the end-to-end implementation of the function


Lead and coordinate systems engineering activities


Developupdate new systems engineering processes


 
WHAT YOU MUST HAVE:


BS, MS, or PhD in Systems Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Aerospace Engineering, Physics, Computer Science, or another related field


8 years of postgraduate experience in systems engineering, robotics, systems analysis, data analysis, statistical analysis, project management or other related fields


Industry experience in system engineering and requirements management including system analysis, requirements authoring, test generation, and validation activities


1 years of industry experience leading small teams


Ability to understand complex technical systems


Ability to adapt and operate under ambiguity


Prioritize while maintaining the tasks at hand


Strong technical and nontechnical communication skills


 
BONUS POINTS!


Consistent track record of successful systems engineering for a safety-critical product


Experience with requirements management tools (Jama, DNG, DOORS, etc.)


Industry experience coding in Python
