What Youll Do:

Workday System Management: Oversee the daily operations of the Workday platform, ensuring system integrity, stability, and security for all the designated modules
Configuration and Customization: Manage system configurations, customizations, and integrations to meet the organizations unique HR and business requirements
Module Support: Provide expert-level support for Core, Payroll, Benefits, Talent Optimization, Advanced Compensation, Absence, or Workday Help modules, addressing user inquiries and resolving system-related issues promptly
System Enhancements: Collaborate with HR and IT teams to identify opportunities for system optimization, efficiency improvement, and user experience enhancements
Testing and Deployment: Lead testing efforts for system upgrades, patches, and enhancements before deploying them to production, ensuring minimal disruption to operations
Security and Compliance: Ensure compliance with data security standards, regulatory requirements, and internal policies governing sensitive HR information
Data Integrity and Reporting: Monitor data integrity within the system, troubleshoot data discrepancies, and generate accurate and timely reports for various stakeholders
Documentation and Training: Maintain comprehensive system documentation, including configurations, procedures, and guidelines. Conduct training sessions for end-users to enhance system understanding and adoption
Vendor Management: Collaborate with Workday consultants and vendors to address escalated issues, track service requests, and participate in system enhancement projects
Continuous Improvement: Stay up-to-date with the latest Workday releases, industry best practices, and trends to propose and implement continuous improvement initiatives

Who You Are:

Bachelors degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field
5  years of experience as a Workday System Administrator focusing on Core, Payroll, Benefits, Talent Optimization, Advanced Compensation, Absence, or Workday Help modules

Proficient in Workday report writing and analytics tools
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with an ability to troubleshoot complex system issues
Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal, with the ability to interact effectively with stakeholders at all levels
