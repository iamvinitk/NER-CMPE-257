Two Six Technologies growing product sales organization is looking to add a Director of Product Sales to the team. If you are an amazing salesperson motivated by the desire to solve critical challenges facing our customers in achieving their mission goals and looking for something more substantial and challenging as your next step, than this position is for you! Ideally, you will have solid experience selling mission related software and technology solutions (on-premise or SaaS) to federal government agencies and organizations, particularly in the defense and intelligence space directly as well as through relationships with system integrators.
This role includes meeting with federal agency customers on site, thus preference will be given to those with an active government clearance residing within the DC Metro area.
What you will do:

Lead and drive product sales engagements
Meet and exceed your quota by crafting and implementing plans to win new customers and cross-sell products into existing accounts
Drive and orchestrate complex sales cycles and work with our internal partners and teams to best serve the customer
Bring your experience and consultative selling skills to initiate long-standing relationships with prospective customers and executive sponsors
Understand the strategic competitive landscape and customer needs so you can effectively position and differentiate Two Six products and capabilities
Engage a programmatic approach to generating new leads and closing complex sales opportunities
Leverage product knowledge and value cases to create a compelling value proposition with insights into needs of each specific account
Travel as necessary to meet with customers, and to participate in internal meetings

What you will need:

5 years of product sales and business development experience mostly within the federal defense and intelligence space
To be highly motivated and a self-starting producer with proven success targeting and establishing credibility with demanding customers
Entrepreneurial spirit with the ability to break new ground with innovative products and technologies
Knowledge or background related to Information Operations; Intel  Analysis; andor Military Planning and Execution will be beneficial to your success in this position
Ability to drive all aspects of the sales cycle with the ability to uncover, qualify, develop, and close new business opportunities
Must be a collaborative team player and quick learner with excellent communication and organizational skills

Clearance Requirements:

You must be willing and eligible to receive a government security clearance
