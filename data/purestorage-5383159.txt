Pure is seeking an experienced DevSecOps Architect to join our dynamic team. As a DevSecOps Architect, you will build, operate, and maintain infrastructure and tools for both traditional on premises and cloud. A successful candidate for this role will continuously improve the security of our infrastructure and CICD pipeline, by resolving gaps, streamlining redundancies, and automating all that can be automated.
Responsibilities:

Consult with development teams on security risks in designs, makes design recommendations and provide a security release gate
Leverage tooling to identify and remediate excessive entitlements, vulnerabilities, and misconfigurations
Develop security policies and standards and help teams to adopt policies and infrastructure to support those policies
Engage with multiple development teams to ensure adherence to secure development standards
Run threat modeling across our cloud services and infrastructure
Work with external vendors on security tools
Work with development teams to help define scope for pentests
Research new security best practices and vulnerabilities in the industry and development actionadoption plans around them

WHAT YOULL NEED TO BRING TO THIS ROLE...

Have a solid background building secure and reliable software systems
Experience with languages such as C, Python, and Java
Possess knowledge of securing, preferably both on-prem and cloud-based, applications, systems, databases, and processes
Familiarity with security considerations within the Software Development Lifecycle
Have excellent communication and collaboration skills
7 years of experience in software development
7 years of working in security operations or security related development
Must be willing and able to work in an open office, team environment

You will be based in Santa Clara. As this is an office-centric role, you are expected to be present in the office for 3 days a week. As outlined in Pures Hybrid Work Policy, there will be variations over periods of time, depending on business need.
