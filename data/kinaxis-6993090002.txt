
About Kinaxis

Job location: This is a remote position. You can work from home and be located anywhere in North America.
 
What you will do
With a background in value selling and processsolution consulting, you will be perceived as an industry solution and value engineering expert.  You will use a consultative approach to create a clear roadmap for technology and process improvements, and the unique value Kinaxis can deliver.  You will possess sound knowledge of industry and customer specific business challenges, and articulate how the right combination of process and Kinaxis solutions can address customer specific needs.  This position will require domestic and international travel.

Acts as pursuit manager on key opportunities working collaboratively with sales, pre-sales and services to position the right solutions to new prospects and existing customers.
Works closely with pre-sales in the discovery phase to uncover differentiated value drivers.
Provides key objectives for demonstrations and works with salespre-sales to align on demo flow.
Generates and presents business case analyses (typically conducted collaboratively with prospectscustomers) to highlight the unique and differentiated value and ROI of our solutions.
Understands key industry challenges and trends, connects those to the customers unique business dynamics, and translates that to the appropriate Kinaxis solutions.
Creates industry and customer specific messaging to support sales cycles.
Understands Kinaxis solutions offerings, and those of our competitors, at a depth to be able to communicate our differentiation and how it ties to unique value.
Assesses how existing and emerging Kinaxis solutions can support supply chain efficiencies and transformation.
Develop and fosters a strong relationship as a trusted industry and solution expert with customers in areas such as business, finance and IT.
Demonstrates strong supply chain and technology domain expertise  Demand Planning, SOP, Supply Planning, Supplier Collaboration, Business analytics  Reporting and Infrastructure Technologies

What we are looking for

10 years of consulting andor pre-sales with industry experience in the area of supply chain management.
Strong knowledge of supply chain fundamentals, industry best practices, and available solutions in the SCM  ERP space.
Ability to articulate the value of supply chain management solutions through compelling presentations. Experience in developing and presenting concise point of views to convey the value of technology solutions and services.
Consultative sales savvy  appropriately positions software capabilities and interacts across all levels of the organization.
Effective public speaking  communication skills with the ability to convey necessary concepts, and overcome objections.
Demonstrated ability to work jointly cross functional (sales, consulting, support, product management) with internal teams to achieve desired revenue and customer satisfaction goals.
Graduate degree (MBA or MS) preferred.
