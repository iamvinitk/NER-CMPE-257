What we do: 
Supernal is seeking an experienced Fixed Wing Test Pilot to join our team in Mojave, California. This position requires professional test pilot support for planning and execution of Supernals research and development (RD) projects on fixed-wing aircraft and other test platforms. These technologies will enable the capabilities of Supernals next generation of electric vertical takeoff and landing (eVTOL) aircraft. These projects will include developing airborne sensors and technologies for future eVTOL aircraft. This role reports to the Chief Test Pilot (CTP) and requires frequent communication with flight test colleagues, aircraft maintenance team, and engineering design teams. This role also involves direct support of advanced RD concepts that will enable the future of the eVTOL industry.
This position will be required to work on-site 5 days a week.
What you can do:

Safely and effectively operate Supernals fixed-wing aircraft as pilot-in-command to support flight testing, including RD projects and safety chase for prototypes
Responsible for the safety and professional conduct of flight operations and flight test operations
Review detailed system functional hazard analysis (FHA) and provide technical review to benefit systems maturity and flight test risk analysis
Support, guide, and advise the development of Supernals prototype aircraft and advanced systems designs
Support, guide, and advise the flight test teams continuous improvement of flight test processes and documentation
Support, guide, and advise the coordination and communication of daily flight operations coordination meetings
Support the Flight Test Team in preparing for and conducting all aspects of developmental flight testing and eventual flight certification program
Assist with the professional flight test training, coaching, and mentorship of the flight test engineer team
Collaborate with flight test colleagues and design engineers for RD project planning and execution
Optimally identify, communicate, manage, and mitigate hazards associated with aircraft operation
Lead and support coordination efforts for aircraft availability associated with project work, scheduled and unscheduled maintenance, and pilot proficiency operations
Effectively document and report test results and recommend improving design performance and efficiency of follow-on evaluations
May require up to 30 of domestic and international travel
Other duties as assigned
