The ideal candidate is a self-motivated team builder with strong organization and project management skills.
Our Tax team serves a central part in the success of our business. We are currently looking for an experienced Tax Manager to join our team. In this role, you will need to:


Be proficient in leading foreign income tax compliance and reporting encountered by multinational technology corporations


Manage business-critical projects and workflows involving multiple stakeholders in a fast-paced environment,


Communicate well with cross-functional business partners with varying backgrounds and levels of experience,


Be a self-starter with deep technical experience,


Keep up on tax policy matters,


Be a strategic thinker who can problem solve and innovate in a evolving business,


Build strong relationships within Tax and across the organization,


The position will report to the Senior Manager, Global Tax Compliance and Reporting (US West Coast-based). 
This role may require being available during US Pacific Time business hours, as needed, for initial training as well as for recurring weekly meetings or ad hoc meetings.
Responsibilities:

Manage the companys non-US income tax compliance and statutory tax provisions across the Americas, EMEA, and APAC, including management of service providersaccounting firms
Review foreign corporate income tax returns, extensions, and estimated payments
Assist with managing income tax audits and notices
Review annual statutory tax provisions and tax disclosures, including liaising with the International Accounting team (UKIreland-based)
Assist with preparation andor review of portions of foreign entity US GAAP tax provisions, including return-to-provision adjustments and other tasks as needed
Assist Transfer Pricing team (US-based) with ensuring statutory financials properly account for transfer pricing adjustments in accordance with the Companys global transfer pricing policies
Assist with special tax projects as they arise, such as tax planning, legal entity rationalization, acquisitions, and new entity formation initiatives as needed
Assist with administrative duties, including approving service provider invoices, budgeting, department planning, and hiring
Other duties as assigned

Required Qualifications:

BABS degree in accounting, finance or similar discipline
At least 5 years of multinational corporate tax experience with a public accounting firm andor corporate tax department
Strong project management skills with proven ability to drive projects to timely completion while managing multiple competing priorities
Experience managing direct reports andor external service providers
Proactive communication skills
Ability to communicate technical tax issues to US-based team providing oversight
Strong analytical skills and high standards of accuracy and attention to detail
Ability to identify issues and implement solutions
Strong Excel skills
Strong English language proficiency (verbal and written)
You embrace and advocate the Okta culture

 Preferred Qualifications:

Masters in Tax or Accounting or Law Degree
