As a Finance Manager on the Operations GA Finance team, you will work with our corporate functions and finance leaders to monitor business health, identify underlying trends and make strategic business decisions. In this role, you will have full oversight into the OPEX forecasting  planning process at Pinterest, as well as the remit to manage  optimize the full book of investments within the Business Organization. This is a critical role that sits at the center of how Pinterest executes on its strategy. You will work closely with multiple teams across Pinterest (CFO Org, Investor Relations, Corporate Finance) as well as key executives (CFO, CHRO, CLO) to help drive clarity in our business performance and manage our revenue  investments to ensure we are delivering on key strategic  financial objectives. This role will have 2 direct reports and report into the Director, Strategic Finance. 
 
What youll do:

Lead the end-to-end delivery of our core OPEX forecasting  consolidation workstreams
Work in partnership with the Business Organization to optimize the allocation of resources across the Business Organization
Own the delivery of all monthly  quarterly financial reporting to Pinterest executives (e.g. CFO, CHRO, CLO, etc.)  
Create dashboards to scale monitoring of key performance indicators and tools to drive business growth
Leverage superior financial modeling skills in order to deliver bespoke analyses for top-line growth  margin expansion initiatives

 
What were looking for:

6 years of experience in corporate finance, banking, consulting or a related quantitative field
Experience partnering with C-level executives
Experience with GABusiness Organization budgeting and forecasting
Exceptional spreadsheet skills with experience creating financial dashboards and models
Excellent communication and presentation skills (Google Slides)
