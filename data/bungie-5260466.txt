Are you on a mission to create games that bond players together into deeply invested communities?  Would you like to work on something comedic with lighthearted and whimsical characters?  Do you think a lot about how art impacts gameplay, how character design can enable fan cosplays, or how character poses and environments speak to different cultures all around the world?
As the Art Director on an incubation, you will define the look of a new Bungie IP and work on all aspects of art as the game moves from incubation through launch and beyond. Most importantly, you will work with a fun, dedicated, cross-discipline team united around our mission to create a positive impact in peoples lives as we bring a new Bungie world to life.
We look for candidates who value joining a mission-focused team with a diverse set of backgrounds, experiences, interests, and viewpoints.
RESPONSIBILITIES

Collaborate closely with team to contribute to and uphold the creative vision of the game
In collaboration with your art team, define the look of the project and elevate other artists work to maintain craft quality
Establish style guides and specs required to define and document the art direction of the project
Develop milestones for art team through clear direction and sustainable scoping
Lead and manage relationships with external art contractors
Help grow, mentor, and guide the team, including directly managing art team members

REQUIRED SKILLS

Demonstrable experience leading, empowering, and collaborating with an art team through multiple phases of a large-scale, high-quality game project
Proven track record of delivering clear and consistent visual feedback that elevates other artists work
A passion for andor desire to explore and iterate on possibilities of stylized (real-time) visuals
Embraces a we culture in addition to being an outstanding collaborator and communicator
Ability to take initiative to solve problems with excellent follow-through
Strong familiarity with the competitive gaming landscape

NICE-TO-HAVE SKILLS

Familiarity with development using Unreal Engine
Experience working on a live game (game as a service)
