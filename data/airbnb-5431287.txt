The Senior Payments Risk and Compliance Officer is a senior leader on the Payments Risk and Compliance Team, leading a large portion of the Payments Risk and Compliance team, overseeing projects and managing Operations.
The Payments Compliance team is responsible for the products, technical services, tools and partner integrations that manage Airbnbs payments risk, ensure network and regulatory compliance, protect Airbnb from financial crimes and frauds, and empower payments operations.

The Difference You Will Make:

The position is responsible for leading the ongoing design, refinement and maintenance of our Payments Risk and Compliance programs.
This role reports into the Chief Compliance Officer (Payments) and serves as a senior leader and people manager tasked with leading a portion of the team to ensure that our policies, programs and operational processes are best in class, designed to mitigate risks to the business, and ready for regulatory examinations in all relevant jurisdictions.

A Typical Day: 

Lead Compliance and Risk team members as a people manager, design our organization to ensure that we continue to scale to match the growth of our business, ensure that our team is appropriately staffed with Compliance and Risk professionals who are empowered to carry out our payments compliance and risk programs
Serve as a leader within Airbnb Payments, use your subject matter expertise to partner with product and engineering teams to ensure that products meet Risk and Compliance requirements
Oversee the Operations team, continuously evolve our operational processes to ensure that they scale with our business and become more effective and efficient over time
Hire and manage teams in international market locations in support of the evolution of our Payments platform in new markets
Work with the Compliance and Risk Product, Engineering, Data Science and Design teams to develop and implement best in class processes and systems, continuously evolve our process
Develop and maintain the companys Risk and Compliance Policies and program for all relevant regulated jurisdictions
Use data to provide impactful quantitative storytelling that enables confident business decisions.
Coordinate with cross-functional business leaders to understand evolving product requirements and ensure that our Compliance and Risk program can support the evolution of our products
Coordinate with the Compliance Officer to understand evolving trends and risks to the business, design controls based on our risk assessment to mitigate risks
Ensure compliance and financial risks are identified and raised for consideration to the CCO board as appropriate
Independently manage your portion of our program and team
Create, manage and maintain metrics to monitor team and program effectiveness, and ensure management has 247365 visibility of the health of the Compliance and Risk programs
