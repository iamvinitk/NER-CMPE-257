Job location: our office in Ottawa, Canada. We work in a hybrid model where the expectation is to work a minimum of 3 days a week in the office.
About the team
The Senior Accounts Receivable Administrator, reporting to the Controller, Financial Services, is responsible for the billing and collection of the global accounts receivable for Kinaxis. 
Detail oriented with exceptional customer service skills, the Senior Accounts Receivable Administrator will be a trusted partner to colleagues within the Finance team and across the organization, ensuring the complete and accurate processing of all invoice-related activities and related journal entries for the Company, in accordance with International Financial Accounting Standards (IFRS).
What you will do

Collaborate with the Sales, Professional Services and Finance Operations teams to ensure timely receipt of customer purchase orders to enable billing and payment by contracted due dates and terms
Review monthly services billings and subscription billings per contracted terms
Manage and maintain overdue accounts lists, ensuring timely follow-up and communication with vendors and customers to accelerate collection
Expedite collection issues for the team by collaborating with other departments and escalating to the Controller, Financial Services when required
Process incoming cash receipts in Netsuite and coordinate the month end process including managing the AR aging report and leading the bi-weekly AR review with the AR team and the Controller, Financial Services.,
Lead the quarterly close process for the AR team and assist with the quarterly audit reviewsyear end audit
Recommend and participate in the implementation of effective and efficient processes and practices
Assist with regular and ad-hoc information requests including but not limited to financial reporting and KPI metrics such as Days Sales Outstanding.

What we are looking for

Post-secondary education in Business, Accounting, or related field, or equivalent experience
5 years of progressive accounting experience, high-tech, global-entity environment is preferred
Experience using ERP accounting systems; Netsuite an asset
Analytical and problem solving skills; Demonstrated ability to think critically when faced with challenges
Strong organization skills and ability to prioritizing tasks based on the needs of the business; proactively seeking feedback to ensure alignment
A clear, concise, and professional communicator with the ability to present information to various audiences

Role Specific Skills and Experience:

Intermediate to advanced level Excel skills
Demonstrated experience participating in systems integration projects
Keen attention to detail and works well under tight deadlines
Excellent customer service skills
