Pure is looking for a highly motivated and proven B2B Product Marketing leader to join our Product Marketing team. As a Senior Director of Product Marketing, Platform your team will be responsible for defining the core platform and product messaging for Pure, including our core differentiators and brand stories, partnering tightly with leaders across the Sales and Marketing organizations to bring that message to market. 
The ideal candidate is a proven enterprise platform marketing leader with experience in enterprise infrastructure. Through a deep understanding of changing market dynamics, category creation, high growth business development, competitor and customer insights, this leader will create differentiated positioning, create thought leadership and accelerate customer demand for the fastest growing B2B data management systems provider in history.
With a passion and skill for creating simple and compelling stories, you will lead a team of PMMs focused on different aspects of our product line and our corporate narrative. This leader will guide the  team as they work together to ensure our product and platform messaging are clear, compelling and driving our GTM across marketing and field channels. 
An experienced product marketing visionary, this role will play an instrumental role in unifying and leading our strategic corporate messaging and cross-portfolio marketing efforts. 
This role will report directly to our VP of PMM and is a highly visible leadership role within the organization which will have a significant impact on the long-term growth of a fast-growing business.

Working with our segment GTM leaders, your team will define, roll out and drive adoption of corporate buyer personas.

Align with senior leaders on core differentiators and GTM themes that can drive demand and also reinforce Pures brand in the market. 
Partner with other Product leaders to help drive ongoing adoption of Pures product portfolio by relentlessly connecting the product roadmap with our go-to-market strategy.
Work with Pures Executives s and marketing leaders to help identify, create and roll out company level thought leadership and brand stories. 
Oversee the development of compelling and powerful content for Sales, Customer Support, Comms, and others including; sales training, positioning, FAQs, battlecards, and more.
Lead a team of high-performing product marketer leaders and effectively prioritize activity and resources within a demanding, high-growth company.
Amplify the power of our culture, connect the hearts and minds of your team members vision and potential for Pure, ensuring that people enjoy, grow, develop, thrive and feel recognized in this period of tremendous growth.
Be an energetic advocate and spokesperson for Pure Storage internally and externally with influencers, press and analysts.
