Airbnb was born in 2007 when two Hosts welcomed three guests to their San Francisco home, and has since grown to over 4 million Hosts who have welcomed more than 1 billion guest arrivals in almost every country across the globe. Every day, Hosts offer unique stays and experiences that make it possible for guests to connect with communities in a more authentic way.The Community You Will Join: 
Over two million people stay on Airbnb every night and the Trust Engineering team keeps our hosts and guests safe and supported throughout the entire Airbnb experience.
As a global platform, Airbnb is faced with constant and evolving fraud attacks. Data plays a critical role in understanding novel fraud patterns, identifying the most optimal defenses that minimize impact to our good users, and ensuring that our models are accurate and can iterate quickly with the ever changing fraud landscape.
As part of the Trust Data Foundations team, you will be in charge of leading Trusts data engineering team and defining the vision to scale data driven decisions to support detection and mitigation across all fraud verticals across our product. You will collaborate closely with product, data science, analytics, and operations teams to understand the Trust product, and establish the data strategy that supports the data needs across our platform to make Airbnb the most safe and trusted community. There are still many unsolved problems to tackle in both online and offline risk, and we are looking for talented engineering managers to solve these challenges with us.
The Difference You Will Make:

Defining and executing on the technical direction and vision for the team to serve the data needs across Trust defenses and products
Raising the quality bar for the data assets owned by the team
Identifying strategic areas to uplevel data accessibility for various Trust x-functional stakeholders
Collaborating with and influencing the data community across Airbnb to uplevel data engineering practices and processes
Providing guidance on technical direction and foundational investments that are expected to uplevel accuracy and efficiency of data-driven decisions across Trust
Proactively collaborating with engineering, product, and operations to identify customer pain points
Optimizing team operations to empower the team to achieve a high level of productivity, reliability, and simplicity (planning, prioritization, knowledge sharing, design review, incident response, etc.)
Hiring and developing talents on your team
Championing Airbnbs culture of belonging

A Typical Day: 

Support the team to deliver data assets (batch, near real-time, streaming) across Airbnbs data warehouses (Hive, Presto, StarRocks, Flink, etc.) with high quality
Influence the technical direction of the projects to meet the data stakeholders needs while upholding quality. Quickly validate project ideas through iterative development, and pivot as necessary.
Streamline the teams practices and processes to optimize the ways of working, both internally and with external teams
Showcase the teams work in front of senior leaders, clearly communicate trade-offs, risks, and opportunities to a broad set of stakeholders
Proactively share context with the team, and help the team connect the dots of their work with broader company and organizational initiatives
Be able to answer questions about ongoing initiatives, architecture and systems, staffing situations, and priorities in details
Have regular 1:1s and meaningful career conversations with your reports
Have the technical acumen to partner with tech leads and are comfortable helping drive data incident resolution and deliver continuous improvement to teams foundational goals
Lead or contribute the efforts that help increase belonging on the team and beyond
