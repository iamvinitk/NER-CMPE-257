SambaNova is changing the world of AI through revolutionary solutions including hardware acceleration for Machine Learning applications. This role is key to achieve on-time and on-quality delivery of the infrastructure hardware that is custom designed to power the AI-enabled hardware manufactured by our partners. 
As a Senior Principal Manufacturing Quality Engineer, you will be working with supplier partners to successfully deliver new and released products at the right cost, schedule, and quality targets. You will collaborate with our ODMs (Original Design Manufacturers) and CMs (Contract Manufacturers) to ensure that production design, workflow, and assembly methods maximize operational efficiency based on current supplier capability. In this role, you will lead manufacturing process improvement to ensure sufficient capacity to meet our demand. This position requires working in close collaboration with Hardware Design Engineering, Strategic Sourcing, and Program Management.
Responsibilities 

Lead manufacturing activities for new product development releases and production volumes to meet cost, schedule, and quality targets for L6 through L10L12 rack assembly
Work with worldwide manufacturing partners to communicate process workflow and any unique assembly methods
Support cross-functional initiatives to drive cost, schedule, and manufacturing capacity improvements
Collaborate with internal and supplier partners to innovate quality improvements to manufacturing and quality processes for achieving higher yield and throughput
Participate in design review with the development team - Design for Manufacturing (DFM)
Support commodity managers to strategically source parts matched with supplier competencies by reviewing and assessing supplier manufacturing qualities and quality systems
Provide supplier selection (RFI, RFP, RFQ, SOW) inputs and recommendations
Qualify new suppliers and reevaluate existing supplier base by performing audits
Lead the nonconformity resolution process with the suppliers in case of escalated quality or technical issues and ensure the effective root-cause analysis and the countermeasure implementation by the suppliers
Monitor and consolidate the supplier delivery and quality KPIs, the critical non-conformities escalated from the factories
Identify the top concern suppliers within the region or specific category
Manage and communicate manufacturing activities within a collaborative cross-functional environment, communicating risk and recovery activities when necessary
Drive manufacturing continuous process improvement
