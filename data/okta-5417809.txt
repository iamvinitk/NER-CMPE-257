We are looking for a Salesforce Product Analyst with a product management mindset to join our growing Business Platforms OE team within the Business Technology organization. The Business Platforms team supports all of Oktas go-to-market technologies, with the OE team focusing on ensuring business continuity.  In this role, you will ensure the stability, reliability, and availability of Business Technology, and provide timely and effective support to end-users. You will also collaborate with other BT teams, stakeholders, and vendors to optimize application performance, implement enhancements, and resolve incidents.
Essential Duties and Responsibilities

Gather and document business requirements from stakeholders for feature enhancements.
Develop technical specifications and solution designs for feature enhancements.
Facilitate production support, including incident management and monitoring.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to troubleshoot and resolve issues in a timely manner.
Conduct root cause analysis and design corrective solutions to prevent recurrence of issues.
Work closely with development and admin teams to ensure the creation of high-quality solutions.
Perform testing and validation of enhancements and production support solutions.
Perform QA testing and support hypercare on large projects.
