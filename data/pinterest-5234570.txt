As a Senior Manager, Product Marketing, you will play a pivotal role in driving the success of our products through strategic planning, market analysis, and effective go-to-market strategies. You will partner closely with product leadership to set the vision, strategy, and roadmap for performance ads solutions and lead a team of product marketing managers to ensure these products are positioned effectively and successfully in the market. 
What youll do:

Lead a team of talented product marketing managers responsible for driving product positioning, go-to-market, adoption, and revenue against key performance ad products 
Shape our product direction through market analysis, research and close collaboration with our Product, Engineering, and Design teams
Serve as an advocate and represent the voice of our customers; ensure broad awareness of and empathy for customer pain points internally
Drive cross-functional alignment throughout the go-to-market process, tracking success metrics accordingly
Define the messaging, positioning, and launch plan for new advertising products
Use data and insights to drive adoption of strategic priorities and influence our overall monetization strategy and growth initiatives 
Educate and inspire internal and external teams on Pinterests solutions for advertisers
Work closely with cross functional partners in product, engineering, data science, design, sales, marketing, finance, and other teams to develop and launch new performance ad products 

What were looking for:

10 years of total experience in advertising with 5 in product marketing at a fast growing ad tech company
Experience leading a team of product marketers or similar roles 
Experience in online performance advertising with a deep understanding of the advertising landscape; subject matter expertise of full-funnelmulti-objective advertising solutions and advertising measurement solutions preferred
Sharp eye for detail and proven project management and operational skills
Keen ability to translate complexity into simple, clear communication and approach
A passion for Pinterest!
