About the Team
Were looking for an intern to contribute to the process excellence function within the Kinaxis Office of Strategy. The ideal candidate has demonstrated analytical skills, a passion for learning, and an interest in project management, process improvement and operational excellence.
What you will do
In this role, you will work with the Strategy Management team and process stakeholders in various business units. You will:

Work with the strategy team and business process stakeholders to improve the scalability, transparency and visibility of key processes across the organization
Collaborate with stakeholders to support managing projects, identify key business processes and opportunities for improvement
Assist with process improvement using various techniques such as business process mapping
Related activities depending upon time available and educational requirements
May perform additional projects upon request

What we are looking for

Minimum 3rd year undergraduate, focused in engineering or business, with a focus on process. Graduate student preferred
Strong technical and interpersonal skills
Passion for project management, process improvement or lean six sigma
A passion for technology and its application to solve business problems
A passion for learning and researching
Strong organizational skills
A self-starter with strong initiative
A natural curiosity and isnt afraid to ask numerous questions
Interest in gaining invaluable, hands-on strategy experience

Things That Would Definitely Help

Experience working in operations
Experience with 6 Sigma and Lean
