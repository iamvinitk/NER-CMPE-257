 

About Gusto
Gusto is a modern, online people platform that helps small businesses take care of their teams. On top of full-service payroll, Gusto offers health insurance, 401(k)s, expert HR, and team management tools. Today, Gusto offices in Denver, San Francisco, and New York serve more than 300,000 businesses nationwide.
Our mission is to create a world where work empowers a better life, and it starts right here at Gusto. Thats why were committed to building a collaborative and inclusive workplace, both physically and virtually. Learn more about our Total Rewards philosophy. Small Business Sales Advisor
Your role will be to help small business customers choose Gusto. Interacting with customers who sign up on our website via email, chat, and live phone interactions, youll help match our people platform offering to their specific business needs, exercising advisory sales skills. Youll help them navigate an important onboarding process, demonstrating sophisticated customer success skills.
This position is a great entry point to growing a sales career in the tech space. Youll learn the SANDLER sales methodology and be trained to move into more advanced sales positions across Gusto. Our team will prepare you for a successful and long-term career in tech sales. 
Heres what youll do day-to-day:

Answer live inbound calls and chats, and engage in email communication at a high volume, with customers at different points in the sales process: discovery, decision, and onboarding 
Support customers through the process of onboarding which is about 70 of your daily conversations
Aid customers in selecting Gusto via the Sandler Selling System - this makes up about 30 of your daily conversations
Develop customer empathy and product expertise
Exercise and master advisory selling of solutions for small businesses
Participate in community and career development opportunities at Gusto

Heres what were looking for:

At least 1 year of previous work experience in any industry or role, including internship experience
0-2 years previous experience working in a call center environment
Previous exposure to sales or selling motions in a customer service position (upselling or cross-selling) highly preferred
Strong sense of personal responsibility and accountability to achieve goals
Strong desire to pursue a career in sales long term 
A commitment to customer empathy and ethical business practices
Excellent communication skills both via phone and emailchat
Motivated by challenge and adaptable to change

This role is a Hybrid position requiring only 2 days in office per week. You must be located in the Metro area of Denver, CO, or Detroit, MI in order to qualify for this role. Our cash compensation amount for this role is targeted between 22.12hr and 25hr in Denver and Detroit. This position is an hourly role without commission or cash bonuses.
