About Stripe
Stripe is a financial infrastructure platform for businesses. Millions of companies - from the worlds largest enterprises to the most ambitious startups - use Stripe to accept payments, grow their revenue, and accelerate new business opportunities. Our mission is to increase the GDP of the internet, and we have a staggering amount of work ahead. That means you have an unprecedented opportunity to put the global economy within everyones reach while doing the most important work of your career.
About the team
The Public Policy team represents Stripe with policymakers and to the public.
What youll do
In this role you would represent Stripe in front of US federal policymakers, develop policy positions to promote positive outcomes for our users and the payments ecosystem, and craft and execute engagement strategies to advance those outcomes. 
Responsibilities

Help colleagues across Stripe understand US policy trends, and legislative and regulatory developments related to policies impacting Stripe and our users; and identify related business opportunities and risks
Analyze proposed legislation and regulation, and work together with product counsel, regulatory counsel and business teams to assess impact on Stripes business and our users
Identify changes in proposed or existing policies that would impact Stripe, our users, andor the broader ecosystem, and work with Policy colleagues and other stakeholders to develop and execute an advocacy strategy for those changes
Directly engage with policymakers and regulators to articulate Stripes business and vision for the digital economy and the future of payments, and to advocate on regulatory policy matters
Manage internal communication and help execute events with policymakers and users

Who you are

At least 5 years of experience at a financial institution, technology company, or in government
Strong analytical skills
Terrific communication, writing and advocacy skills
A proven track record of success in managing complex, technical policy issues, capable of working independently 
Ability to work effectively with a diverse group of policy professionals, lawyers, engineers, and other business leaders

Preferred qualifications

A passion for entrepreneurship, small business and economic growth
Humility and commitment to the team
