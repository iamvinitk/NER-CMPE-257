As the HR Intern, you will work alongside the People Operations team to learn about and contribute to the optimization of our operational processes, as well as contributing to strategic initiatives that focus on improving our employee experience and knowledge management. This internship offers a unique opportunity to gain hands-on experience in process analysis, problem-solving, and process improvement methodologies.
What youll be doing (ie. job duties):

Collaborate with team members to analyze and document current business processes. Identify bottlenecks, inefficiencies, and areas for improvement.
Collect and organize data related to key performance indicators (KPIs) and process metrics.
Create process flowcharts and diagrams to visualize current and future-state processes.
Assist in identifying root causes of process issues and work on solutions to enhance operational efficiency.
Provide input on process improvement recommendations and present findings to the team and stakeholders.
Prepare clear and concise documentation of process improvements, including procedures and guidelines.
Participate in process testing and validation to ensure that changes yield the desired results.
Assist in creating training materials and support the implementation of process changes.
Participate in ongoing efforts to monitor and refine improved processes.
Contribute to strategic projects with a focus on identifying problems and proposing solutions to create better business outcomes.
Researching and collecting data to build informed proposals that influence leadership decision making.

What we look for in you (ie. job requirements):

Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
Excellent organizational and time management skills.
Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
Ability to work effectively both independently and in a team.
An interest in business process improvement and a willingness to learn.

Nice to haves:

Degree or currently pursuing a degree in human resources, business, management, or a related field
Experience using tools such as Workday, Jira or Looker
