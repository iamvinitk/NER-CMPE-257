What youll do: 

Confer with and advise subordinates on administrative policies and procedures and resolve technical problems, priorities, and methods. 
Consult with and advise other sections regarding internal controls and security procedures.

What youll need to succeed: 

A BA or BS with 12 years or MAMS with 10 years experience in IT, Information Assurance, or other cyberIT related field.
Experience creating Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) and Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures (TTP) documentation.
Experience leading a team to accomplish tasks relating to cyber security, information assurance, auditing, and RMF.
An active Secret Clearance.
