The successful candidate will be an entrepreneurial and resourceful self-starter, comfortable working in a fast-paced and dynamic environment and excellent at building strong relationships. You will report directly to the Senior Director, Corporate  Commercial.
Responsibilities:

Serve as a key driver of a wide range of corporate initiatives, including complex MA, divestitures, strategic investments, joint ventures, corporate and tax structuring, capital markets transactions, financing matters, equity matters, and other strategic corporate projects
Partner closely with Lyfts Corporate Development team and Treasury team as well as advise company leadership and cross-functional stakeholders (e.g. people, accounting, tax, treasury, operations, etc.) on novel corporate legal issues and risks of corporate transactions
Responsible for drafting and negotiating NDAs, LOIsterm sheets and definitive transaction agreements

Assist other teams with projects on an as-needed basis

Rock solid foundation working on corporate and securities matters; must have experience with the negotiation, process management and strategy underpinning complex buy and sell side MA and related transactions (mergers, asset purchases, spinouts, JVs, partnerships and acqui-hires); cross-border MA experience a plus

Resourceful self-starter with excellent problem-solving capabilities, judgment, communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal skills
