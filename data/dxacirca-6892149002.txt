WHAT WE ARE LOOKING FORWe are seeking a highly experienced and versatile Senior Content Producer with expertise in art production to join our team. The ideal candidate possesses deep knowledge of production including still photography, illustration, and mixed media and is excited for the opportunity to think outside the box, be creative and do great work.
HERES WHAT YOULL DO (RESPONSIBILITIES)
As Senior Content Producer, your responsibilities will include, but are not limited to:



Manage and oversee multiple content projects, ensuring efficient workflows and timely delivery of high-quality deliverables.


Oversee art originationasset output within the agency from a creative development phase through brainstorming, estimating, negotiations, shootillustration through final delivery.


Contribute to the creative process as needed and offer workable solutions to creative and business leads


Possess an extensive familiarity of industry talent worldwide: photographers, illustrators, CGI, animation vendors, retouching resources, mural artists, and their corresponding representation, as well as discover new and up and coming talent.


Maintain relationships with vendors and continually grow your network. 


Establish and maintain contacts in talent industry, negotiate with talent agents, handle talent payments and navigate paymasters legalese and completion of appropriate and required paperwork to ensure tax documents, production insurance and workmans comp insurance are procured


Translate and communicate client needs to industry standard rights and usage.


Recognize possible legal pitfalls and guards the agency and client against them, particularly copyright and trademark issues as well as risk assessment. Serve as liaison between IPG legal and various business and creative teams across all North American offices


Remain on the forefront of new artists and trends to help elevate the agency and its brands. Find new, obscure illustrators, mural artists, conceptual artists for event collaborations as well as type designers, photographers and performance artists, depending upon the needed niche or specialty.


Build relationships and create collaborative work environments. Important to be able to motivate peers as well as the ability to manage upout


Own the process of Licensed stock photography, stock illustration.


Have in depth knowledge with fonts, the licensing of fonts and maintaining them on the internal server.


On-site agency producer for print productions, photo shoots, mural installations, custom event illustrations, etc


Have video production experience with an interest in cross-training within the department across media

Supporting Momentums values
All other duties as assigned
