The Principal of Product Operations will be responsible for overseeing the product operations for the entire Portworx product portfolio as well as driving key initiatives. This role will focus on rolling out processes, collaborating on major initiatives that go outside the group, and driving the telemetry and product intelligence dashboards. The role will work cross-functionally across the teams of PM, Engineering, UX, CTO office, Partners, Marketing, Customers, Sales as well as the Executive Staff. The Product Operations person will:

Drive key initiatives for the Product Management team and initiatives that go across multiple teams
Setup, maintain, and analyze key dashboards for the PM team to track and report out on user behaviour, feature usage, product adoption, and product metrics
Own the product adoption and growth of the SaaS offerings and work with cross functional teams to develop initiatives to scale the product adoption
Drive organizational process improvements with the insights gained from the metrics
Work with product management leadership to continuously improve data driven decision making processes
Build and deliver reports for key organizational ops reviews and ensure the product metrics stay relevant and current.
Track and ensure accurate reporting on the KPIs and the corresponding OKRs
Plan and organize user interviews and other market research
Overseeing quality assurance checks on new features
Developing business processes to streamline product development
Managing tools (for roadmapping, prototyping, etc.) that the product team uses
Working closely with support and sales to improve the customer experience

WHAT YOULL NEED TO BRING TO THIS ROLE...

12 years of experience designing and shipping products as part of across-functional product team or product operations lead
BABS degree or equivalent
Strong background in product management and operational processes
Strong communication skills and the ability to communicate complex concepts clearly and persuasively across different audiences and varying levels of the organization
Knowledge of UX, Engineering, QEQA, and business operations processes
Experience of enterprise SaaS products is desired
Knowledge of SaaS tools for user and usage monitoring
Experience rolling out process changes across multiple functional areas
You will be based in Bangalore, India. As this is an office-centric role, you are expected to be present in the office for 3 days a week. As outlined in Pures Hybrid Work Policy, there will be variations over periods of time, depending on business need.
