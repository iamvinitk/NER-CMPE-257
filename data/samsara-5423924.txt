We see a profound opportunity for data to improve the safety, efficiency, and sustainability of operations, and hope you consider joining us on this exciting journey. About the role:
We are a fast-paced, creative, and customer-oriented team with employees in San Francisco, Atlanta, and London. We are looking for a manager to help lead our North American Returns and Fulfillment team. This opportunity requires an individual who holds the customer experience as paramount and thinks across groups to enable an operation that runs like clockwork. 
Our customers range from small businesses to Fortune 500 companies. It is a high growth business that demands operational enhancements in process, application and business systems automation. Candidates must be demonstrated leaders as this is a large team spread over multiple locations.
In this role, you will: 

Manage order processing, on-time delivery, sales support, and returns processing, all through performance metrics
Maintain relationship with 3PL that supports EMEA customers
Manage the EMEA Operations team including hiring and building out the team.
Key responsibilities:

Manage a team of 2-3 Operations Specialists located in the EMEA region supporting Returns and Fulfillment Operations
Mentor, train and develop direct reports to elevate skills for team and individual career progression
Act as liaison for various cross functional teams including Sales, Customer Success, Technical Support, Finance, Logistics and Business Operations
Assist with maintaining relationship with EMEA 3PL Distribution Center


Champion, role model, and embed Samsaras cultural principles (Focus on Customer Success, Build for the Long Term, Adopt a Growth Mindset, Be Inclusive, Win as a Team) as we scale globally and across new offices
Hire, develop and lead an inclusive, engaged, and high performing team

Flexible Working 
At Samsara, we have adopted a flexible way of working, enabling teams and individuals to do their best work, regardless of where theyre based. We value in-person collaboration and know a change of scenery and quiet space to work is welcomed from time to time, but also appreciate that the world of work has changed. Our offices remain open for those who prefer to collaborate or work in-office, but we also encourage fully remote applicants. As most roles are not required to be in the office, we are able to hire remotely where Samsara has an established presence. If a role is required to be in a certain location and candidates do not have work authorization for that location, Samsara will conduct an immigration assessment. If the role is not required to be in a specific location, Samsara will move forward with the remote location that works best for the business. All offers of employment are contingent upon an individuals ability to secure and maintain the legal right to work at the company.
