About the Team
New Verticals is a new, lean organization at DoorDash, building the future of local commerce by bringing new ways to delight our customers to market - beyond restaurants. The Shopping Experience teams mission is to build a better way to connect every customer with the items they are looking for in an effortless and delightful way. Together with our Product, Design, and Analytics partners, we obsess over building the future of local commerce by re-thinking the digital shopping experience and by ideating, experimenting and iterating on key pillars such as Search, Merchandising and Personalization.
About the Role
You will oversee key functions of the shopping experience, beginning with driving strategy and partnering cross-functionally on product solutions to improve Conversion and Subtotals across all of DoorDashs non-restaurant merchants. You will report to the Senior Manager, Shopping Experience on the Consumer team in our New Verticals organization. We are currently operating in a hybrid model, some time in-office and some time remote but it is quite flexible!

Build and scale a team from the ground up to help you bring the future forward
Blend product strategy, quantitative insights, and customer feedback to support business operators in growing profitable and scalable businesses
