 
This January, we will host our 6th annual Womens Winternship. This is an exciting program that offers women an opportunity to learn about our firm, the basics of trading and a view of what Virtu does in the global financial marketplace. Youll also get insight into a career in financial services through engaging panels and networking events with top female talent at Virtu, as well as insight into the different roles that exist within our firm.

5 day program, from January 8th - January 12th 
Each day will be a combination of lectures, and fun programming challenges and activities
Engaging social events to get to know our team and culture
A final group contest

REQUIREMENTS:

Exceptional female talent at the undergraduate sophomore level (freshmen may also be considered)
Excellent academic background (all majors welcome)
Outstanding quantitative problemsolving skills and mathematical aptitude
Some programming experience is preferred but NOT required   
Highly motivated and tenacious individual, with a proven ability to take initiative and work independently and within a team
Team player: authentic, genuine person who is capable of working collective goals
Interest in a career in financial services

 
THE PROCESS: 
After applicants pass an initial resume screening, an online programming test may be sent via email. This test is from a service called Codility so please be on the lookout. We will contact you to arrange a phone screen.
