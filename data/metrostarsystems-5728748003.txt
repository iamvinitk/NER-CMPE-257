As a Sr. Cloud Engineer, youll be a key player in architecting, designing, and implementing cloud solutions that empower our clients to leverage the full potential of cloud technologies with the goal of making an impact across the federal government. 
We know that you cant have great technology services without amazing people. At MetroStar, we are obsessed with our people and have led a two-decade legacy of building the best and brightest teams. Because we know our future relies on our deep understanding and relentless focus on our people, we live by our mission: A passion for our people. Value for our customers. 
If you think you can see yourself delivering our mission and pursuing our goals with us, then check out the job description below! 
What youll do: 

Lead the architecture and design of complex, enterprise-level cloud solutions that align with business goals and technology strategies.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop cohesive and robust cloud architectures.
Provide expert guidance on cloud best practices, security protocols, and industry trends to ensure the highest level of system integrity and compliance.
Assess existing cloud infrastructure and identify opportunities for optimization, cost reduction, and performance enhancement.
Mentor and provide technical leadership to junior members of the cloud engineering team, fostering skill development and knowledge sharing.
Collaborate with clients to understand their unique requirements and tailor cloud solutions that meet their specific needs.
Drive the implementation of automated deployment, scaling, and management processes using modern DevOps tools and practices.
Monitor system performance, troubleshoot issues, and implement solutions to ensure high availability and reliability of cloud services.

What youll need to succeed: 

Advanced degree (Masters or PhD) in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field; advanced degree preferred.
Minimum of 10 years of professional experience in cloud engineering, with a focus on designing, implementing, and managing complex, highly available, and scalable cloud solutions.
Extensive expertise in designing cloud solutions on AWS; Azure a plus.
Extensive experience with Kubernetes, including Elastic Kubernetes Service (EKS) and Azure Kubernetes Service (AKS). Experience building andor deploying a data protection as-a-service (DPAAS) solution.
Experience with LinuxUnix system administration in a cloud environment.
Experience working on an Agile development team.
Strong knowledge of DevOps principles and tools (e.g., Artifactory, GitHub, Jenkins, SonarQube), including CICD pipelines, infrastructure as code, and automated deployment.
In-depth understanding of cloud security best practices and experience implementing security controls in a cloud environment.
Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to troubleshoot complex cloud-related issues.
Active TSSCI clearance with CI Poly.
Ability to obtain privileged user access.
Cloud certifications such as AWS Certified Solutions Architect are a plus.
Security certification a plus.
