Role: HR Business Partner (CampusEarly Career) 
Location: Chicago 
At Aquatic, we are actively recruiting for an HR Business Partner to support CampusEarly Career Recruitment. You will play a pivotal role in shaping our organizations future by identifying and nurturing top talent at the University level. This role involves building and executing a comprehensive campus recruitment program, with a strong focus on PhD students. You will be responsible for establishing and maintaining relationships with educational institutions, managing social media and marketing initiatives, and providing support to campusearly career hires throughout their first two years with Aquatic. 
Role Details

Program Development: Develop a campus recruitment program from the ground up, including clear strategies, policies, and procedures to attract and hire undergrad and grad level students.
Sourcing and Relationship Management: Identify, source, and establish strong relationships with target universities, faculty members, and career services to access top PhD student talent.
School Partnerships: Collaborate with educational institutions to establish partnerships, internship programs, and other cooperative initiatives that align with our hiring needs.
Social Media and Marketing: Create and execute effective social media and marketing campaigns to promote our campus recruitment program, engage with potential candidates, and build our employer brand on campuses.
Candidate Selection: Lead the selection process for campus hires, including conducting interviews, assessments, and evaluations to identify the best candidates for our organization.
Onboarding: Develop and implement an onboarding program tailored to campus hires, ensuring a smooth transition into the company and helping them integrate into the workplace.
Mentorship and Support: Provide ongoing support and mentorship to campus hires during their first two years with the organization to facilitate their growth, development, and retention.

Candidate Qualities: 

Bachelors degree in Human Resources, Business, or a related field (Masters degree preferred).
Proven experience in campus recruitment, talent acquisition, or a related HR role.
Strong network within the academic community and knowledge of campus recruitment best practices.
Excellent interpersonal, communication, and negotiation skills.
Proficiency in social media platforms and digital marketing.
Ability to work independently and collaboratively with cross-functional teams.
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
Results-oriented, with a commitment to achieving recruitment goals.
A genuine passion for developing and nurturing young talent.
