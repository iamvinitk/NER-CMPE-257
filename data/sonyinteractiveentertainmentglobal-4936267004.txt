Principal Server Programmer (Media Molecule)
We are Media Molecule, a multi-award winning game developer and a proud member of the Playstation Studios family. We have a history of creating revolutionary games such as LittleBigPlanet, Tearaway and most recently, the BAFTA Award winning Dreams. Its a fantastic time to join us as we embark on our next adventure, defining and executing the vision for our next ambitious project and settling into our spectacular, newly refurbished studio in central Guildford. We are now searching for a Principal Server Programmer to join our team.
Location: Our studio in Guildford or remote (able to work within the same time zone as the UK via video conferencing due to the need for close real-time collaboration).
Role: We are looking for an enthusiastic and creative individual to join our server team and work closely with cross discipline client-side teams to improve upon and scale new and existing server features.
What youll be doing:

You will help to design, develop and support high performance online services.
You will own and implement technical delivery of aspects of our backend server solutions.
Assist with the deployment of new servers and monitor their operations after launch. Improve and iterate on low level networking systems.
Complete profiling, performance analysis and performance tuning to ensure server and game performance goals are met.

Skills required:

Team player with good communication skills.
Good working knowledge of Go-lang andor familiarity with C.
Excellent debugging and analytical skills.
Knowledge of algorithms and data structures.
Knowledge of common networking protocols and network security.

Experience required:

Professional programming experience working as a server programmer developing distributed scalable systems.
Experience with AWS, Docker, and Kubernetes and experience working in Windows and Linux environments.

Advantageous:

Experience building and maintaining online services for games at scale.
