The  Business Operations Manager, Integrated Operations PMO will support key strategic company initiatives including the implementation and rollout of a third-party software applications evaluation and approval process and governance model across Pure. This role will work directly with senior leaders in Integrated Operations, Go-to-Market, Finance, Legal, IT, and more to drive strategic investments in Pures technology ecosystem. The Sr. Business Operations Specialist will primarily design processes that streamline new application purchases and application renewals, drive appropriate levels of decision making, and ensure security and compliance policies are met. This role will build reports and dashboards to track process compliance, application spend, as well as other KPIs to measure the success of the initiative and drive continuous process improvement across the portfolio. The Sr. Business Operations Specialist will also partner with Go-To-Market teams to shepherd new and existing Marketing, Sales, and Customer Support systems through this process with the goal of optimizing the GTM tech stack. 
This is a fast-paced and constantly evolving environment that requires excellent communication skills as well as the ability to multi-task and work toward tight deadlines. The Sr. Business Operations Specialist will possess strong stakeholder management and collaboration skills. Previous experience in project management and software vendor evaluation is highly desired.
WHAT YOULL NEED TO BRING TO THIS ROLE...

Work directly with cross-functional leadership and teams to support strategic company programs and initiatives
Design and implement a scalable process and governance model for business applications selection, evaluation, and approval to drive process standardization, cost optimization, and tech stack consolidation
Create both high level and detailed process flow diagrams
Define requirements, select tool(s), and roll out solution to automate applications review process
Create training presentations, how-to guides, and other enablement materials 
Socialize process and drive change management 
Run monthly application review board meetings and follow up on action items from board meetings with application requesters and approvals 
Partner with GTM groups (Sales, Marketing, CX) to shepherd new and existing Marketing, Sales, and Customer Support systems through the applications review and approval process
Track and report on process compliance, applications portfolio spend metrics, and other KPIs to leadership 
Proactively identify issues and develop solutions to make processes better for the stakeholder teams
Work closely with other team members to share knowledge and best practices and facilitate standardization across the applications review process
Drive continuous process improvement across portfolio of strategic company projects
