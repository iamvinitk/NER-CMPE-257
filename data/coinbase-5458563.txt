This role is in the Product Marketing team supporting the growth of our Cloud Staking business. You will be responsible for building and driving the GTM strategy for our Cloud validator business, supporting the developer product and sales team. With a deep knowledge of our core audiences and B2B marketing tactics, you will have the opportunity to help and lead marketing pushes in our efforts to expand Coinbases brand in the crypto ecosystem. This role is critical to the success of our growth ambitions. 
What youll be doing (ie. job duties): 

Responsible for the development and execution of a large segment of Coinbases marketing strategy for Coinbase Cloud Staking and other related Coinbase products
Be the voice of the customer and trusted advisor as to how current and future clients needs inform product messaging, brand differentiation, marketing content, and collateral
Develop and lead successful product launches and ongoing marketing campaigns
Partner closely with the sales team to develop compelling marketing collateral including presentations, one-pagers, case studies, webinars, and websites
Work cross-functionally with product, sales, design, communications, support, etc. to build our pipeline and improve conversion rates
Measure and report against the performance of product marketing initiatives
Think big about the future of crypto and how we can bring businesses and developers into the cryptoeconomy more quickly

What we look for in you (ie. job requirements): 

Minimum of 5-8 years of Product Marketing in the B2B enterprise space, crypto product marketing or developer marketing experience
Experience working on crypto products and strong knowledge of staking
Powerful storyteller: written, verbal, and visual
Demonstrated track record of creating customer segment oriented campaigns to steer new customer acquisition, deepen engagement  retention
Advanced quantitative and deductive skills. Ability to distinguish between the signal and the noise and make data-guided actions

Self-starter and ability to be ready to roll-up your sleeves and get things done
