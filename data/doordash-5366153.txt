About the Role
Were looking for a passionate Applied Machine Learning expert to join our team. As a Senior Machine Learning Engineer, youll be conceptualizing, designing, implementing, and validating algorithmic solutions to prevent, detect and mitigate fraud. You are excited about opportunities to introduce step function changes to our risking system. You will demonstrate a command of production level machine learning, experience solving end-user problems, and collaborate well with multi-disciplinary teams.
You will report into the engineering manager on our Fraud Machine Learning team in our Operation Excellence organization. Once our offices reopen, we expect this role to be hybrid with some time in-office and some time remote.
