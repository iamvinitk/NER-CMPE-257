The Inbound Marketing, BDR Program Manager is responsible for the synchronized planning, management and optimization of all conversion channels across the Digital Demand Center supporting AMS and EMEA (UK, DE, FR). In this role, you will assess the overall health and performance capabilities needed to drive conversions, qualified leads and pipeline. Your primary focus is to align and streamline all digital channels and innovate to speed lead conversion working across a number of key internal stakeholders including web, paid media, MOPS, salesSDRBDR and field marketing. This role partners closely with SDRBDR leaders and teams to ensure teams are set up to optimize conversion of leads to opportunities.
Key Responsibilities

Inform and enable BDRsSDRs of marketing programs implemented across both theaters of AMS and EMEA that will deliver MQLs.
Work in partnership with productsolution, campaign, and field teams to build and tune the sales engagement playbooks and call scripts leveraged by the BDRSDR teams.
Rationalize and help coordinate and support outbound call campaigns (i.e., GTM Sales Plays, etc.) across sales and 3rd party agencies.
Partner with key stakeholders to operationalize and optimize high intent channels to drive conversions, including Chat, Book a Meeting, and Test Drives.
Accountability to partner with Inbound, Digital, and Field Marketing teams to build, manage and optimize regional Drift playbooks. Explore new avenues to engage prospects using chat.
Conduct an audit of both AMSEMEA localized websites performance for lead conversion across all high-intent channels and build a roadmap to optimize for performance  
Work with Marketing Operations on overall reporting accuracy across dashboards and integrated systems.
Provide insight to campaign and field marketing leadership which programs are generating pipeline, bookings and recommendations to optimize performance. 
Hold bi-weekly reviews with paid, ABM and regional SDRBDR teams to assess opportunities to improve performance and address gaps and issues to increase web conversions; review high-intent funnel dashboard and guide teams where to focus based on data insights
Assess and identify areas for lead funnel conversion improvement, from data insights that inform lead scoring to the lead qualification process and handoff with the sales teams.
