The primary responsibility of this role is to develop, create, adapt, and maintain training materials that drive client success. This role is part of the Intelligence Services organization and reports to the Instructional Design Manager, within the Training Team.
You will work with key stakeholders across the organization; including Product Management, Product Marketing Management, Customer Support, and other client-facing staff. Working together, you will identify client education needs, make recommendations, and drive education program execution.
What youll do as a Technical Curriculum Developer:

In coordination with stakeholder objectives, design, develop, and plan for training materials that address multiple learning styles and modalities of instruction (e.g content, application scenarios, and assessments).
Leverage in-depth knowledge of cybersecurity concepts to define learning objectives and outcomes.
Apply curriculum development best practices to create engaging content that assists in client success and retention.
Work cross-functionally with members of the Product Management team to ensure accuracy and real-world applicability 
Ensure content reflects the current state of the Recorded Future Platform and is consistent with brand and team design standards.
Utilize educational frameworks to design learning content for clients that include various assessment tools. 
Provide constructive feedback to team members and incorporate feedback from subject matter experts and team members.
Self-Identifies and executes on training initiatives with input from Instructional Design Manager.
Track task completion and communicate project status using project management software tools.
Coordinate training audit cycle and archive training materials in order to ensure training content accuracy and applicability.
Report on the effectiveness of training content utilizing organizational tools.
Create documentation outlining course structure, lesson objectives, and technical details.
Maintain accurate records of changes to curriculum, course updates, and version control. 
Communicate effectively with the team.
