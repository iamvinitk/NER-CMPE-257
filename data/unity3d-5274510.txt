The opportunity
Create -Early Career Program is a newly formed initiative to drive a programmatic approach to increase organizational structure and efficiency. Your role is to support two of the initiatives under the Early Career programs: Rotational Program and Organisational Efficiencies. In this role, you will build dashboards, and track the progress of initiatives, and rhythm of business for the Create Early Career Program initiatives.
This role will be part of the broader HR organization to drive critical goals such as Diversity and Inclusion, building high-performing manager frameworks, capability and teams, and Efficiency in Organizational structure and design. This role is especially pivotal as you will be there on day one to help support the growing team. Your role is crucial to the teams success.
This is a rare opportunity to join a 0-1 program team inside a well-established, successful HR organization and support expanding Unitys People initiatives that build rigor, and a culture that supports high performance.
What youll be doing

Coordinate with People Analytics, HRBPs, COEs, and University Recruiting team to design and build dashboards and maintain a clear rhythm of business for the program goals
Use effective communication, tools, and program management strategies to ensure that goals are met, and the team is informed and up-to-date with progress
Always be iterating on program processes and methodologies to continuously improve our goals and KPIs
Track program progress and run regular program update meetings with HR Leadership, COEs to provide updates, communicate milestones and resolve issues.

Outstanding interpersonal, communication, problem-solving, and organizational skills and desire to continuously learn and improve
