The Storage Team is responsible for some of the most critical database and caching systems at Robinhood.
This is a Staff engineering role within Robinhood. The Storage Infra Staff SWE will own everything related to PostgreSQL, AWS DynamoDB and Memcached. We are building out our own storage solutions for Robinhood for the long term and you can have a direct impact in the shape of that technology!
Our workloads are transaction heavy and real time which presents a host of challenges. You will help scale our storage infrastructure to 100 million users and beyond. Few opportunities like this exist elsewhere. The level of impact and ownership can be understood by simply stating: we will not succeed in regards to our objectives without you.
The role is located in the office location(s) listed on this job description which will align with our in-office working environment. Please connect with your recruiter for more information regarding our in-office philosophy and expectations.
