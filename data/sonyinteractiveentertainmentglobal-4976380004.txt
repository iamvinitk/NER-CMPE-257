Sony PlayStations Systems Infrastructure team is seeking a highly passionate Senior Systems Engineer to provide exceptional hosting and infrastructure support for critically important services at Sony Interactive Entertainment (SIE).
As a Senior Systems Engineer, you will work as a technical lead on various infrastructure technologies and cloud initiatives through all phases of design, implementation, and support. While contributing at a high level technically, the role will also require a passion for customer service, problem solving, customer appreciation and communication. The ideal candidate is a self-starter that is eager to learn by taking on new challenges, while also demonstrating an equal commitment to helping others grow. If this sounds like you, please apply!
What youll be doing:

Ensure all systems adhere to robust security standards through vulnerability management, including patching of operating systems and third-party software, system hardening, and configuration management.
Respond effectively to incidents and work well under pressure to promptly restore services.
Enhance team efficiencies through automation, tooling, and facilitating the seamless transfer of day-to-day operational tasks to the IT Operations Centre
Design new services by converting requirements into easily supportable designs, with a focus on the larger teams ability to manage and maintain them.
Take responsibility for seeking out ways to improve efficiency and stability of SIE Infrastructure while balancing innovation and new technologies.
Collaborate effectively with the wider IT teams and business units.
Maintain high standards of documentation and adhere to processes and standards.
Work independently as the sole resource on projects.
Handle your own workload effectively by prioritizing project and day-to-day operational tasks.
Deliver presentations to internal teams and external stakeholders.

What were looking for:

Possess a technical degree or demonstrate equivalent experience.
Have at least 5 years of professional IT experience with a track record of successful project delivery
Proficient in designing and implementing cloud solutions using AWS and Azure
Expert experience in automation and scripting using Terraform, Ansible, Bash, Python, PowerShell, and other similar tools.
Competence in installing, configuring, and running MS Windows Server and Linux operating systems.
Familiarity with Active Directory configuration and management
Understanding of different storage types such as SAN, NAS, and Object Storage.
Knowledge of various backup systems, methods, and data archive.
Ability to set up and configure relevant monitoring tools for systems and services.
Good Understanding of DNS and Networking
Ability to translate requirements into practical solutions.
Ability to identify and pursue opportunities for process improvement, innovation, and pioneering new ways of working.
Understanding of AI technologies and their applications.
