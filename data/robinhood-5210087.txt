Were looking for a Sales Executive to join the Say team at Robinhood! As a Sales executive on the Say team, youll work to scale our innovative shareholder engagement platform and build out a first-class business development and sales process to drive adoption of Says shareholder communication and engagement platform across the industry.
You will be responsible for driving sales with public issuers and empowering investor relations professionals, corporate secretaries, corporate communications professionals with the opportunity to be on the leading edge of innovation in shareholder engagement. Says products impact millions of investors and connect shareholders with the companies they own in new and engaging ways. Your contributions will help public companies better reach, nurture, and understand their shareholders. This position will be instrumental in scaling Says shareholder engagement platform and building deep, long-lasting relationships with the Issuer community.
The role is located in the office location(s) listed on this job description which will align with our in-office working environment. Please connect with your recruiter for more information regarding our in-office philosophy and expectations.
What youll do

Prospect, target, and close deals with public issuers to help them establish a more modern, innovative investor relations, corporate governance and shareholder engagement program
Attend weekly public issuer pipeline meetings to report progress.
Maintain a deep understanding of Says products and the industry we operate in to effectively articulate our value to potential customers
Build and establish deep relationships with industry advisors, leaders and influencers
Work closely with the customer success team to define and implement a strategy to improve customer experience
Collaborate with product and engineering leadership to deliver the best experience for prospective customers

What you bring 

5 years proven experience in sales, business development, relationship management, customer success.
Strong communication and presentation skills; willing to communicate via any number of mediums and to both technical and business teams.
Strong understanding of public issuer client needs and how products drive value to them
Experience across investor relations, capital markets, and corporate communications technology
Comfortable working in an agile, fast-paced environment
Outstanding customer service skills and a commitment to quality and partner success
