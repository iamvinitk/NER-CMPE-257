YOUR MISSION
Glovo is building the future of cities by enabling anyone to get any product from any store in their city delivered in minutes. Our goal is to provide on-demand delivery infrastructure for every city in the world.
Our On-Demand product connects partners to our fleet for delivery of purchases from their own platforms. You will join a fast-moving startup-within-a-startup working in an exciting new sector for Glovo.
In this position you will be contacting potential partners who have expressed interest in working with On-Demand, pitching if necessary, building proposals, and closing. You will spend significant time in direct contact with partners and should be able to understand complex topics and express them clearly and concisely in verbal and written communication.
