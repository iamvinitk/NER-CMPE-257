INTERNATIONAL INFRASTRUCTURE OPERATIONS SPECIALIST (STARLINK)
Starlink offers high-speed, low-latency internet available anywhere on Earth via the worlds largest antenna ground station network (called gateways) and satellite constellation. The gateway team is looking for an Infrastructure Operations Specialist who will help expand the footprint of Starlinks gateways in a particular region of the world. As a Specialist, you will lead negotiations and contracting with a regional supplier base, identify ground station sites, as well as build and operate gateways at scale.  As our gateway footprint expands, you will be faced with challenges in supplier and cost management, speed to activation, and servicereliability. Individuals in this role must be creative, fast problem solvers, and must be ready to take on challenges outside of their comfort zone. As a Specialist, youll build a regional strategy in collaboration with Site Engineers, Network Engineers, and other cross-functional teams to ensure that Starlink can always meet our global growth goals and achieve its mission of connecting the unconnected all around the world.
RESPONSIBILITIES:

Source and establish the regional and global supplier partnerships needed to acquire, build, expand, and successfully operate Starlinks ground infrastructure
Cultivate the regional business, cultural, technical, and regulatory expertise needed to assess and ensure the health and reliability of our current and future ground infrastructure
Develop best practices, tools, and benchmarks for tracking design, build, and construction timelines at scale
Work directly with cross-functional teams, including: Engineering, Growth, Sales, Regulatory, Reliability, Legal, Billing and Payments, Finance, and Design

BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:

Bachelors degree or 2 years of experience working in a supply chain, production, operations, or engineering function

PREFERRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE:

Experience managing an international supplier base or working internationally
Self-starting, able to identify areas for improvement with minimal direction
Experience in managing projects from beginning to end, especially construction projects
Ability to tackle complex and ambiguous problems and define operational processes
Exceptional written and verbal communication skills
Development experience in Python, C or other object-oriented language
Experience or interest in data analysis, data visualization, andor financial analysis
Demonstrates commitment to SpaceXs goals of reusability, renewability, and sustainability

ADDITIONAL REQUREMENTS:

Willingness to travel up to 25
Willingness to work long hours when needed to meet critical deadlines
