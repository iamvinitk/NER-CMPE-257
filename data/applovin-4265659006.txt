Fortune recognized AppLovin as one of the Best Workplaces in the Bay Area 2022, and is a Certified Great Place to Work in 2021, 2022 and 2023. Check out the rest of our awards HERE.Position Overview:
We are looking for a Senior Data Scientist - Analytics to join our New Initiatives team within AppLovin. In this role, you will report into the Director, Product Management and partner with various internal teams including Product and Business Development, to drive meaningful growth across our various new initiatives that reach tens of millions of users.
Responsibilities:

Work with various product and business stakeholders to develop scalable BI reporting solutions; strong data visualization skills 
Analyze performance, identify internal and external trends from data, troubleshoot issues, identify new opportunities and address ad hoc data requests
Design, run and analyze AB test experiments to inform and execute the product strategy, and ultimately improve the product and support business decisions. 
Be a collaborative, cross-functional team player and be comfortable partnering with a variety of teams from Product to Engineering to Business
Maintain the highest bar for data quality

Requirements:

5 years of experience in data analytics
BABS degree in computer science, engineering, math, or a related technical discipline. Masters degree preferred. 
Ability to conduct rigorous analysis and communicate conclusions to both technical and non-technical audiences
Self-starter with the ability of acquiring an in-depth understanding of the relation between data and user experience.
(Preferred) Prior startup or entrepreneurial experience
