Okta is looking for a Sr. Federal Regional Marketing Manager to strategically develop, manage and execute field programs and campaigns specifically targeting the US Federal Government and Dept. of Defense. In partnership with the Public Sector Marketing leader, sales, alliances and the cross functional marketing team, this person will be responsible for quarterbacking and executing strategies to meetexceed quarterly and annual pipeline goals.  
The Sr. Federal Marketing Manager role is an opportunity to make key contributions, working across business units to develop unique customer and prospect experiences that drive business impact. The ideal candidate must be a results-oriented marketer that gets things done- a roll up your sleeves type of person. The role will require proven ability to effectively work and collaborate with cross-functional internal teams, including field sales, partner sales, marketing, and operations, as well as external partners and vendors. Its critical this person understands the federal landscape. 
Job Duties and Responsibilities:

Build strategic marketing plans that incorporate various field  regional events, programs and campaigns designed to contribute to annual pipeline goals and support sales achievement of revenue and pipeline objectives. 
Execute on marketing strategy- define, project manage and execute to program goals, messaging, tactics, audience generationemail campaigns, speaker plan, collateral, on-site execution, post-show leads processing  follow-up, and other logistics.
Monitor and facilitate lead funnel management across all activities, and report program results to all stakeholders.
Work closely with strategic field alliance and technology partners to execute joint marketing programs and co-branded tactics that support public sector strategy and annual revenue goals.
Effectively work cross-functionally with teams across Okta to include field sales, partner managers, SDRs, marketing, operations, legal, and procurement to efficiently and effectively execute plans and achieve goals.
Clearly communicate federalDoD marketing strategy, plans, goals and outcomes to all stakeholders, as well as other internal cross-functional teams.

Minimum REQUIRED Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:

Demonstrated experience in program, event creation and campaign management to include measuring and reporting of success metricsbusiness impact.
Ability to plan strategically while also executing tactically
5-7 years of experience marketing to a Federal audience.
Experience planning and co-marketing with strategic reseller, distribution and or technology partners.
Budget Management to include all aspects of pre-program procurement and post reconciliation.
A proven track record of complex event programs with varied stakeholders, multiple product lines, and representing various cross teams within the organization.
Oversee event management including budgets, venue selection, vendor sourcing and management, project plan, event show flow, promotion and registration, content development, on-site management.
Master of multi-tasking, prioritization, and timelines
Experience with sales and marketing automation systems  Salesforce and Marketo, preferred Clari, Demandbase, a bonus
Excellent oral and written communication skills. 
Thrives in rapid growth, fast paced environment and adapts well to change
Ability to work independently with minimal direction
Team player with a positive attitude.

Additional requirements:

This position requires the ability to access federal environments andor have access to protected federal data.
