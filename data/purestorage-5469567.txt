Pure Storage co-locations are a unique blend of development lab and production data center environments under one roof. We are our own largest customer, and believe that proper utilization of our own products in both development and production capacities are key to our continued growth and success as a company.
In order to achieve success in your role as Data Center Operations Manager, you should expect to collaborate with and facilitate proper communication between multiple colocation stakeholders and data center operations teams on a regular basis. Stakeholders include representatives from various internal teams such as software engineering, hardware development, devops, and manufacturing. As such, you will ensure smooth operations of the facilities and its services to engineering. In order to achieve this, among other things, you should expect to collaborate with various employees and partners.
You will be responsible for leading an existing  growing team of 19 geographically distributed employees composed of Data Center Systems Engineers, supervisors, and contractors in our Sunnyvale Data Center. Leading these teams through implementation standards, infrastructure automation, best practices, and overall vision will be critical to our operational success as we scale to keep up with company demands.
In this role, it is our hope that you will enjoy learning about and contributing to the process of developing a leading edgeinnovative storage technology from the ground up. We are committed to a culture of openness and transparency in both strategy and operations that in turn allows employees a deeper level of engagement in engineering and business decisions.
