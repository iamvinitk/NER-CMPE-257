Harmonia Holdings Group, LLC is an award-winning minority and female owned federal government contractor committed to providing innovative, high-performing solutions to our government clients and focused on fostering a workplace that encourages growth, initiative, creativity, and employee satisfaction.  The Pricing Analyst will act as the liaison between the Program Management team and back-office administration and will support pricing and price-to-win strategy. Additionally, this role will provide strategic support in proposal development specifically as it pertains to solicitation analysis and pricing activities. This is an exciting opportunity to help develop and formalize corporate structure and processes and be an integral part of the future success of a rapidly growing organization. This role does require three to five years of experience in pricing, business proposal development, andor project financial management, in combination with in-depth experience in:

Knowledge of and experience with Federal Acquisition Regulations (FAR) and agency supplements as they pertain to Program Management and proposal pricing activities.
Experience creating sanitized pricing volumes to be submitted to the government.
Experience created pricing narrative for the pricing volume to be submitted to the government.
DCAA  DCMA audit programs and associated internal control requirements.
Ability to prioritize multiple assignments, achieve program goals, and meet team deadlines in a fast-paced environment.
Complex cost allocating structure design and associated cost impacts.
Compile cost, schedule, and technical elements in the development of proposals in accordance with request for proposal (RFP) specifications.
GSA schedules and commercial pricing.
Supporting the PMO team in developing a Basis of Estimate for Pricing activities.
Utilizing pricing tools and supporting Excel files to develop price estimates.
Establish realism and reasonableness of cost and pricing requirements.
Participation in cost and pricing reviews with Management and Executives.
Help create pricing templates for IDIQBPAs.

Desired Skills:

Experience in proposal preparation, such as cost volume narrative writing, pricing justification and reasonableness demonstration.
Advanced Excel skills (write complex formulas, produce live models, code VBA, etc.).
Ability to support the contractive department with contract processes, contracts and subcontract review, check for contract compliance with contract clauses and requirements, and assist in contract close out.

Other Knowledge, Skills  Abilities:

Strong interpersonal and team building skill set; with ability to provide pricing and proposal support across multiple projects.
Strong business acumen: detailed-orientedability to produce high quality work product.
Strong analytical, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills.
Excellent oral and written communication skills, including ability to deliver executive level presentations and business report writing.
Excellent organizational skills: including ability to prioritize tasks, meet project deadlines, and manage multiple engagements simultaneously.
Ability to teach, train, and coach on both technical and industry required soft skills.
Ability to work with minimal supervision.
Proficient in the use of Microsoft Office Suite, specifically Excel, Word, and PowerPoint.

Education: Bachelors degree required.
