About the Role
The growing Flourish Legal and Compliance team is part of the larger MassMutual Legal team. Covering a growing fintech platform, the Flourish Legal and Compliance Team works on a variety of business needs, including fraud prevention; anti-money laundering and economic sanctions; broker-dealer surveillance, anti-corruption; data governance, security and privacy; and elder financial exploitation compliance strategies and programs.
As a Compliance Analyst, you will be part of Flourishs Legal and Compliance Team. You will help review, investigate, document, escalate, and analyze risks related to Flourishs anti-money laundering obligations. You will also monitor applicable laws and regulations relating to money laundering and financial crime, and develop and provide insight into compliance risk assessments, metrics, business awareness, and education needs as part of a team of compliance professionals.
You will also assist the team in conducting surveillance of employee communications, as Flourish is required to do as an SEC and FINRA-registered broker-dealer. You will help the Legal and Compliance team in developing efficient ways to discharge its regulatory obligations in that respect, and provide insight into how Flourish can improve its capabilities to monitor and respond to regulatory risks.
About the Team
Working as an integrated team of subject matter experts, Legal and Compliance aligns various compliance efforts to manage the Firms risks. While the team is organized by risk (anti-money laundering and economic sanctions; data governance, security and privacy; anti-corruption; elder financial exploitation and fraud), functional integration within the same team enables the Flourish Legal and Compliance team to effectively manage risk across the organization. This is accomplished by fostering greater and consistent coordination and communications with business lines and other control functions, developing an overall legal and regulatory strategy, sharing and analyzing key data across areas like financial crimes, fraud and data privacysecurity prevention activities and efficiently managing workflow, cases, and activity.
Principal Responsibilities
As a Compliance Consultant on the Flourish Legal and Compliance team, you will collaborate with team members, as well as internal business partners. Specific responsibilities include:

Reviewing new client data for anti-money laundering risks;
Assist in the surveillance of firm communications;
Provide guidance and training to other Flourish business units regarding various Company policies and procedures involving AML and regulatory compliance;
Assist in responses to regulator and internal audit requests,
Assist in preparation and evaluation of external reviews and regulatory inquiries, including state and FINRASEC examinations.
Monitor applicable laws and regulations relating to anti-money laundering;
Develop and monitor AML compliance risk assessments, metrics, business awareness and education needs.
Work with other business constituencies to address Compliance issues.
