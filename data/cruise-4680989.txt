Cruise is looking for Sr. Software Engineer, ML Deployment Tools to help us build tools and workflows that fully automate ML deployment at Cruise.  Our ML deployment process maps models developed in high level ML frameworks (e.g. PyTorch) to binaries optimized for the resource constrained heterogeneous compute platforms on our cars. This is a unique opportunity to build tools that play a significantly visible role in commercial scaling of AV products.!
In this position, you will be working closely with AV ML engineering teams such as perceptionpredictionplanning  controls as customers of your tools.  You will also be working closely with our ML compiler team to implement applicationtool specific passes in the compiler infrastructure, and contribute to building tools used in the model deployment process - such as linters, debuggers, performance models, parity checkers etc..
If you are looking to solve one of todays most complex engineering challenges, see the results of your work contributing to building the worlds most advanced, self-driving vehicles to safely connect people to the places, things, and experiences they care about and make a positive impact in the world starting in our cities, lets chat!
What youll be doing:

Analyze, understand and articulate the engineering requirements of preparing high level ML models (PyTorch) for predictable, high quality AV stack integration and deployment. 
Introduce innovative tools and techniques into our ML deployment workflow. 
Design and implement highly reliable, maintainable, extensible, scalable software components that can be released into production. 
Participate and lead in full SDLC and standard software engineering practices, including testing and root-causing issues across the full software stack (including 3P and in-house tools)
Lead discussions with 3P vendors (incl. open source communities and closed source support teams) to get issues resolved and to get new enhancements per Cruise roadmap priorities. 

What you must have:

Strong expertise in writing production quality C code
You are comfortable and experienced in software development life cycle - coding, debugging, optimization, testing, integration
You have experience with deep learning and deep learning frameworks (e.g., PyTorch, TensorFlow, etc) and software stack (e.g., TensorRT, MLIR, TVM, XLA etc). 
You have a BS, or higher degree, in CSCEEE, or equivalent, in industry experience

Bonus points!

1-2 years experience in writing compiler passes (such as PyTorch, Onnx, MLIR, LLVM etc)
2 years of experience in developing, maintaining and supporting tool developer APIs across different tool applications.
Experience in design review and merging into community maintained open source code.   
Thrive in a fast paced environment
Able to work in a diverse, cross-collaborative environment
