Skydio is the leading US drone company and the world leader in autonomous flight, the key technology for the future of drones and aerial transportation. The Skydio team combines deep expertise in artificial intelligence, best-in-class hardware and software product development, and operational excellence to empower a broader, more diverse audience of drone users - from first responders to insurance claims adjusters, utilities, and more!About the role:
By combining best-in-class autonomy and cloud-connected real-time visibility, drones are becoming critical and ubiquitous infrastructure that can be deployed and monitored in real time to solve a variety of problems faster, cheaper, and safer than ever before. As a front-end engineer, you will be responsible for building out critical real-time user interfaces and cloud features to make this vision a reality. While focusing on front-end and UIs, you will be working across multiple parts of the Skydio stack and delivering new capabilities continuously to our growing consumer and commercial customer base.
How youll make an impact:

Collaborate across functions (product managers, designers, engineers) to design and deliver customer-facing product features that enable enterprise use cases on the Skydio Platform
Design and implement web front-ends, visualization tools, real-time communications, video streaming, multi-player interfaces for drone applications , and more
Focus on frontend engineering efforts while being able to deliver full-stack solutions including backend work as necessary
Leverage your experience and best practices to not only uphold but improve Skydios engineering standards

What makes you a good fit:

You love having fun building great product and enjoy working on systems that involve hardware and software (5 years of experience preferred)
Demonstrated experience in building performant, real-time user interfaces. Direct experience with WebRTC andor WebSocket preferred.
You have experience building and delivering scalable and secure cloud based systems with a high proficiency in modern web technologies. Direct experience with Typescript and React preferred 
You are user- focused and love shipping products quickly that solve user problems elegantly
You have demonstrated ability to take a concept and systematically drive it through the software lifecycle: architecture, development, testing, and deployment, and monitoring
You are comfortable navigating and delivering within a complex codebase
