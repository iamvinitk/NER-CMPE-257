Pure Storage is seeking an analytical and business-minded strategy and corporate development professional with broad-based industry experience. The Director of Strategy and Corporate Development will report to Pures VP and Strategy  Corporate Development and will  work directly with with key stakeholders to analyze and synthesize product direction and opportunities, trends in the technology, industry, market and competitive landscapes, and to develop strategic plans to support market growth, competitive strategies, and new investment areas and opportunities.
This high-impact role will also play a critical role in driving Pures Corporate Development function to develop a strong network of industry, VC and startup contacts, and to assess new market opportunities that will expand Pures market size and footprint.
Key responsibilities:

Research and critical analysis of current products and markets, market adjacencies and opportunities, and competitive activity and strategies
Developing strategic execution plans to enhance current products and markets, and drive growth and competitive positioning
Identifying and developing strategic execution plans to pursue new opportunities and adjacencies
Developing and maintain assessments of potential strategic investment, partnership, or MA opportunities aligned to strategic plans
Develop and cultivate a sensing function and network for new startups and technology market entrants and disruptors, aligned to strategic plans

Traits of an ideal candidate:

Strong business acumen and experience in the technology industry
Analytical and data-driven, but forward-looking in thinking and decision-making 
Able to distill complex information and incomplete problem statements into their basic elements and actionable strategies and plans
Highly collaborative, with the ability to work cross-functionally and quickly establish functional work relationships

Qualifications and background: 

7-12 years of experience in  enterprise technology  strategy  corporate development  consulting  investment banking  VC  PE
Proven track record of developing and implementing new business and technology strategies
Strong technical skills and knowledge, and ability to synthesize and understand new areas of technology quickly
Experience with business development, strategic investments and partnership, and MA
Strong industry and VC community network
Excellent communications skills, with the ability to translate complex analysis and information into a clear and concise form
