Two Six Technologies is seeking an experienced Contracts Administrator who will thrive in a dynamic, agile organization. We are a high-growth organization with unique expertise and innovative products that support missions critical to U.S. national security.
This position is remote going into the Arlington, VA office as needed.
What you will do:

Cradle to grave contract and subcontract management associated with the business units portfolio of contracts
Identifying and mitigating risk, successfully negotiating favorable terms and pricing, and making recommendations to management
Utilizing your thorough knowledge of government and commercial contracting and procurement regulations to ensure the flow down of appropriate clauses and compliance with contract specifications and applicable laws
Tracking and reporting on the status of contract requirements and deliverables, as well as compliance reporting
Manage and execute subcontracts
Negotiate and maintain subcontracts to include receiving subcontractor projections and providing incremental funding per policies and procedures
Prepare and submit monthly reports to the Government
Participate in the preparation of solicitation documentation, proposal review, and negotiation
Work closely with Management, Project Leadership, and Finance

What you will need:

Bachelors degree in business administration or related field, or equal relevant experience and professional training
5 years of professional experience in a contracts managementprocurement role
Experience negotiating, executing and maintaining subcontracts
Thorough knowledge of FAR, DFARS, relevant contract laws and regulations, and DCAADCMA compliance
Advanced skills with MS Office (particularly Excel)

Nice If You Have: 

Experience supporting RD programs
CFCM Certification
Experience with CAS
Active Secret Clearance or higher

Clearance:

Must be able to obtain a Top Secret clearance
