Would you like to work on a new IP and new genre at Bungie that combines amazing action feel with lighthearted and whimsical characters? As a Senior Animation Engineer at Bungie, you will enable the creation of unique and varied characters. You will ensure that animation is smooth and competitively balanced. You will help players express their personal style through their in-game avatars. Most importantly, you will work with a fun, dedicated, cross-discipline team united around our mission to create a positive impact in peoples lives as we bring a new Bungie world to life.   
RESPONSIBILITIES 

Collaborate with art, design, and engineering to create novel animation behaviors 
Create and document workflows and best practices for team members who will be creating new characters  
Establish and uphold animation budgets and performance goals across multiple platforms 
Contribute additional engineering support to foundational features throughout the game 

REQUIRED SKILLS 

Ability to write, debug, and maintain code in C 
Proficiency in 3D math, data structures, and algorithms 
Ability to diagnose and address performance bottlenecks in animation and gameplay code 
Ability to communicate with customers of different technical and non-technical backgrounds to help them navigate technology and achieve their goals
