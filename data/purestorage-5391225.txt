Optimus Team
The mission of the Optimus team is to keep our cloud infrastructure cost under control. Our activities encompass the whole spectrum of cloud FinOps initiatives including setting up guidelines and best practices, developing fine-grained cost monitoring, anomaly analyses, trend projections, or driving concrete technical improvements to decrease our cloud footprint.
 
IN THIS ROLE YOU WILL BE

Working with stakeholders like Finance, Leadership, Product Management or Engineering to understand their cloud infrastructure costusage awareness needs
Identifying, designing and building costusage reports needed by the above mentioned stakeholders or your team in BI tools, spreadsheets and alike, using the data collected in the warehouse
Reporting cloud infrastructure cost  usage to the above mentioned stakeholders
Identifying data required for the reports and working with the engineers in the team to retrieve it  get it ingested into our data warehouse
Pointing out blind spots in our data and identifying data requirements that will help us improve future reports and analyses
Helping engineers in the team organize and document cloud infrastructure cost  usage data in the warehouse
Monitoring  analyzing our cloud infrastructure costs  usage to identify and document spikes, trends, their drivers
Identifying and assessing cloud infrastructure cost optimization opportunities, helping ensure that they are acted on
Popularizing the usage of the produced reports, helping other teams understand this data, train them on it
