Mission Summary:
Our Advanced Technology Team is looking for an experienced Senior Optical Systems Engineer to lead the development of cutting edge imaging system metrology laboratories. The Senior Optical Systems Engineer will be responsible for defining, implementing, owning and automating optical metrology supporting the development, characterization and calibration of machine vision imaging systems, and establishing what does this mean to the AVs machine learning system.
What youll be doing: 

Develop a deep understanding of imaging sensor performance as it relates to the Machine Learning system, and AV performance 
Hands-on owner of the lab, with focus on designing, developing, and selecting instrumentation to test imaging optics, illuminations systems, and electro-optical devices
Dive deep into developing and  analyzing imaging lens optical and color models
Partner with the Autonomy team to develop Machine Learning relevant metrics for the Vision System
Hands-on developer of analysis and automation methods to ensure relevant performance learning is generated 
Utilize simulation to guide and reinforce experiential learning, and drive insight into what does this mean? 
Establish production partnerships with key vendors and OEMs
Write reports and file patents

What were looking for: 

MS Bachelors degree within Engineering (STEM) or a related field of study with 3 years of experience in optical system design, test and automation
Deep experience in electro-optical system development and performance testing. to include: MTF testers, spectrophotometers, image quality test equipment, measurement microscopes, kinematic position systems, manual and motorized translation stages, test system database integration, general lab automation
Experience with machine vision camera design, development and integration
Understands optical system and component design and construction
Strong background in simulation and test automation using, Python,  MATLAB or C
Strong background in imaging lens design in Zemax, Code V, OSLO, etc, and  mechanical CAD software
Strong knowledge of statistical analysis and gauge RR
Familiarity with quality control systems, engineering design processes and revision control
Familiarity with prototype fabrication techniques such as light machining, 3D printing, soldering, optics cleaning and polishing
Proven experience in the development and automation of optical test laboratories
Proven experience in the development of imaging lens systems

Bonus points: 

Experience reviewing AV  ADAS vehicle logs to understand sensor impairments that challenge the autonomous system performance 
A background in robotics, particularly self-driving, whether on- or off-road
Familiarity with camera calibration
Familiarity with Machine Learning systems
Expert in Python, C, MATLAB, JMP, FRED

Why you should join us: 

Opportunity to contribute across multiple disciplines (sensor hardware, sensor software, Machine Learning)
Speaking opportunities are encouraged and supported
