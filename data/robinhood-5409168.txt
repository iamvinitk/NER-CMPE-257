What youll do

Build the marketing strategy and be the champion for Robinhoods Power Customers
Lead go-to-market strategy and execution for product and feature launches and ongoing marketing campaigns
Partner with performance, lifecycle, and brand marketing teams to define and implement strategic growth strategies
Craft end-to-end marketing campaigns that build relevance and excitement for the Robinhood brand and high value customers in particular
Create differentiated narratives with messaging that continuously improves with user feedback, testing, and a deep understanding of our target audience
Drive market and competitive assessments with research and data teams by surfacing qualitative and quantitative customer insights to craft product and engagement strategies, ensure product-market fit, and shape the product roadmap

Understand the multiple players in the ecosystem (including consumers, policymakers, news media, government)
