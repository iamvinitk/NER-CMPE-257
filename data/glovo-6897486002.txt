YOUR MISSION:
Within our local Finance team, youll be working alongside talented and passionate people, creating value and owning finance processes. We hire for attitude and strongly believe that everything can be learned with enough motivation. As a Finance analyst, youll be exposed to all finance functions across both the established platform business and the newly launched Glovo Express.
THE JOURNEY:

Prepare bi-weekly cash flow forecast and variance analysis;
Produce regular and ad-hoc reports concerning AR, AP, inventories, billing, etc.;
Respond to inquiries from internal (HQ, Regional, other local departments) and external stakeholders (business partners, authorities, banks);
Assist with reconciliation of management and financial reports on a monthly basis;
Communicate with regional and HQ stakeholders on a regular basis;
Support the local implementation of new business processes and optimization initiatives;
Act as trusted advisor to the Commercial team for finance-related topics;
Actively collaborate with your colleagues and learn from each other in a supportive environment that allows you to grow, develop and make a difference together.

WHAT YOU WILL BRING TO THE RIDE:

Bachelors degree in Finance, Accounting, Business or similar; 
2 years of experience in a finance role;
Fluent in English;
Confident Excel and Google Sheets user;
Ability to think critically and problem-solve in a fast-paced environment;
Excellent time management skills and attention to detail;
Effective communication skills to collaborate with various stakeholders;
Demonstrated ability to work independently and as a member of a team;
Continuous improvement mindset.
