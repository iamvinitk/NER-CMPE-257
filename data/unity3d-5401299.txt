The opportunity 
Unity is seeking a Deal Operations Associate Lead to lead our Deal Operations Team for AMER within our growing global core Sales Operations team. This role would support our internal sales teams and their clients with Salesforce ticket management, process improvement, identifying key data insights, building strong processes and maintaining response times. This role will require coordination across multiple teams, following established protocols, multitasking, systems know-how, attention to detail, and excellent communication and problem-solving skills. Your day-to-day will consist of team management, critical issue support, process development, finding opportunities to improve operational efficiency, and being a general go-to person for the America Core Sales organization.
What youll be doing

Work with Sales Leadership to enable success of the sales organization by supporting the order processing and execution of various operational activities for opportunity to cash process
Partner with Sales, Finance, Marketing on data accuracy, order processing, contract review and engagement programs to improve revenue for the business
Assist in the definition of As-Is processes, identify problems  implement improvements  automation ideas to enhance sales admin efficiency globally
Drive operational efficiencies by automating and improving processes, tools, and dashboards that scale

What were looking for

Experience working with a growing sales organization in sales operations, finance, business operations, or consulting
Self-Motivated with outstanding interpersonal skills (written  verbal)
Strong proactive attitude and can demonstrate accountability with small and big initiatives
Understanding of contracting terms and compliance requirements for booking processes

You might also have

Self-starter, independent, and able to execute with high-level mentorship and minimal direction
Strong problem solving, judgment, and organizational skills
Passionate about details, systems, and process
Strong understanding of sales processes, roles, and responsibilities
