We are in search of a top-tier Senior Accountant of Compensation to join our Compensation GL team.  The ideal candidate is a detailed-oriented, self-motivated team player, who enjoys working in a challenging and fast-paced environment.  This individual should have a solid working knowledge of GAAP, SEC regulations, and internal controls and will be responsible for the timely and accurate recording of compensation related activities of the company.  This includes recording bonuses and related accruals; and the Sales commissionsCompensation plan calculation and accruals.  Additionally, this role will be responsible for supporting the month-end close process, SOX compliance control execution and quarterlyannual audit process, as well as identifying and driving project-based work to provide process efficiencies for the team.  This role will be reporting to the Corporate Lead Senior Accountant.  This role will be required to work in the US (Pacific Time) hours (9:00pm - 6:00am IST).
 
What youll be doing 

Perform various GL month-end close activities, including preparation and review of journal entries and balance sheet reconciliations for bonus, commission, and other GL related transactions
Responsible for the preparation of the Corporate Performance bonus plans, other bonuses, and related accruals
Prepare journal entries for commission accruals and Deferred Commission amortization, as well as roll-forward schedules
Prepare and review month-end closing journal entries, including resolving discrepancies and ensuring entries are in accordance with US GAAP
Prepare PL and BS fluctuation analyses
Assist in preparing internal and external financial reporting schedules
Support the quarterly reviews, annual audit requests and inquiries from our external auditors
Ad hoc accounting projects and requests, as needed

 
What youll bring to the role

Bachelors degree, with an emphasis in Accounting or Finance
6-9 years of combined public accounting and private industry at a publicly traded company
CA or CPA candidate a plus
Solid working knowledge and application of US GAAP, SEC regulations, and SOXinternal controls
Familiarity with the operations and related accounting for bonuses, commissions, and payroll globally
Strong problem solving, analytical, and organization skills
Ability to execute, follow-up, and ensure attention to detail     
Strong interpersonal and communication (both verbal and written) skills
A team player, adept at building relationships across the organization
Ability to adhere to deadlines in a fast-paced environment, without sacrificing accuracy
Strong systems knowledge, including the ability to quickly learn new systems
Proficient with Microsoft tools (Excel, Word, PowerPoint)
Experience with NetSuite preferred 
Experience using Workday, ADP, or other comparable payroll software a plus
