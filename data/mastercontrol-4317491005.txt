Summary
MasterControl is looking for an experienced Technical Product Manager (TPM) to work within the Data Platform  Engineering team. The Data Platform team is responsible for building and operating the infrastructure and components that power MasterControls data infrastructure and data first initiatives. This includes the creation and maintenance of all key systems related to the ingestion, storage and processing of all operational and analytical data, while enabling downstream consumers including BI, DS  ML.What you will bring: You are a seasoned Technical Product Manager with strong experience in managing and delivering successful products within the data infrastructure space. You will drive project and program management for foundational components of MasterControls Data Platform. You will work with engineering teams across the larger product organization in the delivery of data centric products and features. You will strive to deliver a best in class features that will enable the next-generation of data first products, including setting team priorities, balancing technical tradeoffs related to cross-team and organizational deliverables, practicing and evolving best practices and contributing to both software and data engineering foundations.This is a highly visible position that will interact across many levels of the engineering and product organization. Your ability to communicate, influence others, and translate product features and requirements into technical roadmaps and deliverables will be critical to the success of MasterControl initiatives.Key Responsibilities:

Manage technical program specifics across multiple data focused disciplines including scope, schedule, quality and critical path features.
Analyze and influence through the use of KPIs to drive technical decisions and tradeoffs while creating sustainable processes and coordination of releases.
Manage and coordinate across multiple functional teams while maintaining a focus on best practices and engineering excellence.
Create an environment that fosters open communication, continuous improvement.
Plan and manage project tasks, dependencies, schedules, and resources related to delivery of complex interconnected systems.

Qualifications and Skills:

Bachelors or Masters degree in Computer Science or another equivalent discipline  experience.
8 years of working directly with engineering teams.
5 years of technical product or program management experience.
Familiarity with big data technologies and frameworks such as Hadoop, Spark, or Kafka.
Experience managing projects across multiple teams, building sustainable and repeatable processes.
Excellent communication and collaboration skills with a track record of technical leadership.
