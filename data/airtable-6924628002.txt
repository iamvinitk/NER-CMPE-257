At Airtable we are passionate about how our product democratizes software creation and empowers anyone to create anything. Just like Lego blocks, what our customers can build with Airtable is virtually limitless. This opens up all sorts of exciting possibilities, and so our Sales team works closely with users on their unique needs.  As part of the Sales Strategy and Operations team, youll be the thought and execution partner to sales leaders, providing insight through hypothesis driven analysis, conducting quarterly and annual planning, defining success metrics and creating scalable reporting, and designing workflows and systems.  Please note: This is an IC Position. 
There is a lot of building to do.  Were looking for folks who know what good looks like, but who are flexible, resourceful, collaborative and really excited to join us on our adventure to figure out both the right long term vision and the right next steps for tomorrow.  
What youll do
As part of the Sales Strategy and Operations team, you will:

Drive forecast management excellence to ensure sustained, predictable growth 
Orchestrate the design and implementation of all aspects of the Go-To-Market plan 
Design and manage core sales ops processes such as target setting and forecasting
Determine sales models by segment 
Act and be seen as a general problem solver able to break down the hardest problems
Define requirements and work with systems teams to implement team tooling
Partner closely with cross-functional partners on priority go-to-market initiatives

Who you are

You have 7 years prior experience in sales or customer success strategy and operations for a high growth company, or operational experience in investment banking, management consulting, private equity, venture capital, or business insights  analytics roles  
You demonstrate the ability to scale and equip the business in a strategic manner, communicate big picture opportunities and make data-driven decisions
You take a proactive, consultative approach, building trust consistently and quickly
You have an enthusiastic roll up your sleeves mentality
You work well under pressure, with a high degree of adaptability and flexibility in a fast paced, rapidly changing environment
You are strong in excel and analytical tools.  You are advanced with SQL or have a demonstrated ability to ramp quickly on analytical tools
You have the ability to translate complex information into easily consumable insights
