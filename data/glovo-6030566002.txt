YOUR MISSION
As part of the Sales team you will be directly responsible for partnering with both smaller local stores and bigger brands to display them on our Glovo platform. You will work with the support of other sales executives and be part of a local cross-functional team.
As a Glovo Sales Executive, you will be a key representative of our companys sales team, responsible for creating a pipeline of and signing up local stores and corporate customers around the cities of your region. In some cases they will be existing cities that will need you focussing on increasing content or figure out the best restaurants in town and convince them to join our platform; in some others you will have to build our portfolios of restaurants and partners from scratch in a matter of weeks and subsequently grow them to ensure enough content quality in the city.
