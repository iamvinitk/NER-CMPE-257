The Finance  Strategy Manager for the Engineering Finance team plays an impactful and critical role in Robinhoods success by driving financial planning processes, providing  decision-making support, and working with various business partners to develop strategic analysis around key initiatives. 
The ideal candidate will have experience influencing business leaders and supporting decision making in evolving and fast paced technology environments. This role requires someone who can navigate ambiguity and interdependence, successfully lead and drive multiple cross-functional projects end-to-end. This role has regular interaction with Engineering and Security leadership at Robinhood and will require strong interpersonal and communication skills and executive presence.
It is preferred that this role is located in one of the office locations listed on this job description which will align with our in-office working environment. This position is only eligible for remote work in limited geographies within the US where we do not have physical office locations. Please connect with your recruiter for more information regarding our in-office philosophy and expectations.
What youll do

Successfully deliver on a wide breadth of financial processes including (but not limited to): month-end close and forecast, expense and headcount plans, financial consolidation, resource allocation, and analysis and recommendations for business partners
Engage directly with Security and Engineering teams to drive the strategic and financial planning processes (e.g., monthly business reviews, forecasts, annual budget, and long range plans), and prepare financial models and analysis to influence investment decisions
 Analyze results of business critical metrics, and present risks and opportunities to Business Partners, Finance Leadership, and the Executive team
 Partner with senior leaders and find opportunities to create efficiencies, measure business performance, and find additional sources for cost savings
 Collaborate with cross-functional People teams such as Recruiting and Workforce Planning
Conduct business case modeling, run analyses and ad-hoc financial analysis as needed
 Improve and simplify processes, drive standardization, and build scalable solutions to support the next wave of Robinhoods growth

What you bring

B.A. in Economics, Mathematics, Computer Science, or a related field
5 years of work experience in Finance, with 2 years supporting business partners (ideally EngineeringSecurity business partners), in high growth technology companies 
Excellent analytical financial modeling, forecasting skills, and the business judgment to balance the details while not losing sight of the big picture
Ability to work independently and operate in a fast-paced dynamic environment 
Ability to influence decisions through use of data, logical reasoning, and the presentation of alternatives
Strong familiarity with analytical tools (Looker, Excel) and financial reporting softwares (such as AWS Cloud Spend, Oracle or Anaplan)
A positive attitude! You have a desire to work hard, be creative, stay curious, and maintain a can-do approach
MBA or an advanced degree in business, management or finance
