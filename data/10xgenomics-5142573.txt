
About the Role:
 
The Sales Associate, Northeast (New York) manages the customer sales process for all products lines, accounts andor sales opportunities. Responsibilities include generating qualified sales leads, developing and implementing sales plans through both strategic and tactical activities, engaging with existing customers, guiding customers to products that best meet their needs, coordinating with internal team to address customer questionsobjections, providing budgetary pricing, negotiating win-win deals and closing the sales. The role also collaborates closely with the wider sales team to meet and exceed sales quotas for a specific product line, account type andor sales territory, accurately forecast sales projections, and provide regular updates on status of sales opportunities.
What you will be doing: 

Manages the customer sales process for specific territories, products lines andor accounts.
Partner with territory sales team to meet sales targets, provide accurate forecasting, and maintain lead, opportunity, and customer information in the CRM database.
Responsible for growth of pre-defined accounts to meet and exceed revenue goals.
Uses in-depth knowledge of 10x technology, products and services to help customers formulate strategy for their project  business direction. Anticipates and identifies customer problems  needs and recommends appropriate solutions.
Travels to customer and prospect accounts, conferences, and 10x sponsored events as needed to drive successful growth of the business.
Independently establishes and executes on strategies for identifying and obtaining new customers and maintains  grows existing customer accounts in order to meet and exceed revenue goals.
Identifies possible risk to business and makes decisions to determine next steps to mitigate risk and ensure growth of sales pipeline.
Engages cross-functional departments to build and deploy specific strategies to drive revenue growth at a district, territory, andor account level. 
Actively pulls together people across functionalproduct lines to solve customer issuesproblems.
Establishes and maintains strong, positive, productive relationships with clients including product end-users, management and purchasing contacts.
Tracks ROI of strategic efforts and reports on all activities to managers.
