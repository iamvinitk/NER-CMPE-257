Your Role
Were looking for a Sr. Manager of Planning, Inventory  Logistics who will be responsible for the oversight and strategic management of applicable activities in our hardware business unit, including production forecasting, customer fulfillment and inventory management. Youll work closely with your team to develop processes that advance the maturity of our planning process, and develop a robust importexport program to enable global shipments of our products.For this role, were currently considering candidates who live within a commuting distance of our offices in Lincoln or Omaha. But with our flexible work policy, there arent any current requirements for the number of days you come to the office.
Must-Haves

Experienced. Youre familiar and knowledgeable with planning global logistics, and have worked with manufacturing operations for five or more years.
Critical thinker. Youre able to reason through complex problems and develop intuitive and creative solutions
A people leader. Youve led a team and understand how to help them achieve their goals while focusing on the bigger team picture.
A multi-tasker. You find it easy to juggle day-to-day activities while driving the most important projects to the finish line.

Nice-to-Haves

E-commerce expertise. Experience managing and supporting e-commerce would be a plus.
Collaborative. Wed love to see a proven record of influencing teams above, below and across your level.
ERP and PLM experience. This role will be heavily involved in guiding new ERP modules, and reporting and participating in the rollout of our PLM system, but experience here isnt a necessity if youre eager to learn.
