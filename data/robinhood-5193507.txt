Robinhood is hiring a Consumer Communications Manager who will be responsible for delivering exceptional media and consumer results focused on our technology, services, and products. Youll help us share stories about our core products and technology across Robinhood to consumer-focused publications and media outlets, including broadcast TV. Youll be adept at bringing colleagues together across product, marketing, and brand to capture the imagination of customers and drive brand awareness and participation. Youll explore innovative ways to tell our story through consumer press, finding new ways to reach people and to help them see themselves as investors. This role reports to our Director of Communications.
The role is located in the office location(s) listed on this job description which will align with our in-office working environment. Please connect with your recruiter for more information regarding our in-office philosophy and expectations.
What youll do

Drive consumer communications around Robinhood products and technology. 
Work cross-functionally to develop and lead consumer PR strategies that amplify integrated campaigns with Product Marketing, Brand and other teams
Develop and execute PR strategies for product launches and updates that reach a consumer audience, supporting the broader product communications strategy.
Manage proactive news engine across consumer print, web and broadcast outlets and reactive inbound media inquiries 
Coordinate press briefings, media tours and events, and create press materials including blog posts, media pitches, FAQs and fact sheets 
Work closely with Compliance and Legal regarding all aspects of your role

About you:

5 years of experience in fields related to public relations, communications, and journalism 
A track record of launching products and identifying evergreenfeature stories that resonate with consumer audiences
Strong relationships with consumer publications, broadcast outlets and culturally relevant media organizations 
Expertise in boiling down complex topics in an easy-to-understand way
Deep experience working cross-functionally and influencing internal stakeholders; communicating up, down and across the organization on media strategy; and managing multiple projects on tight deadlines 
Excellent writing and media relations skills 
Proven ability to reach Gen Z and Millennial audiences 
Experience leading campaigns and product launches globally 
Have a strong pulse on culturally relevant trends
