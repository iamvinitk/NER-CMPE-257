ROLE SUMMARY:
The Production Designer is responsible for supporting the Creative team in asset production and management necessary for a variety of work across all our owned and third party channels. This role will also work in partnership with the Project Management team to establish and maintain efficient processes ensuring easy asset accessibility and control.RESPONSIBILITIES:

Directly support the brands creative needs to support owned, retailed, and third partypartnership channels
Ensure the highest brand standards and design consistency cross-functionally
Understand best practices in both digital and print across all channels of the business through tenured experience
Lead best practices in file management and naming; organize and maintain digital files, assets, and design resources for easy accessibility and version control
Design and translate creative concepts and ideas into visually appealing and on-brand materials for print and digital
Create layouts, mockups, and prototypes for various projects ensuring the design elements are consistent, cohesive and visually engaging
Main focuses will be on versioning content, deck productions, asset creation, and photo retouching

REQUIREMENTS:

BA degree preferred
 3-5 years of relative design experience as part of a creative team with consumer facing work
Must be proficient in retouching; eCommerce experience is required; retail design experience preferred
Experience in Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, Klaviyo required; must have full knowledge of delivery formats for print and digital display
Experience with server organization and establishingmaintaining efficient processes preferred
An eye for design and understanding of how to translate design through different experiences; an understanding of balancing, editing, and overall making the most out of an image through retouching in a consistent, thoughtful, and dependable way
Ability to multi-tasks, organize, and communicate cross-functionally preferred
