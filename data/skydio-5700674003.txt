As a warehouse inventory control analyst you will be responsible for receiving, shipping, and inventorying materials from our Hayward manufacturing site. This will be an individual contributor role taking direction from a warehouse leadsupervisor in a fast paced, high quality, and constantly changing environment. 
How youll make an impact:

Develop, implement and maintain all the procedures and protocols in regard to proper inventory control for the purpose of maintaining accurate financial information
Plan daily cycle count process and set a standard to develop KPI (Key performance Indicator)
Prepare weekly, monthly, and annual summaries related to inventory accuracy and share with team
Recommend process changes or system enhancements based on findings
Develop strong partnerships between planning, finance and other business functions
Audit day to day inventory transactions and correcting any discrepancies before moving items to production lines
Document inventory discrepancies in inventory issue tracker such as mislabeled items, incorrect revs to find out root causes and suggest counter measures
Oversee cycle count program, implementation of visual and systemic tracking
Plan for Physical Inventory quarterly
Improve the accuracy of inventory 99.5 and sustain FIFO methodology
Manage a team in quarterly PI (Physical Inventory) 

What makes you a good fit:

Must be a high school graduate
Analytical skills with strong attention to detail
Keen eye for continuous improvement
Basic knowledge of ERP (enterprise resource planning) system NetSuite
Excellent communication (oral  written) and organizational skills
Experience with tools such as Slack, Notion, G-suite (Google Sheets, Forms, Drive)
Comfortable with pulling reports and collaborating with system experts to create dashboard
