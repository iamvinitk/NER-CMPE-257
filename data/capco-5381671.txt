Role Description 
The opportunity is to join our fast-growing Capital Markets Domain as a Change Management consultant. The role will entail working with some of the leading financial institutions in the market to achieve compliance within tight deadlines against a variety of different regulatory requirements, cost optimisation and revenue generating projects, working predominately on large scale change and transformation. 
You will have deep change management experience and prior success as a management consultant or within a change role in a financial services institution. As a well-respected targeted consulting organisation we are seen as a leader with vision capable of driving high quality deliverables.
