About the Role
You will report into an Engineering Manager. Once our offices reopen, we expect this role to be hybrid with some time in-office and some time remote
DoorDash is building the worlds most reliable on-demand, logistics engine for delivery. We are seeing hypergrowth and expanding our Engineering offices globally! We are looking for iOS engineers to join our Bay Area or Seattle office and to work on building and maintaining a large scale 24x7 global infrastructure system that powers DoorDashs 3-sided marketplace of Consumers, Merchants and Dashers.
Were looking for multiple product-focused iOS engineers who love building reliable, intuitive, and beautiful experiences on mobile. We arent just developing an app; were building a mobile ecosystem from the ground up that integrates the consumers, merchants, and drivers. This is a full-stack role where youll not only build complex apps but will also make impactful decisions on the product.

Work within our product teams to build exciting features
Improve our code base through continuous refactoring
Solve challenging real-time data transfer and synchronization problems
Extend and implement our APIs
Implement unit tests
Keep up-to-date with the industry standards of iOS development
