While Pulumi is headquartered in Seattle, WA., we are dedicated to being a remote first company. This role will be located remotely in the United States or Europe.
Responsibilities:
Our team builds and maintains the github.compulumipulumi open-source project. Thousands of companies rely on Pulumi to program the cloud and safely deploy infrastructure every day. 
On any given day at Pulumi you might find yourself: 

Working on the code generators that translate our universal cloud resource schema into SDKs in Go, TypeScript, Python, C, Java.
Designing programming models and type systems, including our homegrown cloud monads, Input[T] and Output[T]. 
Writing transpilers and program generators that take intermediate targets (such as YAML, or HCL) and produce full fidelity, functional Pulumi programs.
Building multi-language and multi-runtime matrix testing frameworks.
Adding new features to our desired state configuration engine.
Analyzing coverage metrics and writing proposals to add fuzz and property testing to key areas of our codebase.
Designing open-source APIs, SDKs, and public facing developer tools.
