The Pure Storage Federal Principal Technologist (PT) is a senior level technical thought leader within the Global Strategy and Solutions (GSS) organization. They engage with customers, partners, and sales teams at a regional level to provide technical leadership for the most complex strategic pursuits and sales opportunities. The PT is part of the Office of the CTO with all the responsibilities and privileges that confers.
This senior level resource will be comfortable quickly establishing themselves as a thought leader and trusted advisor with their customer stakeholders. The PT must be knowledgeable across industry, business, IT strategy, and technology domains and be comfortable presenting to customer IT and C-suite executives, discussing process with business managers, working with app teams on solutions architecture, and with storage admins designing operating models and integrations  from boardroom to hackathons to storage operations.
In addition, its critical they are accustomed to creating a value proposition that is directly linked to a customers business model and desired outcomes, be skilled in quickly learning new business models and drivers and have the ability to articulate how Pure and our partners can help them meet their goals. They will also keep up with the latest trends, be a visionary thinker, and have experience driving technology change in organizations.
Finally, the successful PT will have come from a technical background and have transitioned through an architecture role before growing their business and commercial skills. They will have gained understanding of customers business fundamentals and industry drivers using consultative methods, and will have built rapport with business leaders in order  to ensure that new disruptive approaches are implemented.
