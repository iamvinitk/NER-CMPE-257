About the job
This position is needed to ensure the Platform team has strong technical leadership to support building the infrastructure tooling necessary to achieve the goals of the Twilio Flex product. 
 
As a Staff Software Engineer on our Platform team, you will help develop repeatable and reusable infrastructure implementations that will allow Core Platform teams to seamlessly build, test, and deploy their code in ways that allow our product scale globally.  Youll work with a passionate, versatile, and collaborative team, tackling tough problems as we continue to push our technology forward. Additionally, youll have the opportunity to influence and implement architectural solutions that will enable us to achieve our goal of being the next generation customer interaction platform.
 
Responsibilities
In this role, youll:

Utilize your knowledge of architectural patterns and archetypes to help build mature, distributed, cross-region services
Create reusable and repeatable infrastructure implementations that delivery teams can leverage to more easily and confidently deliver software to our customers. 
Cultivate a culture of learning, knowledge sharing and collaboration with a focus on operational excellence
Act as a technical leader and a mentor to junior members of the team.
Focus on execution leadership to ensure our team is able to deliver on our commitments.
