Your Mission
As a financial institution moving billions of GBP of customer money in more than 70 countries, Wise must have bulletproof financials. 
Wise is rapidly expanding globally, creating new products to consumers and businesses, and entering new jurisdictions. This is a unique opportunity to  lead complex projects implementing new Wise products and markets into Wises finance system  supporting Wise in being financially compliant and ensures our bigger team can make timely, accurate and sustainable business decisions based on a global set of financials.

How youll contribute to our Mission

Our Finance Implementation team ensures that anything happening in Wise that has a financial impact  is integrated  into our financial systems and is properly recorded and controlled. This requires working closely with our product and engineering teams and finance stakeholders on understanding how things work in Wise and how they translate into compliant financials 
Youll be responsible for understanding the whys and hows of the Wise and Finance integration and communicate across all finance stakeholders and financial concepts to engineers
You will figure out a plan and work with a team of product managers, engineers, analysts and operations specialists to integrate all new Wise products, regions and entities into our finance ledger
You will support the team with clear documentation including requirements, detailed project plans, resource allocation, testing, delivery etc.
You will work with our internal finance teams, such as Accounting, Tax, Reporting and Financial Planning  Analysis to ensure clean, accurate and compliant financials in all our entities worldwide in a timely manner
