At Lyft, our mission is to improve peoples lives with the worlds best transportation. To do this, we start with our own community by creating an open, inclusive, and diverse organization.
Lyft is looking for experienced software engineers from a scope of disciplines. We are growing our team with people who want to build, improve and incorporate technologies that make the lives of our community more enriched. As an engineer at Lyft, youll collaborate with teams like product, data science, analytics, and operations on code that empower us to iterate quickly, while focusing on delighting our passengers and drivers. 
Responsibilities:

Provide technical leadership and direction to the team, helping define the teams roadmap, architecture, processes, and best practices
Write well-crafted, well-tested, readable, maintainable code. Leave the code in a better state than when you found it (progressive refactoring).
Perform thoughtful code reviews for colleagues to ensure code quality and distribute knowledge
Unblock, support and effectively communicate cross functionally in order to achieve results
Evangelize appropriate tech and engineering best practices, and occasionally give internal or external tech talks
Participate in hiring activities: take part in technical interviews, live coding, share detailed feedback to hiring best technical talent in the function
Act as a mentor to other developers on the team and across the company

Mandatory Skills

6 years of software engineering industry experience
Experience with any backend programming language (e.g., Python, Golang, Java, C, node.js)
Solid knowledge of algorithms, data structures, and object oriented programming
Strong experience with relational andor non-relational databases
Ability to design and implement scalable and reliable software solutions
Passion for new technologies and performing research and development tasks
Ability to learn fast, collaborate cross-functionally and communicate effectively.
Write clear, scalable and clear design documentation

Nice to have

Familiarity with Flask
AWS experience (EC2, Redshift for data warehouse, Elastic MapReduce for data processing)
Experience in microservices architecture
Experience managing small engineering teams
