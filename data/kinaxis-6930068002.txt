Job location: our office in Ottawa, Canada. We work in a hybrid model where the expectation is to work a minimum of 3 days in the office. 
About the team
As a member of the Finance team reporting to the Manager, Accounting and Reporting, the Financial Accountant is responsible for assisting with the month end close at the corporate level. This role will also be responsible for balance sheet reconciliations and audit support of all subsidiaries.
What you will do

Record month end transactions in the general ledger, and assist with month end processes
Enter transactions in the general ledger related to bank accounts across all entities, balancing cash and producing the monthly cash report.
Record, maintain and track fixed assets in accordance with internal control procedures
Prepare monthly balance sheet reconciliations
Assist with providing audit support across all entities
Preparation of monthly and quarterly variance analysis reports
Provide backup for general accounting team
Other ad-hoc duties as required
Recommend and implement process improvements where identified

What we are looking for

3-5 years general accounting experience
Post-secondary diploma in accounting related field
Experience in general accounting, intercompany accounting and month end close procedures for a global, public company
Experience in accounting for fixed assets
Proficient in Excel
Excellent communication skills and team player
Able to manage multiple and ever changing priorities
Experience with NetSuite considered an asset
