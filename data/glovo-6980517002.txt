YOUR MISSION
As a Data Science Manager for our Marketplace Optimization team, you will lead the most innovative project at Glovo, aiming at coordinating the different levers of our marketplace (pricing, promos, ranking, delivery time, etc) to maximize long term profitability (while controlling for customer retention). You will leverage our ML platform and Feature Store to build machine learning algorithms and statistical models to deliver robust and highly scalable solutions. You will also integrate models from different teams and orchestrate the different levers to optimize Glovos marketplace.This isnt your average project. Its high risk, high reward, and packed with complexities. But if youre up for the challenge, youll be leading a project thats in the spotlight, with regular presentations to the companys executives.
THE JOURNEY

Recruit, lead and motivate a team of Data Scientists and Product Scientists of different seniority levels.
Work hand-in-hand with business, product, and data stakeholders to craft a practical roadmap, delivering and showcasing value in quick, iterative cycles.
Keep the team focused and fast-paced. Launch quickly and frequently, measuring the impact with a solid experimentation framework.
Provide technical direction to your team, keeping a high bar on technical excellence in your deliverables.
Regularly engage with senior stakeholders to align the project roadmap with the companys direction and various realities. Stand firm on the established direction, providing stability to the project while accommodating company priorities.

 WHAT YOU WILL BRING TO THE RIDE

Experience leading innovation projects (high uncertainty, high pressure) end to end, with a proven track record.
An obsession with understanding the business and translating business problems into technical solutions.
Discipline, structure, and adaptability. You thrive in a dynamic environment and inspire your team to adopt the same mindset.
2 years of experience in a similar role, managing a data science team (5 people) or multiple data science projects. Preferably in a marketplace.
4 years of strong hand-on experience as an individual contributor in industry. Preferably in a marketplace. Knowledge of recommendation systems is highly desirable.
At least an MSc, and preferably a PhD in a scientific discipline such as Physics, Statistics, Computer Science or Mathematics.
Deep theoretical and applied knowledge in one or more of the following areas: supervised and unsupervised learning, deep learning, reinforcement learning, game theory, statistical inference, bayesian statistics, causal inference, time series analysis, decision theory, mathematical optimisation
