The opportunity
The Unity Editor and its pervasive runtime engine powers the creation and publication of real-time digital content in over 190 countries worldwide. With more than 5 billion downloads per month supporting Video Games to Film, Automotive to Construction, all while supporting a large array of 25 digital platforms. Unitys ability to deliver engaging digital experiences is only limited by ones imagination.
The User Profile Experience team sits at the center of Unity, providing Authentication, Login, Licensing, and Financial services to all products that enable our customers to quickly engage with the content, projects, and software tools they love. Our organization is looking for a highly collaborative and seasoned software engineer to work with our team in Bellevue on these core systems and infrastructure.
While reporting to the Head of Engineering, you will engage with Unitys global development teams to identify and act on areas to increase developer productivity, review technical plans and architecture, write technical design documents, and act as an engineering guide for individual contributor engineers.
What youll be doing 

Responsible for enumerating, understanding, and detailing the composition of engineering systems using first-hand analysis of source code, systems, data, and prototyping
Navigating the internal Unity engineering ecosystem through partnership and collaboration
Actively seek out alignment with leadership to align, guide, and craft the future of Unitys technology horizon
Participating in regular architecture reviews

What were looking for 

Deep experience building production software with Java, Golang, and Node.js
Expertise with CICD systems deploying code to production cloud environments
Senior technical experience working across different teams and projects
Demonstrable experience with software architecture and API design
Broad knowledge of architecting systems across multiple platforms, devices, and ecosystems at scale

You might also have

Experience with the Unity Editor and ecosystem
Deep knowledge of software authentication, authorization, and licensing systems
Experience working in a globally distributed, multi-cultural, and diverse environment

Life at Unity
Unity (NYSE: U) is the worlds leading platform for creating and operating real-time 3D (RT3D) content. Creators, ranging from game developers to artists, architects, automotive designers, filmmakers, and others, use Unity to make their imaginations come to life. Unity is the foundation upon which the worlds most powerful digital content is created. Specifically, Unitys platform provides a comprehensive set of software solutions to create, run and monetize interactive, real-time 2D and 3D content for mobile phones, tablets, PCs, consoles, and augmented and virtual reality devices.
In the fourth quarter of 2022, more than 70 of the top 1000 mobile games were made with Unity as derived from a blended number of the top 1000 games in the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. In 2022, Made with Unity Applications had more than 4 billion downloads per month. For more information, please visit www.unity.com.
