The opportunity
On the Unity PolySpatial team, we are bringing Unity to a new class emerging platform environments. Were looking for technical experts who can contribute to both low-level architecture and high-level feature implementation. Does the thought of diving into multithreading, graphics, data architecture, and networking get you excited? Does working with brand-new technologies such as Apples Vision Pro sound fun? Weve got the role for you! We write C (and some C) code daily to connect Unity to visionOS and beyond. Ideal candidates know something about a lot of areas related to high performance interactive content, and a lot about some of them.
Our team combines a startup mentality with the quality-of-life and work-life balance of a more established company. As soon as you join, well quickly bring you up to speed. Youll dive into implementing features, designing systems, doing hardware testing, and interacting with users who are actively using your work. You will work with the broader team to guide architectural decisions, and work with partners and customers to identify areas that might need your attention.
If you want to push the boundaries of whats possible, are willing to roll up your sleeves and jump into diverse areas, and want to have an enormous impact on the industry, Unity should be the next stop in your career!
What youll be doing

Writing fast, maintainable code in C (and some C)
Working with new platforms and ecosystems, such as visionOS
Collaborating with other teams within RD to help bring cohesive and user friendly experiences to our users
Helping accelerate a variety of projects, both as an individual contributor and as a mentor

What were looking for

Fearless attitude and willingness to jump in to unfamiliar areas
Technical experience across a range of technologies - graphics, netcode, authoring tools, animation, physics, script interpreters and compilers, etc.
Advanced C andor C development experience
Passion for performance, code analysis, and debugging tools
Excellent verbal and written communication skills with the ability to build relationships across the organization

You might also have

Experience developing game engine cores, middleware, or creating games
Experience with building and improving multiple game engine components; for example: graphics, animation, scripting, authoring tools, etc.
Experience with existing XRARVR technologies, such as ARCore, ARKit, OpenXR, Oculus, WinMR, Magic Leap, etc.

Life at Unity
Unity (NYSE: U) is the worlds leading platform for creating and operating real-time 3D (RT3D) content. Creators, ranging from game developers to artists, architects, automotive designers, filmmakers, and others, use Unity to make their imaginations come to life. Unity is the foundation upon which the worlds most powerful digital content is created. Specifically, Unitys platform provides a comprehensive set of software solutions to create, run and monetize interactive, real-time 2D and 3D content for mobile phones, tablets, PCs, consoles, and augmented and virtual reality devices.
In the fourth quarter of 2022, more than 70 of the top 1000 mobile games were made with Unity as derived from a blended number of the top 1000 games in the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. In 2022, Made with Unity Applications had more than 4 billion downloads per month. For more information, please visit www.unity.com.
