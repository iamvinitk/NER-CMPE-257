Email Deliverability is a core pillar to our customers success in delivering the right messages to the right people at the right time. The Email Deliverability Consultants provide technical deliverability support to our customers by root causing Klaviyo email services issues and providing solutions to support sending best practices. Deliverability Specialists ensure that Klaviyo and our customers are always up to date with the most recent industry best practices and changes. 
We are looking for a Email Deliverability Consultant who is excited about providing the best support to customers who are scaling their businesses with Klaviyo by demystifying deliverability and leveraging it as a tool for sustainable growth. 
DutiesResponsibilities:

Develop an in-depth knowledge of the Klaviyo platform, sending infrastructure, and technical configurations 
Provide high-quality technical support for Klaviyo customers, partnering with them to effectively understand and address their deliverability issues through email and phone. 
Communicate thoughtfully and effectively to provide answers to questions for both technical and non-technical end users 
Investigate and address email delivery problems by communicating with Internet Service Provider (ISP) abuse desks, email blocklist, and anti-spam technology providers in a professional manner
Provisioning of new customers with a new IP address and new DNS records 
Ensuring that Klaviyo and its clients are always up to date with the most recent industry best practices to improve sending reputation 
Establish and maintain documentation such as sending best practices and playbooks to support repeatability as we expand 
Radically collaborate with internal and external stakeholders to drive positive deliverability outcomes
Take an active role in thought leadership through writing blogs, articles, and whitepapers
