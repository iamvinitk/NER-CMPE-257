What we do: 
Working with the Manager of Supplier Development, this role will be responsible for leading supplier quality and development activities from supplier assessment and evaluation to production approval throughout the product life cycle. The ideal candidate will be encouraging supplier partnerships while improving supplier performance to meet Supernals expectations for quality, delivery, and cost goals.
This position will be required to work on-site 5 days a week.
What you can do:

Lead supplier evaluation process by assessing suppliers manufacturing technology, process capability, quality management system and HSE risk
Participate in Long Term Supplier Agreements to provide technical support, supplier knowledge, negotiation input and alignment of business awards to support procurement strategies
Drive supplier advanced product quality planning process (APQP) to meet quality and timing expectation for production part approval (PPAP)
Function as technical liaison between suppliers and internal technology development team to ensure design and process requirements are correctly interpreted and properly driven in suppliers manufacturing processes
Strong Technical project management skills, facilitate design for manufacturing communication between design engineering and supplier to improve producibility of the product design
Review supplier manufacturing processes, collaborate with suppliers on process improvement and value enhancement opportunities (VAVE)
Promote use of process focused continuous improvement tools such as Lean, Six Sigma, Mistake proofing(Poka-Yoke) , Measurement system analysis (MSA) and Failure mode and effect analysis (FMEA)
Perform supplier production readiness review audit to ensure suppliers manufacturing process is capable of meeting quality and capacity for volume production
Evaluates and monitors overall supplier performance metrics trends vs. contracted scope including supplier quality, reliability, and schedule. Identify gaps, root cause and drive supplier corrective actions for improvements
Support organizational change process on build to print projects
Identifyassess Risks  Opportunities throughout the duration of the program
Ability to find effective solutions; a make it happen demeanor is a must
May require up to 50 of domestic and international travel
