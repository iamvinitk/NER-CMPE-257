As a Customer Experience Liaison, youll act as the bridge between our client and their customers, ensuring their needs are met, issues are resolved, and their overall experience with the products or services is exceptional, with the goal to make an impact across the federal government.

Because we know our future relies on our deep understanding and relentless focus on our people, we live by our mission: A passion for our people. Value for our customers. 
If you think you can see yourself delivering our mission and pursuing our goals with us, then check out the job description below! 
What youll do: 

Play a critical role in maintaining and enhancing customer satisfaction, loyalty, and advocacy.
Serve as the primary point of contact for customers, addressing inquiries, concerns, and feedback via various communication channels (phone, email, chat, social media, etc.).
Build strong relationships with customers, understanding their unique needs and preferences to provide tailored solutions and recommendations.
Proactively engage with customers to gather feedback, assess satisfaction levels, and identify areas for improvement for products or services.
Collaborate cross-functionally with internal teams such as support, product development, and operations to advocate for customer needs and contribute to productservice enhancements.
Investigate and resolve customer complaints or issues in a timely and effective manner, escalating complex matters as needed to ensure swift resolution.
Maintain accurate and detailed records of customer interactions, feedback, and resolutions using CRM software or other relevant tools.
Assist in the development and implementation of customer support processes and guidelines to streamline communication and improve customer experiences.
Conduct regular follow-ups with customers to ensure their concerns have been addressed and their expectations have been met.
Contribute to the creation of customer-focused content, such as FAQs, knowledge base articles, and tutorials, to empower customers to find solutions independently.
Monitor customer satisfaction metrics and KPIs, preparing and presenting regular reports to management to highlight trends, opportunities, and challenges.

What youll need to succeed: 

At least 5 years of experience in a customer service or customer-facing role, ideally in a liaison or advocacy capacity.
Exceptional communication skills, both written and verbal, with a keen ability to empathize, persuade, and influence.
Strong problem-solving and conflict-resolution abilities, with a demonstrated capacity to handle challenging customer situations with tact and professionalism.
Proficiency in using customer relationship management (CRM) software and other relevant tools.
Highly organized and detail-oriented, capable of managing multiple tasks and priorities simultaneously.
Ability to collaborate effectively with diverse teams and departments to drive customer-centric initiatives.
A passion for delivering outstanding customer experiences and a genuine desire to continuously improve customer satisfaction.
Familiarity with customer experience best practices and industry trends.
Flexibility to work in a fast-paced and evolving environment.
An active TSSCI with CI poly or TSSCI with ability to pass a CI poly
Previous experience in B2B or B2C industries (depending on the companys focus) is a plus.
