What Youll Do:

Work with cross collaborative teams to expand upon, and maintain the security and stability of our endpoint management infrastructure with a primary focus on macOS.
Build out, support and maintain management tooling for ChromeOS, Windows, and mobile phones.
Assist in the building of new tools based on internal requirements and specifications.
Act as a point of escalation for other Corporate Engineering teams and assist in triaging issues related to CPE tooling.
Perform code reviews, write documentation and drive engineering best practices.

What Youll Bring:

Previous experience supporting a fleet of macOS devices
Have a basic understanding of modern endpoint management tooling
Have a strong IT support background
A scripting background using languages such as Python, or Bash
Be a creative thinker and problem solver that can just as easily work on their own or as part of a team

Bonus points:

Experience supporting Windows and ChromeOS
Experience building out API integrations for SaaS tools.
Experience with Open-Source tools
