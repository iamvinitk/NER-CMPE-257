Were looking for a Technical Program Manager to join the Strategic Programs team. You will be responsible for the strategy, planning, and execution of crypto-focused programs that are critical to Coinbase. What Youll Be Doing (i.e., Job Duties) 

Lead large scale programs that are crypto-focused, highly technical, and cross functional in nature
Set goals, align cross-functional teams, and execute
Be accountable for roadmap delivery including adoption and success criteria as defined in collaboration with your stakeholders
Team up with external  internal stakeholders (data Science, product, compliance, marketing, customer experience, engineering etc) to gather business requirements and define objectives
Manage requirements documentation, milestone mapping, prioritization, and program communication
Create thorough functional specs, project  release plans, and roadmaps
Monitor program status, identify issues  risks, succinctly communicate issues, and proactively collaborate to ensure timely resolution
