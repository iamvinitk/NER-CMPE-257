Executive Assistant
 
Why We Have This Role
Qualtrics is on the lookout for a proactive individual to assist two Directors. In this role, your primary focus will be on administrative tasks, while also being given opportunities to support team objectives, basic project coordination, and even take ownership of select projects. This role offers a significant chance for career development and learning. The ideal candidate would be someone who is organized, attentive, and eager to grow professionally. You should be skilled at prioritizing tasks and supporting the team efficiently. An understanding of the companys basic goals and guiding the executives towards those objectives will be expected. Adaptability, maintaining confidentiality, and a positive approach will be paramount.
 
How Youll Find Success
 
 
How Youll Grow


Project Coordination: The role involves opportunities to coordinate specific projects and manage business gatherings - experiences that can significantly advance your project management skills. Witnessing projects from conception to completion, you will understand the challenges and learn how to overcome them.
Interdepartmental Collaboration: Supporting teams across different locations and assisting in driving team initiatives can broaden your organizational reach and deepen your understanding of cross-functional processes. This will foster your ability to work effusively with diverse groups while improving your relationship-building and interpersonal skills.
Process Improvement: The chance to be involved in process improvement initiatives allows for a deeper understanding of the organizations operations. With time, youll cultivate problem-solving and analytical skills, and gain a valuable perspective on improving efficiency and productivity within a corporate setting.
Task Prioritization and Time Management: Managing the master schedule and ensuring executives are spending their time effectively requires significant prioritization and time management skills. Over time, mastering these skills will position you as an efficient manager, a trait valuable not only in this role but throughout your professional journey.
Communication Skills: Given the nature of the role, constant interactions with different stakeholders, both internally and externally, are inevitable. This will refine your communication skills, particularly in articulating ideas effectively, managing expectations, and resolving conflicts.


 
Things Youll Do


Project Management. While you will assist in the coordination of team meetings and business gatherings, there will be opportunities for you to lead specific projects, furthering your professional growth.
Cross Team Collaboration. Playing a crucial role in supporting teams across different locations, youll ensure smooth communication and even help drive certain team initiatives. The EA will assist with planning and execution of team all hands meetings, offsites, and socials.
Rhythm of Business Coordination. The EA will manage the master schedule (including annual and quarterly meetings) by arranging meetings and ensuring that the executives are spending their time effectively on the priorities that matter.
Process Improvement. Part of the day-to-day role for the EA will involve taking over some of the basic process improvement to support the business (e.g. see around corners to proactively solve potential issues and understand if we are making progress in areas necessary to goal completion).
Administrative Assistant. The EA will help set and track agendas for given meetings, take notes andor action items to document the decisions made in meetings, submit expenses monthly, and provide other administrative support as needed.
Travel and Logistics.  As an essential part of your role, youll be tasked with ensuring that every journey—whether by land, air, or sea—is smooth, efficient, and aligned with our executives schedules. This will involve researching the best travel options, coordinating with travel agencies, managing itineraries, and ensuring that any potential hiccups are addressed proactively. Your expertise will make certain that travel is not just about reaching the destination, but about optimizing the entire experience.
