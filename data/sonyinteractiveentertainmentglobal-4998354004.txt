The PlayStation brand falls under Sony Interactive Entertainment, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Sony Corporation.Role Overview:
Sony Interactive Entertainment (SIE) - PlayStation is looking for a Supply Chain Program Manager, PSD Commercial Operations, to join our e-commerce Commercial Operations team. PS Direct (direct.playstation.com) is PlayStations Direct-to-Consumer platform connecting millions of fans globally to the wide range of PS products. Come join us at PlayStation!
What youll be doing:
This is an exciting opportunity to be a key figure in the Commercial Operations team, focused on the direct-to-consumer channel (PS Direct). Highly cross functional and dynamic environment, where new products and initiatives will need your strong assessment to maintain the go-to-market performance in Europe. Youll have the opportunity to help build a best-in-class E2E supply chain. This is a busy environment, managing multiple product launches, performance of those launches to our customers  developing relationships within SIE. This role will need to be very comfortable in using data, SAP systems, Excel, and scenario planning as your main allies.
Key Areas of Responsibility:

Be a key contributor in an impactful, dynamic, data focused team.
Manage go-to-market strategy of new product launches from FG availability to delivery on Launch Day to our loyal fans.
Help drive continuous improvement efforts to support commercial strategies, building a streamlined fulfilment experience globally.
Communicate and influence cross-functional partners to support on Supply Chain initiatives.
Cultivate a collaborative and customer focused culture.
Maintain Inventory Availability for PSD across EU marketplaces at 100 availability.

Global PS Direct Commercial Operations:

Manage and communicate cross-functionally the Go-to-Market operational strategy for new product launches (NPI) as well as preorder operations tailored to each market.
Adapts the supply chain capabilities to the ecommerce business dynamics of growth and change.
Responsible to deliver OTSOTD (98), as well as a high NPS fulfilment survey score for preorders and new launches.
Inventory  Planning Stock Availability Management to ensure 100 stock availability across Europe and actively manage all SKUs.
Define operational requirements, adapt regional capabilities to a standardised operational mentality globally.
Supports test plan creation and UAT.
The voice of PSD Europe and striving for SME across systems, data and performance.

What were looking for:

8 years professional experience in Supply Chain, E-Commerce, Distribution or Logistics (including 3PL).
Demonstrated experience in e-Commerce order fulfilment, including distribution centre operations and supporting technologies.
Ability to work in a highly cross-functional dynamic and ever-changing environment and prioritise on the go.
Solid understanding of SAP system with advanced excel skills, whilst using data to create visual representation of results, projections, gaps and process.
Bachelors degree in business, project andor Supply Chain Management  equivalent experience would be desirable
Ability to communicate to different audiences, adapt information and present according to different needs
