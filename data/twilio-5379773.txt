About the job
This is an exciting opportunity to join a dynamic, growing training and enablement team that is responsible for onboarding and upskilling new Twilions in Customer Support on topics such as Twilios products, support processes and tools, technical skills, and soft skills. You will work with a team of instructional designers and technical trainers, as well as various stakeholders within Customer Support to assess organizational needs and to design, develop, and deliver training materials via different learning modalities to Twilions across the Support organization.
Responsibilities
In this role, youll design, create, and deliver engaging learning content (e.g. job aids, eLearnings, vILT courses, micro-learning video content, learning plans, and learner communications). Specific examples include:

Develop ILT courses using Google Slides and facilitate learning sessions and workshops; provide feedback and an expert learning opinion on ILT content and format to partners and stakeholders.
Create and maintain eLearning and microlearning content with a variety of authoring tools - e.g. Articulate 360, Camtasia, and SnagIt.
Develop additional content as required - e.g. course outlines, training aids, assessments, and facilitator guides.
Support the development of processes for training content pipeline, including intake, validation, review, launch, etc.
Manage the end-to-end learning development process, including stakeholder request management, needs analysis, development, delivery, evaluation, and revision.
