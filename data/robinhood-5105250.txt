Robinhoods network engineering team is in search of a hardworking and proficient Network Engineer with extensive experience to become a valuable member of the team. The ideal candidate will have a deep understanding of IP networking and be able to architect, implement, and maintain diverse network infrastructure. Additionally, the candidate will also have experience with network automation tools and programming languages such as Python, Ansible, and Terraform.
You will be working within a team of engineers and architects responsible for automation, monitoring, network deployments, AWS service management, and production peering support. You will be working closely with teams such as security, corporate engineering, business automation and third party partners to ensure a secure and reliable access to internalexternal resources and services.
What youll do

Design, develop, and maintain automated network solutions
Working in our cloud environment designing and maintaining end to end connectivity using Terraform
Fix and resolve complex network issues in a timely manner
Monitor network performance and proactively identify potential issues and participate in on-call rotation.
Develop and maintain network documentation and diagrams
Stay up to date with industry trends and standard methodologies for network engineering
Design and maintain automation scripts for the purpose of network device management.
Collaborate with other IT teams to ensure effective network operations

What you bring

5 years of experience in network engineering or a related field
Experience with routing and switching protocols including OSPF, BGP, MPLS, VLAN, and VPN
Solid understanding of network security and standard methodologies.
Experience with automation and scripting tools such as Ansible, Python, Terraform
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills along with excellent communication and partnership skills
Experience with cloud infrastructure and automation solutions such as AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud Platform

Core Tech Stack:

AWS Networking (Transit Gateway, SGs)
Infrastructure Security (Multi Factor Authentication, Access control ListsSecurity Gateways, ZeroTrust)
External  Internal Monitoring (SNMP, Netflow, Looking Glass)
Routing protocols (OSPF, BGP)
Network hardware (Juniper, Mist, Arista, PAN, Cisco, Zscaler)
Network architecture and design
Infrastructure as Code (Terraform, Ansible)
