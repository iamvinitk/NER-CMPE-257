About the team
The Enterprise Solution Architect, as a seasoned professional with in-depth experience, is responsible for a wide range of activities related to enterprise level solution architecture, change management, technology selection and alignment, master data management, integration planning, data flow management, and adherence to architecture standards, working predominantly on enterprise level solutions.
As a SME and trusted advisor to the business, the Enterprise Solution Architect consults and collaborates with internal stakeholders, IT Business Partners, developers, and project leads to assess and understand business objectives, business cycles, operating models, and processes, to recommend the technical solution architecture for the business, and to ensure that it aligns with our enterprise architecture and with our overall corporate strategies.
What you will do

Conduct full architectural review when a new technology is evaluated; provide recommendations with supporting documentation
Consult with stakeholders across the organization to ensure solutions are designed to scale, meet predictable future additions and enhancements, manage risk, and provide long term benefits to Kinaxis
Leverage technical and business expertise to identify, re-engineer, digitize, andor implement efficiencies and performance improvements to solutions and business processes.
In collaboration with business analysts and other architects, elicit requirements and functional specifications; assess current software systems in place to identify opportunities for improvement
Design solutions using existing and new technologies to meet current and predictable future business requirements.
Support the development and implementation of a value driven AI integrationutilization strategy for improved operability, productivity and integration of applications and related technologies.
Translate the vision of the project andor platform to the application and development team, while ensuring the new solution fits within the existing ecosystem.
Define features, phases, and solution requirements including solution architectures, frameworks, and strategies to meet business, functional and non-functional requirements including security.
Deliver architecture, integration, and migration strategies, ensuring execution is consistent with architectural standards and development practices.
Lead our Architecture Review Board processes to ensure the backlog of Architecture Reviews are planned, resourced and scheduled to support project timing and to meet business delivery expectations.
Ensure effective integration of applications to enable business improvements for analytics and reporting
Research industry trends, policies, and directives to ensure that business solutions align with corporate strategy; remain knowledgeable of new and emerging technology trends pertaining to applications including integration, development, authentication, automation, generative AI, digital transformation, analytics, etc.
Define application tiers, frameworks, component types and interfaces, as necessary to design, communicate and develop a business solution.
Define and document interfaces of manual to automated operations within application sub-systems, to external systems and between new and existing systems.
Provide specifications according to which the solution is defined, managed, and delivered.
Provide insight to identification and documentation of system-specific standards; programming, documentation and testing, program libraries, data dictionaries, naming conventions etc.
