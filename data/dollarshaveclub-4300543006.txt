ROLE SUMMARY:
We are looking for a Senior Analytics Engineer to lead the development of our analytics framework. Youll play a key role in amplifying our data-driven culture, architecting our reporting infrastructure, and partnering with the business to derive maximum value from our data. Youll work with Data Engineering and business end-users to make sure that data sources and key reports are accessible and discoverable, manage data source development and maintenance, data modeling, governance, and report design.RESPONSIBILITIES:

Partner with business stakeholders to establish and refine key metrics, creating dashboards that empower stakeholders to make data-informed decisions
Curate and publish standardized data sources for Explorers and Creators to access via Tableau Cloud
Develop, maintain, and manage data models that underlie Tableau data sources using dbt, leveraging tests and data development best practices
Design and implement Tableau Cloud data and workbook security model, and partner with IT to manage access, user roles, and permissions
Work with business partners to develop new reports and dashboards as needed and  optimize key reports
Draft and maintain comprehensive documentation detailing data models, data dictionaries, and related database specs
Lead the charge for making data discoverable by making documentation available and navigable for end-users so they can self serve
Work with end-users to educate them about getting the most out of Tableau
Collaborate with cross-functional teams, understanding their data needs and architecting tailored solutions
Proactively solve issues or errors within the data pipeline
Ensure data integrity, security, and adoption of best data management practices
Continuously evaluate and introduce cutting-edge technologies that enhance efficiency and value addition

REQUIREMENTS:

5 years of experience in data and analytics
3 years of experience with Tableau, with at least one year in an administrative capacity
2 years of experience working with dbt
Proficiency in SQL and experience with modern databases like Redshift, Snowflake, Postgres, MySQL, or Google BigQuery
Experience with data modeling and query performance optimization
Advanced experience in Tableau Desktop and Tableau Online or Tableau Server
Excellent problem-solving and quantitative skills, including the ability to identify root causes and recommend solutions
Familiarity with git workflows
A proactive, solution-oriented mindset with a predisposition towards collaboration and communication
Strong knowledge of data manipulation, database design, foundational data analytics and data platform structures; ability to manage large data sets and databases
