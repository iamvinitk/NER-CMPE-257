Robinhood is looking for a Staff Technical Program Manager to develop and drive high impact programs in Reliability and systems resilience that support the companys top most values of Safety First and Radical Customer Focus.
The role will be centralized within our growing Technical Programs team, and will partner with the cross-functional stakeholders to help craft programs to support the overall strategy, establish effective communication processes, build strong collaboration across the organizations, and ultimately drive execution within the Engineering org.
To be successful in this role, you will need to be able to be agile and adapt to the growing needs of the business, and have the ability to help build the sustainable practices that lead to a successful team.
The role is located in the office location(s) listed on this job description which will align with our in-office working environment. Please connect with your recruiter for more information regarding our in-office philosophy and expectations.
What youll do

Hands on, end-to-end program management from design to delivery
Establish, monitor, and analyze reliability metrics and devise plans for continuous improvement.
Establish robust processes to help the engineering teams to establish appropriate Reliability metrics, incorporate them into regular planning, and goal settings. 
Help evangelize the Reliability perspectives to include in the teams plans and overall company strategy (alt:  Facilitate communication between teams to ensure effective and efficient implementation of reliability practices.)
Collaborate with different engineering teams to troubleshoot and improve system performance and reliability.
Identify potential areas of weakness in our infrastructure and proactively drive changes to enhance system resilience.
Build strong relationships with XFN partners on the business side to ensure the alignment of needs and priorities with Engineering initiatives
Integrate High Availability (HA) and Disaster Recovery (DR) strategies and ensure they are incorporated into all projects.

What you bring

Proven experience as a Technical Program Manager or similar role in an engineering or software development environment, ideally with a focus on system reliability and availability.
8 years of experience with managing technical programs at scale
Strong technical background with advanced understanding of high availability strategies, disaster recovery measures, and system uptime. Familiarity with software development and system operations is a plus.
Distributed Systems: Experience working with large-scale distributed systems, which requires an understanding of how systems work together to provide a seamless user experience.
Cloud Providers: Extensive experience working with major cloud computing providers such as AWS, Google Cloud, and Azure. This includes a solid understanding of cloud architecture, cloud deployment models and cloud services reliability.
Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS), Platform as a Service (PaaS), and Software as a Service (SaaS): Familiarity working with these service models, and understanding their impact on system reliability and application performance.
Comfort with navigating ambiguity and translating it to impactful results
Effective written and verbal communication skills with both engineering and non-engineering audiences
Ability to juggle multiple responsibilities and prioritize automation over manual processes.
Familiarity with AWS or other cloud systems.

Bonus points:

Experience in Finance and Brokerage space.
