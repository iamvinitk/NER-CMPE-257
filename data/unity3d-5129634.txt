The opportunity
As an agile servant leader for the XR Input Engineering team, you will be joining the XR leadership team, having a direct impact on developers and end users. Your mission will include bringing improved XR input features and solving customers most pressing XR input issues. You will also have the opportunity to form a new team working on an industry leading and rapidly growing product. If making all things XR related reach their full potential drives you, Unity should be the next stop in your career!
At Unity, we are passionate about the new creative avenues that AR and VR bring to our developers. We are investing heavily in these futures, from integrating third party hardware to developing mechanisms to allow developers to easily take advantage of these new platforms.
Joining XR Engineering team means helping build one of the most popular game engines, integrating foundational ARVR technologies and devices. This role directly engages you with partners that are eager to see their platforms running experiences powered by Unity.
What youll be doing

Build and lead a new engineering team to support XR Input
Collaborate closely with the product manager of XR Input and the entire XR team to drive the vision and accelerate the improvement of new input capabilities into the Unity engine focused on hands, eyes and voice
Support business development and quarterly planning activities by reviewing contracts or features and providing estimates, risks and mitigation plans.
Work to implement processes, systems and culture for the team which result in high team morale and increased customer happiness
Ensure cross team collaboration with other XR engineering teams, other functions and groups across Unity

What were looking for 

Five years of software lead experience including having direct reports
Prior development experience with ARVR runtimes such as OpenXR, Oculus, SteamVR, WinMR, Magic Leap, etc
Advanced CC skills and C skills (intermediate)
Ability to mentorcoach team members, lead by example and build trust and positive relationships
Experience shipping and maintaining shipped products

You might also have

Experience steering the direction of strategic, tactical and operational roadmap of products
Familiarity with Unity Input System
Deep understanding of low-level input programming

Life at Unity
Unity (NYSE: U) is the worlds leading platform for creating and operating real-time 3D (RT3D) content. Creators, ranging from game developers to artists, architects, automotive designers, filmmakers, and others, use Unity to make their imaginations come to life. Unity is the foundation upon which the worlds most powerful digital content is created. Specifically, Unitys platform provides a comprehensive set of software solutions to create, run and monetize interactive, real-time 2D and 3D content for mobile phones, tablets, PCs, consoles, and augmented and virtual reality devices.
In the fourth quarter of 2022, more than 70 of the top 1000 mobile games were made with Unity as derived from a blended number of the top 1000 games in the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. In 2022, Made with Unity Applications had more than 4 billion downloads per month. For more information, please visit www.unity.com.
