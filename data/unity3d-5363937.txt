The opportunity
We are the Unity Digital Twins (Industry) Studio, an innovative team building custom software for a wide range of clients! Our professional services team works across multiple platforms incorporating groundbreaking technologies like XR, robotics, machine learning and IoT. We work on a diverse range of business verticals including automotive, retail, manufacturing and construction.
Were looking for a motivated Unity Engineer to help us build client solutions using Unity in Japan. Youll help deliver client projects, which vary in scope and duration.
A proven record of delivering high-quality software, with an eagerness to learn and grow. You have confidence and skills to foster and drive outstanding interactive experiences for discerning clients.
What youll be doing 

Work with clients on technical solutions from technical design through to implementation and deployment
Develop and deploy high-quality applications
Work with project managers, other developers, technical artists and creative members to help design the projects and implement them.

What were looking for 

Experience in real-time 3D products from concept to submission, using Unity
Strong understanding of the software development lifecycle
Good communicator in Japanese and English, with the ability to interface directly with clients

You might also have

Experience with CAD or BIM
Experience with Embedded HMI development
Experience with cloud development, IoT, simulation, or machine learning
Experience integrating new software development with existing enterprise systems

Life at Unity
Unity (NYSE: U) is the worlds leading platform for creating and operating real-time 3D (RT3D) content. Creators, ranging from game developers to artists, architects, automotive designers, filmmakers, and others, use Unity to make their imaginations come to life. Unity is the foundation upon which the worlds most powerful digital content is created. Specifically, Unitys platform provides a comprehensive set of software solutions to create, run and monetize interactive, real-time 2D and 3D content for mobile phones, tablets, PCs, consoles, and augmented and virtual reality devices.
In the fourth quarter of 2022, more than 70 of the top 1000 mobile games were made with Unity as derived from a blended number of the top 1000 games in the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. In 2022, Made with Unity Applications had more than 4 billion downloads per month. For more information, please visit www.unity.com.
