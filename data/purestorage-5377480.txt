As Product Manager in Pures Cloud Native Business Unit (Portworx), this role will be responsible for expanding our market leading container storage offering - Portworx Enterprise. Your focus will be to  align on themes and feature prioritization, distillation of features into user stories, ensuring Engineering can execute stories by working with the UX team and meeting the definition of ready, day-to-day prioritization, engagement in customer calls, and owning the overall execution. You will be part of the larger initiative that shapes how customers build new age application platforms using cloud native services.
WHAT YOULL BE DOING...

Lead the market leading product into the next stage of growth by shaping the roadmap in key areas of simplicity, automation and scalability.
Represent the customer for the Portworx Enterprise product and help identify new use cases and requirements by understanding customer pain points and business value drivers.
Collaborate with engineering, UX, and the overall Product Management organization to elevate the product experience and bring new capabilities to the customers.
Work closely with the stakeholders in roadmap execution by driving grooming sessions and handle day-to-day prioritization when new work should be planned  backlog needs attention 
Follow the lean startup methodology to ensure that the productmarket fit is constantly measured, product direction continues to delight customers and velocity of execution remains high
Align with the leadership team to establish the scope and goals of the effort and provide leadership for the program teams
Assist the go-to-market team in showcasing product capabilities through blogs, demos,webinars and solution briefs.

WHAT YOULL NEED TO BRING TO THIS ROLE...

5 years of experience in product management and 10 years of experience in building EnterpriseCloudSaaS products
Demonstrated ability to take charge of tough problems, attacking them from different angles and bring together a diverse array of stakeholders on the same page and drive great business results. 
Broad understanding of the cloud native services in areas of storage, data management, backup and security
Good knowledge of how cloud-native technologies like Kubernetes are transforming the infrastructure landscape and prior experience with cloud infrastructure technologies
Ability to define the right metrics, track and refine them and extract insights to drive decision making. 
Ability to operate in a fast-paced, agile development environment and manage multiple, concurrent priorities and projects
Bachelors Degree in Information Systems Management, Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or equivalent, Masters DegreeMBA a plus
Must be willing and able to work in an open office, team environment.
