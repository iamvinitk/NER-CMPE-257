What youll do: 

Engage team members constructively to select and implement data science tools to empower data for the Air Force.
Analyze results and work closely with senior Air Force clients to define follow-on actions.

What youll need to succeed: 

Ability to drive solutioning involving both unstructured and structured data to enable advanced problem-solving.
Ability to interpret results from multiple sources using a variety of techniques, ranging from simple data aggregation via statistical analysis to complex data mining independently.
Ability to lead the design and development of data-centric solutions.
Ten (10) years or more of progressive experience in data science.
DoD Secret clearance (minimum).
