The role:
Do you have a passion for security? OpenTable is looking for a highly motivated and experienced Principal Information Security Engineer. This individual will be responsible for ensuring the security and protection of the companys information assets and infrastructure. The Principal Information Security Engineer will also be a key player in developing the goals and mission of the security program and facilitating its advancement. This person will also:

Provide subject matter expertise on architecture, authentication, and system security
Collaborate and make proposals across several teams on cross-functional security initiatives
Teach, guide, and mentor new and existing team members
Play a central role in technical, business, and organizational contributions affecting the company
Provide a point of escalation for security incidents
Define security requirements for the implementation of new applications and projects
Assist in conducting risk assessments to evaluate the effectiveness of existing controls and determine the impact of proposed changes
Provide technical support to regulatory agencies, external auditors, and internal auditors
Develop and implement new initiatives to simplify, standardize and optimize threat monitoring operations methods
Create and implement new tools to reduce analysis and reporting times and increase security, availability, and service agility
Manage and support the development of security operations playbooks to ensure threat detection, monitoring, response
Provide tooling for complex investigations and deep analysis of security events focused on rapid containment, remediation, and mitigation
Architect and implement on-premise, cloud-based, enterprise-scale, and SaaS software solutions and the associated infrastructure and tech components

Please apply if you: 

Are a critical thinker and seek to understand the business and its control environment
Have a solid understanding of enterprise security best practices and compliance frameworks
Believe insight and objectivity are core elements to assure the effectiveness and efficiency of governance, risk management, and internal control processes
A steadfast focus on quality and timeliness
Embrace bold ideas and are intellectually curious; you like to ask questions, test assumptions, and challenge conventional thinking
Develop influential relationships based on shared risk objectives and trust to deliver outstanding business impact and elevate Audits value proposition
Are an educator - you have a passion for coaching and invest in the improvement of your team
Lead through change with candor and optimism
Create energy and an environment that fosters trust, collaboration, and belonging

If you are a highly motivated and experienced information security professional with a passion for security, we encourage you to apply for this exciting opportunity.
