Company Overview
At Zuora, we do Modern Business. Were helping people subscribe to new ways of doing business that are better for people, companies and ultimately the planet. Its an approach resulting from the shift to the Subscription Economy that puts customers first by building recurring relationships instead of one-time product sales and focuses on sustainable growth. Through our leading expertise and multi-product suite, we are transforming all industries and working with the worlds most innovative companies to monetize new business models, nurture subscriber relationships and optimize their digital experiences.

What youll do 
Youll help expand our relationships and value within strategic accounts and work with sales and marketing to break into new accounts.

Work with an incredible marketing team to target the right accounts based on ideal customer profiles, real-time engagement signals, and a finely tuned demand capture engine


Work with the global ZBR Center of Excellence and field enablement teams to identify the best ways to engage decision makers, communicate our value, and develop opportunities for our sellers


Strategize with your sales partners on account and territory plans, partner on campaign execution, nurture potential new business, and persuade C-level executives to engage


Master modern, best-practice prospecting methods and tools to be successful- Outreach, Gong, Salesforce, Alyce, Marketo, and others


Leverage multi-channel call, event, email, and social selling sequences 


Be entrepreneurial and constantly look for ways to better yourself, your team, and our departments 


Learn how to own your personal target, drive repeatable success, and prepare to manage your business as a seller
