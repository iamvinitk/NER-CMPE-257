Design, document, deliver design specifications to build and maintain the Pinterests design system Gestalt

Design strategic and tactical solutions to complex Pinner problems in close partnership with design and engineering
Collaborate with and manage feedback from stakeholders across the company

Proven track record envisioning, building, and iterating on digital products in close partnership with product management and engineering
