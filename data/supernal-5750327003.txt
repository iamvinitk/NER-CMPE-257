What we do: 
The Sr. Powertrain Test  Verification Engineer will lead all testing and verification activities for the Propulsion  Dynamic Systems team, including maturing interfaces with the Energy Storage System and Flight Control Systems. This position provides guidelines through defining Powertrain verification requirement document, which details the procedures and processes while performing Powertrain teams verification activities and supporting aircraft level verification and tests. The position ensures respective suppliers follow the processes put forth by the team. This role also includes organization of Safety of Flight testing, failure testing, any in-house high level and low level testing for software build release, coordination with Systems Engineering on aircraft level testing, and flight test management.
The Sr. Test  Verification Engineer will also help facilitate the Powertrain team in their integration testing of all the associated systems. This role will work closely with the leaders of the respective teams within the Rotor Systems, Electric Power Unit, Thermal Management, and Flight Controls Systems on scheduling content and release dates. The engineer compiles all test data and documentation artifacts, and the build release final package.
This position will be required to work on-site 5 days a week.
What you can do:

Accountable for oversight and planning of all Powertrain testing and verification activities for compliance with Safety of Flight, DO-178C, ARP 4754A, ARP 4761, and other standards
Support definition of System Integration Lab test equipment and build up of test capabilities to support flight control system test activities
Organization of Powertrain high-level testing in systems integration lab
Coordinate with suppliers on low-level software and hardware testing efforts
Coordinate aircraft level testing pertaining to the systems with the Test and Evaluation team to meet testing and certification verification, and collaborate with certification authorities in topics related with verification
Oversight and configuration of Powertrain flight testing and participation in the flight test campaign
Build test plans for both systems integration labs and on-aircraft testing
Compile all test and documentation artifacts for software build release packages
Compile and present test results, controlling dispositions, and open issues
Control issues using a formal CR process
Coordinate with the Powertrain team and associated suppliers to be able to make a flightworthy recommendation of the systems based on various metrics like level of testing new changes, regression testing, and outstanding issues
Evaluate change impact analysis to the various parts of the systems based on issues found or when evaluating change requests
May require up to 10 of domestic and international travel
Other duties as assigned
