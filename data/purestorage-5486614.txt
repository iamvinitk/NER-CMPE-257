The Senior Manager, Marketing Campaign Operations will report into the Director, Marketing and Campaign Operations, and help Pure blaze a constantly improving trail of operational excellence. One that is powered by leading products such as Marketo and SFDC, supported by various tools like Knak, Zendesk for ticketing, and Workato to drive automation.
In this role you will lead and develop a team of email campaign specialists located across the globe, supporting email and nurture campaigns, as well as segmentation strategy. This includes strategizing with the Marketing Digital Demand Center and Web teams, implementing processes, SLAs and best practices, and leading quarterly email campaign reviews with each marketing theater (AMS, APJ, EMEA, Global, Portworx, Partner). 
You will work with our marketing contact data strategist, as well as our team of technical Marketo Admins, to design and implement an end-to-end Marketo email campaign operations strategy and set up for each campaign, focused on buying group segmentation.
In This Role You Will

Manage Global Email Campaign Operations, leading a team of 7 Marketing Specialists to execute on global strategic marketing campaigns, and create alignment across all theaters.
Collaborate with Marketing Web and Digital teams for email marketing campaign, nurture, and segmentationaudience strategy with a focus on buying groups 
Lead quarterly email performance reviews with all Marketing theaters (AMS, APJ, EMEA, Global, Portworx, Partner), alongside the Digital Demand Center
Be the Marketing Email Campaign Operations main point of contact globally
Assist with the rollout of Campaign Hierarchy (tied to Integrated Marketings Global Campaign Framework) with the Marketing Operations team (led by the Director, Marketing and Campaign Operations)
Review processes, SLAs, email tools utilized, and forms for optimization and operationalization, as well as socialization, ongoing.
Oversee ticketing system for Marketing Email Campaign Operations
Document email campaign processes and work with the Marketing Operations enablement and change management lead to communicate changes globally 
Work with the Marketo technical admin team to build Marketo workflows
Provide strategic guidance and delegate operational support for email campaign buildout and execution (e.g., landing pages, templates, forms, workflows, list building, etc.)
Collaborate with the Digital Demand Center to drive global email campaign strategy
Troubleshoot and QAUAT email campaigns
Own email campaigns from end-to-end: managing build, segmentation, planning, execution, and reporting
