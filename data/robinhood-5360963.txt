The Fraud Detection and Prevention Senior Lead is responsible for developing, managing, monitoring and continuously optimizing fraud detection strategies, rules and procedures. Youll also partner with data modeling and fraud operations teams, and leverage 3rd party and in-house capabilities to detect and deter fraud related to Robinhood products (Brokerage, Money, Crypto).
It is preferred that this role is located in one of the office locations listed on this job description which will align with our in-office working environment. This position is only eligible for remote work in limited geographies within the US where we do not have physical office locations. Please connect with your recruiter for more information regarding our in-office philosophy and expectations.
What youll do

Analyze data to discover novel ways to detect fraud
Work with Fraud Operations, Data Science, and Engineering to develop features and implement fraud rules
Optimize fraud rules to ensure effectiveness and impact (e.g. balance fraud losses against operational efficiency and customer friction)
Develop fraud dashboards to monitor fraud rule performance
Partner with card networks and payment processors to enhance fraud detection techniques
Leverage 3rd party fraud detection services to compliment in-house processes
Summarize high level fraud trends, behavior patterns, and financial metrics in both informal (ad-hoc meetings, Slack) and formal (presentations) deliverables

What you bring 

Bachelors degree in Finance, Mathematics, Computer Science or other related field
7 years experience in Risk andor Fraud detection
5 years of hands-on SQL experience
Proficient experience with Python or other scripting languages
Proven understanding of analytical techniques like unsupervised learning, pattern recognition, and statistical analysis on fraud datasets
Proficient experience in data visualization tools such as Tableau, Looker etc.
Strong communication and influencing skills, experience working with cross-functional teams, including operations, data science, product, and engineering teams
