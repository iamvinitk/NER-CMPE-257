We are looking for YOU! - a motivated and experienced Senior Sales Operations Analyst to join our team! As a Sales Operations Analyst, you will be responsible for supporting all sales operations activities, including sales forecasting and making sure sales teams are appropriately maintaining accurate sales activity records. The Sales Operations Analyst will be a valued business partner to the German leadership team and support our German field sales teams to provide timely and accurate analytics to help them manage the teams selling efforts. This is a fast-paced and constantly evolving sales environment that requires excellent communication skills, the ability to multi-task and work under and towards a deadline. The Analyst will possess superb analytical skills and the ability to work as a team, not only with the field sales team but also with the EMEA, APJ and US HQ Theatre Ops environment.
