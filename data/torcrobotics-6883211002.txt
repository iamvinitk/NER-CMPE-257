Serve as Counsel or Senior Counsel to support the legal team and assist handling a wide range of legal matters for the company. Provide collaboration, leadership, legal counsel and advice to staff and management primarily on a wide range of business matters, which may include one or more areas of subject matter expertise, such as employment law or commercial transactions, but also supporting the team in a wide variety of other legal matters that could include areas such as litigation management, insurance, privacy, corporate governance, and compliance.
What youll do: 

Draft, negotiate, review, and approve agreements and other transactional documents, which may include customer or vendor agreements, licensing agreements, services agreements, confidentiality agreements, severance agreements and various other documents requiring legal expertise and in compliance to federal and state laws and associated government guidelines
Consult with other legal team members on legal issues of significance to the Company
Participate in the design, development, and maintenance of corporate policies, procedures, and programs
Provide quality, sound, ethical and prompt legal services
Perform research and provide interpretations of rules, regulations, and policies
Investigate legal issues, thoroughly document information and activities, and manage risks associated with the companys business
Provide counsel and support when necessary to any internal investigations, contract disputes, or other legal matters. Provide litigation support as needed

What youll need to Succeed: 

Law degree from an accredited institution
Membership and good standing in a state Bar; eligibility and willingness to be admitted in Virginia or other applicable bar under either a Corporate Counsel Certificate or waiver
Counsel requires at least 2 years of experience in a law firm or in-house corporate legal department with strong substantive experience one or more relevant subjects.  In-house corporate experience is strongly preferred.
Senior Counsel requires at least 6 years of experience with demonstrated ability to lead significant transactions and other significant legal matters
Significant contract experience required
Experience advising management on legal, risks, and other issues
Experience in leading multifaceted projects using strong organizational, interpersonal and presentation skills
Leadership skills, including the ability to lead cross functional projects, articulate standards and expectations as outlined in the relevant policies and procedures, and ensure that these are applied consistently
Highly analytical with strong attention to detail
