The Lean Implementation Leader will join the team to build and grow Robinhoods Lean and Operational Excellence capability and program.
The ideal candidate will be able to work across functions to identify, map, and optimize operational processes using LeanSix Sigma toolkit and frameworks. You will be a strategic problem solver, who can scope projects, conduct process mapping, analyze data, and identify process improvement opportunities. This role will work cross functionally at all levels of the organization to gather information, test hypotheses, and inspire change.
What youll do

Root Cause analysis: Source, structure, and analyze data to find opportunities and key gaps.
Program Management: Support complex programs, align scope with cross functional partners and present results to team members at all levels of the organization.
LeanSix Sigma Expertise: Become a go-to expert for helping the organization build and implement a successful Lean program. This includes bringing a toolkit, training, facilitation, and execution capabilities to the team.
Navigating Ambiguity: Distill complex business problems into their essential elements and prioritize work that helps Robinhood improve and grow.

8 years of related experience, ideally building and leading a LeanSix Sigma program in a complex organization, with a proven track record of reducing costs and improving customer experiences.
Robust qualitative and quantitative analytical skills, excellent attention to detail, and strong business acumen. Ability to analyze Pareto charts, identify root causes, and propose process improvements.
