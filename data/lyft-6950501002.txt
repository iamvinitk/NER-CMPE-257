Senior Marketing Operations Manager
At Lyft, our mission is to improve peoples lives with the worlds best transportation. To do this, we start with our own community by creating an open, inclusive, and diverse organization.
The marketing technology team at Lyft owns the tools and processes that enable how we communicate with our 30MM riders and 1.4MM drivers.  We are looking for an experienced marketing technologist to be the solutions consultant for how we enable paid media campaigns across a diverse set of marketing channels, across Lyfts different lines of businesses.  You will work closely with marketers, engineering, data science and third-party vendors to own and administer marketing tools such as our customer data platform, attribution and reporting, data sharing, data enrichment, tag management, web analytics, and mobile measurement. Be at the frontline of building a world-class marketing tech stack at one of the most dynamic companies in the world.
Responsibilities:

Be the solutions consultant that interfaces with internal clients on requests related to marketing partner onboarding, campaign tracking, data sharing with ad platforms, and attributionreporting issues and requests.  Campaigns can range from web to native mobile app, and support acquisition, brand, partnerships, and product marketing. 
Administer and own the governance of marketing tools such as Google Tag Manager, mobile app tracking, web analytics, CDP, spend ingestion, and tracking URL taxonomy. 
Together with Data Engineering, ensure marketing data in downstream reporting tables and dashboards are timely and accurate. 
Be the company expert in marketing technology.  Stay ahead of the curve on industry trends in ads measurement, personalization, (re)targeting, and how privacy will impact how we do marketing.  
Work closely with Marketing, Data Science, Engineering and Product to define the roadmap for marketing enablement tools. Identify improvements in processes and technology to allow for rapid scaling for all paid media campaigns across Lyft.

Experience:

5-7 years of experience working with B2C marketing tools, such as tag management, mobile app tracking, measurementattribution, data enrichment, web analytics, and CDP.
Familiarity with marketing data pipeline (conversion and spend tracking, data ingestion and export), including using SQL to troubleshoot data issues.
Familiarity with both web and mobile app tracking methodologies.
Ability to effectively communicate and work with cross-functional teams including marketers, product managers, engineers and data scientists.  
Great multi-tasking and prioritization skills.
