Would you like to upgrade the animation systems powering Bungies next great game? Do you enjoy working with animators and empowering them with tools to build new experiences? As a Senior Animation Engineer at Bungie, youll help build the roadmap for our animation technology, driving investments in smoother workflows, new capabilities, and features that delight millions of players. 
RESPONSIBILITIES 

Collaborate with art, design, and engineering to create novel animation behaviors   
Update and document workflows for animators creating new content both on the player and AI side  
Uphold animation budgets and performance goals across multiple platforms  
Build new core features in the animation system 

REQUIRED SKILLS  

Ability to write, debug, and maintain code in C   
Proficiency in 3D math, data structures, and algorithms   
Proven experience building and maintaining modern video game animation systems  
Ability to diagnose and address performance bottlenecks in animation and gameplay code   
Ability to communicate with customers of different technical and non-technical backgrounds to help them navigate technology and achieve their goals
