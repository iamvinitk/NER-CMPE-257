We are seeking a forward-thinking Technical Architect who will spearhead the design and development of Large Language Models (LLMs) and Chat AI applications. As a key member of our team, you will leverage your deep technical expertise and vision to architect solutions that will shape the future of human-AI interaction.
WHAT YOULL NEED TO BRING TO THIS ROLE:

Collaborate with a cross-functional team to design, develop, and deploy new chatbot applications.
Integrate our models into various platforms, applications, and interfaces.
Continuously research and implement best practices in user experience and conversational design.
Optimize performance, scalability, and responsiveness of applications.
Provide technical feedback and insights during product iteration cycles.
Maintain code integrity and organization.
Conduct tests and troubleshoot to ensure application stability.

BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:

8 years of software development experience in AIMLLLM used in a product or research program.
Bachelors degree or Masters degree in Computer Science
Proficiency in Python and familiarity with deep learning frameworks (e.g., TensorFlow, PyTorch).
Proficiency in writing complex SQL queries for PostgreSQL or MySQL
Strong understanding of REST and GraphQL APIs, web services, and integration methods.
Understanding of JavaScript and TypeScript

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:

Expertise in training, fine-tuning, and deploying models in real-world applications. Specific experience with fine-tuning processes and tools for large-scale models is highly regarded
Expertise in designing user-centric conversational flows, understanding user intents, and crafting contextually relevant responses.
Experience with Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) methods or other techniques that combine retrieval and generation in AI systems.
Hands-on experience with vector databases such as Weaviate, Milvus, or Pinecone, understanding the nuances of efficient storage, and quick retrieval of high-dimensional vectors.
Experience with patterns and techniques for integrating private data into Large Language Models (LLMs). Understanding of differential privacy and other mechanisms to ensure data privacy during the training phase.
Expertise in fine-tuning LLMs and applying methodologies to adapt models to specific tasks without compromising the models integrity or security.
Skills in optimizing and deploying large-scale search and retrieval systems.
