THE ROLE
Virtu is looking for an enthusiastic and proactive Information Security Engineer to join our global security team. In this role, youll perform security assessments and analysis, as well as design, implement, and coordinate with global IT teams to mitigate discovered risks. In this role, youll get exposure to different technologies, such as breach detection and prevention, user and entity behavior analytics, network flow monitoring, vulnerability management, and security automation. 
Additional duties include: 

Evaluate, design, and deploy emerging security solutions, including but not limited to: vulnerability management, anti-malware, network IDSIPS, security information and event monitoring (SIEM) systems, network flow technologies, and user behavior and entity monitoring


Maintain awareness of emerging threats, specifically those within the financial services sector; this position requires self-motivation, a strong willingness to learn, and passion for the information security field

Perks of the role: 

Projects with quick life cycles, from concept to production in three months
