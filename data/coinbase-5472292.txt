About Coinbase Design Systems
The UI Systems team at Coinbase plays a critical role in executing on our vision to create more economic freedom for the world, and is at the center of execution for products across the entirety of the company. Were building the most secure, trusted, and easiest to use cryptocurrency products for the millions of people just getting started with crypto, as well as long-standing loyal customers with advanced knowledge of the cryptocurrency space. 
As a Motion Designer youll contribute to our mission, vision and strategy by creating stunning visuals and scalable motion systems that simplify complex interactions and solve challenging design problems. Youll work closely with Product Designers, UX Researchers, Product Managers, and Engineering teams to help build simple, easy-to-use systems that express the Coinbase brand and values.
What youll be doing (ie. job duties):

Develop a user-centric, utilitarian Motion Design System that scales beyond our mobile  web products
Embed within product teams and provide hands-on support to up-level craft, improve XFN efficiencies and drive system wide adoption
Create, maintain, and distribute easy to use components and documentation for our design system
Up-level the design quality of the UI Systems team by bringing inspired ideas to the table and guiding those ideas through to fruition with attention to detail
Produce clear specs that advocate our design language system
QA work to ensure designs and creative assets are polished and ready for implementation handoff or final delivery

What we look for in you (ie. job requirements):

7 years of motion design experience in a product setting
Ability to use motion to narrate user experience based on users behaviors, needs and emotion
Exceptional process thinker, and problem solver. Successful designing Principles, timing frameworks, and hand off documentations
Experience successfully designing and building durable motion systems at scale within a large product organization
Exceptional collaboration skills and ability to partner with cross-functional partners, to drive towards successful outcomes
Demonstrates a strong foundation in basic animation principles, often related to physics and patterns 
Demonstration of inclusive motion principles and interest in accessibility best practices 
Self directed, solution oriented, and ability to work autonomously
Thrives in a fast-paced (sometimes ambiguous) environment
Strong written and verbal communication skills
Low ego, collaborative, positive attitude, and open-minded
Master in After effects and Figma
Knowledgeable about Lottie Files and Rive
Proficient in Photoshop and Illustrator
