WHAT YOULL DO: 
Were looking for a Senior Account Associate to become a key member of the agencys Social Impact  Inclusion practice  a team of subject experts who lead Golins work in sustainability, social impact and inclusiveDEI communications. You will support two of Golin top social impact and sustainability accounts to create and execute social good strategies centered in thought leadership and reputation management. Youll work with a top team of specialists across digital, creative, strategy, analytics and earned media to manage the business of the account and execute campaigns for these clients.
You are high energy, have an utter commitment to quality, love managing the details, are always a step ahead in what needs to be done, and have a zeal for making the world a better place.
This role is hybrid, with the expectation of 2 days a week in the office working with Golin colleagues, and a 3rd day spent onsite with clients as needed. 
 You will:

Participate in client and account team interaction, assist in creating client meeting and call reports, activity reports, status documents, and results reports for agency team
Manage day-to-day activities across several work streams, owning trackers and workback documents to ensure the team is on track, on time, and delivers against objectives
Demonstrate strong writing and analytical skills, working collaboratively with our writers and designers on communications documents
Research and vet major events and conferences, and shape recommendations for clients to help drive their thought leadership plans
Scour sustainability and social impact news and newsletters to shape weekly digests for clients on trends leading their industries
Provide basic client counsel on tactical matters with supervision and develop client-facing activity reports and weekly status update documents
Attend client meetings, observe client and account team interaction
Manage vendors and purchasing process
Have a strong eye for detail and quality control of ideas, client and team correspondence and plans, and media materials
Develop timelines, budgets and staffing plans for all projects and ensure team adheres to them
Support the development and execution of integrated communications campaigns including events and experiential activities for media and influencers
Develop campaign wrap reports that celebrate our successes and results
Produce a variety of client reports, conduct competitive analysis and develop research briefs
