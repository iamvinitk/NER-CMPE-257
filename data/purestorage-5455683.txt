PORTWORX
Portworx helps enterprises reduce the cost and complexity of deploying applications on cloud and on-prem environments. Database administrators and platform architects can manage data services on any infrastructure with a single data management layer for all services. Portworx innovates and is a market leader for databases as a service platform. 
 
Currently we have 3 different products- Portworx Enterprise, Backup and Data services with different backends and front end libraries. We are unifying these products into a single platform. This horizontal role for a platform designer will work on features like self service onboarding, cross product navigation, user management, billing and settings. 
SHOULD YOU ACCEPT THIS CHALLENGE...

And youll leverage the expertise of our incredible partners in product, marketing, sales and engineering to see that your solutions can get realized.
What are some of the high impact opportunities youll tackle? 

Drive discovery work to build personas and user journeys

Create inclusive designs for all users; advocate for accessibility, encouraging product and engineering management to prioritize accessible features and bug fixes
