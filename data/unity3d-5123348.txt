The opportunity
As a XR graphics developer in the Render Pipeline group, you will participate building ground breaking XR features for Unity real-time graphics. Working with other engineers you will help craft the future technology of our Scriptable Render Pipelines for cross reality. Our groups mission is to build the most scalable and extensible render pipeline environment. Our work runs on billions of devices from the lowest-end mobile to the highest-end desktops and consoles.
Imagine a team of developers crafting outstanding AAA-quality content all by themselves! Imagine an artist, having access to powerful technology to fulfill their vision! That is the world we at Unity want to create. And we need your help to build it.
You will learn our engines capabilities, then help us expand them for XR to empower Unity game developers to craft incredible experiences. Most importantly, you will work with an exciting and high-energy team at a company that jealously guards work-life balance.
We are committed to maintain a diverse team, and we welcome and encourage applicants from all backgrounds. The desire to learn and the willingness to solve problems are more important to us than your experience. We encourage you to apply even if you do not think you tick all the boxes. Our diversity is what we believe makes our teams thrive.

Life at Unity
Unity (NYSE: U) is the worlds leading platform for creating and operating real-time 3D (RT3D) content. Creators, ranging from game developers to artists, architects, automotive designers, filmmakers, and others, use Unity to make their imaginations come to life. Unity is the foundation upon which the worlds most powerful digital content is created. Specifically, Unitys platform provides a comprehensive set of software solutions to create, run and monetize interactive, real-time 2D and 3D content for mobile phones, tablets, PCs, consoles, and augmented and virtual reality devices.
In the fourth quarter of 2022, more than 70 of the top 1000 mobile games were made with Unity as derived from a blended number of the top 1000 games in the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. In 2022, Made with Unity Applications had more than 4 billion downloads per month. For more information, please visit www.unity.com.
