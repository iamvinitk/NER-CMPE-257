About the job
As a Staff Accountant specializing in Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) at Twilio, you will play a pivotal role in our accounting team, responsible for overseeing and optimizing our COGS accounting processes. Your expertise in cost accounting, financial analysis, and Excel proficiency will be instrumental in ensuring accurate reporting, cost management, and strategic decision-making.
Responsibilities
In this role, youll:

Manage and maintain the accounting processes related to COGS, including cost allocation and expense recognition.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to analyze and interpret cost data, identify cost-saving opportunities, and provide financial insights.
Develop and execute process improvements, automation solutions, and efficient reporting mechanisms, with a focus on enhancing the accuracy and efficiency of COGS-related activities.
Assist in month-end and year-end financial close activities, reconciling COGS accounts and preparing financial statements.
Ensure compliance with accounting standards, internal policies, and regulatory requirements related to COGS.
Participate in ad-hoc financial projects and analysis to support strategic initiatives.
