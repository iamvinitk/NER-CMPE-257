
Korean Financial Translator 
 
About:
Due to the rapid growth and expansion of our business activities in various markets, we have the following full-time opening for an experienced, motivated, and technology-oriented individual for a Korean Financial Translator (English to Korean and vice versa).
 
Responsibilities:
We are looking for a professional translator with a strong focus on economics and finance and the ability to work efficiently to meet tight deadlines in a fast-paced environment.
 
In this position, you will be required to:

Translate various content, such as Account Management, Registration System, Trader Workstation, etc.
Translate client communication letters, bulletins, and announcements
Translate Webinar notes and Knowledge Base articles
Plan and carry out research projects, including extraction of terminology from texts and use of termbase
Perform quality assurance on existing translations (proofread and edit)

 
Qualifications: 

Native Korean speaker
Proficient in English
Experience with the translation of large volumes of text on a deadline. English language or comparable degree. Translation or related discipline training is a major plus.
Excellent written and oral communication skills
Familiar with MS Office. Experience with CAT tools is a plus.
Minimum of 2 years of work with financial terminology in a professional context.
Proven time-management and organizational skills
Personality: Self-confident, open, flexible, and able to work independently in a team-oriented environment
