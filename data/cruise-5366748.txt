We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Package Design Engineer to join our Silicon Systems team. In this role, you will work with our silicon partner, internal board design team, and chip architects to perform package design, analysis, and evaluations for high performance automotive compute silicon. You will work to help define ball map assignment, layout, routing and die placement.
