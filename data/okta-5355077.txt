Were building a world where Identity belongs to you.The Role
We are looking for a Senior Engineer who is self-motivated with a proven track record in service management technologies and who is comfortable in the dynamic atmosphere of a technical organization with a rapidly expanding end-user base. In this role, you focus on the technical aspects of the platform, working directly with the Service Management team. From an operational standpoint, the successful candidate will adhere to best practices and provide an advanced understanding of ServiceNows functionality to the ServiceNow ecosystem surrounding Okta and its end users.
As a ServiceNow Senior Engineer, you get to work closely with a global team and embark on a journey of growing the Okta ServiceNow platform by shaping how we deliver experiences to our customers. This is a technical role which requires strong communication and collaboration skills. 
In this role, youll get to:

Work with the ServiceNow team to deliver platform solutions across the organization
Enhance and support current solutions that solve business challenges and improve processes
Build core functionality with a focus on platform capabilities, ITSM, integrations, and more
Work with core team to ensure environments maintain the highest levels of availability, security, and quality
Identify opportunities to innovate, scale, extend and enhance service delivery where possible
Develop and execute on comprehensive product strategies that align with Oktas overall business goals and objectives, broader industry and market trends
Work with the product team to review and execute requirements, test plans, technical specification, and provide input in the design, maintainability, scalability and usability of platform implementations. 
Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies in service management to identify opportunities for innovation and automation.
Empower our engineers through a culture of continuous learning by helping to field system related questions and share out best practices with the broader team
