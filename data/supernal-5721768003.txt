What we do:
The Sr. IT Enterprise Application Engineer is responsible for the strategy, planning, integration, and management of enterprise projects, applying technical and managerial skills to satisfy business, technology and systems project objectives. You will act as liaison between business multi-functional teams and service vendor solution team to lead project strategy and project planning and execution across various functions, driving projects to achieve critically important outcomes for our partners. The Sr. IT Enterprise Application Engineer will also ensure the stability, security, and optimal performance of the applications.
This position will be required to work on-site 5 days a week.
What you can do:

Lead sophisticated application technology projects related to enterprise systems such as Customer Relationship Management (CRM), Supply Chain System (SCM), Manufacturing Execution System (MES), Manufacturing Operations Management (MOM), and etc.
Develop and drive enterprise project strategy and plans, aligning project tactics with overall IT project strategy
Design and implement scalable and efficient enterprise-level applications based on business requirements
Work closely with company partners to translate business needs into technical specifications and application designs
Ensure that all enterprise applications aligns with business objectives and adheres to industry standard methodologies
Support the implementation and integration of critical enterprise applications with other business systems, ensuring flawless data flow and process synchronization

Develop a harmonious and positive working relationship with all partners, promoting the value of IT solutions and services in driving business success

Stay updated with emerging technologies and industry trends to continuously improve application development practices
