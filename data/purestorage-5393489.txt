The Monetization Team at Pure is the core of our business engine that provides our sales organization with the strategy, tools and support to commercialize Pures highly-differentiated technology sales to our customers while keeping our business model intact. The team works in a highly cross-functional environment to enable various functions to be on the same page with how we want to deliver value while creating value for Pure managing enterprise risk, profitability, and win rates. This entails active engagement from our Deals Monetization team that oversees all pricing proposals working with our sales and channel teams, Subscription Operations and Governance team that oversees the governance, operations, and renewals for our Evergreen Subscription portfolio, and International Monetization Strategy team that focuses on the International growth and expansion strategy to enable the overall corporate strategic plan. Said simply - the team sits between Finance, GTM, and Product, helping drive commercial strategy in alignment with our various business models 
As an extension to this team as well as looking at the growing opportunities in India, we would like to initiate hiring and build for a small team in the India office that will be focused initially on scaling the global team, learn the global business, and work towards becoming a center of excellence for frameworks, operational excellence projects, and direct engagement with the Indian GSIs and sales teams as the business grows.
