Supernal is at the forefront of creating emerging mobility solutions that will foster the development of human-centered cities. We are designing a completely new electric vertical take-off and landing (eVTOL) aircraft tailored to the mobility needs of future cities. This allows passengers a seamless intermodal journey that safely transports them to their final destination. We fuse research in autonomy, robotics, aviation and services to define a new category of mobility for the worlds communities. We believe in creative thinking and collaboration to help build a better mobility experience for everyone, improving peoples ability to move  whether for work or play.
What we do: 
The Flight Control Systems Software Engineer helps support embedded coding decisions, supplier management of software development, and flight control computer architecture design. The role assists in managing low-level requirements and any in house software efforts. This position will also help support and follow the system best practices and system philosophy.
This position will be required to work on-site 5 days a week.
What you can do:

Work closely with Flight Controls Systems team in support of flight control computer architecture including hardware, processors, and compiler decisions
Manage software team requirements to support Flight Controls Systems development and certification
Define IO requirements for flight control software with other aircraft systems
Follow coding standards and software process definitions
Support supplier management of coding and low-level testing efforts
Collaborate with Intelligent Systems, Avionics, Powertrain, and other aircraft systems in the development of the full software platform
May require up to 10 of domestic and international travel
Other duties as assigned

What you can contribute:

BS degree in Engineering required; Electrical, Mechanical, or Aerospace Engineering preferred
Minimum of 2-4 years of experience (an equivalent combination of education and experience may be considered)
Experience with embedded coding and auto-coding
Experience with defining software requirements
Exposure to software supplier management
Understanding of communications protocols (CAN, ARINC 429, RS-485, etc..)
Familiarity with DO-178BC, DO-160, DO-254, ARP 4754A, ARP 4761
Proficiency with CC
Comfortable with MATLAB, Simulink, and Stateflow
Some understanding of real time operating systems
Highly collaborative and phenomenal communication skills
Familiarity with FAA part 23252729 or other certification experience
Excellent verbal and written communication skills
Proactive delivery of communication and follow up
Excellent organizational skills and attention to detail
Must have the ability to follow a prioritized plan and accomplish work within time constraints
Self-motivated and forward-thinking personality
Proficiency with Excel VLOOKUP, pivot tables, MS Office Suite

You may also be able to contribute:

M.S. in aerospace, electrical, or a relevant engineering field, is a plus but not required
