Supernal is at the forefront of creating emerging mobility solutions that will foster the development of human-centered cities. We are designing a completely new electric vertical take-off and landing (eVTOL) aircraft tailored to the mobility needs of future cities. This allows passengers a seamless intermodal journey that safely transports them to their final destination. We fuse research in autonomy, robotics, aviation and services to define a new category of mobility for the worlds communities. We believe in creative thinking and collaboration to help build a better mobility experience for everyone, improving peoples ability to move  whether for work or play.
What we do: 
The Automation Engineer, MES (Manufacturing Execution System) provides design and technical support for MES solutions. This role plays a pivotal part in short-term and long-term MES planning and development. The engineer collaborates with other stakeholders and subject matter experts spanning across the entire product life cycle at the component, system, and aircraft-level. The position aims to optimize our manufacturing processes and ensure seamless data flow throughout our systems for our new eVTOL aircraft.
This position will be required to work on-site 5 days a week.
What you can do:

System Support  Maintenance: Maintain and troubleshoot the current MES solution, ensuring consistent performance and rectifying any issues promptly
Strategic Development: Participate in the long-term planning and enhancement of the MES system, aligning it with our companys manufacturing and data goals
Integration: Ensure that MES integrates with other systems
Training: Provide training to super users and area SMEs on the MES system, ensuring they can effectively use the platform and train their respective teams
Continuous Improvement: Monitor MES usage and performance metrics to identify areas of improvement and implement necessary changes
Documentation: Maintain up-to-date system documentation for troubleshooting, training, and reference
Collaboration: Work closely with the MES and operations management team members to ensure consistent data architecture and system performance
May require up to 25 of domestic and international travel
Other duties as assigned
