Skydio is the leading US drone company and the world leader in autonomous flight, the key technology for the future of drones and aerial transportation. The Skydio team combines deep expertise in artificial intelligence, best-in-class hardware and software product development, and operational excellence to empower a broader, more diverse audience of drone users - from first responders to insurance claims adjusters, utilities, and more!About the role: 
Skydios RF system connects users with our autonomous ecosystems. As intelligent flying machines gain more independence, the demands on range and bandwidth are increasing; there is a huge opportunity to innovate within existing architectures to bring an incredible, highly-engineered product to market, and to define the next generation of RF frameworks and technologies. Skydios highly integrated electromechanical systems provide a rich set of engineering challenges and a huge opportunity to make an impact!
How youll make an impact:

Collaborate with EE, RF, Antenna design and PD teams to maximize the performance of the system designs
Define system level requirements and specifications
Design and implement wireless solutions in a variety of technologies and standards across multiple frequency bands
Perform circuit level analysis to debug design and desense issues and characterize nature of aggressorvictim
Identify noise source and support system level integration
Support end to end design for the product from conception, through qualification, certification and production testing

What makes you a good fit:

MS in Electrical Engineering
5 years of experience in wireless design and desense evaluation of consumer grade products associated with one or with multiple wireless standards
Knowledgeable in analog, digital, and mixed-signal circuit design and analysis
Excellent technical understanding of one or more of the following radio technologies: WiFi, LTE, Bluetooth and GPS
Good understanding of physics of radiated noise coupling mechanisms
Experience with PCB layout design and schematic
Experience in debugging and optimizing wireless products
Experience using simulation tools such as Ansys and Keysight ADS
Experience with lab equipment such as network analyzers, CMW500, spectrum analyzers, power meters, near field probes, etc.
Experience with best design practices for EMIEMC, ESD and RF signal routing
Experience with performing TRP, TIS and Rate Vs Range measurements is preferred
Proven ability to work independently
