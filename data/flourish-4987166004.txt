



Flourish was founded in 2017 with the goal of helping financial advisors to better secure the financial futures of their clients. Today, we work with over 600 RIAs that collectively represent more than 1.5T in assets under management across two products: Flourish Cash, a cash management solution with more than 3B in deposits, and Flourish Crypto, a turnkey cryptocurrency offering built for financial advisors and their clients. Headquartered in New York City, we are an independent subsidiary of MassMutual Life Insurance Company1.

About the Role
Flourish works 500 RIAs, and we are now building out a team dedicated to helping these clients succeed post-sale. As Relationship Manager, you will be responsible for managing a book of clients, dedicated to their success, while simultaneously learning and developing best practices that can be applied across all of our clients.
You will personally work with a set of RIAs with the objective of increasing their usage and adoption of our growing list of product offerings. You will work in conjunction with our sales team to provide a white-glove experience to our clients post-sale. You will support your clients day-to-day needs, while developing a deep understanding of each firm and cultivating key contacts internally. You will work strategically on projects to improve the client experience both for your clients and across our entire book.
The Relationship Management team is a small but growing team within Flourish. As an early member of the team you will have a hand in shaping the future of relationship management. You will help build the team from the ground up; developing processes and procedures, strategically defining metrics, and more. We are in the unique position of serving as the voice of our clients internally, while working cross-functionally to influence our product and overall business directions. We prioritize clients above all else and always strive to provide them with an amazing experience.
