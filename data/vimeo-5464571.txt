We are looking for an experienced and passionate manager who can drive complex cross-team programs in an agile way, and continuously improve the ways Vimeo gets things done.  You will be working closely with Product, Engineering, GTM, BI teams, but also interacting and building relationships with other Vimeo departments.  Vimeo enables individuals and businesses to harness the power of video with state-of-the-art video creation and editing solutions, including AI-powered tools such as script generators and text-based video editing. This role will focus on various web and mobile programs, targeting the creators experience on vimeo, monetization and conversion initiatives. 
What you will do:

Facilitate planning, execution and visibility for initiatives (and their expected product outcomes) spanning multiple product and  engineering and non-engineering teams; be responsible for artifacts reflecting plans, progress, issues and risks.
Facilitate collaboration between teams, understand the system level dependencies;  ensure cross-team dependencies are identified and acted on, mitigate delivery risks
Facilitate the adoption of Product and Engineering outcome based delivery process; contribute to improving Vimeo process
Improve prioritizationalignment across the organization, create forums for more effective prioritization and execution
Based on teams capacity, perform better expectations management on what is not going to get done
Help teams improve their internal operation; and ways of working with other teams
Help teams and stakeholders understand empirical and iterative product development
Actively support and encourage continuous improvement culture

Skills and knowledge you should possess: 

5 Years working in a capacity of Program Manager, (with experience in Agile Roles as Scrum Master, Product Owner or Coach)
Worked closely with multiple engineering and non-engineering teams on a couple of  parallel projects simultaneously to facilitate planning and product execution, work decomposition.
Tailored cross-team processes depending on unique needs, resulting in an effective delivery
Ran cross-team retrospectives and rolled out agreed upon changes, successfully built the new habits 
Defined valuable process metrics, and associated data collection 
Configured and managed Jira workflows, team boards, built information radiators in the Atlassian suite (or comparable).
Demonstrated strong presentation and communication skills
Deep understanding of Agile and Lean values and principles 
Flexible and open minded with a positive attitude; servant leader that easily builds trust with various groups
