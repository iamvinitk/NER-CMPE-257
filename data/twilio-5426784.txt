About the job
As the Senior Accountant - Accounts Receivable you will ensure AR is accurate and balanced across multiple subledgers (and systems) and the general ledger.  This is an accounting focused role for all things AR. While cash application and accounts receivable transactions will be performed by a separate team, it is important for this role to maintain in-depth knowledge of their processes to ensure collaboration and alignment between all the teams.   
Responsibilities
In this role, you will:

Interface with key partners across internal departments and subsidiaries to ensure AR is accurate and reconciled between the general ledger and multiple subledgers.
Prepare month-end entries and reconciliations, as well as assist with monthly management reporting.
Assist in providing reporting on key KPIs such as DSO, and write-off trends within business lines, as well as other ad hoc analysis to help add value by making recommendations to continuously drive performance for all things AR. 
Interact extensively across all functional teams within Twilio and assist as queries arise.
Update processed and procedures as required for your tasks
 Improvise  automate the AR processes.
