Lead Solution Architect
Washington, D.C.
As Lead Solution Architect, youll be dedicated to supporting our government clients technology initiatives. You will play a pivotal role in designing, developing, and implementing innovative, secure, and scalable IT solutions that address complex challenges faced by our government client. Your expertise will be critical in aligning technology strategies with the clients mission and objectives, while ensuring the highest standards of security, compliance, and efficiency.
We know that you cant have great technology services without amazing people. At MetroStar, we are obsessed with our people and have led a two-decade legacy of building the best and brightest teams. Because we know our future relies on our deep understanding and relentless focus on our people, we live by our mission: A passion for our people. Value for our customers.
If you think you can see yourself delivering our mission and pursuing our goals with us, then check out the job description below!
What youll do:

Collaborate closely with government stakeholders, project managers, and technical teams to understand business needs, goals, and requirements.
Lead the design and development of comprehensive, end-to-end technology solutions that meet functional, non-functional, and security requirements.
Create architectural blueprints, diagrams, and documentation to communicate technical designs effectively.
Provide hands-on technical leadership, guiding development teams in the implementation of complex solutions.
Mentor and coach junior architects and engineers, fostering a culture of learning and growth within the team.
Ensure architectural consistency and adherence to best practices across projects.
Conduct regular meetings and presentations to update stakeholders on project progress, technical decisions, and potential challenges.
Collaborate with senior management and government officials to define long-term technology strategies that align with the clients mission and goals.
Evaluate emerging technologies and industry trends, recommending innovative solutions to enhance efficiency and effectiveness.
Integrate robust security measures and compliance requirements into solution designs, ensuring the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of sensitive government data.
Lead the evaluation and selection of third-party technologies, tools, and vendors that support project goals.
Provide oversight throughout the project lifecycle, ensuring that architectural designs are executed as planned and addressing any deviations or challenges.

What youll need to succeed:

Bachelors or Masters degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field.
At least 15 years as a Solution Architect, with a significant portion dedicated to government projects or clients.
Experience designing and architecting a wide variety of solutions to include platform, cloud, system, etc. for large-scale projects.
In-depth knowledge of government technology standards, regulations, and security requirements.
Strong expertise in solution design, system integration, and architecture patterns.
Proficiency in a wide range of technologies and platforms, both established and emerging.
Excellent leadership, communication, and interpersonal skills.
Relevant certifications such as TOGAF, CISSP, or similar are a plus.
Active TSSCI with CI poly preferred or active TSSCI clearance with ability to pass a CI poly
