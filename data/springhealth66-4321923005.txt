The Global Clinical Recruiter will be focused on sourcing, vetting, and hiring top-notch international mental health providers to join our network. What youll be doing:

Build a robust pipeline of high-quality therapists and psychiatrists (both virtual and in-person) via creative sourcing strategies, incoming application management, and referrals
Help build and utilize our provider hiring model to create hiring goals  priorities, as we continue to grow internationally
Conduct interviews to assess the clinical and technical abilities of each prospective provider, pitch the mission and benefits of working with Spring Health, and facilitate information channels on local country nuance and culture surrounding mental health service
Lead  manage negotiations with providers to ultimately hire the best of the best while balancing cost efficiency
Work closely with our Provider Success  and Credentialing teams to ensure a smooth onboarding process for all provider new hires
Partner with the Marketing team to ensure we are reaching  attracting the best provider talent via partnerships, job postings, and beyond
Bring bold and innovative ideas to the table, as the Global Clinical Recruiting team continues to improve the way we hire and grow our provider network
