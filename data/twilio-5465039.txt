About the job
This position is needed to support global payroll operations.The person in this role will either deliver first in class payroll service to our employees, ensure compliance,accuracy and timely delivery of payroll related tasks and support projects across a number of jurisdictions.
Responsibilities
In this role, youll:

Skillfully handle payroll processing for employees in Colombia, ensuring accuracy and compliance with local regulations.
Participate in special projects and initiatives related to payroll and employee benefits specific to the Colombian workforce
After receiving proper training, contribute to the payroll processing for employees in various countries, maintaining accuracy and compliance.
Handle payroll elements specific to Colombia, such as housing allowance and income tax withholdings, ensuring precise calculations and compliance with local laws
Ensure timely payments of AFC and AVP and stay updated on SENA regulations.
Work closely with the payroll vendor to ensure all payroll-related transactions are collected, processed, and reported accurately and in a timely manner.
Support the accounting team by assisting in the preparation of payroll journals and accruals, and conduct research to resolve inquiries related to payroll general ledger balances.
Build and nurture relationships with key stakeholders within the organization, offering support and input when necessary, particularly in matters related to payroll.
Serve as the main point of contact for employees in Colombia and other designated payrolls, addressing their queries and concerns related to payroll.
Continuously identify opportunities for process improvement within the payroll function and implement enhancements to ensure efficiency and accuracy.
Ensure a high-quality employee experience by assisting with payroll onboarding, addressing ad-hoc queries, and managing the annual tax reporting process effectively.
