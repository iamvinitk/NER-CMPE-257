The Analytics Engineering team provides all parts of Intercoms business with the right set of accurate and up-to-date data tables that form the basis of all of our most important operational and strategic data-driven decisions.
The team uses their deep knowledge of how GTM and Product teams work to anticipate their data needs. They use their deep technical expertise to design the right data models and reliably turn these models into high-quality core data assets in our data warehouse. The team are experts at driving data model efficiencies and solving data quality issues at their root cause.
This team is the glue between all of our Analyst teams and our Data Infrastructure teams. They speak everyones language and make everything go faster.
Were looking to hire an experienced Lead  Manager to grow this vital function within Intercom.
What will I be doing? 

Partnering with the data infrastructure engineering team to understand and help evolve and maintain our data warehouse and the ETLs that feed it.
Build a best in class data platform to drive critical business decisions
Partnering with all of our internal analyst teams to understand the questions they are trying to answer and help them arrive at the right answers.
Work with your team to continuously think ahead of your key stakeholders and design the data assets for the future.
Hiring and managing your team, building a team that youre proud and excited to be a part of.
Creating a collaborative, inclusive and supportive team culture focused on fast, focused, high impact execution.
Be a core member of the higher level Business Systems Leadership team, being a key contributor to the orgs business, technical and cultural strategy.
