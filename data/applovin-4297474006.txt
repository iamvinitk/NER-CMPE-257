About AppLovin
AppLovin makes technologies that help businesses of every size connect to their ideal customers. The company provides end-to-end software and AI solutions for businesses to reach, monetize and grow their global audiences. For more information about AppLovin, visit: www.applovin.com.
To deliver on this mission, our global team is composed of team members with life experiences, backgrounds, and perspectives that mirror our developers and customers around the world. At AppLovin, we are intentional about the team and culture we are building, seeking candidates who are outstanding in their own right and also demonstrate their support of others.

AppLovin is looking for a highly qualified Senior Counsel to join our legal team. As a key member of the legal team, you will help drive efficiencies in our overall contracting strategy with partners and suppliers. You will have exposure and interaction with departments across the company, as well as our clients and vendors. You will be a trusted partner and advisor to colleagues, providing practical and timely guidance regarding contracts and the contract management process, escalating issues to counsel when necessary. You will work with teams across the globe to enable the efficient and effective delivery of legal services to our business partners. If you are looking to make an impact, and the opportunity to work with a fun, collaborative team - wed love to talk to you! 
The Impact Youll Make:

Navigate Legal Terrains: Guide cross-functional teams with clear legal direction, ensuring we balance our goals and legal risk, taking into account the companys policies and positions
Shape Business Outcomes: You will help to the company manage complex transactions, including negotiating complex commercial agreements including day-to-day business development as well as ad hoc MA transactions and new initiatives
Champion New Initiatives: You will be a strategic problem solver for the business team and bring a business-oriented, practical mindset to solving problems and closing transactions
Drive Commercial Initiatives: Serve as a strategic consultant to the business team, employing a pragmatic, business-focused approach to problem-solving and finalizing deals.
Collaborate for Collective Success: Support the continuous improvement of standard form agreements and legal processes

Who You Are:

Must have a JD or equivalent and admission to and active status in at least one state bar (and eligible to register for California or an applicable state bars in-house counsel program)
8 years of experience as a practicing attorney, either in-house at a public company or at a law firm advising public technology companies, specializing in drafting, editing, and negotiating contracts
Meticulous attention to detail, organized, thorough and efficient in driving results
Ability to effectively communicate and collaborate with a diverse group of individuals in different geographies, at all levels of management and across different business groups 
Capable to enhance and streamline the legal teams continuous initiatives in refining internal procedures, establishing best practices, and keeping contract templates up-to-date 
Ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment with a high level of professionalism and outstanding business judgment
A willingness to help out with whatever legal work needs doing, whenever it needs to be done
A team player with a great sense of humor

Additional Strengths:

Hands-on experience in a public technology company setting
Proficient in handling international tech transactions
Mastery in understanding specific tech sectors like mobile applications and adtech
Concrete results in implementing proactive risk management frameworks in previous roles
