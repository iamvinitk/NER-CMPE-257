Firmware Engineer, TBS
At Lyft, our mission is to improve peoples lives with the worlds best transportation. To do this, we start with our own community by creating an open, inclusive, and diverse organization.
Lyfts Bikes  Scooters team is developing the future of micro-mobility and we are looking for a Firmware Engineer to help drive the development of our newest vehicles and sustain our fleet in operation. Our active fleet includes state-of-the-art electric bikes and scooters, services tens of millions of rides per year, and is rapidly growing. Our engineers are smart, flexible, and love solving difficult challenges, and were looking for a motivated professional to join the team in San Francisco. 
Responsibilities:

Develop features for embedded systems across our micro-mobility product line (vehicles, docks, internal tools)
Work with cross-functional teams to prototype, develop, and take to production our next generation of world-class mobility products
Work on refinements and updates to to improve the overall experience on existing products 

Experience:

Bachelors degree in Computer Science or related field
Experience developing in CC for embedded systems
Familiarity with using Python for scripting 
Understanding of low-level OS concepts(memory management, scheduling, threads, interrupts)
Experience using an embedded real-time OS (FreeRTOS, ThreadX, etc)
Familiarity with ARM Cortex-M line of processors
Hands-on experience in supporting electrical bring-up and debugging
Strong embedded debugging skills including ability to use debug probes, signal analyzers, oscilloscopes and power analyzers
Experience translating product needs into actionable schedules
Strong communications skills for articulating technical approaches and sharing out risk and progress to non-technical audiences
Strong understanding of distributed systems concepts
