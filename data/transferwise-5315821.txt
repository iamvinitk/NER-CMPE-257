Wise is one the fastest growing companies in Europe and were on a mission: to make money without borders the new normal. Weve got 15 million customers across the globe and were growing. Fast.
Current banking systems dont let us send, spend or receive money across borders easily. Or quickly. Or cheaply. 
So, were building a new one.
And, we need a Backend Engineer with a Java focus to join our mission and help us build a global platform thats scalable, reliable and secure. Were working on lots of algorithmical challenges, and youll dive right into projects on reconciliation, integrations, authorisations and authentications.
The team you will join: Contact Handling team 
The Contact Handling team at Wise is dedicated to connect Wises Customers with our Customer Support Agents (CS) around the world (from the Philippines to Austin, USA) and proving the tools for them to resolve Customers issues via chat, email and voice contact channels. Our team is also responsible for delivering accurate data that powers CSs efficiency metrics and Customers perceived effort when contacting us. Software engineers, data analysts, designers, UX researchers collaborate on a daily basis to continuously improve our systems and provide support to the whole CS team (over 1500 Wisers!).
Our vision is to provide effortless and (cost) efficient resolution to our customers - so Wise can keep growing exponentially, backed up by a strong and efficient Customer Support while also fostering a strong partnership between CS and the product team to develop solutions that leverage their expertise.
