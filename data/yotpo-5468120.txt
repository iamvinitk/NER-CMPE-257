Responsibilities

Active monitoring of clients deliverability rates on a daily basis
Help, coach, and monitor a portfolio of assigned clients on deliverability topics
Troubleshoot and collaborate to find solutions to deliverability issues as they arise.
Manage deliverability and delivery dashboards, reports and alerts, including bounce logs.
Manage IP pools and IP reputation.
Maintain relationships with ISP and other email providers.
Conduct in-depth investigations, reviews, and audits into email sending practices.
Assist customers with blocklists, spam traps, and issues with mailbox providers.
Support clients migration.
Staying up to date on trends and technologies in the deliverability and delivery space.
Educating customers on email marketing best practices.
Monitor delivery metrics and create strategic plans to improve their outcomes.
Provide feedback to assist in improving tools and capabilities offered by the deliverability Product  RD team.

Requirements:

3 years of professional work experience in an email deliverability role
Analytical, data-driven background, able to independently run reports, analyze data and provide insights and concrete recommendations
Experience in a customer-facing role, preferably working at an ESP, familiar with approaching clients and assisting with their needs in the delivery and deliverability needs.
Have good technical understanding of email authentication protocols, email-related DNS record types, bounce handling, spam traps, etc.

Experience with third-party analysis, monitoring and testing tools, like Litmus, Google Postmaster Tools and SNDS
