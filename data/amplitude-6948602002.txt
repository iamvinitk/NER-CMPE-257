As an Anaplan Model Builder at Amplitude Analytics, you will be a critical part of our finance team, responsible for designing, building, and maintaining Anaplan models to support financial and operational planning needs.
 As an Anaplan Model Builder, you will: 

Model Development: Design, build, and maintain Anaplan models that align with clients business requirements and objectives. This includes developing complex financial models, data integration, and scenario planning.
End User Collaboration: Collaborate with end users to understand their planning and modeling needs, and translate those requirements into Anaplan solutions. Act as a subject matter expert and provide guidance on best practices.
Training and Support: Provide training and support to end-users and clients, ensuring they can effectively utilize the Anaplan models for decision-making and planning.
Data Integration: Integrate data sources into Anaplan models to ensure accurate and up-to-date information for planning and reporting purposes.
Model Optimization: Continuously improve and optimize Anaplan models for performance, scalability, and usability. Implement changes based on feedback and changing business needs.
Documentation: Create comprehensive documentation for Anaplan models, including design specifications, data mappings, and user guides, to ensure transparency and knowledge sharing.
Quality Assurance: Conduct thorough testing and validation of Anaplan models to ensure data accuracy and model reliability.

Youll be a great addition to the team if you have:

Proven experience as an Anaplan Model Builder or similar role.
Strong understanding of financial planning and modeling concepts.
Proficiency in Anaplan or other similar planning and modeling platforms. Preferably a level 2 or 3 certified Anaplan Model Builder
Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment.
