As an engineer on Teamwork, the work youll do will have lasting and outsized impact on Figma as a successful business, and youll have plenty of opportunities to both leverage your existing strengths and learn new skills as the team grows and takes on new challenges.
 
The Explore team owns Figmas main home page experience, Figma.com. Explore is responsible for helping users create and manage their design and FigJam files, discover and connect with ideas, and collaborate while gaining inspiration from each other. This is an opportunity to own a core product surface for the company, help the team tackle difficult and nuanced performance and product problems, and improve the day-to-day experience for many engineers building on top of the space.
