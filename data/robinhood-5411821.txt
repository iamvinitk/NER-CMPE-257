Robinhood is looking for a Corporate Applications Engineer to join the Corporate Engineering team. This role will be a part of the Corporate Applications team within Corporate Engineering. As a member of the team, youll be responsible for planning, designing, implementing, and maintaining company SaaS applications and tools. This includes partnering with cross functional teams (HR, Finance, Security, Legal, and other organizational stakeholders) to assist with any and all application integrations. You will work to ensure that the corporate SaaS technology architecture and governance are aligned to strategic business initiatives, company goals, and the mission to democratize finance for all. This role is a great opportunity to join the team on the ground floor and help build out the corporate applications function at Robinhood.
The role is located in the office location(s) listed on this job description which will align with our in-office working environment. Please connect with your recruiter for more information regarding our in-office philosophy and expectations.
What youll do

Application administration, installation, configuration and upgrades of corporate SaaS solutions in the collaboration and productivity space.
Implementing SaaS system changes and administering access to systems and information within approved IT security policies, guidelines, and standards.
Assessing business requirements, evaluating possible solutions and then implementing andor recommending solutions.
Working closely with cross functional teams to develop scalable and reliable solutions.
Assisting in technical support problem resolutions related to business application software systems.
Maintaining standards, procedures, security controls and documentation related to areas of responsibility. May participate in system audits and perform testing for disaster recovery or SOX controls, ensuring a secure processing environment that protects the integrity and availability of information.
Participate in recommending budget items; implements and allocates resources following budget approval.

What you bring

5 years of experience in the IT space with 3 years of experience in design, development, implementation, and support of corporate SaaS applications (Google Workspace, Slack, Okta, Atlassian tools, etc)
Familiarity administration with SSOSAMLIAM systems.
Proven experience with managing cloud applications and platforms.
Ability to find out-of-box solutions to best fit Robinhoods needs.
Excellent verbal and written communication skills with both engineering and non-engineering audiences with customer focus and problem solving mentality.
Self-starter with the ability to overcome all obstacles and challenges with little to no supervision.
Ability to juggle multiple responsibilities and prioritize automation over manual processes.
Experience working with teams across different offices and timezones.
