Robinhood Markets (RHM) is the parent company of various lines of business, including Brokerage, Crypto, and Cash services. We are looking for a Risk Governance Manager to join Risk  Audit at Robinhood. You will be charged with executing on the companys comprehensive risk management governance and reporting framework, helping the organization identify risks arising from internal processes, people, and systems. This position requires a knowledgeable risk management professional with experience in brokerage and clearing operations, technology and information security, counterparty and third-party risks in a financial services institution.
As a key member of the Risk  Audit team, you will partner closely with the leaders and risk managers of each business unit (or subsidiary) to map and assess the operational activities, risks, controls, and issues within the business. The successful candidate is a highly motivated, collaborative, and analytical self-starter with the ability to build and scale risk governance programs across the enterprise to support Robinhoods growth.
What youll do

Play a leadership role in enhancing Robinhoods risk management governance program, including: establishing processes such as risk identification, assessment, monitoring, and reporting practices. 
Partner with functions such as Brokerage, Crypto and Cash payments operations, finance,  to develop and rollout enterprise level risk standards. 
Conduct risk governance activities such as assisting with risk and controls assessments, oversight of risk registers, issues tracking, corrective action plans, and leadership reporting.
Build partnerships with other organizational lines of defense (i.e. Operations, Customer Experience, Finance, Compliance, etc).
Participate in ad hoc projects and initiatives that enhance the risk and control environment.
Gain product knowledge of Robinhood products, processes, and systems. Stay abreast of current industry standards.
