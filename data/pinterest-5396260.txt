As a Lead Product Marketing Manager for our Programmatic Ads solutions, you will play a pivotal role in driving the success of our programmatic ad products through strategic planning, market analysis, and effective go-to-market strategies. You will partner closely with product leadership to set the vision, strategy, and roadmap for programmatic ads solutions and ensure future investment in solutions are positioned effectively and successfully in the market. 
 
What youll do:

Shape our product direction through market analysis, research and close collaboration with our Product, Engineering, and Design teams
Assess the capabilities and value proposition of our programmatic ad products through strategic, qualitative, and quantitative analysis
Serve as an advocate and represent the voice of customers; ensure broad awareness of and empathy for customer pain points internally
Drive cross-functional alignment throughout the go-to-market process, tracking success metrics accordingly
Define the messaging, positioning, and launch plan for new advertising products
Use data and insights to drive adoption of strategic priorities and influence our overall monetization strategy and growth initiatives 
Educate and inspire internal and external teams on Pinterests solutions for advertisers and partners alike 
Develop market understanding to build business requirements for new products
This is a highly cross-functional role that requires close partnership across Product, Engineering, Business Development, as well as Sales, Marketing, Communications, PR and Legal

What were looking for:

8 years of total experience in advertising with 3 in product marketing at a fast growing tech company
Experience in online brand and performance advertising with a deep understanding of the advertising landscape; subject matter expertise of programmatic advertising such as DSPs, Ad Exchanges, RMNs, etc desired 
Sharp eye for detail and proven project management and operational skills
Keen ability to translate complexity into simple, clear communication and approach
A passion for Pinterest!
