The consumer BD partner management team at Coinbase is focused on maximizing the value that Coinbase generates from its partner relationships.  These relationships are critical to the health and growth of Coinbases largest and most important business.  Within the team there is a vertical focus on payments, stablecoin, mobile and growth, and brand, and the role will work closely with the deal leads in each area.  In some cases the deals are first-of-a-kind, and we are learning what works, and in others the deal types are more well-established and the team is focused on optimizing and scaling processes.   Each vertical team works in support of the shared objectives developed by the product org, overseeing successful launches, driving performance and renegotiating terms for existing deals.
Coinbase is seeking a high performing and dynamic individual to focus on stablecoin partnerships as the Senior Associate, Stablecoin Partner Management. This person will manage and grow Coinbases strategic relationships to help accelerate our stablecoin growth and adoption efforts.
What youll be doing (ie. job duties):

Collaborate with deal lead and product teams to understand key terms and transition from deal negotiation phase to implementation and long term management and optimization.
 Work cross functionally with key stakeholders, e.g. finance, legal, compliance,  security, to operationalize partnerships, set business goals and coordinate success metrics.
Maintain healthy and productive relationships with stablecoin partners, establishing shared milestones and KPIs and resolving business, policy and technical issues.
Complete regular business reviews and look for opportunities to improve and grow our partner relationships.
Provide strategic recommendations to stakeholders for expansion opportunities with existing partners that have direct impact on product roadmap and business goals.
Support all elements of the partner management  life cycle:  scoping of opportunities, identifying potential partners,  framing of value, negotiating agreements,  and implementation and optimization.
Negotiate changes to existing agreements in addition to new amendments meant to expand relationship scope.

What we look for in you (ie. job requirements):

Proven ability to manage complex projects and partnerships: developing strong relationships with key internal and external stakeholders, communicating clearly, solving problems, holding difficult conversations and driving towards key milestones  outcomes.
Strong attention to detail, analytical skills (quantitative and qualitative), and organizational ability to manage several initiatives at once.
Creativity, resourcefulness and persistence to drive towards outcomes while navigating a dynamic environment.
Identify opportunities to shape and improve the partner management process, share best practices, enhance partner engagement and drive value creation.
5-7 years relevant experience in FintechCryptoPaymentstech business development, strategic partner management, or management consulting.
