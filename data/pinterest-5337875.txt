The ML Data team enables rapid development of ML features and trainingevaluation datasets at scale. We support hundreds of ML engineers and dozens of applications across Pinterest, including recommendations, ads, visual search, growthnotifications, trust and safety as well as content and user understanding.
The team provides:

A centralized feature store that manages and distributes ML features across batch, streaming and real time applications.
Trainingevaluation dataset store responsible for managing petabyte scale training  evaluation datasets.
Large scale data processing solutions and libraries for feature backfilling and last mile data processing.

The team utilizes many open-source projects including Spark, Parquet, Flink, Ray, Iceberg, Airflow, Finagle and Thrift, as well as systems built in-house to support large scale requirements. 
What youll do:

Lead the technical strategy and development of large-scale machine learning data management systems over 12 months horizon. 
Work with internal customers, ML engineers and data scientists to solve development velocity pain points in the ML data domain, and unlock innovations.
Proactively identify and address technical dependencies by collaborating with internal partner teams as well as the open-source community. 
Provide technical leadership and mentorship to engineers in the ML Data team.

What were looking for:

Deep expertise in big-data processing, storage and serving technologies (e.g. Spark, Parquet, Flink, Ray, Finagle)
Strong empathy for our customers. Someone who finds joy from helping our customers solve their problems. 
Strong communication skills, both verbal and written. Somebody who can share knowledge and vision within the ML Data team, as well as cross functionally. 
Coder: A strong software engineer who can lead and mentor through their own code and design choices.
