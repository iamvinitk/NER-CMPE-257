
Junior Associate, Client Experience, Consumer 
Weber Shandwick is seeking an ambitious and detail-oriented Junior Associate who wants to be part of a dedicated and engaged team that creates earned media communications solutions for clients. Our dynamic, integrated account teams deliver breakthrough ideas, innovative programs and executional excellence  all of which result in superlative client experiences and longstanding relationships. The ideal candidate will have some experience (academic andor internship) in PRjournalism, and a strong interest in consumer categories such as food and beverage, consumer packaged goods, home and entertainment.   
PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES

Provide operational support on client projects, as directed
Monitor and track PR campaign coverage, management of all relevant tools and internal and external measurement documents
Conduct media outreach, build relationships with reporters on behalf of the agency and clients
Ability to spot consumer trends and build a proficient understanding of the food and beverage media landscape
Draft written media materials
Share results of the work in a structured way
Proactively offer solutions for executional issues specific to assigned programs
Support multiple client and brand programs

MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS

Bachelors Degree
Strong written and verbal communication skills
Timely communication of significant issues and challenges
Confident presenter and persuasive speaker
Self-motivated, entrepreneurial and endlessly curious

ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS

PR agencyjournalism internship experience
Proficient written and oral communication skills
Available to work a full-time schedule
Professional demeanor
Meticulous attention to detail
Resourceful and effective problem solver
