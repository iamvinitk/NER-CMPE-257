We are seeking an exceptional Senior Finance Analyst to support the Marketing organization from our Shared Services Center in Prague.  This role is part of the Go-to-Market Finance team and will work closely with multiple stakeholders across Accounting, Procurement, Operations, Corporate Finance, and the Marketing organization among others.
This role requires a self-starter with strong communication skills, attention to detail, and business modeling skills. This role will focus on developing high quality financial analysis to derive business insights and growth opportunities for the Marketing team, enhancing existing financial models  processes, and be a key team contributor to running smooth close, forecast, and planning cycles. 
What You Will Be Doing

Provide timely close, forecast, headcount key data models with insights, risks and opportunities
Maintain and ensure accuracy when reporting forecast from Alteryx  Allocadia trackers each quarter 
Review and approve international purchase requisitions and accruals
Partner cross functionally with the Partner, Legal, and Accounting teams on Marketing Development Funding, Incentive programs, track efficacy and compliance performance, and maintain Partner ROI models
Strategically partner with Marketing  Procurement teams to provide financial analysis with insights and recommendations to new and renewal contracts
Drive automation in PO, TE, and Budgeting processes by leveraging Lean Six Sigma principles and Digital Technologies
Develop financial models  frameworks that support top line growth and facilitate investment prioritization 
Be the go-to subject matter expert on all things finance for the broader Marketing organization, providing financial insights and direction as needed
Perform ad-hoc projectsanalysis as needed

What You Bring To The Team

BA degree in Finance, Accounting, Economics or equivalent; MBA a plus
2-4 years of experience in FPA
Advanced proficiency in financial analysis and modeling with strong Excel skills
Great systems experience in Excel, Google Sheets, Anaplan, NetSuite, Allocadia, Alteryx, Tableau, Salesforce, or similar systems and tools. High-level experience in methodologies and processes for managing large scale databases. Proficiency in MS Access, SQL, or database programming languages is a plus
Attention to detail when necessary and ability to synthesize large volumes of data and summarize into presentationmanagement views
Ability to manage multiple priorities simultaneously, work well under pressure and adapt quickly to change.
Excellent writtenverbal communication skills, collaborative, positive attitude, and a strong team player
