Aim Leon Dore is looking for a Controller  Senior Accounting Manager to join our team in-person in Queens, New York, reporting directly to the Chief Financial Officer. This position will assist in overseeing the finance team, continuously identify system and process improvement, prepare combined financial statements, and executing financial close and reporting processes 
 
Duties and Responsibilities

Manage the preparation of combined financial statements including the Balance Sheet, Statement of Income, Statement of Changes in Stockholders Equity and Statement of Cash Flows
Assist CFO with overseeing and executing the monthly close process and ensure it is completed in a timely manner with the proper accruals and other journal entries, bank and merchant reconciliations fixed asset and depreciation reconciliations, reporting and account review and analysis (such as cost of goods sold, inventory, sga)
Support finance team with timely monthly, quarterly and year-end financial close process
Execute in the development, implementation and training of: 

Internal controls
Inventory count processes
Budgeting and tracking processes 
Internal Financial procedures 
ERP implementation

Manage cash flow projections for valuation model
Support leadership in completing special projects and analysis requests; serving as main point of contact within financial team to team ALD leadership
Ensure completion of daily transactions including general ledger and bank entries
Assist with consolidation and lease abstracts
Support in contract negotiations including lease agreements and vendors
Run ad-hoc financial reports, budgets and other analysis including project-specific profitability, accounts payable aging and accounts receivable aging 

 
Qualifications

Bachelors degree in accounting, finance, or business administration
6-10 years of related experience in corporate accounting or bookkeeping; experience within retail environment preferred
Experience at a high-growth company preferred
CPA strongly preferred
Experience with general ledger functions, the month-endyear end close process and implementation of new processes (controls, inventory, budgeting)
Proficiency with accounting software (e.g. Quickbooks) and Microsoft Excel
In-depth knowledge of USGAAP, IFRS, VAT, business taxes accounting principles and procedure
Understanding of US Sales tax and use tax, VAT, Corporate income tax and business tax 
Ability to self-start, roll-up the sleeves and prioritize in a fast-paced environment 
High degree of accuracy and keen attention to detail
Strong oral and written communication skills
