 The role
Were looking for web engineers. The most important skills youll need to have are making sound product decisions and shipping high-quality products fast. We look for engineers with great product instincts who can own whole projects from start to finish.
The software team has a lot of individual responsibility and many more freedoms, so we need to hire people we can trust. Your ability to make good trade-offs and exercise good judgment is essential to us.
 About You  

4 years of frontend engineering experience. 
Bachelors degree in Computer Science, a related field, or equivalent work experience.
You are known for shipping products and features lightning-fast.
Mastery of at least one language (we primarily use Python, React, Elixir, JavaScript, and are open to others).
You can execute pixel-perfect designs and have a strong knowledge of CSS.
Excellent product instincts. You first think about users rather than the best technical solution.
Youre an excellent problem solver and dont need to be told exactly what to do.
Proven track record of delivering features.  
Product Management experience (optional).

Startup experience at a high-growth company.
