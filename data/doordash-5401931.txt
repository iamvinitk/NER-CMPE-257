About the Team
The incident management team is a new add to our workforce planning team that will be responsible for managing all S0 and S1 issuesoutages to mitigate outageissue impact from an SLAASA and experience perspective. The team will achieve this through the proper following of outageissue management playbooks that they will help create and update as needed. Incident Management Analysts will work closely with Engineering, Business Applications, Support Operations, and Workforce Management to ensure the business continues to operate at optimal levels during S0S1 outagesissues.
About the Role
The Incident Management Analyst will be the subject matter expert on incident management fundamentals including outage management, Interactive Voice Response system engagement, general outage messaging, communicating with operations, engineering, Customer Experience Strategy team and BizApps and drafting an incident post-mortem to share with all cross-functional stakeholders. The Incident Analyst will also be responsible for Okta administration for our BPOTier 1 support partners (password resets, lockouts, troubleshooting, etc.) and will flag any routing issues to the Business Applications Team for research and resolution, as well as be in charge of setting nesting configuration in SalesForce.
You will report into the Workforce Manager on our Workforce Planning team in our Global Support and Integrity Operations organization.
Youre excited about this opportunity because you will
Follow incident management playbooks based on severity level to mitigate outage impact to SLAASA and overall experience.
Alert Engineering, Customer Experience Strategy, Business Applications Team, and Operations for action on Severity 0 and Severity 1 level incidents.
Serve as the primary point of contact for all severity 0 and severity 1 incidents
Report and give updates to all cross functional stakeholders during the incident.
Serve as main POC for outage cleanup (issuance for credits and refunds to Consumer, Dasher, and Merchant)
Partner with the Business Applications Team to optimizeresolve IVR routing when a routing issue is raised.
Work with departments and teams to deliver steady operations around our contact centers during an incident.
Lead Okta administration (password resets, agent lockout, and troubleshooting) for our BPOTier 1 support partners.
Perform initial analysis to categorize incident severity level (may also share with RTA).
Drive communications and collaboration with all relevant stakeholders (Engineering, Business Applications, Customer Experience Strategy, and Operations).
Perform Post-Mortems to identify opportunities for improvement and communicate impact assessment to all relevant stakeholders (M-Team, Customer Experience Leadership, Strategic Operations, Engineering, and Business Application Team).
