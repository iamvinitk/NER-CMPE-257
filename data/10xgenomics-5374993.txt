About the role
At 10x Genomics we innovate constantly, empowering our customers to discover new biology with previously unimagined breadth, depth and accuracy. As a Scientist, Applied Bioinformatics you will be at the center of innovation that builds and maintains an optimal Customer Experience for 10x Genomics products. We are seeking a multi-talented individual to join our 10x Applied Bioinformatics team. This individual will be a cross between a bioinformatics scientist, software engineer, and support engineerscientist. 
You must be an effective communicator and have a sincere desire to provide great support for our customers. You will work directly with our customers to provide first-class bioinformatics and software support. This is an exciting opportunity to work with a world-class team and become an expert with groundbreaking 10x Genomics software and bioinformatics applications. 
What will you be doing

Serve as a customer-facing expert on 10x Genomics software and bioinformatics products providing timely and expert resolutions to issues and questions
Manage the issue escalation process. Hold peer-level consultations with scientists and bioinformaticians at top academic, research, and corporate institutions. Partner daily with in-house RD scientists, computational biologists, and software engineers to provide timely resolution. 
Be the Voice of the Customer as a member of core product development teams. Distill, quantify, and actively represent customer feedback to internal teams. Use your Customer Perspective to advocate for the User Experience. 
Develop and maintain accessible software training material for internal and external training. 
Constantly learn new tools, algorithms, and analysis strategies being applied to 10x Genomics data developed both in-house and in the community. 
Bioinformatics outreach, virtual and in-person participation in trade shows, seminars, and technical symposia. Remote members must be able to travel up to 20, HQ based members up to 10.

4 years of Bioinformatics experience
Masters or PhD in Biology, Bioinformatics, Computer Science, or similar discipline or equivalent work experience 
Demonstrated experience implementing, troubleshooting and supporting bioinformatics software and pipelines.
Experience in UNIXLinux environments, and scripting or programming experience.
