The Team:
As a member of the Engineering organization, the  Product Analytics  Data Sciences team provides analytic support and a strategic perspective to the product and engineering teams to enable them to make evidence-based conclusions. We deliver actionable insights by collecting, organizing, and interpreting data about user journeys, product telemetry, incidents, infrastructure utilization, sales motions, and much more. We transform data into analytic assets by conducting exploratory analysis, identifying outliers, validating hypotheses, building statistical models, quantifying risk; communicating findings through well-crafted stories and critical recommendations. The Role:
As a Staff Product Analytics Engineer in the Product Analytics  Data Sciences team, you will play a key role in defining and building the analytic infrastructure that will enable strategic and tactical decision-making in a hyper-growth environment. As one of the most senior technical experts on the team, you will provide technical leadership to design, build and maintain a data ecosystem for analytic needs within the product and engineering organization. In this exciting role, in addition to defining and creating the analytic infrastructure, you will also need to facilitate data-informed discussions between stakeholders across the organization, through effective storytelling with meaningful insight.
The work youll do:

Engage with stakeholders from product, engineering, and GTM teams to understand the analytic needs, identify the underlying data engineering requirements and help build the core technical foundations needed to support product analytics. 
Design, build and maintain resilient data infrastructure that gathers data from a variety of sources - cloud billing, product telemetry, and observability systems. Ensure the continuous and reliable flow of data needed to support all the product analytics activities.
Work closely with the product and engineering teams to understand business requirements. Design and build analytic solutions that can deliver data-informed insights to stakeholders.
Continuously engage in refining data engineering processes to ensure data accuracy, consistency, and integrity through continuous audit and review
Conduct in-depth analytic investigations to find answers to business questions or support making the case for product andor engineering ideas
