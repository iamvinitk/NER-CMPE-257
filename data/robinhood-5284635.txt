Our mission is to allow our customers to invest in whatever asset they want (equity, option, crypto), whenever they want (24 hours a day), and however they want at the best execution quality while ensuring market integrity.
We own critical services that facilitate the routing of trades to exchanges and market makers. Our Order Routing infrastructure encompasses several critical components, including:

Order Routing Service: This high availability, low latency, scalable service efficiently routes trades to exchanges  3rd parties, while ensuring correctness and reliability.
Order Execution Management System (OEMS): Our OEMS enables Trading Ops to manage time-sensitive trading exceptions, which are essential for a smooth trading process and regulatory compliance.
Order Risk Checks System: This system enables risk management at the firm-wide level for assets, venues, and trading sessions.
Smart Order Router: Ensures the highest quality trade execution.
