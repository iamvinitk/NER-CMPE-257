If you think you can see yourself delivering our mission and pursuing our goals with us, then check out the job description below! 
What youll do: 

Collaborate with clients to understand their business needs and technical requirements, translating them into comprehensive cloud-based solutions.
Design and architect cloud solutions that are scalable, resilient, secure, and cost-effective, using best practices and relevant cloud services.
Provide expertise and guidance on cloud-native services, serverless computing, microservices architecture, containerization, and related technologies.
Develop architecture diagrams, technical specifications, and documentation to clearly communicate design and implementation approaches to the project team and stakeholders.
Lead technical discussions with clients, project teams, and cross-functional groups to make informed decisions regarding cloud technologies and solution design.
Collaborate with development, operations, and security teams to ensure the successful deployment and operation of cloud solutions.
Evaluate and mitigate risks associated with cloud solutions, including security vulnerabilities, performance bottlenecks, and scalability challenges.
Provide hands-on support during implementation and troubleshooting phases to ensure successful delivery of projects.
Mentor and guide junior architects and technical team members, fostering a culture of knowledge sharing and continuous learning.

What youll need to succeed: 

 At least 12 years of experience as a Cloud Solution Architect or similar role, with a strong emphasis on cloud-based solution design and architecture.
Extensive experience with major cloud platforms such as Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, or Google Cloud Platform (GCP).
In-depth understanding of cloud architecture patterns, microservices, serverless computing, containers (Docker, Kubernetes), and related technologies.
Solid understanding of cloud security best practices and compliance standards.
Strong programmingscripting skills in languages such as Python, Java, or JavaScript.
Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written, with the ability to convey complex technical concepts to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.
Strong problem-solving and analytical abilities, with a track record of developing innovative solutions to challenging technical problems.
Experience working with and leading Agile development teams.
An active TSSCI clearance with CI poly preferred or Active TSSCI clearance with ability to pass a CI poly
Relevant certifications in cloud architecture (e.g., AWS Solutions Architect, Azure Solutions Architect) are highly desirable.
