Harmonia Holdings Group, LLC is an award-winning minority and female owned federal government contractor committed to providing innovative, high-performing solutions to our government clients and focused on fostering a workplace that encourages growth, initiative, creativity, and employee satisfaction.  Harmonia Holdings Group, LLC, an award-winning federal government contractor, is seeking an Organizational Change Manager for a growth opportunity providing a suite of services promoting andor developing the effective use of technology by establishing and maintaining enterprise-wide solutions.
This position is remote.
Minimum Required Qualifications:

1 year of experience supporting knowledge management and content management activities
3 years of experience supporting Organizational Change Management activities (training, communications, stakeholder management, user readiness, to name a few)
1 year of experiencing supporting federal cloud technology transformation activities
1 year of experience supporting knowledge management and content management activities
Citizenship required
Ability to obtain Public Trust Clearance

Desired Qualifications:

Certification in an industry recognized change management program
Knowledge, skills and abilities of conducting user research
Knowledge, skills and abilities of implementing human centered design thinking and activities
Knowledge, skills and abilities of implementing customer centricity activities
Knowledge, skills and abilities in working with customers to understand their requirements and deliver superior solutions
Knowledge, skills and abilities in troubleshooting customer inquiries
Knowledge, skills and abilities in managing content in Confluence
Knowledge, skills and abilities in managing content in SharePoint Online
Knowledge, skills and abilities in Microsoft Teams
Ability to support customers that possesses varying degrees of knowledge in cloud technology
Knowledge, skills and abilities in creating communication plans
Knowledge, skills and abilities in resistance management
Knowledge, skills and abilities in developing training plans
Knowledge, skills and abilities in articulating the value proposition of a change to customers
