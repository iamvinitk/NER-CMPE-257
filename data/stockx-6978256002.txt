Why youll love this role
The Team Lead, Operations and Supply Chain will lead, mentor and coach the day-to-day operations for operations as well as supply chain. They will help manage the inbound, storage, organizing, picking of finished goods inventory, and manage supply levels of raw materials needed on site. This role will also be responsible for using analytical and quantitative methods to understand, predict, implement, manage and enhance operations and supply chain processes. They will be responsible for assembling data, making supply chain decisions, analyzing performance, identifying problems, and developing processes and solutions that support supply chain planning and operations.
 
What youll do

Lead the team of Operations and Supply Chain Specialists in the management of supply chain and finished goods inventory 
Supervise and train the team to optimize quality, productivity, and costs 
Develop, implement and maintain standard operating procedures 
Analyze trade velocity of product categories and evaluate inventory levels that the Authentication Center should have on-hand 
Gather and interpret relevant data from inventory levels, Authentication Center capacity, costs, productivity, demand patterns, etc. 
Review inventory levels of packaging materials and work with suppliers to bring in replenishment supplies for daily operations 
Investigates problems, finds root causes, develops and implements solutions within Operations and Supply Chain processes 
Monitors contract compliance of carriers and other logistics service providers
Provides analytical support for projects, system implementations, market expansions, geographical expansions, new business opportunities, and proposals 
Coordinates delivery schedules and other supply chain activities 
Leads weekly meetings with key suppliers to review actual performance, estimate future demand and resolve operational issues and provides reporting on daily, weekly and monthly key performance metrics 
Establish team member staffing to meet the daily business demands, quality and productivity targets 
Ensure accurate and on-time fulfillment and shipment of all customer orders
Analyzes production metrics and recommend process and system improvements to increase quality and lower cost 
Create, maintain and foster a safe and team-based work environment 
Work cross-functionally with all departments to identify, develop and execute on process improvements
