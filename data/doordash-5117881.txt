About the Team
Data is at the foundation of DoorDashs success. The Engineering team in India builds data tools for our internal and external consumers like Data as a Platform (DaaP) solution - which enables merchants and partners to retrieve the data they need from DoorDash and integrate it with their data pipeline, configuration tool which enables configuration management for ETLs , and few other internal tools to solve other data related use cases.
About the Role
As a part of Data Engineering, DoorDash is hiring a Full Stack Software Engineer to build APIs as part of DaaP solution and end-to-end solutions for various in-house data tools supporting other data initiatives. Youll collaborate cross-functionally, with multiple internal stakeholders and help solve some complex problems. This role is located in Pune, India. You will report directly into a Data Engineering Manager. This will be a hybrid position.
