The National VP of Payer Channel Growth reports to and supports the VP, Payer Growth on efficiently operationalizing Mavens commercial agreements with Health Plans, and maximizing revenue opportunities within a Health Plan.  In this role, you will own a book of business of national and regional health plans, and oversee a cross-functional team responsible for driving the success and growth of these partnerships. You will be responsible for owning the overall partnership strategy, revenue growth, and executive level payer relationships, in addition to serving as a leader on the Payer Growth team. You will serve as the internal Maven expert on your partners strategy and priorities, working effectively across multiple internal teams (including sales, product marketing, and client success) to remove roadblocks, enable the partnership to run smoothly, and foster new areas of partnership growth. Your super power is your ability to strategically develop meaningful and enduring relationships at all levels of a health plan, in order to drive a mutually beneficial growth strategy with our health plan partners.  
As the National VP, Payer Channel Growth, you will:

Strategically enable new revenue growth through an assigned Health Plan book of business that may include reselling agreements and embedded programs across commercial and medicaid plans
Serve as overall account relationship, pipeline, and partnership success owner for each assigned health plan with primary responsibility for overall bookings target, program enrollments, and channel profitability
Identify joint Maven  Health Plan priorities to develop an overarching partnership strategy, including priorities and account plan
Develop and drive the cross-program payer strategy, identify priorities for team, unblock most strategic revenue obstacles, and advise on largest reseller deals
Own executive relationships at the health plan, and serves as the escalation point for day-to-day operations and sales teams
Ensure positive relationships with health plan product and sales teams are driving Mavens commercial strategy forward
Collaborate across payer channel team and with cross-functional Maven teams to identify and deliver cross-payer initiatives and best practices
Drive new opportunity identification, prioritization, and facilitation, including ownership of all contracting  pricing (new SOWs, renewals, and reseller models)
Oversee large product initiatives and requests from the payer requiring custom investment and provide payer feedback on product roadmap to expand and enhance Mavens product offering  capabilities 
Demonstrate a broad comprehension of our partner and client needs, market trends, industry challenges, major players (payers and providers), relevant products and technologies
Manage 1-2 RVPs responsible for multiple regional plans
