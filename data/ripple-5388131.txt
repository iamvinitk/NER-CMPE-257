Own the product vision and roadmap for onboarding interfaces

4 years of product management experience with top Internet, e-commerce, enterprise software andor financial services companies

Experienced in user-centered design with a customer-centric mindset to product development
Experience partnering with cross-functional teams of engineers, designers, sales, marketing, operations, and data scientists to deliver extraordinary products.
Exceptional communication skills, both within your team, with leadership, and with stakeholders
Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
Proven track record of leading and influencing a Product-driven organization
A passion for payments and a strong interest in the blockchain and crypto ecosystem
