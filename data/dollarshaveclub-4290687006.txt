ROLE SUMMARY:
The PMO team at Dollar Shave Club serves as the central hub for upholding operational excellence across all cross-functional projects. We are a strategic group of project managers known for our executional prowess and meticulous attention to detail, ensuring the timely and complete delivery of every project. Beyond our operational efficiency, we also serve as strategic thought partners, committed to driving the success of the business.
This particular role is integral to the Creative Ops team and entails the management of creative projects from their inception through to final delivery, with exposure to various production aspects, including video and photoshoots. Given our businesss heavy reliance on marketing endeavors, this role holds significant importance in shaping the execution of our commercial activities. It plays a crucial role in ensuring that all creative output aligns seamlessly with our brand DNA and tone.
RESPONSIBILITIES:

Collaborate closely with the Project Managers and cross-functional teams to define project scope, objectives, and requirements.
Assist in building project plans and timelines while maintaining detailed project documentation.
Facilitate smooth communication and workflow between cross-functional teams, including key stakeholders, ensuring strategy and deliverable needs are met.
Oversee marketing campaigns, social campaigns and communications, and support smaller packaging projects.
Assist in resource management and maintain an updated capacity planner for all your projects.
Prepare for sprint planning sessions.
Maintain vigilance over the briefs for all your projects. As strategic thought partners, our team diligently reviews all briefs to guarantee they are well-prepared for a strategic kick-off.
Challenge the current state of affairs. Every Project Manager should actively contribute opinions on how we can enhance our team and further foster exceptional project management across the organization. Dont hesitate to be bold, share your insights, and help us achieve greatness.

REQUIREMENTS:

Bachelors or equivalent work experience
2 years of PM experience in a fast-paced creative department or agency
Proficient in creative project management
Skilled in constructing project plans and actively participating in creative workstreams, encompassing internal reviews, stakeholder management, legal review, and final project delivery

Well-versed in assisting creative teams with preparations for stakeholder presentations
Accomplished in crafting timelines and comprehensive project plans
Able to demonstrate the sensitivity and exceptional communication skills necessary to enhance our creative endeavors and effectively manage our clients
High level of discretion and professionalism
