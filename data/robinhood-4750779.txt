The preferred location for this position is in or around Robinhoods offices in Menlo Park, CA, or New York City, NY, with in-office work capabilities of at least 3xweek.
Robinhood is looking for a senior Program Manager to provide horizontal support for our Design and Creative teams with expertise and passion for program strategy and operations in a product-driven environment.
The ideal candidate is a problem solver who takes responsibility and ownership to make processes and programs run smoothly, and they understand needs and dynamics of the team as they evolve. This role requires a breadth of project management experience, as well as deep expertise in both the Design and Creative development processes. Strong organization skills, communication skills, and operational excellence are critical, as this person will lead workflows, communicate and remove roadblocks, and continuously iterate on and improve the teams operating model. They will be the right hand of Design and Creative leadership, make sure everything is running like clockwork, and ensure that our Design managers, Creative Managers, and Producers are set up to make the best work possible for our customers.

Your day-to-day will involve:

Supporting and liaising for the Design and Creative teams - connecting these functions to product development and marketing, ensuring inter-dependencies are understood and planned
Establishing and driving planning processes with leadership, budgeting (with finance), reporting, and communications cadences
Serving as air traffic control and being the central source for project launch prioritization and partnering with leadership to resource new initiatives
Developing and maintaining tracking solutions for launches, ensuring systems connect strategically to existing trackers and communication centers for reporting to leadership
Managing information and archiving centers - ensuring documentation is updated and that files are properly archived and reflect appropriate status and content
Working with finance to actualize functional costs at the end of each month (i.e. for license assets, stock photos, animations, music, etc.)
Managing legal, compliance, and procurement processes for all vendors and softwares, as well as licensing and employee access
Establishing onboarding toolkits and templates for new hires, ensuring documentation and resources are up to date

Some things we consider critical for this role:

5-7 years of experience in Design or Creative program management or project management in a product development or agency environment
Expertise in Design and Creative development processes
Excellent communication, project, and time-management skills with an ability to learn quickly and accommodate rapid change
Able to organize information quickly and at a high level in order to address potential roadblocks and define the best way forward, keeping in mind the quality of the work while giving it room to develop
Strong understanding of digital and video asset creation and approval cycles
Able to work independently and have a strong sense of ownership
