About the team
The Business Application Developer, ServiceNow contributes to the work on highly integrated applications and platforms that drive the business forward. Partnering with senior developers and business analysts, the incumbent supports the team in developing new user experiences and creating digital solutions to solve challenges within ServiceNow. The incumbent ensures that business applications meet compliance requirements and provides efficient solutions to team members within Corporate IT on the necessary support and enhancement of our business applications.
What you will do

Support the implementation of new application projects, and provide support for our platforms and business applications, specifically ServiceNow, including upgrades, enhancements, fixes, and features, in collaboration with business SMEs, and team members within Solution Architecture and Business Analysis.
Participate in delivering system testing; configuring, automating, documenting, and performing test cases and test scripts to identify and correct issues or to verify desired functionality.
Design, develop, and document solutions on new platforms and applications, and support the testing of new features and capabilities on existing applications and platforms, providing best practices and analysis to the rest of the team.
Contribute to code reviews, adopting and documenting code and configuration management best practices.
Work in collaboration with your team to deliver projects on Agile methodology.
Act as the technical subject matter expert on some of the ServiceNow Modules such as: 

IT Service Management
IT Operations Management
HR Service Delivery
Strategic Portfolio Management
Employee Center Pro
