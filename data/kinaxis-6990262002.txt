Job location: This is a remote position. You can work from home and be located anywhere in the USA.
About the team
Reporting to the Payroll Manager, this role is responsible for handling all aspects of payroll processing of US payroll, benefits and compliance in a fast-growing business. This role will ensure that payroll transactions are well documented, executed appropriately and completed in a timely manner, and assist in developing and building cost-effective and efficient payroll processes. This will include month end closure responsibilities related to payroll activities. This is a very analytically focused role and is a great opportunity for someone experienced with multi-national payroll to join a dynamic team.
What you will do

Responsible for full-cycle semi-monthly payroll processing for employees across multi-state locations, timely distribution and reviewing accurate calculation of payroll checks, taxes and other withholdings including frequent new hires.
Working with cross-functional stakeholders to ensure benefits and remittances are delivered.
Respond timely to all payroll and related queries.
Prepare and complete payroll related journal entries and month end close procedures and reconciliations.
Process withholdings and complete monthly payroll reconciliations and annual reporting
Prepare reconciliations, payments, and payroll changes relating to various types of leaves and terminations.
Compile and prepare monthly, quarterly, and annually payroll related reports and remittances after each pay run, and as needed.
Work closely with Human Resources to facilitate and ensure accurate data entry of benefit, employee data, pay and other payroll changes into the payroll system.
Participate  support HR new programsinitiatives and work on changes impacting payroll.
Support month-end closing procedures and regulatory and financial audit requirements.
Implement new benefit and pay element items as required.
Registering New States as required
Continuously evaluate, suggest, and implement improvements to payroll processes.
Assist with the preparation  maintenance of the monthly payroll for our Global Entities in Europe and Asia.

What we are looking for

Completed or working towards Payroll Compliance Professional (PCP) Certification
5 years payroll and benefits administration for a multi-country environment. Multi-state US experience is
Knowledge of federal and state payroll regulations and requirements.
General ledger experience and a solid understanding of accounting concepts.
Experience in general accounting and month end close procedures.
Proficiency with Excel
Excellent written, interpersonal, and verbal communication skills and team player
Ability to multitask and work in a fast-paced and demanding global environment with an ability to prioritize.
Highly discreet when dealing with sensitive employee payroll and benefit matters.
Organized, efficient, with the ability to handle multiple tasks.
