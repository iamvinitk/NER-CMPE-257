
About Kinaxis  
Kinaxis is the global leader in modern supply chain management. We serve supply chains and the people who manage them in service of humanity. Our software is trusted by renowned global brands to provide the agility and predictability needed to navigate todays volatility and disruption. We combine our patented concurrency technique with a human-centered approach to AI to empower businesses of all sizes to orchestrate their end-to-end supply chain network, from multi-year strategic planning through down-to-the-second execution and last-mile delivery. For more news and information, please visit kinaxis.com or follow us on LinkedIn. Business Consultant - Pre-Sales 
Job location: Taipei, Taiwan
What you will do

Assist in identifying the customers needs and key stakeholders in the purchase decision.
Effectively deliver a standard product demo while emphasizing elements that are most aligned with the urgent needs of the customer.
Construct and deliver custom demos, utilizing customer-provided data that address the customers urgent needs. This may involve the configuration of unique RapidResponse resources (workbooks, task flows, scorecards, etc.).
Consistently follow the sales process which includes ensuring that key deliverables are delivered
Proactively provide effective feedback to product management on product gaps and improvement areas.
Where necessary create and deliver supplementary presentation materials in support of customer-specific demonstrations.
Produce sales transition documentation for Professional Services, and participate in Sales to Services transition meetings to ensure proper handoff of newly won accounts to the deployment team.

What we are looking for

Relevant experience and domain expertise in one or more of the 7 key industries served (Automotive, High-Tech, PharmaLifesciences, Industrial, Aerospace and Defense, CPG, or Retail)
Minimum of five years experience in a pre-sales position within the supply chain management software industry
Ability to work independently within a team environment
Excellent communication, presentation, facilitation, and time management skills
Adaptability and strong initiative are required for success in this role
Ability to manage multiple priorities and perform well in a fast-paced environment
Ability and willingness to travel primarily in North America (Global when necessary)
Kinaxis RapidResponse certification in the role of Contributor, Author, or Admin is considered an asset
Familiarization with database technologies and any related data manipulation or technical skills will be considered an asset
