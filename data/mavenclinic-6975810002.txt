Maven is the worlds largest virtual clinic for women and families on a mission to make healthcare work for all of us. Mavens award-winning digital programs provide clinical, emotional, and financial support all in one platform, spanning fertility  family building, maternity  newborn care, parenting  pediatrics, and menopause. Employers and health plans trust Mavens end-to-end platform to improve clinical outcomes, reduce healthcare costs, and provide equity in benefits programs. We are looking for a mission-driven Director of Care Advocacy who will lead a team that provides world-class care delivery services to Maven members.  Our Care Advocacy team is the voice of Maven, and the leader in this role will be deeply attuned to the needs of our members, translating this into a highly reliable, highly effective care model. You and your team will develop the member engagement strategies, processes, and metrics to cultivate a high performing environment that is collaborative, moves fast, and embraces a continuous improvement mindset to deliver on Mavens clinical outcomes goals. Your team will support our members by jointly developing care plans, guiding them to care on Maven and in their community, and sharing health and benefits education - ultimately helping them manage their health needs, make informed decisions, and navigate complex healthcare systems effectively. 
To make our mission a reality, your skills will be accountable for: 

Learn the ins and outs of Mavens business and the day-to-day Care Advocate responsibilities, diving in to directly master our care delivery methods, tools and processes
Lead and inspire a high performing Care Advocacy team while fostering a culture of learning, accountability, connectedness, and engagement
Define goals and KPIs for the Care Advocacy team that are aligned to clear business and clinical outcomes
Establish ongoing monitoring processes to track the operational health of the team
Hire, train, direct and develop Care Advocacy Managers and Care Advocates through role-modeling, coaching, feedback, and training
Develop and implement member engagement techniques, protocols and best practices to ensure seamless access to care
Use your continuous improvement mindset to lead with data and iterate on our care programs, going deep where necessary, and act as the final back-stop for member escalations 
Lead strategic initiatives with an aim to improve clinical outcomes, streamline operations, and build for scale
Collaborate closely with cross-functional stakeholders across our Care Support, Product, Clinical Innovation, Medical Group, and Growth departments

Minimum Qualifications:  

8 years leading member facing care teams in a fast-paced, high-growth environment
People management experience within digital clinical care, navigation or coaching programs - particularly with a non-exempt workforce
Familiarity with health promotion and behavior change interventions
Strong organizational skills, detail orientation, and bias to action, with an ability to actively track performance over time and rapidly implement processes to improve performance
Excellent interpersonal and communication skills, with an ability to communicate clearly and persuasively to your team and leaders in other parts of the organization at Maven
Empathetic and passionate about creating positive experiences in member and team interactions
Strong critical thinking skills with an ability to diagnose problems, evaluate, and drive improvements
Metrics-driven with excellent management skills and an ability to inspire and empower employees
Skilled change leader, with a proven track record of motivating team through periods of rapid change and growth

Preferred Qualifications: 

Clinical practice background in nursing or behavioral health
Health coaching certification (NBC-HWC or ICF)
Experience in family planning and maternal-child health areas such as pregnancy, postpartum, adoption, surrogacy, and fertility
