About the Role:
We are seeking a highly skilled and visionary Platform Product Marketing Director to lead our product marketing efforts and drive the success of our Intelligence Cloud platform. As the Platform Marketing DirectorPrincipal at Recorded Future, you will be a trusted expert in Recorded Futures Intelligence cloud platform. You will help develop and evangelize the value of Intelligence Cloud to the market. 
What Youll Do as Platform Marketing Director 

Platform Positioning and Messaging:

Develop a clear and compelling value proposition for our platform, effectively communicating its unique capabilities and benefits to various target audiences.
Craft consistent and powerful messaging that resonates with key stakeholders, including customers, partners, and internal teams

Go-to-Market Strategy:

Create and execute comprehensive go-to-market plans for platform launches and major updates, including market segmentation, and promotional activities.
Collaborate with sales and partner teams to drive platform adoption and revenue generation, providing them with the necessary tools and resources for success.

Market and Competitive Analysis:

Conduct in-depth market research and competitive analysis to identify market trends, customer needs, and opportunities for differentiation
Stay ahead of industry developments and emerging technologies to ensure the platform remains competitive and aligned with customer expectations

Product Content and Collateral:

Oversee the development of high-quality marketing content, such as whitepapers, case studies, videos, and presentations, that showcase the platforms value proposition and customer success stories.

Customer Insights and Feedback:

Engage directly with customers to understand their needs, gather feedback, and incorporate insights into product marketing strategies and product roadmap planning

Thought Leadership:

Establish the company and platform as a thought leader in the industry through speaking engagements, webinars, industry events, and strategic partnerships

Sales Enablement: 

Support our sales channels by developing a comprehensive content plan, training them on the problems our platform solves, and creating internal tools and external collateral. Ensure the sales team knows when and how to leverage these resources effectively


What Youll Bring as Platform Marketing Director 

10 years of experience in product marketing management, preferably in the technology  cybersecurity industry
Proven experience working extensively with enterprise-class platforms and a strong understanding of new technologies. Solid knowledge of cybersecurity is mandatory 
Strong relationship-building skills and a customer-centric mindset. Ability to understand customer needs and translate them into effective marketing strategies
Strong understanding of platform and ecosystem dynamics, including APIs, workflows and integrations
Demonstrated success in crafting and executing go-to-market strategies for platform products
Exceptional leadership and communication skills with the ability to influence and collaborate with cross-functional teams. Be confident and comfortable presenting at conferences and events
Analytical mindset, capable of leveraging data-driven insights to inform marketing decisions
Strategic thinker with the ability to see the big picture and identify growth opportunities
