About the Team:
The Learning  Development (LD) team partners closely with the executive leadership team, business leaders and HR Business Partners to identify learning needs and to design, develop and deliver relevant training programs for our consultants.
About the Job:  
As an LD Specialist, you will require four key skills:  the ability to assess, design, develop, and deliver.  This role will enable you to create opportunities for knowledge sharing across the business.  You will also have the scope to proactively manage the end to end delivery of several core training programs. You will engage with key stakeholders to update and work with them on LD initiatives aligned to their specific business strategy. You will also engage with the global learning strategy to ensure alignment to our culture, values, company goals and objectives.
What Youll Get to Do: 

Design, develop and deliver scalable learning, leadership, and career development programs for both face to face and virtual consumption
Continually assess, review, and evolve existing programs as the business continues to grow
Proactively resolve project, design, or resource issues
Provide outstanding program management support
Foster an environment of social and blended learning using LMS
Measure training effectiveness and produce analytics to inform any program updates
Manage external vendor relationships
Facilitate instructor-led, face to face and virtual soft skill trainings
Build and maintain strong relationships with subject matter experts across the business
