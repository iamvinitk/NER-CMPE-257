Supernal is at the forefront of creating emerging mobility solutions that will foster the development of human-centered cities. We are designing a completely new electric vertical take-off and landing (eVTOL) aircraft tailored to the mobility needs of future cities. This allows passengers a seamless intermodal journey that safely transports them to their final destination. We fuse research in autonomy, robotics, aviation and services to define a new category of mobility for the worlds communities. We believe in creative thinking and collaboration to help build a better mobility experience for everyone, improving peoples ability to move  whether for work or play.
What we do: 
The Facilities Site Manager plays a crucial role in supervising and optimizing the operational aspects of our facilities. You will oversee the day-to-day operations of our facility, ensuring a safe, efficient, and well-maintained environment. Responsibilities include, evaluating and design space, coordinating onsite vendors, and supervising facility projects and build outs to support our organization goals and deliver an exceptional workplace experience.
This position will be required to work on-site 5 days a week.
What you can do:

Lead a facilities team in alignment with the Facilities Operations Manager in addition to external vendors.
Assist Facilities Management with planning and supervising large construction and renovation projects.
Plan office space according to current and future needs.
Assist Facilities Management in creating a long-term facility strategy  be a key contributor.
Work Closely with the Environmental, Health  Safety Manager to support company compliance requirements.
Lead the onsite food  beverage program.
Consistently monitor the culture and propose ways to make this an exciting place to work.
Ability to accurately forecast department spend for budget cycles.
Liaise with building management where and when appropriate.
Work with Engineering teams to design and build out facilities.
Supervise all building functions including day-to-day maintenance.
Pro-actively recommending any upgrades, changes, and improvements to our facilities and services.
Control activities such as parking, building security, waste disposal, service contracts, vendor services, etc.
Lead other fringe benefits such as game rooms, social events, team building offsite events, massage program, etc. that brings value and helps with attrition.
Hire and train staff as needed to build the organization under this position.
Handle day-to-day operations until the Facilities team is hired.
May require up to 10 of domestic and international travel.
Other duties as assigned.
