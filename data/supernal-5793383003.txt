What we do: 
The Lead Flight Test Engineer works closely with core engineering and operations teams on planning and execution of flight test campaigns. This role is a critical contributor in enabling safe, efficient experimental flight testing. The role is responsible for developing test plans, writing test cards, and participating in flight readiness reviews, test readiness reviews, and risk assessments. The Flight Test Engineer will work closely with aircraft maintenance to ensure that the flight vehicle is in proper configuration for testing, coordinates ground  flight tests, performs data analysis, writes test reports, documents test results, and troubleshoots issues. An understanding and working knowledge of flight test instrumentation and data acquisition and have strong configuration management skills. This role also has responsibility to provide mentorship to more junior Flight Test Engineers.
This position will be required to work on-site 5 days a week.
What you can do:

Technical lead of a vehicle flight test engineering team
Acquiring comprehensive knowledge of air vehicles systems
Experience in leading the development and execution of flight test campaigns and associated processes
Generating system-level flight test procedures and deriving and tracking flight requirements
Collaborating with engineers, flight test pilots, and the test operations support team to develop flight test plans and write flight test cards
Planning, organizing, and executing flight test operations
Ensuring the test vehicle is properly configured to meet test objectives
Leads test daily test conduct
Leading daily flight test coordination and communication
Analyzing data, documenting results, and troubleshooting obstacles
Coordinating with multiple teams in support of flight test objectives and risk hazard assessments
Experience in data reduction and analysis to compare against expected test outcomes
Advocate and leader of a strong safety culture
May require up to 25 of domestic and international travel
Other duties as assigned
