With millions of diners, tens of thousands of restaurants, and 23 years of experience, OpenTable, part of Booking Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: BKNG), is an industry leader with a unique insight into the world of hospitality. We champion restaurants, bars, wineries, and other venues around the world, helping them attract guests, manage capacity, improve operations and maximize revenue.
 
Every employee at OpenTable has a tangible impact on what we do and how we do it. Youll also join a global network that includes OpenTable and KAYAKs portfolio of travel brands including Swoodoo, checkfelix, momondo, Cheapflights, Mundi and HotelsCombined.
 
Hospitality is all about taking care of others, and it defines our culture. Youll work in a welcoming and inclusive environment, and get the benefits, flexibility, and support you need to succeed.
 
As an OpenTable Sales Development Representative, you will be an integral part of our sales team, responsible for generating new business opportunities and driving revenue growth. You will engage with potential restaurant clients, educate them about OpenTables reservation and guest management solutions, and ultimately secure appointments for our Account Executives. Your ability to effectively communicate the value of our products, build relationships, and meet sales targets will contribute to the overall success of our company.
 
In this role, you will: 


Qualify restaurant leads through the application of an ICP (Ideal Customer Profile), assessing their fit with OpenTables offerings, ensuring that only qualified prospects are passed on to Account Executives


Secure sales appointments with qualified leads for Account Executives


Conduct outbound prospecting activities, including cold calling, emailing, and social media outreach, to identify and engage potential restaurant clients.


Educate prospects about OpenTables products, services, and features, highlighting the value they bring to restaurant operations and customer experiences.


Collaborate with Account Executives to strategize on target accounts, share insights, and align efforts for successful conversions.


Build and maintain strong relationships with restaurant owners, managers, and decision-makers, fostering trust and credibility as a knowledgeable resource.


Use customer relationship management software (i.e Salesforce) to track prospect activity, update information, and maintain accurate records of interactions and opportunities.


Stay informed about industry trends, competitive landscape, and restaurant market conditions to identify new opportunities and challenges.


Attend sales meetings, training sessions, and professional development activities to enhance product knowledge, sales skills, and industry expertise.


 
Please apply if: 


3-5 year experience in sales development, business development, or a similar role, preferably in the hospitality, technology, or SaaS industry.


Proven track record of success in generating leads, qualifying prospects, and meeting or exceeding sales targets and quotas.


Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to build rapport, listen attentively, and influence potential clients.


Proficiency in delivering a compelling basic sales pitch and conducting basic selling techniques to effectively engage potential clients and generate interest.


Strong presentation and negotiation abilities, with the capacity to tailor messaging to specific restaurant needs and struggles.


Self-motivated, with a proactive approach to lead generation and pipeline management.


Ability to work collaboratively in a team-oriented environment, collaborating with Account Executives and cross-functional teams.


Familiarity with CRM software (e.g., Salesforce) and other sales productivity tools (e.g, Groove).


Understanding of the restaurant industry and familiarity with OpenTables platform and solutions is advantageous.


Bachelors degree in business, marketing, or a related field is preferred.
