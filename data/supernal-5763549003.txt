What we do:
The Quality Assurance Manager, New Product Development (NPD) will play a pivotal role in ensuring the highest standards of quality, safety, and regulatory compliance of Supernal products and processes. This role reports directly to the Sr. Manager of Quality and will work in close collaboration with cross-functional teams, including engineering, safety, supply chain, manufacturing, legal, and policy.
This position will be required to work on-site 5 days a week.
What you can do:

Quality Strategy: Develop and implement a rigorous quality strategy for NPD projects, meeting or exceeding industry standard methodologies and regulatory requirements
Quality and Safety Promotion: Promote a culture of continuous improvement, emphasizing the importance of quality and safety in all aspects of aerospace operations
New Product Development: Develop and implement a quality focused strategy to NPD using a phased planning process with specific results that are established, supervised, and tracked to closure, while highlighting and mitigating risks as they are identified
Inspection and Testing: Manage and mentor a team of quality engineers and inspectors in the development, implementation, and operation of quality processes for product and process designdevelopmentvalidation and part approval
Nonconformance Tracking: Responsible for the nonconformance tracking system including the disposition and control of nonconforming material
Corrective Action Tracking: Responsible for the investigation and analysis of root causes associated with quality issues and non-conformances as well as implementation corrective and preventative actions
Records Control: Responsible for the control of quality records including identification, storage, protection, retrieval, and retention
Performance Monitoring, Measuring, and Reporting: Responsible for the preparation of reports on quality performance, key performance indicators, and improvement initiatives and presentation to Sr. Manager of Quality
Equipment Control: Responsible for the calibration and control of all inspection, measuring, and test equipment used in determining conformity of product to design
Training Program: Responsible for the QMS Training Program including content development, schedule preparation, delivery, and effectiveness verification
Quality Management System (QMS): Support the development, implementation, and maintenance of Supernals QMS policies, manuals, procedures, work instructions, and forms to ensure compliance with all applicable regulatory requirements and industry standards, such as FAA 14 Code of Federal Regulation Part 21.137 and Aerospace Standard 9100
Audit Program: Support the execution of internal audits to evaluate compliance, performance, and effectiveness of the QMS
Continuous Improvement: Support the continual improvement of the suitability, adequacy, and effectiveness of the QMS via implementation of best practices and lessons learned, monitoring and measuring activities, audits, and outputs from management review meetings
QMS Digital Tools: Support the evaluation and implementation of digital tools necessary to operate and measure the performance of the QMS
Supplier Control: Support the approval of suppliers, contract reviews, and resolution of supplier quality issues
May include 25 of domestic and international travel
Other duties as assigned
