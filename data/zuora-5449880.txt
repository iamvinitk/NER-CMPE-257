Over the past 15 years, we have seen a shift in the focus of business models across every industry  from selling physical products via one-time transactions to monetizing services via ongoing customer (aka subscriber) relationships. This is the Subscription Economy a phrase coined by our CEO, Tien Tzuo, he even wrote the book on it: Subscribed.
Companies have realized that the path to growth going forward is to establish direct, digital relationships with their customers, and monetize these relationships through an ever growing set of digital services. 
Our vision is simple: we call it The World Subscribed. Its the idea that one day every company will join the Subscription Economy -- a 1.5 Trillion opportunity by 2025 according to UBS.
Our mission: to power the worlds best companies to win in the Subscription Economy.
Zuora Company 
Newly acquired by Zuora, Zephrs platform powers nearly eight billion requests a month and dynamic, personalized subscriber experiences. When combined with data from Zuoras Billing, Collect, and Revenue systems  the resulting solution will empower companies across industries, especially media, to nurture and monetize their subscriber relationships by better understanding their behaviors, experimenting with the right digital offerings, and optimizing their digital experiences.
The Team  Role
The Digital Experience and Security (DES) team focuses on the entire engagement lifecycle from Zuora prospect to Zuora customer. We build foundational frameworks aimed at providing our customers with a consistent, frictionless and ultimately delightful journey throughout their entire interaction with Zuora. 
Functional areas of responsibility include Authentication and Data Access Control, Tenant Management (Tenant Lifecycle, Features and Entitlements, Configuration Management), Test Environments, and general Self Service capabilities.
As a Software Engineer, you will be working with a group of talented engineers within Zuoras greater platform organization, on building the foundational infrastructure and functionality that facilitates the entire customer interactions lifecycle with Zuora (Digital Experience).
This includes, but is not limited to, authentication systems, audit trails, internal data intensive workflows, tenant management and self service initiatives.
Our services are built using a modern cloud-based tech stack and run on AWS.
