Mission Summary
Motional is actively looking for a Senior Hardware Design Engineer to join the team and focus on our next generation high performance computing platforms. Its an exciting time in the autonomous space and were looking to expand our engineering team onsite in the Silicon Valley office.
What youll be doing: 

Work cross-functionally to define and deliver High Performance Automotive grade ElectricalElectronic Hardware Platforms to support Motionals business objectives.
Develop new Hardware concepts and technologies to enable aggressive priceperformance automotive computer products.
DevelopExecute Hardware Design documentation, Schematics, Code reviews and Test Plans.
Work collaboratively, both with internal and external partners, to execute PCB Layout, SignalPower Integrity, EMC, Test Engineering, Mechanical and Thermal Engineering
Work closely with firmwarediagnostic engineering to ensure effective bring-up and functional test.
Support Manufacturing, Quality and Reliability teams to enable successful prototyping, NPI and Sustaining.
Some international travel may be required during critical project phases.

What were looking for: 

BSMS (MS preferred) in ElectricalElectronics Engineering or equivalent experience. 
5-10 years of experience with HW development of High Performance ComputeNetworking.
Proven competence in high speed board design, simulation and validation techniques.
Strong documentation, presentation and collaboration skills.
Ability to work through ambiguity to define an executable path.
Aptitude for learning in a fast paced environment.
Advanced knowledge of hardware engineering principles including requirements gathering, architecturedesign, source control management, build processes, reviews, testing methods. 
Solid foundational knowledge and hands-on experience using system communication protocols such as PCIe, I2C, UART, SPI and Ethernet.
Experience with computing architectures such as FPGAs, GPUs, MCUs, SoCs and their associated hardware computing platforms.
Lab working experience with Logic Analyzers, Scopes, VNAs.
The ability to coach and mentor other engineers.
Customer focus, strong collaboration skills, and the ability to pull engineers together across teams to accomplish objectives.
The ability to collaborate with and manage vendors to ensure quality and timely delivery.
The ability to drive and manage complex projects according to program schedules within a fast-paced, dynamic environment.
Comfortable working within Google Docs, Atlassian and Safety frameworks.
Experience with Polarion, JIRA, and Quality Management Systems.

Bonus Points (not required): 

Experience with Cadence HW Design Tools.
Automotive Design Experience including Power, GMSL, Automotive Ethernet, CAN.
2.5D3D Simulation Experience.
Python (or equivalent) Test Script development.
