Come join Unity as a Senior SEC Reporting and Technical Accountant! This role will join the SEC Reporting and technical accounting team responsible for the accounting and reporting of all external reporting processes, including 10-Qs and 10-Ks, as well earnings releases, and proxy statement filings. This position is an excellent opportunity to join an exciting and passionate team at a fast-growing company, while developing strong working relationships with cross-functional partners and various levels of management.
What youll be doing 

Participate in the preparation of all external financial reporting, including Forms 10-QK, earnings releases, proxy statement, and other required SEC filings.
Assist in preparation of the balance sheet, income statement, statement of cash flows, statements of stockholders equity, earnings per share, GAAP checklist, and other financial disclosure requirements with such filings.
Perform global accounting for share-based compensation awards, including RSUs, stock options, and ESPP by performing the valuation of compensation granted, calculation of quarterly expense, and updating quarterly memos.
Coordinate the information gathering process across the organization including Stock Admin, Technical Accounting, FPA, legal, tax, treasury, and other internal business partners.
Review XBRL interactive data files for all Form 10-Q and 10-K filings in accordance with the SEC filing requirements.
Assist in development and maintenance of policies and procedures related to SEC Reporting and share-based compensation, and ensure compliance with SOX and internal controls.

What were looking for 

Certified Public Accountant with public accounting andor public company experience
SEC Reporting and technical accounting background with a knowledge of US GAAP and SEC reporting standards
Experience with SEC Reporting filings and writing technical accounting memos
Excellent written and verbal communication skills
Ability to work in a dynamic, fast paced environment

You might also have

Workiva and Workday Financials experience
Experience working with a geographically dispersed team
Experience working with technology or SaaS companies
