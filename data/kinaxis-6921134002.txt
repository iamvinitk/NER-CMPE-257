
About Kinaxis

What you will do
As a key contributor to the success of our sales objectives, you provide market domain expertise and product knowledge during all pre-sales engagements. You will work in tandem with the sales organization focusing on all activities that will ensure target sales achievement and total customer satisfaction

Assist in identifying the customers needs and key stakeholders in the purchase decision.
Effectively deliver a standard product demo while emphasizing elements that are most aligned with the urgent needs of the customer.
Construct and deliver custom demos, utilizing customer provided data that addresses the customers urgent needs. This may involve the configuration of unique RapidResponse resources (workbooks, task flows, scorecards, etc.).
Consistently follow the sales process which includes ensuring that key deliverables are delivered.
Proactively provide effective feedback to product management on product gaps and improvement areas.
Where necessary create and deliver supplementary presentation materials in support of customer specific demonstrations.
Produce sales transition documentation for Professional Services, and participate in Sales to Services transition meetings to ensure proper handoff of newly won accounts to the deployment team.

What we are looking for

7 years of relevant experience and domain expertise in one or more of the key industries served (Automotive, High-Tech, Industrial, PharmaLifesciences, Aerospace and Defense, CPG, Retail)
Prefer previous experience in a pre-sales position within the supply chain management software industry
Ability to work independently within a team environment;
Excellent communication, presentation , facilitation, and time management skills
Adaptability and strong initiative required for success in this role
Ability to manage multiple priorities and perform well in a fast paced environment
Ability and willingness to travel within your designated region.
