We have an exciting Job Opportunity for a Hardware Technician to join our Team in Ogden Utah, Mechanicsburg PA and Oklahoma City OK
Job Summary
Hardware Technician to work onsite . The Technicians role is to support and maintain in-house technology equipment and IT assets. This includes installing, configuring, diagnosing, repairing, and upgrading all organizational hardware and equipment while insuring its optimal performance.
 Role and Responsibilities  This task requires Secret access to work on both NIPRNet and SIPRNet environments. It requires Hardware Technician at this location, see 6.3.5 for minimum qualifications. FTEs performing this task shall be able to lift boxes weighing up to 75 lbs. and shall be able to complete tasks that require moderate walking, standing, bending over, stooping, crawling, kneeling, climbing, and reaching.
 FTEs performing this task shall be able to distinguish between shades of colors visually and to see clearly up close to work with small wires (e.g., 132).  Installation, Integration, and Configuration of IT hardware (including server, storage, and networkcommunications) in designated facilities.  Cabling  De-Installation. The contractor shall perform the following de-installation tasks for IT hardware and associated components, including server, storage, and networkcommunications, in designated facilities  Administrative, Policy, and Documentation Requirements.
