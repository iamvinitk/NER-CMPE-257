As a leader on the Information Security team, you will help us achieve this mission by actively working to protect our staff, company, and the larger crypto communities we engage with.

This role reports directly to the Head of Information Security.

Partner with Head of Security to plan security engineering and operations strategy
Collaborate cross-functionally, including with engineering, legal, product, and IT teams, to build and strengthen information security and privacy across our service and infrastructure

Build and lead a team of security engineers and security professionals
Ensure all security services and processes are working at peak effectiveness
Drive, design, and implement innovative services to improve our public cloud security infrastructure
Relentlessly focus on prioritizing, automating, and scaling our security capabilities
