About the team
At Stripe, were constantly working on ways to optimize our benefits programs and improve the experience for our users. We are looking for a Benefits Specialist to join our International Benefits Team to focus on designing, scaling, and administering Stripes benefits programs as well as Stripes time off and leave administration in EMEA and beyond.
What youll do
In this role, youll also help the Benefits team maintain best-in-class benefits support to employees. Your work will be instrumental to Stripe achieving its mission by supporting every Stripe to take care of themselves and their loved ones, to build their best self, and to do their best work.
Responsibilities

 Administer employee benefit programs such as health and risk insurance, retirement plans, leave of absence, and disability programs 
Assist with benefits and leave of absence program design and implementation of new and existing programs
Assist with global benefits programs in EMEA
Help to maintain program renewals, vendor changes and new program expansion
Improve employee-facing benefits communications 
Provide a positive user experience as you answer questions and address issues for employees
Manage the completion of EMEA-specific compliance items
Work closely with partner teams from HR, Payroll, Legal, Mobility, Systems and People Operations to manage programs
Work closely with Stripes global benefits broker and local brokers in each country 
Oversee ongoing maintenance and enhancements of Workday Benefits and Time off modules
Participate in benefits surveys and benchmarking exercises in order to provide recommendations to maintain competitive benefits programs 
Make data based recommendations on plan provisions and design to ensure we offer cost effective and competitive benefits programs
Assist with benefits integrations for mergers and acquisitions
Help maintain the fidelity of our People data through auditing and entering data used during the employee lifecycle and helping our HRIS in maintaining job profiles, job families, and comp codes
Support and partner across the People team to process changes in Workday when Stripes are hired, change managers, cost centers, teams or locations, ensuring a high degree of accuracy
Assist all Stripes by responding to employment verifications and other people-related data requests
Create and maintain employee files, track annual trainings, review candidate documentation and manage background checks
Proactively seek and take initiative for process improvements and automation to assist in scaling the People Ops team
