Okta is searching for a SOX Program Senior Manager to join its internal audit team and manage Oktas global SOX program. The ideal candidate is a self-motivated team leader capable of leading with confidence who possesses strong organization and project management skills. Reporting to the Senior Director of Internal Audit, key operational responsibilities include:

Oversee Oktas global SOX program, including coordination with Internal Audit leadership, internal business process and system owners, and external auditors
Direct the planning, scheduling, and execution of the annual SOX program to determine whether internal controls over financial reporting are designed and operating effectively
Lead the annual SOX program with confidence and minimal supervision
Proactively identify business changes that have an impact on the design or operating effectiveness of controls.
Develop collaborative relationships with business and IT stakeholders
Identify and direct opportunities to leverage technology in order to rationalize or automate control activities
Direct managements annual segregation of duties analysis and fraud risk assessment
Oversee the quarterly control certification process

Required Qualifications:

BABS degree in accounting, finance or similar discipline
At least 8 years experience in Big Four andor internal audit with a publicly traded company
Strong project management skills with proven ability to drive projects to timely completion while managing multiple competing priorities
Demonstrated experience directing resources in the execution of evaluating SOX processes and controls across both business processes and IT general controls
Deep knowledge of risks across business processes and technology, including experience evaluating and reviewing controls across both domains
Experience operating independently while collaborating with a global team
Strong analytical skills and high standards of accuracy and attention to detail
Strong English language proficiency (verbal and written)
You embrace and advocate the Okta culture

 Preferred Qualifications:

CPA, CA, CIA, andor CISA certifications
Knowledge of the COSO framework
Auditboard experience

The ideal candidate will work as part of a high-performance culture built around autonomy and responsibility.
