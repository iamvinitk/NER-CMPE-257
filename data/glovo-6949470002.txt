YOUR MISSION
Our HR Operations Analyst will support the payroll team in the monthly payroll procedures to best fit our different demands making sure that we are meeting local laws and regulations over Europe in countries where we currently have activities.
THE JOURNEY


Handle payroll administration: collect all payroll-related information from managers  prepare, generate, check and submit payroll figures on a monthly basis


Ensure any required adjustments are reflected in the payroll


Taxes: help our employees with any tax-related questions and advise them on what could work better for them


Payroll coordination  be the intermediary between HR, Finance, Legal and the Outsourced Payroll Provider to ensure there is clear communication between departments for efficient payroll service


Sick Leaves  make sure all our employees provide the locally required documentation and make sure our payroll provider is informed


Explain the payroll and benefits for all new hires and current employees


Administer changes to salary, benefits, promotions, bonus, etc.


Support the rest of the People team or any other department that needs it


Help keep our colleagues happy!


Actively collaborate with your colleagues and learn from each other in a supportive environment that allows you to grow, develop and make a difference


WHAT YOU WILL BRING TO THE RIDE


At least 1 year of experience working in payroll


Strong knowledge of  payroll, Social Security, tax regulation and administration


Excellent data management skills - strong Excel and database management skills


Strong communication skills (both verbal and written) in English and the local language


Proactive, hands-on, and eager to get your hands dirty!


Detailed oriented  you are a freak when it comes to details


Always looking for ways to improve the process and optimizing coordination between colleagues


An empathetic, inclusive and curious attitude
