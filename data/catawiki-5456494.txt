We are looking for a Finance Business Partner to partner and support Central Functions including Tech whilst also being responsible for the key FPA activities across Catawiki. Because we are growing fast, the tasks and responsibilities of this role may change over time and you will be excited to take on new challenges and responsibilities as they occur.

What youll do
Finance business partner to the CPO, CTO, CFO, CEO and their teams:

Drive performance through the budgeting and forecasting of key metrics and drivers, and translation into related financial costs and KPIs
Cooperate with these teams to set their respective financial and KPI targets, reporting and sharing actual performance, providing insight into variances and highlighting future implications
Attendance at cost centre review meetings, reviewing cost base (FTE and costs) on a monthly basis and discuss any variances to determine the impact on the future PL
Attendance at relevant business team meetings, sharing insight into performance and being the wider finance representative
Act as financial advisor to these teams, and to be integral to commercial decision-making and driving improved performance
Become closely involved in, and provide financial support and analysis in relation to, commercial contract negotiations and partnerships
Work closely with engineering and product teams to provide support on key projects and strategic pillars initiatives e.g. ISS (Integrated Shipping Service), Demand initiatives
Drive continuous improvement with your partners by providing support on business initiatives and ad hoc projects e.g. CX models regarding First Time Right, Allocation keys, Requests for proposals (RFPs) and the FTE process

Besides this, you will support and lead the overall FPA activities for Catawiki including but not limited to:

Working with the Commercial Finance Director to drive and lead the quarterly forecasting and annual budget cycles;
Monthly and quarterly business performance reviews, including monthly and quarterly board reporting;
Further improving and aligning Catawiki reporting and dashboards (e.g. FTE and forecast modelling)
Support in business planning (including weekly forecasting, quarterly detailed planning) by providing financial analysis and modelling.
