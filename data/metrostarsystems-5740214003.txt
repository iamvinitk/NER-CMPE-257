As a Network Engineer, youll be responsible for designing, implementing, and managing complex network infrastructure to ensure the security, scalability, and efficiency of our government clients cloud-based networks, with the goal of making an impact across the federal government.

What youll do: 

Collaborate with government program stakeholders to design and architect cloud-based network solutions that align with program requirements and objectives.
.Evaluate and recommend network technologies, tools, and services to enhance performance and security.
Lead the deployment and configuration of cloud-based network solutions.
Implement robust security measures, access controls, and encryption protocols to protect sensitive government data.
Ensure compliance with government regulations and security standards, such as FISMA, NIST, and FedRAMP.
Monitor network performance, identify bottlenecks, and implement optimizations to maximize network efficiency and reliability.
Provide expert-level support for network-related incidents and outages, conducting root cause analysis and implementing corrective actions promptly.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to resolve complex issues.
Maintain comprehensive network documentation, including network diagrams, configurations, and change management records.
Generate regular reports on network performance and security compliance for program stakeholders.

What youll need to succeed: 

At least eight years of experience as a Network Engineer with a focus on cloud-based network solutions.
Certification in relevant network technologies, such as Cisco CCNACCNP, AWS Certified Solutions Architect, or equivalent.
Strong knowledge of cloud platforms (e.g., AWS, Azure, Google Cloud) and their networking services.
Familiarity with government security regulations and compliance standards (e.g., FISMA, NIST, FedRAMP).
Exceptional problem-solving and troubleshooting skills.
Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
Ability to work independently and lead complex network projects.
Exposure to DevOps practices and tools.
Active TSSCI clearance with CI poly preferred or active TSSCI with ability to pass a CI poly.
