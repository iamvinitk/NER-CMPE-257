Harmonia Holdings Group, LLC is an award-winning minority and female owned federal government contractor committed to providing innovative, high-performing solutions to our government clients and focused on fostering a workplace that encourages growth, initiative, creativity, and employee satisfaction.  We are looking for AWS Cloud Architect  who will be responsible for overseeing and informing the development of the enterprise cloud computing system, including cloud application designs, cloud approval plans, and cloud storage and management systems. The ideal candidate will have a minimum of 7 years of experience in designing, implementing, or engineering production-scale applications in regulated, secure environments, as well as a minimum of 5 years of experience developing and delivering Cloud IaaS strategies and using industry leading platforms, including Amazon Web Services (AWS) and VMWare, or Microsoft Azure. In addition, the ideal candidate must possess a Cloud Professional Certification, such as the AWS Associate Solutions Architect. This role is Hybrid at the moment.
Essential Job Functions:

Conduct Analysis of Alternatives (AoA) and provide guidance on what Cloud provider or combination of providers will be best suited for the environment.
Propose cloud adoption plans, cloud application design, and cloud management and monitoring.
Ability to work and help with Cloud migration, Data Security and deployment strategy in cloud environments.
Experienced in implementing and overseeing cloud computing strategy, and delivery of cloud solutions.
Assist with Application Rationalization.
Demonstrated understanding of IT infrastructure tools, processes, and practices, including Cloud resource provisions, deployment, microservices, and DevOps is a BIG PLUS.
Ability to work independently within a complex environment to evaluate issues and address any questions from internal and external shareholders regarding Cloud ImplementationMigration.
Review security documentation, including ATO FedRAMP packages, to understand the responsibilities of the tenant users, and associated risks for the organization. 

Minimum Required Qualifications:

TSSCI Clearance required.
Requires a BABS or equivalent experience and 7 years of prior relevant experience.
Cloud Professional Certification, such as the AWS Associate Solutions Architect

 Experience, Skills and Abilities:

The successful candidate will have related multiple years of hands-on experience of supporting  AWS, and other Cloud implementations. 
Experienced in cloud deployment, operations, maintenance, and troubleshooting.
Professional Cloud Certifications such as Azure, AWS, or Google Cloud are desired.
