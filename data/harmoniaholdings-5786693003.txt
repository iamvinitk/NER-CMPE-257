Harmonia Holdings Group, LLC is an award-winning minority and female owned federal government contractor committed to providing innovative, high-performing solutions to our government clients and focused on fostering a workplace that encourages growth, initiative, creativity, and employee satisfaction.  We have an exciting opportunity for a SharePoint Administrator to join our team. The ideal SharePoint Administrator will have experience in the system administration for the On-Prem SharePoint Servers.
Essential Job Functions: 

Maintain and administer Microsoft SharePoint 2010, 2013 and 2016 Servers, including daily monitoring, troubleshooting, patching and performance analysis.
Install and configure SharePoint software and related components at the server level, including SQL Server, web server and application server.
Manage the functionality of features such as Search, view the Audit Log Reports, work with site collection policies, manage the site collection caching, and activate or deactivate Site Collection features.
Participate in planning and execution of tasks related to the evaluation of new SharePoint based initiatives (upgraded versions, patching, and integration with additional Enterprise Systems).
Perform typical system administrative activities such as backup, restore, and issue resolution.
Provide guidance and recommendations for enterprise deployments of SharePoint Able to understand, develop, and expert in PowerShell commands.
Maintain current industry knowledge of development concepts, best practices, and procedures for SharePoint solutions.
Ensures compliance with policies and procedures including STIG requirements.
Troubleshooting, customer support and tickets, incident handling for SharePoint sites

Minimum Required Qualifications: 

Bachelors degree in computer science or related degree preferred.
Due to customer requirements, US Citizenship is required.
Active Secret Clearance. Top secret Preferred.
5 Years of Hands-on experience as System Administrator for maintaining SharePoint 2010 2013 2016  SharePoint Online, MS SharePoint Server, SharePoint SME, and disaster recovery fluency for SharePoint.
Experience with addressing server upgrades, patching, backups, licensing.
Strong experience with modern SharePoint architecture and implementation.
Solid experience in Troubleshooting, Help desk support and SharePoint housekeeping.

Knowledge, Skills and Abilities: 

Excellent written and oral communication skills.
Possess excellent problem-solving and organizational skills and be self-motivated.
Proven ability to manage several tasks simultaneously.
Excellent attention to detail.
Strong customer serviceclient facing skills

Preferred skills

Experience with SharePoint onlinehybrid setup.
Knowledge of O365 platform
Ability to design and develop SharePoint sites.

Other:  

Hybrid Position, travel to the client (Washington DC) or Harmonia office (Mclean)
