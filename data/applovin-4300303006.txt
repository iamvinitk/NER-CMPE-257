Our in-house creative team is looking for a Video Producer and motion designer in North America. You will report into the Creative Production Manager, Video. You would be joining a team that is responsible for creating a variety of videos including filming, editing and motion graphics across the wider brand teams. This is a great opportunity for a creative individual who is interested in all things motion and wants to grow in a fast-paced, highly collaborative and international work environment. 
This is a completely remote position that does require travel to corporate HQ in the Bay Area, various event locations throughout North America, Europe, and Asia based on projects.
To be considered for this position, you must include your resume and portfolio.

Responsibilities:

Collaborate with cross-functional teams in different time zones to conceptualize, plan, and execute video projects that align with the companys brands and messaging.
Take leadership and manage the end-to-end video production process, from storyboarding to filming, post-production, editing, sound and final delivery
Utilize your knowledge of camera, composition and lighting techniques to ensure optimal video quality both in studio and on location
Operate a variety of video equipment, including cameras, audio recording devices, and lighting setups
Edit and enhance video content. This includes color correction, audio balancing and adding motion graphics.
Collaborate with stakeholders, content and design teams to create concepts and bring them to life with motion graphics.
Be able to wear different hats as a video all rounder and independently manage video production and motion graphics projects
Keep up with industry trends in motion graphics, editing and filming and share new creative ideas
Be able to travel and film our in-house events at various locations around the globe.

Basic Qualifications:

Bachelors or Masters degree in Film Production, Communications, Media, or a related field.
4 years of experience in a video all rounder position. Experience in corporate tech or agency is a plus.
Strong portfolio showcasing diverse video projects that demonstrate proficiency in filming, lighting, editing, and animation.
Proficiency in Adobe creative suite and Davinci resolve
Strong storytelling abilities, with a keen eye for detail and composition.
Ability to juggle multiple projects and meet deadlines.
Ability to work independently in a fast-paced, remote work environment.
