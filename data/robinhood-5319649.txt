Robinhood is looking for a Communications manager to support our engineering organization. This role is a blend of internal communications, tech PR, and content development for multiple audiences. This person will partner with business leaders to own the engineering organizations communication strategy to ensure an engaged, informed, and aligned workforce. This is a complex organization with a diverse audience, including platform teams, business teams, and functional areas. You will drive engineerings editorial calendar and manage all internal communication content, events, and channels. This person will also manage content for external audiences across the blog, conferences, tech PR coverage, and more. The ideal candidate has excellent writing and editing skills, good business judgment, strong intuition, and experience managing strategic communications and employee engagement in a fast-moving environment.
The role is located in the office location(s) listed on this job description which will align with our in-office working environment. Please connect with your recruiter for more information regarding our in-office philosophy and expectations.
What youll do

Develop clear narratives and message maps across engineering, internal and external
Drive communication channels and content calendaring for the team, including new channels and events
Provide executive communications support and communications counsel, including speaker prep and coaching
Coordinate and manage organizational programs and events, including the organizations All Hands and Engineering Manager forums
Support, write, and edit communications content, leadership messages, and technical updates during complex, ambiguous, and fast-moving situations
Lead agency on tech PR efforts, including tech media coverage, awards, and conferences.
Mine for story ideas and produce content related to elevating Robinhoods tech brand across multiple internal and external audiences.
Develop a technical branding strategy to showcase and evangelize complex, fascinating and cutting edge engineering work and culture with the broader tech and fin tech industry.
Embed into the engineering org culture to better influence, communicate and articulate decisions and rationale to engineers.
Develop a strong pulse for engineering sentiment across all eng groups, and a clear understanding of how day to day work maps back to company priorities. 

What you bring

7 years of experience in communications, PR, employee experience, program management, or similar areas of expertise
Exceptional writing, editing, and presentation skills
Experience building relationships with diverse stakeholders and company leaders
Experience managing programs, events, and organizational changes
Experience in a tech-first company or engineering domain
Experience building robust communications programs and plans
Eagerness and aptitude to learn quickly and explain broad subject matter effectively
Expert use of information and collaboration apps such as Slack, Atlassian, Coda, Airtable, and more
Track record of cultivating community in work, school, or volunteer settings
