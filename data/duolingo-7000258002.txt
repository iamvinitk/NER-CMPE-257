As Corporate Counsel, MA and Securities you will have primary responsibility for legal oversight of our public company reporting and mergers and acquisitions activities. You will work closely with teams throughout the company, including finance and corporate development, to ensure compliance with securities laws and to support the companys growth through strategic acquisitions and investments. You will also provide support on corporate governance and equity related matters and other areas that might interest you.
You Will...

Working with the finance team, coordinate and handle the companys public reporting obligations, including 10-Ks, 10-Qs, and 8-Ks, ensuring compliance with SEC regulations.
Collaborate with investor relations and external reporting to prepare and review financial disclosures, earnings releases and scripts to ensure accuracy and transparency.
Stay up-to-date with evolving securities laws and regulations and provide guidance to internal partners on compliance and disclosure matters.
Assist in the preparation of the annual proxy statement and in preparing for and conducting annual shareholder meetings.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to complete MA transactions, including due diligence, negotiations, and integration planning.
Draft, review, and negotiate transaction documents, including letters of intent, purchase agreements, and ancillary agreements.
Provide legal support and advice to senior leadership during MA processes.
Provide support as needed on corporate governance, equity compensation plan and corporate subsidiary maintenance matters.

You Have...

5 years of relevant legal experience, with a strong and proven background in public securities reporting, mergers and acquisitions and strategic investments.
Strong negotiation, drafting, and analytical skills.
Strong advocacy and interpersonal skills, both written and verbal. Ability to articulate complicated issues and represent Duolingos interests persuasively to various parties..
Outstanding problem-solving skills, good judgment, and the ability to make well-informed decisions in a dynamic and fast-paced environment.

Requirements: 

Juris Doctor (JD) degree from an accredited law school and active membership in a state bar association.

Exceptional Candidates Will Have...

Experience in-house.
Experience with consumer facing technology companies, particularly those with a freemium or subscription model.
Experience in both domestic and international transactions.
