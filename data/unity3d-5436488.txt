The opportunity
We are seeking a skilled and experienced GTM (Go-To-Market) Technologies Program Manager to join our dynamic team. As the GTM Technologies Program Manager, you will be responsible for coordinating the successful implementation, integration, and optimization of key technologies, including Salesforce, Commerce, and Billing. Your role will be pivotal in driving our go-to-market strategy by ensuring seamless execution and effective utilization of these systems. This is an exciting and rewarding opportunity for an individual with a solid technical background, outstanding project management skills, and a passion for driving business growth.
What youll being doing

Lead end-to-end project management for GTM technology initiatives, including planning, coordination, execution, and monitoring of projects.
Identify and mitigate program risks, resolve issues, and drive effective decision-making to keep programs on track.
Collaborate with key customers to capture requirements, understand business needs, and align GTM technology solutions accordingly.
Define and track key program performance indicators (critical metrics) to measure the success and efficiency of GTM technology programs.
Identify areas for program operating model improvement, and proactively recommend enhancements and optimizations.

What We Are Looking For:

Bachelors degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Business Administration, or a related field. A masters degree is a plus.
Demonstrable experience as a program manager or project manager, specifically in implementing and managing GTM technologies like Salesforce, Commerce, and Billing.
Strong understanding of GTM strategies, sales processes, and customer lifecycle management.
Proficient in programproject management methodologies, tools, and techniques.
Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to establish relationships and collaborate with complementary teams.

You might also have:

Proven analytical and problem-solving abilities, with a keen attention to detail.
Prior experience in e-commerce and billing systems implementation is a plus.
Ability to adapt to a fast-paced and multifaceted work environment.

Join our team and take on this exciting opportunity to craft the implementation and optimization of GTM technologies that drive our business growth. Apply today and become an integral part of our success!
Life at Unity
Unity (NYSE: U) is the worlds leading platform for creating and operating real-time 3D (RT3D) content. Creators, ranging from game developers to artists, architects, automotive designers, filmmakers, and others, use Unity to make their imaginations come to life. Unity is the foundation upon which the worlds most powerful digital content is created. Specifically, Unitys platform provides a comprehensive set of software solutions to create, run and monetize interactive, real-time 2D and 3D content for mobile phones, tablets, PCs, consoles, and augmented and virtual reality devices.
In the fourth quarter of 2022, more than 70 of the top 1000 mobile games were made with Unity as derived from a blended number of the top 1000 games in the Google Play Store and Apple App Store.
