The Infrastructure Security team is seeking an experienced Infrastructure Security Engineer to help keep our million users safe from real world threats. You will build large-scale, distributed security solutions to improve our overall security posture. We are looking for a candidate with a passion for security and innovation, who will research and develop new solutions to protect our users.
What youll do:

Design, implement, and maintain mission-critical software that will help us meet our ambitious security, scalability and reliability goals
Interface with other partner teams, and take a leadership role in driving our security strategy
Conduct design and code reviews
Interact directly with the security community regarding vulnerabilities and threats
Code using primarily one of these languages: Golang, Python, Java

What were looking for:

5 years of experience in Infrastructure Security covering network, data, systems and public cloud environments
A strong command of Java, Python, or Golang
Enthusiasm for the constant fight to ensure security and privacy
Understanding of large scale AWS security and multi account architecture
Meaningful experience in some of the following areas:

Infrastructure provisioning tools (Terraform, Puppet, Chef, etc.)
Operating System internals (Linux strongly preferred) 
AWS, GCP or other public cloud infrastructure
Authentication, authorization and directory protocols
Networking protocols
