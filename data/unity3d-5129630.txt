The opportunity
Virtual and Augmented Reality are colliding and now we have AR on phones, passthrough cameras
on VR headsets and headsets with transparent displays. How do creators build experiences for this diverse range of devices? Thats what were figuring out; the XR future is coming, we intend to be there, and if you have ideas you should come and join us!
Unitys XR Features team builds the frameworks that let developers rapidly iterate on XR experiences and deploy them to multiple XR platforms. We create the APIs and workflows to let creators bring their ideas to XR as easily as possible.
As a software engineer on the XR Features team you will be making the tools that enable creators to ship immersive experiences to all the XR platforms that Unity supports, from smartphone AR, VR  AR head mounted displays, and as yet unannounced hardware. Youll be working in the technology stack that reaches from C platform APIs like OpenXR up to the C interfaces presented to Unity developers. Youll also work with designers, product managers and other fields to build the best developer experience we can.
XR is a growing team within Unity and youll have opportunity to shape the future of this exciting area and learn and grow along the way.
What youll be doing 

Working with our foundations team and platform providers to let developers write once and ship anywhere.
Defining how developers will interact with new hardware capabilities and software platforms.
Participating in an active development team. You will be reviewing code, improving the quality of our products and giving input to our future direction.

What were looking for

C and C development experience.
Experience or interest in XR application development.
Experience with real-time 3D graphics or game engines.

You might also have

Experience interacting with clientsusers of 3d software.
Experience with XR content creation.

Life at Unity
Unity (NYSE: U) is the worlds leading platform for creating and operating real-time 3D (RT3D) content. Creators, ranging from game developers to artists, architects, automotive designers, filmmakers, and others, use Unity to make their imaginations come to life. Unity is the foundation upon which the worlds most powerful digital content is created. Specifically, Unitys platform provides a comprehensive set of software solutions to create, run and monetize interactive, real-time 2D and 3D content for mobile phones, tablets, PCs, consoles, and augmented and virtual reality devices.
In the fourth quarter of 2022, more than 70 of the top 1000 mobile games were made with Unity as derived from a blended number of the top 1000 games in the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. In 2022, Made with Unity Applications had more than 4 billion downloads per month. For more information, please visit www.unity.com.
