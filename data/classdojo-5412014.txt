What youll do:
Join our team as a Senior Data Engineer - Privacy and play a pivotal role in advancing data privacy practices at ClassDojo. Youll collaborate closely with cross-functional teams, including legal, compliance, and data governance experts, to ensure rigorous data privacy controls for student, teacher, and parent data. Your expertise will contribute to the development and implementation of essential privacy measures, such as data masking, encryption, and access controls, guaranteeing the protection of sensitive educational data. Through your efforts, ClassDojo will achieve enhanced compliance with data privacy regulations, reducing the potential for data breaches and associated fines. Additionally, you will streamline privacy workflows, automate processes, and create data anonymization pipelines to facilitate efficient data handling for educational analysts and stakeholders. Your work will also bolster our data governance efforts by building a comprehensive data catalog and metadata management system, ensuring a well-documented and compliant data landscape in the realm of education technology. Join us to make a significant impact on data privacy and governance within the ClassDojo community.
