As an Program Manager Intern, you will be responsible for managing and optimizing operational processes and leveraging technology to significantly boost product adoption, retention and quality to craft exceptional customer experiences. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to streamline operations, improve efficiency and change manage new concepts and processes to a large network effectively. Youll thrive in this role if you enjoy forming relationships and influencing cross-functional teams, analyzing data to produce concrete recommendations, and delivering high quality projects on time.
The ideal candidate will have a customer-centric mindset, a general understanding of customer service, strong project management skills and adaptability to a fast paced environment. Top candidates will have; a natural desire to continually improve customer  agent experiences,  enormous user empathy and can maintain a calm demeanor in high stress situations. People describe our ideal teammateas innovative, creative, data-driven and customer focused. 
What youll be doing (ie. job duties):

Support program managers in planning, organizing and executing projects and initiatives. 
Collaborate with various cross-functional teams, including (but not limited to) Customer Success, Operations, Product, Tooling, Analytics, Knowledge Management, Training and Workforce Management
Lead deep dives of customer contacts, identify defects and opportunities, and feed those ideas back to operations, program, and product leaders for cross functional solutioning. 
Help streamline program operations and improve processes for agents that benefit our customers.
Assist in creating and maintaining project schedules, timelines and milestones for solutions youve identified. 
Monitor and track project progress, identifying and mitigating risks and issues, and prepare and deliver regular project status reports.
Act with urgency to ensure priority items are progressed through having a bias for action and focusing on the 20 of work that will get 80 of the impact
