About the job
As our RVP, Global Accounts you will be responsible for managing the largest multi-sourcing customers and CPaaS providers from a relationship, trading and commercial standpoint. You will manage senior relationships, negotiate complex bilateral commercial agreements, have deep CPaaS expertise, build two sided marketplaces, manage global teams and rely on data at the core of every decision. In addition, you will play a key role supporting regional sales leaders in growing their revenue and gross profit contributions to the company. Creative deal-making experience, professionalism and PL management are critical to your success. This position reports to the Vice President of Global Trading and Carrier Relations.
Responsibilities
In this role, youll:

Author and execute the strategy to maximize revenue and gross profit performance across a portfolio of accounts. This includes a bilateral motion with connectivity partners, defining and applying routing strategies and price to address customer needs.
Own first line communications and business relationships with telecom providers, CPaaS partners and global multi-sourcing customers.
Build an exceptional global team with superior business performance, relationship management and communication skills
Provide timely and accurate forecasting. Stay current on competitive trends. Act swiftly based on the ever changing market opportunities.
Functionally manage business performance, business development strategy and operational improvements across the global business
Partner and communicate effectively with sales, finance, legal, operations, product and engineering. Be a team player and a thought leader.
Drive business rigor through data, analytics, standardization and operational excellence.
Develop a scalable foundation for Twilio to be the highest quality, lowest cost and preferred partner across our top global markets
