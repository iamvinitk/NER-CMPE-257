ROLE SUMMARY:The Office Manager is responsible for facilities related matters to include ordering supplies, tidying the office, managing safety and security, partnering with vendors, sorting packages and mail, and event coordination. The Office Manager will work closely with the People Team and Facilities and serve as a point of contact for employees in the office.
RESPONSIBILITIES:

Manage the relationship, communication, and needs between Dollar Shave Club, property owners, and property brokers
Greeting guests and employees at the door, ensure visitors are connected with the appropriate employee point person, and maintain building security
Provide employees with office information, guide them around the office and serve as a point of contact for office related questions
Create badges for employees and ensure appropriate access is assigned
Collect and organize incoming mail and packages and monitor the facilities email account
Manage office vendors, such as landscaping, HVAC, etc. and schedule repairs for general office space and equipment as needed
Manage partnership and communication with parking vendors; manage parking enforcement in companys front lot
Keep office snacks and beverages stocked, monitor and order inventory for office supplies
Ensure the continual cleanliness of the office
Facilitate the relationship with our third-party janitorial cleaning service
Establish and implement office procedures and practices, conduct routine safety checks and ensure full safety compliance in the office
Own in-office event coordination and food catering orders
Consistently find innovative ways to improve the in-office experience for both new hires and current employees
Submit invoices for approval and work with finance to ensure accurate billing
