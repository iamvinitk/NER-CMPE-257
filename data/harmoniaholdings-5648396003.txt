Harmonia Holdings Group, LLC, has an exciting opportunity for a Key Personnel Recruiter to join our team.  The ideal Senior Key Personnel Recruiter will have experience managing and leading various recruitment efforts in support of proposal teams to secure and maintain mission critical IT Projects for the Federal Government.
This position is remote; however, ideal candidates will be within the Washington, D.C area.
Essential Job Functions

Work directly with proposal managers to establish the requirements for Key Personnel recruitment efforts
Utilize resources, such as LinkedIn, social media, Internal Project Teams and other innovative means to source and recruit highly qualified candidates for consideration to be added to proposals
Incumbent personnel recruitment and Incumbent personnel research
Be responsible for the creation and maintenance of the KP Matrix for each proposal to track and map qualified candidates
Review documentation provided by the Government to determine key technologies and certifications required (i.e. RFQs, RFPs, etc.)
Troubleshoot roadblocks and barriers and create a new action plan to secure qualified resumes
Work in a timely manner with proposal team to ensure resumes are submitted on-time
Responsible for formatting resumes, gathering required documentation including letters of commitments, and mapping resume requirements for proposal submissions
In certain cases, act as the subcontractor for additional resources for recruitment efforts with our partners

 Minimum Required Qualifications:

5 years of experience serving as a government technical recruiter.
3 years of experience as a Key Personnel Recruiter
Demonstrated experience leading recruitment efforts for proposal submissions
Working knowledge of SharePoint and MS Office tools
Proficient in LinkedIn recruiter
