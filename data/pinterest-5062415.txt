Pinterest is one of the fastest growing online ad platforms, and Advertiser Solutions Group (ASG) is building advertiser products by applying the ML technologies to improve performance and drive value for the advertisers. ASG particularly focuses on product innovation and improvement, and connects all the advertiser products across the entire advertiser lifecycle.  
What youll do:

Lead ads product development that involve end-to-end engineering in both frontend and backend and ML 
Work closely with Product on planning roadmap, set technical direction and deliver value
ASG builds models and systems to deliver an integrated, performant, and hassle-free product for the whole advertiser lifecycle.  

Examples of specific application areas and projects are:

Auto campaign optimization
Creatives optimization product
Targeting and audience modeling
Advertiser growth 
Guidance and Media Planning
Measurement Modeling
Ads Integrity, e.g., ads review
Signal modeling 



What were looking for:

10 years of industry experience, with 5 years tech leadership experience  
Rich experience in building Ads products by leveraging ML technologies
Experience in both designing and implementing large scale ML application
Strong execution skills in project management
Masters or PhD in Comp Sci or related fields
Understanding of an object-oriented programming language (Go, Java, C, Python) 
Generative AI experience is a plus, not required
