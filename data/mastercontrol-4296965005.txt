Release Train Engineer
Release Train Engineer (RTE) manages events, processes and assists the teams in delivering value. RTE communicates with stakeholders, escalates impediments, helps manage risk, and helps derive a release schedule.
Responsibilities

Facilitates Initiative Backlog readiness with PM and Engineering leadership.
Tracks the execution of Initiative objectives.
Facilitates regular synchronization events.
Helps manageidentify risks and dependencies across release train
Escalates and tracks impediments.
Plans and manages product development stages.
Collaborates with developers and software architects.
Reports product development progress to senior managers.
Ensures that products are delivered on time.
Oversees product deployment and integration.
Oversees product performance metrics and facilitates adjustments.
Documents processes and ensures compliance with industry standards.

Scope

This role operates at the Initiative level
This role works as a bridge between teams and leadership in execution of current initiatives and in backlog preparations for upcoming epics

Interaction

Scrum Masters  Close interactions with all Scrum Masters to review progress, risks, and dependencies for objectives
Leadership Team  Facilitates sync up meetings. Coordinates risk mitigation and next initiativeepic planning preparation.
Stakeholders  Communicates status across engineering teams, risks, dependencies, and metrics.
