Join us and catapult your career with the company that helped pioneer autonomous technology, and the first AV software company with the vision to partner directly with a truck manufacturer.Meet the team:   
The Safety and Security team ensures that Torcs products meet the highest safety and security standards. The Director of this group will provide senior-level leadership to the function charged with the definition, design, and development of critical elements of Torcs safety case across a wide array of engineering, product, safety, and legal teams. They will also interface with Torcs suppliers and partners to ensure safety and security homogeneity.   
What youll do:   

Provide leadership, direction and definition for the Safety Engineering function including planning, scheduling, coordination, and implementation 
Develop and maintain best in class state of the safety framework 
Define targets for product risk, reliability, availability, robustness, and security 
Drive execution toward the target via validation and release strategies 
Develop metrics to demonstrate release readiness and drive adoption via development cycle modifications 
Assess technical quality of system, safety, and security artifacts 
Lead team of technical experts and engineering managers  
Effectively manage individual goals and objectives, performance, and employee development 
Accomplish department objectives by supervising staff 
Developing and monitoring work processes and organizing and monitoring work processes with the highest degree of quality and safety 
Collaborate with executive leadership in engineering, PMO (Program Management Office), product management, System Engineering, Testing, Rigor and operations on workflows and artifacts
