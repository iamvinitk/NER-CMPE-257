YOUR MISSION

Q-Commerce is the second biggest category inside Glovo and we are currently building a strong Local team in order to re-invent how consumers buy their groceries and any other product in their city making it more convenient and easy than ever before.
As a Category Manager within Q-Commerce, you will be responsible for delivering best-in-class user experience for our customers. Leading the implementation of the right assortment, categorization, content, pricing, and promotion strategies.

THE JOURNEY

Be responsible for offering an amazing shopping experience at Glovo Express micro fulfillment center by categorizing  sorting the in-app assortment based on user needs
Negotiate with suppliers to achieve the best prices for goods
Leading assortment definition and optimization strategy
Dynamic pricing analysis and execution
Promotions management and creating cross-selling opportunities
Be part of local projects that drive the growth of the vertical
Develop, implement, and track processes to guarantee content strategy execution
Engage with regional and local teams to implement the global strategy

Strong analytical skills, willing to test and solve problems based on data
Great interpersonal skills
Comfortable working in a high-growth and high-performance start-up with a fast pace
Fluent in English
An empathetic, inclusive and curious attitude
