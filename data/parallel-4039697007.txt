Parallel Systems is a Series A startup company developing the future of intermodal transportation. Our mission is to decarbonize freight while improving supply chain logistics and safety. We are developing vehicles and software to create new autonomous and electric transportation systems for existing rail infrastructure.
At Parallel Systems, we value the team as much as the mission. We want to work with talented people who are humble, motivated, and persistent. Our most successful team members are self-starters and collaborate with others to reach our collective goals.


Were looking for a senior functional safety engineer with a strong understanding of engineering principles, experience with the analysis of safety critical systems, and desire to lead. In this role, youll work with multidisciplinary engineering teams to shape the product and lead the assessment of innovative hardware and software for autonomous rail. Youll lead the effort for functional safety compliance with regulators and work closely with engineers and test staff to verify and validate systems.
Responsibilities

Work with cross functional teams on the strategy, development, implementation, testing, and approval of safety critical systems
Define and derive safety-related requirements for on board electronics, braking, and train control systems
Participate in internal design reviews and ensure projects in development will meet safety requirements and that verification and validation plans are in place
Lead and perform hazard and risk analysis of specific subsystems
Author safety assessments and safety plans in accordance with applicable regulations
Lead and manage regulatory application projects
Communicate and present safety narrative to regulators

Basic Qualifications

BS in Engineering or related field
5 years of experience in functional safety field
Experience with IEC 61508, IEC 62278, IEC 62289, EN 5102x, ISO 26262 or related IEC 61508 derived functional safety standard
Experience applying functional safety processes to off-the-shelf and custom-designed electronic systems, software systems, and concepts of operation

Preferred Qualifications

Experience with new product introduction
Experience managing a schedule, risks, and execution, of a complex project involving multiple people
Experience with computer-based train control systems
Experience with U.S. railway regulations including 49 CFR 236 Subpart H, 236 Subpart I, and 229
Experience with international standards and regulations
