WHAT YOULL BE DOING...

Plan, implement, and co-develop HW design and qualification projects 
Participation in all phases of the product development cycle including design, documentation, eDVT, FA, and support for custom HW designs
Define, implement and automate HW validation and qualification tests
Review, Audit, and Validate reports from JDM partner and retest certain portions of the design
Ability to design and test small FPGACPLD projects
Write functional specifications (HWSystem level), test plans and test reports
Perform schematic capture of designs
Analyze and review of device and system-level power, vendor selection (design), cooling, and electrical (SI) functionality
Improve design and qualification processes to enhance efficiency, consistency, and product robustness
Qualification of subsystems, including CPU complex, networking, power, etc.
Perform failure and root cause analysis activities in support of system hardening efforts and customer escalations
Work with diagnostics engineers by providing direction and reviewing test plans to ensure proper coverage
Work closely with other team members and providing support on cross functional projects as needed
Help contribute to creating a culture of quality by design


WHAT YOULL NEED TO BRING TO THIS ROLE...

A degree in Electrical or Computer Engineering (desired), with either:

Masters with 2 years of professional work experience.
Bachelors with 4 years of professional work experience

Experience working at enterprise hardware companies preferred (Storage, Server, Networking)
Knowledge of hardware design, x86 architectures and interfaces.
Ability to qualify x86 hardware at the electrical, SI, protocol and functional levels
Ability to work cross-functionally with engineering, operations, Mfg. Test, and support to deliver high quality products to our customers
Strong knowledge of selecting x86 subcomponents, SSDs, ACDC Power supplies, and discrete power circuits
Ability to architect or assist in architecting complex systems, involving compute, storage and IO
Experience with Verilog, System Verilog and simulation tools
Experience with HW design tools (i.e. Schematic capture tools, Allegro, Signal integrity tools (HyperLynx, etc))
Experience implementing and automating HW qualification processes
Experience with scripting languages (i.e. Python, BASH, EXPECT, etc) and test development
Experience with High Speed Oscilloscopes and bus analyzers (get trace capture and perform analysis)
Proficiency in diagnosing and debugging all aspects of hardware systems
Solid understanding of Major BusSignaling technology PCIe, Ethernet, Serial (SPI, I2C)
Strong understanding of signal integrity (Create routing rules, run analysis of complex layouts)
Highly self-motivated with the ability to work autonomously in a fast-paced, technical development environment
Ability to operate in a fast-paced changing business environment
High level of teamwork and collaboration skills
Strong verbal and written communication skills
