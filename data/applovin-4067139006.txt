Introduction:
We are seeking a highly motivated and experienced SeniorStaff Software Engineer with a focus on improving the performance of AppLovins Android SDK. In this role, you will work closely with our mobile engineering team to identify and implement measurable performance improvements across all aspects of our Android SDK. You should have a strong background in Android development, and a passion for optimizing app performance.
 



Responsibilities:


Identify, prioritize, and implement performance improvements for the AppLovin Android SDK
Design and implement systems to analyze and monitor performance at scale
Stay up-to-date with the latest developments in Android performance optimization






Requirements:


Bachelors or Masters degree in Computer Science or a related field
5 years of experience in software engineering, with a focus on Android development
Strong knowledge of the Android SDK and OS
Experience with performance optimization on Android
Excellent communication and collaboration skills






Bonus points if you have:


Experience with native code on Android (CC)
Experience with performance optimization on Unity
Experience or interest in AdTech andor game development
Familiarity with web technologies like HTML and JavaScript






Perks:


Competitive salary and equity compensation
Free medical, dental, and vision insurance
Work from home stipend each paycheck
401k matching and employee stock purchase plan
Flexible time off - take time when you need it
