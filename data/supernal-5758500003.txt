Supernal is at the forefront of creating emerging mobility solutions that will foster the development of human-centered cities. We are designing a completely new electric vertical take-off and landing (eVTOL) aircraft tailored to the mobility needs of future cities. This allows passengers a seamless intermodal journey that safely transports them to their final destination. We fuse research in autonomy, robotics, aviation and services to define a new category of mobility for the worlds communities. We believe in creative thinking and collaboration to help build a better mobility experience for everyone, improving peoples ability to move  whether for work or play.
What we do: 
The Associate Chief Engineer (ACE) will partner closely with the Chief Engineer, other ACEs, Technical Fellows as well as Department Heads to ensure cross-functional optimal aircraft solutions that meet all technical requirements. The ACE is responsible for the technical oversight of key capabilities and performance requirements for eVTOL aircraft, including mission performance; flight critical system design for aircraft propulsion, control, and management; architecture design with optimized redundancy levels; specification of hardware elements and technical support to procurement; vehicle functional and software specification; fault management design; lab testing of flight hardware  software; functional and physical integration of the aircraft; on-aircraft testing; and anomaly resolution among other responsibilities.
This position will be required to work on-site 5 days a week.
