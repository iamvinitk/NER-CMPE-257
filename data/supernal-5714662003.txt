Supernal is at the forefront of creating emerging mobility solutions that will foster the development of human-centered cities. We are designing a completely new electric vertical take-off and landing (eVTOL) aircraft tailored to the mobility needs of future cities. This allows passengers a seamless intermodal journey that safely transports them to their final destination. We fuse research in autonomy, robotics, aviation and services to define a new category of mobility for the worlds communities. We believe in creative thinking and collaboration to help build a better mobility experience for everyone, improving peoples ability to move  whether for work or play.
What we do: 
The Product Engineer is responsible for delivering flight-worthy products and maturation of technologies to meet production cost and rate requirements. Success requires collaboration with other collaborators and subject matter experts spanning across the entire product life cycle at the component, system, and aircraft-level. The Product Engineer works closely with internal and external stakeholders from Airframe  AIT, VMS, Power Train, Flight Technology, Test  Evaluation, and the Program Management Office teams to ensure progress.
This position will be required to work on-site 5 days a week.
What you can do:

Assist Sr. Product Engineer(s) with the completion of engineering projects and schedule deliverables in Airframe  AIT domain
Assist in the development and execution of build plans driven by program milestones, critical paths, and risk register for the Airframe  AIT domain deliverables
Assist in the planning, execution, and trackingreporting of various assigned aspects of the engineering development for assigned project(s)
Support plans to conform to technical performance, cost, and schedule commitments
Support collaboration with supplierspartners to ensure program milestones are achieved
Support development of interface control documents and technical specifications
Support the development and execution of test plans and guide the integration, verification, validation, and documentation of requirements through the development cycle
May require up to 10 of domestic and international travel
