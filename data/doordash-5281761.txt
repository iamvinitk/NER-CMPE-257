About the Role
Were looking for a passionate Applied Machine Learning expert to join our team. In this role, you will utilize our robust data and machine learning infrastructure to implement top-notch AIML solutions to build a seamless consumer voice ordering experience, and improve delivery quality for merchants, dashers, and consumers for the Drive Business. As a Staff Machine Learning Scientist, youll be conceptualizing, designing, evaluating, and implementing new AI solutions to DoorDash, building and fine-tuning Large Language Models. You will be expected to demonstrate a strong command of production-level machine learning, a passion for solving end-user problems, leadership skills to collaborate well with multi-disciplinary teams, and execution focused to prioritize effectively in a dynamic environment.  You will be reporting into the Machine Learning Engineering Manager. This will be a hybrid position in San Francisco, Sunnyvale, Los Angeles, Seattle, or New York.
