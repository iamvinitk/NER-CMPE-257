Lyft is looking for an experienced Legislative Counsel to join our fast-growing and fast-paced team. Analyze and evaluate proposed legislation on various issues and provide our Public Policy team recommended changes based on the interests of our users and our company

Coordinate with Lyfts compliance, legal, and operations teams as new regulatory frameworks are implemented
Collaborate with Lyfts Public Policy team members on market-specific legislative efforts

Self-starter; intrinsically motivated
