Coinbase is looking for a highly motivated finance professional to join our Investor Relations team and play a key role in our continued growth and achievement of operational excellence. The Investor Relations associate must possess a deductive mind, an ability to communicate complex ideas to leaders internally and investors externally over time, and a desire to make a meaningful impact on a dynamic, high-growth technology company. The ideal candidate is extremely detail-oriented, well-organized, highly analytical, possesses exceptional written and verbal communication skills, and thrives in a fun, fast-paced, results-oriented environment.
What youll be doing (ie. job duties):

Prepare and maintain accurate consensus models, valuation analysis, stock market trading summaries, and other quantitative materials as needed and on a recurring basis
Monitor and report on industry and peer company information and practices by tracking research reports, news, earnings calls and other relevant resources
Track and summarize institutional ownership trends, and influence our corporate access strategy using data driven insights
Coordinate cross-functional workflows across finance, accounting, data, legal, communications, and other product groups across Coinbase, to gather information in preparation for quarterly earnings and other ad hoc projects
Partner with internal stakeholders and senior executives to develop financial presentations, QA documents, and other relevant materials
Drive and effectively manage ad hoc projects and investigation as required
Collaborate with other functional areas to drive processes forward and increase efficiency
Work closely key stakeholders to build the infrastructure to support the companys current and future business reporting requirements

What we look for in you (ie. job requirements):

Deep understanding of financial modeling, valuation, investing, and investment management landscape - particularly within the financial services, fintech,  consumer tech
2 years of experience in one or more of the following areas: equity research, investment banking, investor relations, or management consulting.
Excellent communication, presentation and interpersonal skills - ability to distill complex topics into a concise, consumable format
Track record of successfully building and managing processes
Ability to build strong relationships internally and externally with numerous constituencies, including senior executives, analysts, and investors
Experience working at a fast-growing technology company or in the financial services industry
High attention to detail
Ability to thrive in a dynamic  fast paced environment

Nice to haves:

Advanced degree in business or finance
Factset and Google suite experience
Passion for cryptocurrency, financial markets, and the Coinbase mission
