The Okta Japan Solutions Engineering Team
The Okta Japan Solutions Engineering team is involved in pre-sales stages of the customers development lifecycle and are experienced using presentations, email, phone and social media to connect with customers. We are looking for a great team leader that can build sales presentations, product demonstrations, execute Proof of Concepts and Workshops to educate customers (everyone from developers to product managers to C-level executives) on the best ways to cloud security technology. 
 
The Solutions Engineering Manager Opportunity
Our Solutions Engineering organization is seeking a Manager of Solutions Engineering for our Corporate  Emerging team (Commercial business) to lead a highly skilled team of Solutions Engineers. In addition to managing a team you will work directly with our sales team and channel partners to understand the needs of our customers, strategize on how to navigate sales cycles, provide value-based demonstrations, support POCs and close business.
 
What youll be doing 

Manage a team of talented solutions engineers responsible for supporting our SMB customers.
Provide coaching and development to your team ensuring professional growth and career development. 
Partner with sales leadership to identify and execute strategies that will scale quickly and meet business goals
Build effective relationships with cross functional leaders such as Marketing, Alliance, Customer Success and more.
Partner with HR, Finance and Operations to look at skills gaps, workforce planning and budget alignment.
Partner with Recruitment to build best in class hiring plans and go to market strategies. Interview, assess, conduct case studies and navigate offers to secure the best talent for Okta.
Collaborate with your Manager peers around best practice, market trends, employee internal moves and the overall functions strategy to support the team as they scale.
Act as an ambassador for your team with the Product functions to deliver feedback from the field.

 
What youll bring to the role

5 years of industry experience in a direct pre-sales capacity with 2 years of direct line management experience (hiring, training and retaining top talent)
Familiarity with the MEDDICC or some other sales qualification framework
Outstanding presenting skills to both technical and executive audiences, whether impromptu on a whiteboard or using presentations and demos.
University degree in computer science, engineering, mathematics or related fields, or equivalent experience preferred
Fluency in English and Japanese is essential

 
And extra credit if you have experience in any of the following! 

Technical skills gained in the security or identity space, such as IAM, CIAM, VPN, MDM, Active Directory, LDAP.
Knowledge around the Okta platform and competitor product offerings
Experience in presales or presales leading focusing on PartnerAlliance business.
