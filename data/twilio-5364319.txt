About the job
The Internal Audit is central to Twilios continued growth. We work closely with senior management to identify and evaluate key business risks and recommend actions to mitigate risks while improving and maturing processes
Twilio is growing rapidly and seeking an IT Internal Audit Manager to join Twilios Internal Audit organization. The team is focused on process improvements through operational internal audits, including data analytics to help the business mature processes, allowing efficiency and scalability while maintaining internal controls to mitigate risks. Reporting to the Director of Internal Audit, this position will work closely with internal audit teams and other Twilio functional teams to facilitate and manage the SOX assessment program and identify process and system gaps.
Responsibilities
In this role, youll:

Plan, Manage and Perform IT SOX assessment (For ITGCs and IT Application controls), including managing internal and co-source resources, document requests and review of workpapers
Drive all aspects of internal audit projects, including planning, testing, execution and reporting.
Participate in identifying and documenting control deficiencies, performing impact assessments and partnering with IT compliance team to develop remediation solutions
Build relationships and collaborate across functional teams within Twilio to identify process improvement opportunities for efficient, effective and scalable processes, utilizing industry best practices tailored to Twilio.
Assist with developing and delivering meaningful data driven executive presentations, including senior leadership
Guide and review the work performed by other internal audit personnel, including co-sourced firms
Provide advisory support related to SOX considerations for new system development and system changes implementation
