Mission Summary:
The Senior Automated Driving Systems Application Engineer will be responsible for creation, compilation, and management of all requirements for a specific AMoD application. This includes specifically creation of Operational Envelope Specification, creation and management of UseCases (database), management of legal requirements as well as all other related systems requirements.
What youll be doing:

Manage global UseCase database for AMoD System
Define product UseCases
Define and manage system requirements for AMoD Application(s) (functional, maintenance, and service) using Polarion
Coordinate with other teams to ensure UseCases and requirements are consistent with stakeholder expectations and technological feasibility
Support defining of test cases for development testing, verification, and validation

What were looking for: 

MS degree in engineering, physics, or relevant equivalent experience
Strong systems engineering experience
Cross-functional knowledge of Systems Engineering, Electronic, Software, and Mechanical Engineering, and Vehicle Integration
Ability to understand, decompose, and write formal requirements
Ability to understand and manage projects to a product schedule
Self-motivated, organized, flexible, and able to balance and prioritize multiple diverse tasks
Comfortable working with other teams having diverse perspectives
Strong interest in next-generation automated driving technologies (including hands-on evaluation)

Bonus Points (not required):

Good knowledge of common vehicle electrical and networking systems (CAN, LIN, Ethernet, etc)
Experience using requirements management tools
Experience working with Operational Design Domains
Knowledge of automotive industry standards and technical language 
Experience analyzing vehicle data logs
Experience using OpenScenarioOpenDrive files
Experience creating and maintaining databases
