What we do: 
The Test Program Integration Engineer is responsible for coordinating and handling all high-level test requirements, including those for laboratory, ground, and flight tests. This includes ensuring that all program requirements are met, tracking test deliverables, and maintaining traceability throughout the process. The Test Program Integration Engineer will oversee the critical path visibility of these events to ensure that program milestones are met on time. They will also coordinate the procurement of test aircraft and engineering parts, track instrumentation requirements, manage configuration changes, and monitor ground and flight test limitations and advisories. Additionally, this role will help manage integrated Test Program Risks and Opportunities and support program-level status updates.
This position will be required to work on-site 5 days a week.
What you can do:

Assess the Change Management system for test requirement tracking, visibility, and coordination
Ensure requirement traceability from driving requirements to test deliverables
Track the technical, schedule, and budget risks and opportunities in the Test Program
Analyze program limitations and advisories
Test aircraft configuration requirements
Test program conformity requirements
Support the development of processes and high-level program status
Collaborate with program leaders, engineers, safety personnel, quality management staff, procurement specialists, and members of the test organization
May require up to 10 of domestic and international travel
Other duties as assigned
