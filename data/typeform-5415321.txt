Are you passionate about enhancing user experiences through the power of words? Do you thrive on creating content that not only informs but also delights users? If so, we have the perfect opportunity for you!
At Typeform, we believe that exceptional content is at the heart of a great user experience. As a Senior XU (User Experience) Writer, you will play a pivotal role in shaping the way our users interact with our products and services.
Things you will do:

User-Centric Clarity: Your primary mission is to be the voice of the user. Youll craft clear, concise, and user-centric copy that simplifies complex concepts, making digital products more intuitive and enjoyable our users. Your words will be the guiding light that leads users through their digital journey seamlessly.
Consistency  Brand Voice: Youll serve as the guardian of our brands linguistic identity. Ensuring that our language, tone, and voice are consistent across all user touchpoints is a key part of your role. Your expertise in maintaining brand integrity will contribute to a cohesive and memorable user experience.
Collaboration: Collaboration is at the core of what we do. Youll work closely with designers, developers, and product managers, integrating your content seamlessly into the product design process. Your ability to collaborate effectively will ensure that design and content work in harmony to achieve our user-focused goals.
Content Strategy Development: Your insights will drive our content strategies. Drawing from user feedback, analytics, and business needs, youll create and refine content strategies that resonate with our audience. Your strategic thinking will shape the future of our user experiences.
Guidance  Advocacy: Youll be the users advocate within our teams. Every content decision you make will be aimed at enhancing usability and user satisfaction. Your dedication to putting the user first will lead to products that truly resonate with our audience.
Mentoring: As a senior member of the team, youll play a crucial role in mentoring junior writers and team members. Youll promote best practices in UX writing, ensuring our team continues to grow and excel.
Content Testing  Iteration: Youre not just a writer; youre a data-driven content creator. Youll use AB testing, user feedback, and analytics to continuously refine and improve our content. Your commitment to data-backed decision-making will drive our content to new heights.
Documentation  Standards: To maintain consistency, youll develop and maintain content guidelines, style guides, and best practices. Your standards will be the foundation upon which we build a user-centric content ecosystem.
Cross-functional Communication: Youll bridge the gap between design, development, and content. Your ability to align content with overarching business and product goals will ensure that our content contributes to the success of the entire organization.
Stay Updated: The world of UX writing is constantly evolving. Youll stay updated on the latest trends, tools, and methodologies to ensure that our content remains relevant, effective, and at the forefront of industry best practices.
