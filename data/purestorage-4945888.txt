As a Sr. Manager - GTM Systems, you will partner with business owners and product management leaders to understand priorities and deliver a product road map contributing to the overall technology strategy.
 
What Youll Do

Serve as the technical leader for IT GTM Systems across Pure Storage - review existing systems landscape and provide guidance on optimization, automation, best practices and new technologies.
Manage and drive development efforts to ensure compliance with business requirements, go-live dates, scalability, platform stability and security.
Lead (mentorcoach) and build a highly skilled, geographically distributed engineering team to define and implement technical solutions that help users perform their work effectively using GTM Systems.
Strong organizational and communication skills that create effective cross team collaboration between business and technical focused teams and personnel.
Work with product managers, designers, QA and Application Support teams to maintain and surpass product usability and quality standards.

Requirements

Minimum 8 years of experience working in the technology industry with proven delivery of digital transformation projects.
Demonstrated experience operating within a matrixed organization with a strong focus on enabling business capabilities through partnership and collaboration
Comfortable working with a global admin and development team working across many different time zones, geographies, and cultures
Proven experience with Salesforce Architecture, People Management, Stakeholder Communication
Experience working with technical  functional resources and following an Agile based delivery methodology
Exceptional analytical reasoning and problem-solving skills
