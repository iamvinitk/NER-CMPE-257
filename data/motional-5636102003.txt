Mission Summary: 
Motional is seeking an experienced engineering Director to help define and lead our growing subsystem analysis teams. You will oversee the Systems Engineering workstreams to gain confidence in our various in-house hardware and software subsystems, including our Autonomy stack. In this role, you will lead a team of engineers to develop appropriate requirements for each subsystem, identify hazards and failure modes, and develop tests to properly assess the performance and safety of our system. You will also be responsible for deriving and communicating the highest priority workstreams and adapting to business needs as appropriate.
What youll do: 

Build a systems engineering effort from its early stages, combining well-known processes in similar industries with new, innovative analysis techniques to grow towards a streamlined internal assessment process
Collaborate with systems engineers, development engineers, and safety engineers to ensure a uniform understanding of the highest priority systems engineering efforts to expand a system with an exceptionally high bar for safety
Adapt applicable systems engineering processes to self-driving car development, unifying the approach across several subsystems, while following relevant analysis and test methods
Regularly report to the Head of Systems Engineering and CTO on current risks and assessment progress
Hire, train, and mentor a talented set of engineers and engineering managers
Willingness to fill in high priority gaps in subsystem assessment, as necessary

What were looking for: 

A track record of success developing practical solutions in extremely technically challenging engineering disciplines, ideally from inception through product introduction
Excellent verbal and written communication skills; effective in communicating complex concepts and driving resolutions
An interest and appreciation in developing a positive culture throughout the organization
An ability to focus on MVP8020 solutions that enable current day-to-day priorities that are compatible with and contribute to a final productsolution
A strong physics and data analysis background
Experience with a rigorous systems engineering process to assess the readiness of a safety-critical software or hardware system
5 years of management experience
Experience with Vehicle, Hardware-in-the-loop, Simulation, Software-in-the-loop, Fault injection, unit, andor integration testing, including the trade offs between them
Masters degree (or equivalent experience in a related technical field
Comfortable with basic engineering tools, including navigating a filesystem with with a terminal (UNIX) and basic SQL querying capabilities
