The CX Training  Development Specialist is a highly motivated and skilled team member within the Knowledge  Improvement Team. Reporting to the Head of Knowledge  Improvement, you will play a key part in enhancing the competencies of our employees by developing and delivering training programs that align with our companys core values. You will be responsible for conducting training needs assessments, designing engaging curriculum and learning materials, and overseeing all aspects of training interventions. Your primary objective will be to boost workplace performance within our CX organization. As a Training  Development Specialist, you will collaborate with various stakeholders to identify training gaps and develop solutions to address them. You will also be responsible for evaluating the effectiveness of training programs and making necessary adjustments to ensure continuous improvement. 
To excel in this role, you should have a strong passion for learning, excellent instructional design skills, and the ability to effectively communicate and engage with employees at all levels. Your creativity, attention to detail, and organizational abilities will be critical in delivering impactful training initiatives.
What youll do

Identify training needs by evaluating strengths and weaknesses
Translate requirements into training that will upskill employees in tolling, process, and other operational ways of working.
Build and assist in building Live training programs and prepare teaching plans
Develop or oversee the production of classroom handouts, instructional materials, aids and manuals
Direct structured learning experiences and monitor results via varied assessment approaches
Assess training effectiveness to ensure incorporation of taught skills and techniques into employees work behaviour
Acclimate new hires to the business and create, conduct, and maintain onboarding sessions remotely and in office when required
Effectively deliver various training courses
Periodically evaluate ongoing programs and resources to ensure that they reflect any changes
Stay abreast of the new trends and tools in employee training and incorporate new styles of training and learning into your practice.

Ability to travel to Catawikis offices in the Netherlands when required
