As a Manager in Merchant Strategy and Operations you will support the end to end process for Merchant acquisition through 2 key surface areas:
Market Selection: youll be responsible for finding the best restaurants, figuring out how to get them on DoorDash as efficiently as possible and supporting our go-to-market initiatives for programs that impact our merchant base. Youll work cross-functionally between our sales, partner management, operations, product and analytics teams to build the merchant foundation.
Revenue and Pricing: youll help create a truly merchant first platform by devising pricing strategies and initiatives to accelerate the growth of our restaurant businesses. You will find the balance of creating new pricing packages, bringing to life our suite of products we offer merchants and drive revenue building initiatives for DoorDash and our partners. 
This is an exciting role as you will drive the selection and growth of the New Zealand Merchant organisation through data-driven decisions and coordination of cross functional partners to execute against your plan. This will be an ambiguous role, and will require high amounts of autonomy and planning to continue pushing the envelope of success in New Zealand.
