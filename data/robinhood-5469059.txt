As the Head of Collections, youll be responsible for operationalizing collections strategy and policies for Robinhoods credit card products.
It is preferred that this role is located in one of the office locations listed on this job description which will align with our in-office working environment. This position is only eligible for remote work in limited geographies within the US where we do not have physical office locations. Please connect with your recruiter for more information regarding our in-office philosophy and expectations.
What youll do

Design, test and iterate on collections programs (including hardship programs) and processes across communication channels including chat, text, and phone
Define key performance indicators and track and report on operational success metrics
Drive process improvements to continuously make collections more efficient and more effective
Partner with credit team to define collections strategy and policies for existing and new credit products
Design operational tests to refine product and credit strategy
Ensure compliance with all relevant regulations
Recruit and build a collections team of contractors andor full time employees as needed
Communicate performance and strategy with senior leadership

What you bring 

No less than five years of experience designing, scaling and managing credit card collections programs
Strong ability to design and execute operational programs
Strong quantitative analytical skills
Strong working knowledge of federal and state debt collection laws
Experience recruiting and managing operations teams
Strong communication skills and ability to simplify complex programs and focus on key areas of leverage
