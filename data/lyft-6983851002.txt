The Payment, Identity and Integrity(Pii) team is core to how Lyft operates. Within the Pii Strategy and Solution team, youll partner with our external vendors along with our peers in Product, Engineering, Finance, and other cross-functional areas as part of a continuous feedback loop, so that we can deliver the best possible Rider and Driver experience. You will be responsible for managing key financial products and work with multiple cross-functional teams to across the company and will need to have a deep working knowledge of payment and fraud product offerings in the US.
From enabling passengers to take rides to paying the drivers, we ensure frictionless interactions on our platform. The team is responsible for every dollar that moves through our acquiring partners, our networks, and our internal systems. The Pii teams mission is to reduce fraud and make payments efficient, effective, and low-cost for Lyft. Trusted payment is fundamental to Lyfts marketplace, and the Pii team is charged with ensuring all money flows through our payments system reliably and accurately. The engineering challenge and opportunity is to enable a superior and secure experience through the Lyft app for passengers and drivers resulting in a near instantaneous flow of funds for all participants. The Pii team has a history of enabling rich and creative features that set the standard for the entire sharing industry. In addition to launching new features, the Pii team is also the architect and owner of a robust and flexible payment and risk platform which enables other teams at Lyft to leverage.
Were looking for a rockstar to join a fast-paced and transformational company to manage various relationships and programs with our payment processors, fraud partners and internal business lines. Youll build our innovative payment and risk platforms to optimize for a frictionless user experience.
