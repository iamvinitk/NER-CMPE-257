In this position you will collaborate with teams working on scaling and our technical advisors.
If youre interested in making an impact and helping define new products, were looking to chat with you!

 
What youll be doing: (approximately 3 - 5 responsibilities; to provide a realistic image of the role)

Define the scope and operational domain of a new robotics systems.
Prototype electromechanical systems to solve challenging problems for our operations teams
Working with mechanical, electrical and software engineers to define and execute on platform requirements.
Integrate this new platform with our existing hardware and cloud services.

M.S or Ph.D in Robotics, Electrical, Mechanical or related field and 5 years of experience. 
You should have experience integrating payloads onto robotic systems to solve problems.
You have a solid understanding of the software stack for a robot and how different modules work together.
