YOUR MISSION:
In the context of giving users easy access to anything in their city, Q-commerce is the business unit that covers 3 verticals: Groceries, Retail and Micro Fulfillment Centers (our own fulfillment channel). We aim to provide immediacy to all product needs that a user might have, from delivering a video game, to sending a gift... to filling up their fridge.
The Q-commerce team is organized in 6 sub-teams: Analytics, Micro Fulfillment Centers Operations  Launch, Delivery operations, Front-end (Marketing, Sales, Account Mgmt. and Category Management), Finance and Retail.
As a Category Manager, you will be responsible for delivering a best-in-class user experience for our customers leading the implementation of the right assortment, categorization, content, pricing, and promotion strategies to win the convenience baskets.
THE JOURNEY:

Negotiate with suppliers to ensure new products with correct margins, and in stock
Lead assortment definition and optimization strategy
Be responsible of offering an amazing shopping experience by categorizing  sorting the assortment based on user needs 
Develop, implement and track processes to guarantee content strategy execution
Manage promotions and capture cross-selling opportunities
