Santa Monica Studio is seeking a Sound Design Intern to join our team for our Summer 2024 Internship Program!
Established in 1999, Santa Monica Studio became renowned for excellence in the actionadventure genre with the worldwide award-winning success of the 2005 release, God of War, and its subsequent sequels. God of War (2018) became one of the fastest selling PlayStation 1st-party games of all-time and has since won over 190 Game of the Year awards including DICE, BAFTA, The Game Awards, Game Developers Choice Awards, IGN, Game Informer, EW, and Variety. Our most recent title, God of War: Ragnark (2022), has since continued this run of commercial and critical success.
Responsibilities:

Support audio needs for game design, cinematics, marketing materials, etc.
Work within the set standard for all sound asset implementation (environmental, character and music) within the production path, as it relates to design, art and otherwise
Collaborate creatively on sound implementation to enhance the gameplay experience
Work with sound designers, artists, programmers, technical sound designers and other teams to accomplish audio consistency and quality
Consistently evaluate the audio for the design assets being created for all of the levels and characters you are responsible for
Address all issues and assist with fixing audio related problems within levels or characters you are responsible for as they arise

Qualifications:

Currently enrolled in an accredited institution, pursuing a Bachelors or Advanced degree majoring in a related field
Projected to graduate between December 2023  June 2025
Passion for game audio and the drive to implement top-rated game sound
Basic experience working in a game editor and with structural data-driven scripting for level and character design
Ability to quickly become proficient with proprietary game engine tools and production processes
Working knowledge of game engine and design tools, especially as they relate to sound
Ability to think logically with a high level of attention to detail
Must be available to work for 12 weeks from May  August or June  September
Internships are available to students who are US citizens or international students with an F-1 visa
