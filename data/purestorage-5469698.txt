About the Role
Pure Storage is seeking a goal-oriented and strategic individual to join our Communications team as a Partner and Vertical Communications Manager. This person will be responsible for building and executing communications strategies that drive increased awareness, advocacy, and preference for Pure Storage among our partner community and in key vertical segments. The ideal candidate will be creative in her or his approach to storytelling, with strong attention to detail, and excellent written, verbal and interpersonal communication skills. An understanding of the enterprise technology landscape is strongly preferred in order to effectively solicit and synthesize various viewpoints into clear, digestible messages andor stories. A demonstrated ability to build and maintain meaningful, ongoing relationships with media and across internal stakeholders is a must. She or he must work well in a fast-paced work environment, under deadlines, and be able to clearly define priorities. Candidate must be a self-starter who works well both independently and as part of a team.
Youll be joining a fun and dynamic team that values collaboration, innovation, measurable results, and learning fast.  
Responsibilities:

Story Development: Advance Pures narratives specific to key verticals (Healthcare, Public Sector, Fin Services) in partnership with vertical marketing leads for use with press. Advance Pures differentiated messaging targeted to partners to support Pure strategy and portfolio. 
Partner  Vertical Media Relations: Strategize and execute PR tactics to get media coverage in channel- and vertical-specific press, including leveraging third party references (partners, customers) and running speaking and awards programs.
Executive Communications: Support Pures VP, Global Partner Sales in external and internal communications to position as thought leader externally and clear, effective communicator internally. 
Other Communications: Produce partner newsletters and partner-specific social media guidance.
PR Agency Management: Manage day-to-day relationships with healthcare and public sector PR agencies.
