With 1,000 employees, over 250M in sales, 1,500 clients, and rapid year-over-year growth, Recorded Future is the worlds most advanced, and largest, intelligence company!We are seeking a Sales Commissions Analyst to support our rapidly growing sales team!  You will work hand in hand with Sales Operations and Sales Management teams to assist in building compensation plans, maintaining our commissions platform, and implementing process improvements to increase the productivity and effectiveness of the sales team. Ability to work independently and provide high-quality support is paramount to success. The incumbent will manage a variety of day-to-day tasks associated with front-end sales support and sales data analysis while also considering long-term strategy.
What youll do as the Sales Commissions Analyst:

Assist in monthly end-to-end global commission calculation process - accrual, sales review, payment approvals, commissions platform maintenance, and payroll submittal
Complete reconciliations to ensure accuracy of commission payments
Document  administer various sales spiffs and incorporate into rep performance results
Provide ongoing support and resolving commission calculation-related inquiries and exceptions with the sales teams 
Assist in sales incentive plan creation process
Support finance and external auditors with commission audit requests
Continuously assess new hire ramp and ramped rep capacity to inform appropriate quota assignment
Assist with forecasting commissions trends and compensation modeling
Drive structure and organization to the overall incentives management function 
Experience with commissions software (QuotaPath- preferred, but not required)

What youll bring to the Sales Commissions Analyst role:

2 years experience in sales operations, finance, or business operations  

Or 1 year of direct commissions analyst experience

SFDC proficiency
Previous experience administering and calculating sales commissions
Superb attention to detail
Excellent communication skills
Inquisitive, problem-solver, and highly analytical
