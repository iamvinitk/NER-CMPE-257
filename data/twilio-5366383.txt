About the job
This position is needed to help build up the core platform for one of the most strategically important products in Twilio.
Our team is responsible for Notifications service, which supports multiple customers along with other Twilio products by sending push notifications to different types of devices. Besides the challenge of continuously enriching our solution with new and exciting features, there is also the need to scale and care for our ever growing customer base - expected to increase exponentially during these next years. 
 
Responsibilities
In this role, youll:

Contribute to designing and building of Services and Tools
Work with Product on defining requirements, and working to provide estimations, based on collaboratively defined requirements. 
Participate in Product Backlog grooming and estimation processes
Contribute to design and feature ideas and betterments to the backlog
Automate manual processes and develop tools (in accordance with Rules of Engagement) to avoid repetitive work
Write unit, integration, cluster and end-to-end tests to achieve high levels of code coverage and detect defects early
Deploy code to the cloud; maintain operational excellence through Runbooks  Metrics
Break down large epics, lead grooming, and work to ensure larger goals are delivered on time.
Mentor junior engineers
