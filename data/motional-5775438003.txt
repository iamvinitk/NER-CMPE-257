Motional builds state-of-the-art fully autonomous vehicles. Our Embedded Real-time Platform team delivers robust, high performance software on a real-time vehicle-integrated compute platform. The team takes care of supporting different vehicle platforms provided by OEMs, embedded controller hardware bring-up, and safety feature implementation. This includes system health monitoring, backup strategy when failure happens, and redundant system design to meet ASIL-D standards for autonomous driving at scale. Motional specializes in providing robo-taxis for passenger rides and goods delivery, currently operating in Las Vegas and planning to scale to LA soon.
Role responsibilities:

Lead and grow an Embedded Software team to deliver embedded software from design, prototyping, testing, deployment, to production for autonomous driving vehicles.
Provide technical leadership to make consequential decisions on architectural direction, strategic investments, tactical execution, and technical debt reduction.
Oversee the development and optimization of critical platform control and monitoring components, such as body control, sensor cleaning, temperature control, and vehicle component monitoring.
Identify and review technical requirements in collaboration with partnering teams, such as Systems Engineering and Safety, to drive completion of features and projects.
Lead from a strong technical background in embedded software engineering best practices.
Coordinate with Motional partners and suppliers for the purpose of vehicle platform bring-up and next-generation platform design.
Mentor, provides feedback (technical and professional) to team members, and collaborate with project managers to roadmap and execute plans on schedule.
Understand and explain trade-offs and complex concepts to peers and leaders to drive decision-making.
Self-motivation to generate new ideas and provide a vision for the team and our system.
Utilize your experience in personnel leadership to help the group stay engaged, evolve, and adapt to meet new challenges across the organization
