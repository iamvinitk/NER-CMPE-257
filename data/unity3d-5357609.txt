Unity is looking for a versatile Senior Integration Developer to join our multifaceted IT team. In this role, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and implementing sophisticated integration solutions that seamlessly connect various systems and applications within our organization.
The opportunity
This role is responsible for supporting Enterprise technologies used by the IT Team. As a key member of the Enterprise Technology team, you will own the development and support of integration and automation solutions, provide technical expertise, and collaborate with multi-functional teams to deliver scalable and efficient integration solutions. Your deep understanding of integration patterns, APIs, and best practices will be crucial in optimizing the orchestration workflows and supporting our business processes!
What youll be doing 

Capture and understand requirements, conduct design sessions with Architects for technical solutions. Enable teams to self serve integrationautomation needs.
Build and support integrations in various business areas such as Finance, HR and Legal and communicate optimally with internal teams, business partners
Develop, test, and document integration solutions and data migration elements of business applications in the CPQ, CRM, ERP, HRIS domains
Build and maintain integration strategy and repository based on principles of reusability, security, scalability and quality.
Build common frameworks for monitoring, logging and alerting.

What were looking for 

Expertise in developing application integration solutions using tools such as Workato, Zapier, Tray.io, MuleSoft, on cloud service providers like AWSGoogle Cloud.
Knowledge of enterprise integration patterns and approaches (APIs, Microservices, File based integration, PubSub, Batch, Streaming, etc.).
Knowledge of integration capabilities of applications like Salesforce, Workday, NetSuite, Zuora, etc. and knowledge of advanced troubleshooting techniques in integration layer and across these systems.
Hand on experience on one of the programming language such as Node.js, Python, Go or Java.

You might also have

Certified developerarchitect in any of the iPaaS platforms (i.e. Workato, MuleSoft etc.)
Adopt new technologies and work with various tools and technologies to achieve desired functionality in integrationautomations space. Ability to program in at least one of the following: Node.js, Python, SQL, Ruby and experience in process flow orchestration using Salesforce flows
