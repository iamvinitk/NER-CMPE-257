Role Description

Participation in challenging and agile consulting projects with regards to setting up, developing and optimizing the data governance organization from business analysis and design to implementation
You will use modern processes and technologies to enable a highly efficient, transparent and high quality data management
You will support our clients in the implementation and development of a data governance organization with new roles and responsibilities for the change from a process-based to a data-based organizational structure
Step by step you will take on more responsibility for topics, as well as the management of sub-projects and smaller project teams
Beside the project related activities, you will have the opportunity to contribute to various internal topics, to develop them and to implement own ideas

 
Skills and Expertise

A successfully completed university degree in economics, (business) computer science, (business) engineering or a comparable course of study is what sets you apart
Several years of relevant practical project experience regarding the necessary approaches and methods of data management or BCBS 239
Experience in designing and implementing data management standards and best practices, especially in development of a Data Governance organization with the introduction of data management tools (e.g., Collibra, Ab Initio), creating a data catalog, conducting data lineage, or designing and implementing data quality management
Strong analytical skills and team skills paired with assertiveness
