Do you love imbuing digital characters with poise and dynamism? Do you enjoy collaborating closely with creative talent and industry veterans? Would you like to help Bungie create our next enduring game?
As a Senior Animation Engineer at Bungie, you will collaborate with animators and other artists to build the animation technology that brings Marathons simulated combatants to life. You will help drive the future of Bungies animation workflow and content pipeline, you will architect systems for new kinds of locomotion and interactions, and you will empower our content creators to bring even more depth and variety to the creatures that inhabit Marathons evolving living world. Most importantly, you will work with a fun, talented, and energetic team invested in creating a new, world-class gaming experience.
RESPONSIBILITIES

Learn, extend, and maintain character animation systems and tooling in C with some C
Learn the needs and workflows of animators and other artists to develop improvements
Collaborate daily with a cross-disciplinary team to translate goals into actionable plans
Work directly with customers to triage, prioritize, and provide time estimates for feature requests
Provide craft mentorship and share subject matter expertise to other engineers

REQUIRED SKILLS

Build and maintain production-quality C
Proficiency in 3D math, algorithms, and contemporary animation techniques
Autonomously navigate complicated systems to make improvements and upgrades
Effectively communicate with artists of different technical backgrounds to help them navigate technology and achieve their goals
