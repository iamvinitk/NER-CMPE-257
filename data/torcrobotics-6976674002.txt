About the Company
At Torc, we have always believed that autonomous vehicle technology will transform how we travel, move freight, and do business.
A leader in autonomous driving since 2007, Torc has spent over a decade commercializing our solutions with experienced partners. Now a part of the Daimler family, we are focused solely on developing software for automated trucks to transform how the world moves freight.
Join us and catapult your career with the company that helped pioneer autonomous technology, and the first AV software company with the vision to partner directly with a truck manufacturer.Meet the Team: 
The Imaging Specialist will assist the camera hardware team in writing, deploying, and running validation and Hardware-In-Loop tests for cameras and sensors bound for Torcs autonomous fleet. You will be responsible for assisting in the design, build and operation of camera test setups for image quality assessment, calibration and lens focusing.
Essential Duties As part of the Camera Hardware team, youll need to:

Work efficiently with both hardware and software in a lab environment
Write, execute and validate tests on hardware using Python 3
Improve quality of camera focusing and calibration workflow
Build optical test setups that meet the design requirements of the camera team
Collaborate with team members to address gaps in current validation requirements

What Youll Need to Succeed 

Currently enrolled in one of the following disciplines: o Physics, Engineering, Computer Science
Familiarity with Python best practices
Basic competency in Linux
Experience with version control and git
Good written and verbal communication skills
Knowledge or experience with cameras and optical systems is a plus
Understand the difference between a -20 and M6 bolt
