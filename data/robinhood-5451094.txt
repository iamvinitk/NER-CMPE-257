As the new member of Robinhoods Marketing Technology team, youll play a key role in helping us build and support a world class Marketing Technology stack.
Our mission is to enhance, build and transform systems for the global Marketing organization. With product and engineering, we are building a best-in-class, privacy-first martech stack from the ground up.
Performance marketers, lifecycle marketers and the global Marketing organization will be your customers. Youll work to understand what they need to drive KPIs across Acquisition, Activation, Engagement and Retention. Youll meet those needs by building or extending martech capabilities with the help of product, engineering, data engineering and data science teams.
As a Marketing Technology Manager, you will be the subject matter expert for:

Collection, storage, and usability of marketing data
Data movement from storage to channel destinations and capabilities
Data infrastructure that enables all of marketings customer segmentation

Examples of projects youll work on include:

Implementation and maintenance of regulatory compliant and privacy-first marketing database
Building out a complex multivariate experimentation infrastructure for the lifecycle team
Creating faster experiment loops with self-serve analytics and dashboards
Adding new and optimizing existing performance marketing pipelines using our customer data platform
Deliver new segmentation capabilities using first and third party data
