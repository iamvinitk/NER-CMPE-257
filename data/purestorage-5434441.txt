Pure Storage is seeking an experienced, energetic and highly motivated Global Event Manager to assist with planning and execution of our large Tier 1 events.  The right person has the ideal mix of creativity and logistics, who pays close attention to detail, and knows how to manage all types of events.  
This individual possesses skills in global events and tradeshow management. You know how to manage both onsite and digital events and are familiar with different platforms, working with various marketing teams for event promotions, marketing operations to track progress and the field to drive marketing sourced opportunities. Youll coordinate global and domestic logistics of programs, including event services, budgets, demos, giveaways, program timelines, and marketing materials needed pre- and post-event. You know how to coordinate all the logistics from rooming lists to food and beverage and meeting room set ups and are able and willing to travel to provide on-site event support. You have mad skills in simultaneously managing multiple projects with attention to detail, achieving all deadlines, and performing analysis for each event to determine ROI. You have the ability to coordinate with multiple internal and external stakeholders at all levels, balancing a collaborative approach with the right measure of assertiveness and a customer-first attitude. 
Mental Outlook. You understand the show must go on attitude. Event dates do not move so you are always working to a deadline. This will drive a sense of urgency that you need to be comfortable with as part of your day to day work.
What You Will Be Doing

Contributing to large events and roadshows, support on other internal events
Developing out of the box ideas and industry leading events
Support regular planning meetings in various time zones with internal teams such as product marketing, corporate marketing, and third-party vendors
Manage timelines and deliverables for logisticscreativemarketing teams and ensure critical deadlines are communicated, tracked and met
Assist with food and beverage, evening events, onsite staffing and scheduling, AV, meeting room set ups and other logistical components
Work with  global third-party vendors such as decorator, AV, catering, freight shipping, laborshow unions, and show management
Coordinate on-site setup, support during event and breakdown of events; organize and run after hour events and transportation as required
Manage small events and programs dependent on event team needs and calendar in the form of conferences, c-level events, sponsorship programs, roadshows, etc.

WHAT YOULL NEED TO BRING TO THIS ROLE...

Bachelors degree from an accredited four-year institution
Familiarity with digital event platforms
CMP a plus
Cvent proficiency
Ability and flexibility to travel up to 20, and work overtime as needed to attend events and meetings (including weekends)
Physical ability to lift up to 40 pounds
Proficiency with basic computer programs, including Google Suite 
Experience with other technologies: Zoom, SFDC, Marketo, Cvent, Mobile event apps, BriefingSource, Google Workspace, NetSuite, Allocadia
