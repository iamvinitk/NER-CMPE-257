This role is responsible for managing programs that support how we measure and assess talent and making recommendations for new approaches to talent assessment. Through partnership and experimentation, the Senior Assessment Specialist will establish Coinbase as an innovative talent leader. 
What youll be doing (ie. job duties): 

Program Design: Drive the selection, execution and improvement of pre-and post-hire Talent Assessments by helping to identify and recommend solutions 
Repeatable Innovation: Establish a framework for piloting and experimenting new Talent Assessment solutions and tools
Program Management: Pilot potential new solutions and tools to determine best-fit assessments within strategy and culture
Reporting and Analytics: Work People Analytics to report on appropriate success metrics; identify key insights to inform decision-making
Trusted Partnership: Partner with stakeholders across Talent and the People Success Team (PST) to ensure implementation of new assessments aligns with overall Talent Assessment strategy
Continuous Improvement: Identify new opportunities to advance our assessment approach to support business demands

What we look for in you (ie. job requirements):

Degree in IndustrialOrganizational Psychology or Quantitative Psychology (PhD or Masters preferred) and at least 5 years experience managing programs related to talent andor measurement (talent assessment, research methods, talent management, etc.)
Proven ability to manage multiple complex projects, meet deadlines, and work with cross-functional partners
Highly motivated to try new things and not afraid to challenge common practices
Proven experience utilizing data and feedback to develop new andor innovate existing processes
Ability to adapt communication style to reach various audiences and build deep relationships with people from a range of backgrounds
Ability to think clearly under pressure, both autonomously and in team environments

Nice to haves:

A degree in or experience with advanced statistical analysis or psychometrics
Experience with international talent assessment strategies, reflecting a comprehensive global outlook
Experience running adverse impact analysis to ensure fairness and equity in talent measurement practices
Comprehensive understanding of General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Automated Decision-Making (ADM) laws as they pertain to assessments, ensuring compliance in all processes
