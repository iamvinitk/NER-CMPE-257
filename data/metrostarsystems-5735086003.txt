What youll do: 

Manage and maintain the organizations IT infrastructure, including servers, networks, and storage systems.
Monitor system performance, availability, and security to identify and address potential issues proactively
Diagnose and resolve technical issues related to systems, networks, and applications promptly to minimize downtime and disruptions.
Implement and enforce security best practices, including regular updates, patches, and vulnerability assessments to protect against unauthorized access and data breaches.
Develop and maintain backup and disaster recovery plans to ensure data integrity and rapid system recovery in case of emergencies.
Provide technical support to end-users, addressing their inquiries, resolving problems, and assisting with software and hardware-related issues.
Plan and execute system upgrades, patches, and software deployments with minimal impact on operations.
Maintain accurate and up-to-date documentation of system configurations, procedures, and troubleshooting guides.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand their technical requirements, provide solutions, and ensure smooth integration of systems and services.
Identify opportunities for automation and scripting to streamline system administration tasks and enhance operational efficiency.
Monitor resource utilization and plan for future growth by recommending hardware and software upgrades as needed.

What youll need to succeed: 

At least five years of experience as a System Administrator or similar role, with a track record of managing complex IT environments.
Strong proficiency in operating systems such as Windows Server, LinuxUnix, and virtualization technologies.
Familiarity with networking protocols, firewalls, and security practices.
Experience with cloud services (e.g., AWS, Azure, Google Cloud) is a plus.
Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to think critically under pressure.
Strong communication skills to interact with technical and non-technical stakeholders effectively.
Relevant certifications like CompTIA A, Microsoft Certified: Azure Administrator Associate or Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP) are advantageous.
Attention to detail and a commitment to maintaining a secure and efficient IT environment.
Ability to work independently as well as part of a team, with a proactive and collaborative approach.
Active TSSCI clearance with CI poly preferred or Active TSSCI clearance with ability to pass a CI poly
