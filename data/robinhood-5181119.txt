Privacy Infrastructure teams mission is to build tools and infrastructure that helps our customers manage privacy of their data effectively. This team sits in Data Infrastructure organization and works very closely with our Security Engineering team. Team is responsible for building tools and features that improves Robinhoods overall Privacy posture and ensure we not only meet but exceed our regulatory requirements.
The role is located in the office location(s) listed on this job description which will align with our in-office working environment. Please connect with your recruiter for more information regarding our in-office philosophy and expectations.
What you bring

Passion for Privacy.
Lead a team of software engineers, developing tools and infrastructure to support data Privacy initiatives and promote Privacy by Design principles across the organization.
Collaborate with cross functional partners to create a Privacy strategy and Roadmap for Robinhood, involving Product Engineering, Legal, Security, and Platform engineering.
Promote inclusive conversations and leverage your technical expertise to make informed engineering decisions that drive progress.
Coach and mentor engineers, prioritize quality, and recruit talented individuals to enhance the teams skills.
Monitor and contribute towards improving the stability of our engineering solutions.
Proficiency with Python or Golang.
Ideally you have previously led a Privacy Engineering team focused on GDPR or CCPA implementation.
