The opportunity
Unity Engine is used by millions of creators around the world to build powerful and engaging real-time experiences. At the heart of this is graphics, from the initial point of content creation down to the final pixels on the target device.
In the platform graphics team, we focus on bringing the state of the art graphics features on all the platforms Unity supports now and in the future! Our mission is to enable Unity developers to write rendering code without foreknowledge of what API it will ultimately be running on.
As a Graphics Engineering Manager, you will be in an outstanding position working alongside other graphics teams in Unity to improve the quality of the rendering systems, the performance of cross platform high level graphics architecture.
This role is primarily people-focused, but will also require hands-on technical leadership. The right person will help us attract and retain high quality engineering talent. If you thrive on shaping software that is loved and used by millions, and leading the engineers who build and maintain it, then weve got a great job opportunity for you!
What youll be doing 

Take ownership of and drive the technical strategy and roadmap of the team
Collaborate with product management, design, and architecture teams across the platform and graphics organizations
Communicate your teams vision internally and externally to various partners
Support the members of your engineering team as they advance in their career goals

What were looking for 

Experience leading a team
Strong collaboration and communication skills
Understanding of the modern engine systems and rendering architectures and experience of working on real-world productions or large multi-platform engine
Past experience as an engineer, and a working familiarity with CC

You might also have

Prior experience using the Unity Engine
Experience with low-level modern graphics APIs DX12, metal
Prior XR development experience andor passion for VR

Life at Unity
Unity (NYSE: U) is the worlds leading platform for creating and operating real-time 3D (RT3D) content. Creators, ranging from game developers to artists, architects, automotive designers, filmmakers, and others, use Unity to make their imaginations come to life. Unity is the foundation upon which the worlds most powerful digital content is created. Specifically, Unitys platform provides a comprehensive set of software solutions to create, run and monetize interactive, real-time 2D and 3D content for mobile phones, tablets, PCs, consoles, and augmented and virtual reality devices.
In the fourth quarter of 2022, more than 70 of the top 1000 mobile games were made with Unity as derived from a blended number of the top 1000 games in the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. In 2022, Made with Unity Applications had more than 4 billion downloads per month. For more information, please visit www.unity.com.
