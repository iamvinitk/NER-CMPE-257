About the Role:
The compensation range for this role is: 169,374 - 272,290
Are you a seasoned Data Scientist with a strong focus on AdTech, looking to take your career to the next level? We are seeking a Staff Data Scientist with extensive experience in AdTech to join our team. In this role, you will play a key role in driving innovation and optimizing advertising strategies, leveraging your deep knowledge of data science and AdTech to deliver impactful results for our customers.
Key Responsibilities:

Product and Engineering: Collaborate with our product and engineering teams to pinpoint the precise objectives of advertising campaigns and architect machine learning models that optimize these campaigns with precision.
Account Ranking in B2B Buying Journey: Spearhead the development of algorithms to rank accounts in the B2B buying journey, shaping the direction of targeted advertising campaigns.
Intent Taxonomy: Create and refine a browsable taxonomy of intent signals, enabling advertisers to gain a deeper understanding of user intent.

What Youll Be Doing:

Advanced Algorithm Development: Drive the development of advanced machine learning algorithms that optimize advertising strategies and deliver immediate business impact on KPIs.
Model Deployment: End-to-end, fullstack ownership of ML models. Take charge of building, testing, and deploying custom MLAI models and algorithms on extensive datasets, establishing rigorous processes for monitoring and analyzing their performance in production environments.
Effective Communication: Excel in effectively communicating complex data science methods, statistical findings, and algorithmic insights to technical and non-technical stakeholders.
Championing Innovation: Stay at the forefront of AdTech and data science, actively leading innovation initiatives within the organization.

What Were Looking For:

Experience: A minimum of 5 years of robust data science experience in AdTech.
Educational Background: A degree in Statistics, Computer Science, Machine Learning, Mathematics, Computational Psychology, Operational Research, Physics, or a related field.
Project Leadership: A strong track record of leading greenfield projects from conceptualization to production release.
Analytical Skills: Proficiency in using analytical and database tools such as Jupyter notebooks, Hive, SQL, and No-SQL databases.
Coding Expertise: Demonstrated ability to write clean and high-performance code in Python.
AIML Proficiency: Proficiency in one or more of the following AIML technologies is advantageous: TensorFlow, scikit-learn, Spark MLlib, Bigquery Machine Learning, large-scale datasets, Natural Language Processing, and Graph algorithms.

Nice to Have Skills:

Team Leadership: Previous experience in leading teams and managing complex projects.
Cloud Competency: Familiarity with Google Cloud or AWS cloud platforms.

Desired Qualities:

Ownership Mentality: Willingness to take full ownership of end-to-end machine learning products you develop or contribute to.
Self-Driven: A highly self-motivated individual with the ability to operate with significant autonomy.
Problem-Solving Acumen: Proficiency in breaking down complex problems and delivering innovative solutions.
Goal-Focused: An ability to maintain a sharp focus on project goals without getting lost in the minutiae.
Data Enthusiast: A passion for data, metrics, and using them to drive the success of projects.

As a Staff Data Scientist specializing in AdTech, you have the opportunity to make a significant impact in the ever-evolving landscape of data-driven advertising. Join our dynamic team today and take on a role that offers both challenges and rewards in the world of Advertising.
