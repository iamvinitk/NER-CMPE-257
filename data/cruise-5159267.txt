About the role:
Machine Learning Infrastructure (MLI) is looking for a Senior Staff Machine Learning Engineer to bridge the gap between our Machine Learning Engineer (MLE) customer needs and our teams offerings. MLI is composed of 120 engineers and the initial focus will be on improving MLE productivity  iteration speed. This work is instrumental in enabling Cruise to gracefully scale up its operations to more cities.
In this position, you will work closely with MLI area Technical Leads, Staff and Senior Staff Engineers, and our customer teams composed of over 200 MLEs. Through these collaborations you will solve one of the problems that is a bottleneck for Cruises city expansions - MLE productivity.
You will be setting the direction for MLI and many of its teams, working closely with our customers working on cutting-edge autonomous vehicle problems, and with high autonomy going deep in many novel directions to bridge the gap between applied ML and infrastructure.
What youll be doing: 

You will embed yourself, as needed, in customer MLE teams to deeply understand their workflows and identify tooling and infrastructure improvements. Your first goal will be improving our customers productivity.
Collaborate with teams across MLI, learn their areas and offerings, foster a culture of customer empathy and support other Staff  Senior Staff Engineers in working closely with our customers to better identify and address their problems.
Stay visionary. Lead the team to identify technical direction and build the vision and strategy for upcoming company pivotal milestones. Make sure that our team stays focused on the right MLE problems.

What you must have:  

You have 8 years of end-to-end experience in working as a machine learning engineer to ship machine learning products at scale and have deep knowledge of the workflows involved
Experience in building and leading platformsinfrastructure to support hundreds of engineers
Technical leadership track record in leading and getting results from cross-organization initiatives
Track record of owning processes end-to-end and working closely with customer teams on multi-quarter long projects

Bonus points! 

Experience with working in the autonomous vehicle technology space
Experience working closely with product managers andor org leaders to set the strategy
Experience building tools that are core to machine learning engineers daily work
