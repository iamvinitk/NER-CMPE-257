About the job
You will be collaborative, but also work with significant autonomy. To be successful in this role you will be highly analytical; think strategically and globally; exhibit curiosity; have a sense of urgency to meet customer timelines, succeed in a fast-paced environment; and have a high level of customer focus and business judgement. This leader lives the Twilio Magic and has a demonstrated track record managing large-scale supply chain and procurement improvement projects. This role is high visibility and will interact with various senior leaders in the company. The ideal candidate for this role has a history of delivering results, has executive presence, is proactive, can influence stakeholders, and simply gets things done. 
 
Responsibilities
In this role, youll:
As the Senior Strategic Sourcing Manager, you will be responsible for conducting category spend analyses and reporting, supplier stratification, understanding supply market, owning deals from source to contract, and negotiating complex deals with high visibility. In addition:
 

Developing plan on cost savings strategy for technology services and products  primarily focusing on sourcing and qualification of third-party cloud, SaaS, data center, and related service providers and cloud management tools. 
Partnering with stakeholders to fully understand business roadmaps and creating sourcing pipeline  developing supplier strategy  plan for your categories.
Gaining understanding of industry trends to make educated recommendations about pricing, competition, supplier optimization, and market compression.
Leading the negotiation of contracts with new suppliers andor the renegotiation of existing contracts to accomplish lowest total cost of ownership and meet savings objectives.
Optimizing and bringing in efficiencies in source-to-contract process through standardization of commodity code structure.
Continuously measuring, managing and improving supplier actual performance against negotiated SLA.
Driving request for information (RFI) and request for proposals (RFP) with vendors, overseeing review of the proposals with business, compliance, security and legal teams and managing the entire bid to business award to contract execution process.
Managing and influencing internal stakeholders to make the best business decisions that optimize value and cost.
Quickly adapt and support other categories as needed such as professional services, HR and benefits, real estate and workplace, and legal services. 
Deliver on complex S2P improvement projects or other improvement projects.
