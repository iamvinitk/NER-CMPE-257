Robinhoods Account Operations team is critical to supporting the growth of Robinhood and supporting our customers at critical points of their relationship with Robinhood. The team handles various account servicing tasks including application review, customer information updates, reviewing levy notices, and much more. As the first point of contact for most customers, the Account Operations team values customer understanding, emotional intelligence, and technical expertise.
As an Account Maintenance Associate, youll serve as an integral part in our account opening and maintenance processes by reviewing new account applications, analyzing documents with keen attention to detail, working with cross-functional partners to address customer escalations, and performing daily account maintenance activities. This is an opportunity to be a part of one of the most efficient and important pillars of Robinhoods business.The role is located in the office location(s) listed on this job description which will align with our in-office working environment. Please connect with your recruiter for more information regarding our in-office philosophy and expectations.
What youll do

Review new account applications with a mindset of risk mitigation
Review customer-related documents for completeness and process any necessary changes in customer accounts
Consistently meet productivity goals with minimal error
Act as subject matter expert in the firm account opening and maintenance processes with the ability to clearly communicate this with other teams and customers with outstanding customer service.
Interact and troubleshoot with customers for more complex account application issues
Perform daily account maintenance requests related to customer accounts such as deactivating and reactivating accounts, levy notice processing, and updating customer information after their account has been opened
Adhere to the Customer Identification Program.
Assist in all account maintenance activities through document review, restriction placing and lifting, and information maintenance

Excellent written and oral communication skills

Ability to work some weekends
Bonus points: SIE active FINRA license, 2 years in a financial services role, familiarity with brokerage operations and consumer technology
