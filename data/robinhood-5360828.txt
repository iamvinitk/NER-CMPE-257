What youll do

Craft and refine our go-to-market strategies and positioning for Robinhoods core crypto products by collaborating with Marketing, Communications, Product, Data Science, and Partnerships teams
Lead go-to-market execution for product and feature launches and ongoing marketing campaigns

Craft end-to-end marketing campaigns that build relevance and excitement for the Robinhood brand
Create differentiated narratives with messaging that continuously improves with user feedback, testing, and a deep understanding of our target audience
