Okta is looking for Business Development Representatives (BDRs) to qualify inbound leads and set meetings that generate pipeline for our sales organization. We are looking for individuals who are dedicated, curious, and motivated. The ideal candidate will possess strong organizational skills, attention to detail, and communication skills. A strong candidate is flexible with change, comfortable in a fast-paced environment, and eager to begin a career in sales. 
BDRs are responsible for connecting with prospects and customers who may have interest in Okta or have downloaded Okta related content or materials. potential customers who have shown interest in Okta content. The BDR will make calls and send emails to prospects to determine how Okta can help, then set up a meeting with an Account Executive. 
Responsibilities

Follow up on a substantial volume of inbound leads via phone and email to progress prospects further into Oktas sales process
Generate qualified meetings and opportunities for Account Executives 
Research contacts and generate interest through discovery calls and email campaigns
Utilize active listening skills to understand and uncover customer needs and business problems to effectively communicate how Okta can solve them
Consistently achieve quota by hitting call, email, and meeting targets
Use and maintain Salesforce.com to keep data accurate 
Willingness to collaborate with other members of the sales organization to drive deals forward

Qualifications

Bachelors degree or equivalent work experience
Excellent written and verbal communication skills
Excellent time management and organizational skills 
Ability to navigate different systems and tools and follow process 
Highly motivated and driven individual who thrives working towards monthly and quarterly quotas
Ability to work in a fast-paced, collaborative environment
