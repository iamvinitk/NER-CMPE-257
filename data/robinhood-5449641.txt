The Learning  Development (LD) team works cross-functionally to enable Robinhoods growth and business success. We act as a new hire (on boarding) and skill building utility that enables associates to provide positive client interactions, help drive business impact and allow Robinhood to grow faster and scale more effectively. We develop data-driven insights that enable our business partners to make decisions related to service model design and talent strategies. We program manage high impact, cross-functional learning projects. Ultimately, we are a team of curious, creative, humble, and analytically-minded individuals with a bias toward action and impact.
As a Learning Partner, you will play an integral role in shaping the portfolio of work we commission in support of the business roadmap and key metrics. You will do this by helping us to understand and respond to various business needs, identifying areas for performance optimization through learning, and ensuring we have the resources to execute on approved work. Additionally you will facilitate projects to ensure our LD teams work with our cross functional business partners in a coordinated, efficient, and focused way.
The role is located in the office location(s) listed on this job description which will align with our in-office working environment. Please connect with your recruiter for more information regarding our in-office philosophy and expectations.
