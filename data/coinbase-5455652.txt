Were looking for an intern to help Coinbase scale our operations and expand globally! This role will sit between business and engineering organizations, acting as the voice of both internal and external customers and a leader of our products built on enterprise-level platforms.
In this role, you will be responsible for building cross-functional capabilities to help improve and scale our global operations, ranging from conventional areas like marketing  sales to more unconventional areas like crypto asset risk management. You will partner with various teams across Coinbase and the Group Product Manager to identify problems, develop requirements, and work with an engineering team to ship solutions that will immediately help businesses and customers use crypto all around the world.
As with all aspiring product managers, you will need to be excited to develop a healthy mix of product, process, design, analytical, and technical skills to deliver simple and intuitive products. For our team, you will also need to have a passion and interest for operations and systems thinking.

Work with one or more engineering teams to develop requirements and ship solutions to help internal and external customers use crypto.
Partner with operations teams to implement changes and gather feedback.
Develop metrics to measure performance and impact of our operations.
