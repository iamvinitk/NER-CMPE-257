Supernal is at the forefront of creating emerging mobility solutions that will foster the development of human-centered cities. We are designing a completely new electric vertical take-off and landing (eVTOL) aircraft tailored to the mobility needs of future cities. This allows passengers a seamless intermodal journey that safely transports them to their final destination. We fuse research in autonomy, robotics, aviation and services to define a new category of mobility for the worlds communities. We believe in creative thinking and collaboration to help build a better mobility experience for everyone, improving peoples ability to move  whether for work or play.
What we do: 
The Manufacturing Engineer is responsible for delivering flight-worthy products and maturation of technologies to meet production cost and rate requirements. Success requires collaboration with other stakeholders and subject matter experts spanning across the entire product life cycle at the component, system, and aircraft-level. This role embodies the integrated approach to design, build, test flight-worthy components, systems, and aircrafts with an emphasis on airframes and compositemetallic components.
This position will be required to work on-site 5 days a week.
What you can do:

Develop and complete build plans driven by program breakthroughs, critical paths, and risk registry
Develop work instructions to build and test flight-worthy components and systems
Define statement of work and on-board equipment, hardware, and fixtures at work centers
Drive quality assurance efforts such as incoming inspection, functional testing, processproduct buy-off
Identify and procure the necessary equipment to perform functional testing
Work with technicians and subject matter experts to build and test flight-worthy products
Advocate build team objectives with other verticals, stakeholders, and suppliers
Provide design for manufacturing guidelines to ensure build success across the organization
Prioritize build initiatives according to product and tech development roadmap
Apply expertise of technology development partners to speed up product development
Develop and implement certification plans for novel manufacturing technologies
Support the fabrication efforts for structural test and demonstration articles
Develop manufacturing standard practices and guidelines
Support other teams in detail design, systems architecture, and systems integration work
Support in the review and approval of suppliers statement of work, work instructions, and specs
Contribute to the development of the aircraft maintenance manual
May require up to 25 of domestic and international travel
Other duties as assigned
