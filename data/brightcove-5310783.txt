About the role:
We are searching for an exceptional person to take a position as a Senior Account Executive of our growing Media and Enterprise sales team in Japan and help Brightcove achieve breakout growth. This person must have a proactive attitude and a proven track record for delivering on or exceeding revenue goals and take pride in getting it done.The candidate we will hire will be highly self-motivated, extremely organized, team-oriented, creative, and possess outstanding communication and negotiating skills. Identify, negotiate and close deals with new customers. 
Work with project champions at clients and within organizations to make sure needs are met. Interact both internally with product team, and professional services and externally with customers.
Recruit new Partners through outreach, connections. and promotional activity.
