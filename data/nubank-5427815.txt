Our  team breaks the status quo by facing challenges from the perspective of technological innovation as a tool to automate and make our processes run more efficiently. Our goal is to build a strong and diverse team that looks to create something worthwhile. And has at its core values to pursue smart efficiency, this comes hand in hand with our drive to remain as a strategic partner for all cross chapters teams within NU Mexico. The role of Workplace Experience Analyst is about to be the focal point of everything that involves the Nu Mexico Office employee experience, responsible for all processes and developing all actions related to Food, Catering, Internal Events and Administrative services , willing to seek innovation and great solutions and offer the best experience to all employees. 
Required Skills And Expertise:

At least 3 year of experience in internal events andor food andor administrativefacilities services
Food services (catering, beverage, honest market)  experience
Third-party and contracts  management
KPIs, SLA, NPS, and CSAT monitoring and report
Event planning and logistics experience
Financial control and purchase order issuance
Ability to deal with  multiple stakeholders and interface with different areas (REWS, HR, Internal Comms, EAs)
Excellent organizational skills to meet deadlines, ability to write reports and presentations
Have empathy , be collaborative, and use emotional intelligence for quick decision-making and problem-solving with the information you have at the moment.
Be willing to listen and leverage the data to understand what people really want in the food, internal events, and amenities services provided by the Workplace Experience team.
Ability with google workspace (Slack is a plus) 

Responsibilities

Be a focal point of contact for food services (NUCaf, Honest Market, etc.),  internal events (request process, event spaces, catering, etc) and hospitality services
Manage, improve, and explore solutions to maintain a harmonious environment with the best experience (Library, Common Area, Nubreak, etc)
Perform analysis of food services and internal events KPIs, CSAT, amount of sales, and product diversity. Provide insights into implementation and improvements after analyse all data
Develop actions to surprise and delight the Nubankers, providing an even more differentiated experience
Responsible to implement the  guidelines  to help the local services provided in all Nubank offices be standardized
Be up-to-date to bring innovation to the services we provide and seek ways to make our process less and less bureaucratic and efficient
Responsibility for month end finance close and all reconciliations (OPEX)
Support Facilities Team with the administrative activities
