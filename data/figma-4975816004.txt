In this role, you will be responsible for building models to optimize our marketing efforts, uncovering insights to guide new strategies, and collaborating with a variety of cross functional peers to help leverage data throughout the organization. The ideal candidate will have a strong track record of executing analytical projects as well as partnering closely with business peers to guide decision making.
 
What Youll Be Doing:

Develop and maintain datasets, KPIs, and reporting tools for the entire Marketing organization
Craft and maintain data infrastructure with the Data Engineering team by translating business requirements from business partners to data models that will enable reporting, dashboards, and segmentation; responsible for data integrity
Perform impact analysis and modeling for a variety of different marketing initiatives and projects
Evaluate and size the projected impact of new marketing strategies and how they connect to broader company goals
Collaborate with marketing cross-functional partners to integrate data-driven insights into marketing strategies and decision-making processes

Lifecycle Marketing and Emerging Products:

Own key areas of Lifecycle Marketing end-to-end, including optimizing email channel performance
Lead experimental design and process and evaluate conversion lift experiments to optimize campaigns and measure incremental impact
Build up audience segmentation capabilities to personalize customer communication journeys across lifecycle channels

Sales-Assisted Marketing:

Oversee and improve data reporting to enable real-time, data-driven decision making across multiple teams
Generate action-oriented insights and business recommendations
