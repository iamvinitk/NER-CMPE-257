About the job
This position is needed to craft and deliver on the vision, strategy, and roadmap for Twilios Messaging product.
Twilio is looking for a Director of Product to join the Messaging product team of our Communications business unit. You will understand the needs of our Communications Platform customers and build products that solve that need at global scale. Leading a team of product managers and working with engineering counterparts, you will craft and deliver on the vision, strategy, and roadmap for Messaging at Twilio.
This role  will work cross-functionally with teams across Twilio to understand access patterns and scale requirements. This role will help take our Communications Platform to the next level of growth, while working with some of the worlds leading organizations.
Responsibilities
In this role, youll:

Be the voice of the customer in every conversation and decision, and ensure we build world class products that our customers love and value.
Drive the strategic direction and coordinate efforts around the Developer Experience for our APIs
Collaborate cross-functionally to ensure alignment on the best strategic direction and market opportunities to tackle first, and liaising between Go-to-market (GTM,) Legal, Security and Marketing.
Manage a team of senior product managers 
Work directly with an incredibly talented team of senior engineers and product leads to help ship products that help create amazing customer 
Build a company defining product-offering from the infrastructure layer up, and lead a critical new initiative for Twilio.
