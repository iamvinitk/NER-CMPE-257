About the job
This position is needed to work on all aspects of the revenue accounting process and ensure accurate and complete financial information related to revenue.  You will collaborate with various business partners in Sales, Deal Desk, FPA and Billing, among others.  This is a great role for someone excited and passionate about revenue accounting and contributing to the success of the team and company. Reporting to the Manager of Revenue Accounting, youll join a supportive team excited to help you succeed.
Responsibilities
In this role, youll:

Lead parts of the revenue accounting close process driving timely and accurate reporting of revenue on a monthly basis 
Understand the Company product offerings, routes to market implications on revenue recognition
 Liaise with various business partners upstream in the sales cycle to influence accuracy and efficiency in the revenue accounting process
Support reporting with new business units
Identify, facilitate and drive improvements of processes to increase internal efficiencies
Lead automation efforts within the team and business partners to drive efficiency initiatives within the quote to cash business cycle
