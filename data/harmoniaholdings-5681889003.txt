Harmonia Holdings Group, LLC is an award-winning minority and female owned federal government contractor committed to providing innovative, high-performing solutions to our government clients and focused on fostering a workplace that encourages growth, initiative, creativity, and employee satisfaction.  
We have an exciting opportunity for a Power Platform Developer to join our team. The ideal Power Platform Developer will have experience with Power Platform and SharePoint Online architecture, experience developing canvas and model-driven apps, experience with developing workflows in Power Automate, and experience creating dashboards in Power BI.
Essential Job Functions:

Translate complex functional and technical requirements into custom design and development of Microsoft Power Platform-based solutions.
Deliver services and solutions using the Power Platform suite: Microsoft PowerApps, Power Automate, Dataverse, Power BI, custom development, and data integrations.
Support project and tech lead in development plans, estimation, and code and design reviews.
Work within Power Platform Environments, Dataverse tables, and app deployments.
Convert SharePoint 2010 and 2013 workflows to Power Automate flows.
Convert InfoPath forms to Power Apps forms.
Provide Power Platform architecture designs, configurations, administration or governance, and functional support.
Use Power Platform built-in connectors to connect to data sources.
Use Agile development best practices such as develop a minimum viable product (MVP), develop in sprints, demo at the end of each sprint, and emphasize creating simple solutions over complex ones.
Perform unit and functional testing on the application.
Check in source code to source control (e.g., GitHub).

Experience, Skills, and Abilities:

Minimum 8 years of experience with IT applications design and development
5 years of experience with Microsoft Power Platform-based development and in the use of Microsoft 365 components, including SharePoint, Teams, Power Automate, and Power Apps
3-5 years of experience with conducting and participating in design reviews and code reviews
5 years of experience with developing and reviewing SDLC work products and artifacts
Experience with using Agile development methods and processes
Ability to analyze time and effort estimation of design and development work to support sprint planning
Experience with development or migration of SharePoint applications
Microsoft certification preferred (e.g., PL-900, PL-400, etc.)
Ability to obtain and maintain a security clearance (Public Trust)
Must be a US Citizen
