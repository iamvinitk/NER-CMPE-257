Research, define, extend, and maintain production pipelines, processes, and workflows

Intimate knowledge of Houdini andor procedural asset creation
Proficiency with Maya
Familiar with artist workflows and processes

Proficient scriptingprogramming experience in Python, PyQTPySide
Ability to write clean, maintainable and extensible code with attention to reusability
