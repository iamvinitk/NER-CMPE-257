Mission Summary
The Onboard Data Management and Monitoring team is building Motionals next-gen onboard communications, data capture, and safety monitoring systems. We are seeking a Senior Software Engineer that will help shape the technical vision, design, and implementation for our infrastructure, enabling high performance autonomy components to reliably and safely operate on our vehicles at fleet-scale.
As a Senior Software Engineer in the logging systems group, you will work with the team to design and implement the infrastructure for the next generation of on-vehicle logging. You will have direct contributions ensuring the performance, repeatability, reliability, and testability of the entire data logging pipeline from data origination onboard to data retrieval off board. This infrastructure interacts directly with our simulation, performance tracking, metrics, and event analysis tooling. This is a unique opportunity to have an impact on mission critical software that must operate efficiently and reliably in a constrained compute environment. You will also work with off board teams to help them tackle the challenges of managing big data in the cloud and with autonomy teams to help ensure their data logging needs are fulfilled.

What youll be doing:

Youll use your expertise and experience to build the future of our high-performance on-vehicle logging infrastructure
Design, plan and implement Python and C APIs to access the logs for analysis and playback
Work with smart and motivated teammates in a fast-paced, collaborative environment
Work with teams across the organization to gather requirements and set priorities
Work across the organization to set priorities and accomplish Motionals goal of offering a world-class robotaxi, built for everyone

What were looking for:

You have strong programming skills with C and Python
You are passionate and opinionated about building robust software
You care about holding yourself and your teammates to a high standard of development
You are an excellent communicator and can present complicated technical concepts in an understandable way

Bonus points (not required):

Development experience with C17 and later
Experience with Linux
