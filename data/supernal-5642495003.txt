What we do: 
The Sr. Flight Control Systems Engineer works with the systems lead to establish architecture, define requirements, create and analyze system safety in development of the flight controls systems. This position helps to define the system standard methodologies and system philosophy.
This position will be required to work on-site 5 days a week.
What you can do:

Establish and review architecture including hardware, actuator, and sensor decisions in creation of flight-critical flight controls system
Aid development of redundancy management system with supplier
Partners with team leader to develop and review flight control system requirements process in defining the fly by wire system
Collaborate with colleagues on powertrain, avionics, and other engineering teams for flight controls interface
Work with certification team to aid in establishing certification basis for flight controls
Participate and review system safety analysis to ensure aircraft safety within the flight controls design architecture
Participate in system integration with actuation and avionics teams
Develop and participate in system level testing of flight control system verification
May require up to 10 of domestic and international travel
Other duties as assigned

What you can contribute:

Bachelors degree in aerospace, electrical, or a relevant engineering field
Minimum of five (5) years of experience in flight control systems or similar highly integrated and complex systems (an equivalent combination of education and experience may be considered)
Familiar with system safety including analyzing fault trees, system safety reliability, FHA, SSAs, common mode analysis, zonal analysis
Bulk of flight controls experience in fly-by-wire piloted andor autonomous aircraft control systems design
Some experience with hardware development of flight control computers
Development standards: Basic understanding - ARP 4761 and good understanding - DO-160 and ARP 4754A
Experienced with Flight Controls System (or similar system) integration in the aircraft
Experienced with verification of requirements during flight andor lab testing
Solid understanding in defining, reviewing, and maintaining system requirements
Highly collaborative and phenomenal communication skills
Proactive delivery of communication and follow up
Excellent organizational skills and attention to detail
Must have the ability to independently prioritize and accomplish work within time constraints
Self-motivated and forward-thinking personality
Proficiency with MS Office Suite

You may also be able to contribute:

DO 178BC and DO 254 understanding is a plus
