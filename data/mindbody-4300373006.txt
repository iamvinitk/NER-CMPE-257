About the role
As the Diversity, Equity, Inclusion, and Belonging Manager, you will be responsible for developing and implementing a cohesive DEIB strategy to promote diversity, equity, inclusion, and a sense of belonging throughout the global organization. You will work closely with various departments and stakeholders to foster an inclusive culture, create equitable policies and practices, and champion diversity initiatives. This role requires a deep understanding of diversity, equity, inclusion, and belonging principles, strategic development, and the ability to drive meaningful change within the organization.
Youll likely spend time working on

Assess the current state of diversity, equity, inclusion, and belonging within the organization through data analysis, surveys, and other assessment tools  including bringing in best-in-class research and benchmarks. 
Work with Chief People Officer, Leaders of Talent Management and Recruiting to develop and implement a comprehensive diversity, equity, inclusion, and belonging strategy aligned with the companys goals and objectives- through the full lifecycle of our talent touchpoints.  
Develop clear roadmap for implementation, with recommended measures to address gaps and challenges related to diversity, equity, inclusion, and belonging, and track against those milestones.
Collaborate with senior leadership to integrate diversity, equity, inclusion, and belonging principles into the organizations overall strategy and decision-making processes.
Design and implement diversity and inclusion training programs to increase awareness, sensitivity, and understanding across the organization.
Collaborate with HR and Recruiting to develop and implement inclusive recruitment and talent acquisition strategies that attract and retain diverse talent.
Shepherd and support the employee resource groups and affinity networks to ensure they are working in support of the DEIB strategy and have the resources needed to thrive.
Establish metrics and key performance indicators (KPIs) to measure the effectiveness and impact of diversity, equity, inclusion, and belonging initiatives and develop reports to communicate progress and outcomes.
Stay up-to-date with best practices and emerging trends in diversity, equity, inclusion, and belonging, and provide guidance and recommendations to leadership.
Foster relationships with external organizations, community groups, and industry associations to enhance the organizations diversity and inclusion efforts and promote collaboration.
Act as a resource and advisor for employees, providing guidance and support on diversity, equity, inclusion, and belonging matters.
Serve as a visible advocate and spokesperson for diversity, equity, inclusion, and belonging both internally and externally.

Excellent interpersonal and communication skills with the ability to engage and influence stakeholders at all levels of the organization.
Experience designing and delivering diversity and inclusion training programs.
Knowledge of talent acquisition strategies and practices that attract and retain diverse candidates.

Demonstrated ability to collaborate and build effective relationships with internal and external stakeholders.

High level of integrity and commitment to fostering a diverse, inclusive, and equitable work environment.
