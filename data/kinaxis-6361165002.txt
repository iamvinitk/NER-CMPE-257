
About Kinaxis

About the team
As a member of our Consulting Team, you understand our customers most pressing business performance challenges and you are committed to helping our customers solve complex challenges in the distributed value chain that is prevalent in manufacturing today.
What you will do
Working with new and existing customers, you will provide expert guidance in leveraging our product offerings and deliver value in the following ways:

Provide creative solutions to complex business problems while maintaining best practices;
Guide and mentor the consulting project team during project;
Drive client participation in project design activities;
Leading Deployment Workshops to help customers achieve immediate value from their investment;
Facilitating business process design and development that leverages RapidResponse capabilities;
Resolving complex data and analytic configurations to ensure that the solution is producing the expected results;
Configure the RapidResponse interface according to our customers distinct needs as defined within the Agile user stories and blueprint designs;
Working with our customers to develop a roadmap to solve multiple supply-chain problems leveraging the power of RapidResponse;
Providing high levels of customer service whenever the opportunity arises;
Work with Account Executives and Client Partners to articulate the value of RapidResponse and be able to discuss our implementation methodologyapproach.
Support the services sales opportunities as the solution and technical leader working with the Account Executives and Client Partners

Multiple cycles of leading development teams in complex supply-chain solution design, development, and testing
