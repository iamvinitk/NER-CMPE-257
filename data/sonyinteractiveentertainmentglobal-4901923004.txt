As Corporate Counsel for Network Advertising, you will support PlayStations global network advertising business--the team responsible for the development and sale of advertising products on PlayStations owned and operated platforms. PlayStations Network Advertising Team generates significant revenue and is experiencing sustained growth, both in terms of volume of ads sold as well as deployment of new ad offerings.
You should have the ability to manage a large number of transactions simultaneously, and you must have excellent judgment in balancing legal risks with business goals to enable this teams growth. You should also have experience with the digital advertising business, its major players, and relevant laws and regulations, and the ability to share this experience in effective collaboration with and counseling of legal and business stakeholders.
Responsibilities: 

Support PlayStations Network Advertising business by managing and closing a wide variety of business-critical agreements in a high-volume environment,
Understand and stay up to date on the latest legal developments related to marketing and advertising worldwide, and proactively advise the business of relevant legal developments,
Identify areas where PlayStations internal legal processes can be improved to better support the Network Advertising business, and work with internal stakeholders to drive those changes forward,
Collaborate with colleagues in legal and the business to anticipate and address challenges related PlayStations Network Advertising business, and
Have strong commercial transactional experience and the ability to draft clear, effective agreements.

Qualifications: 

Minimum 5 years relevant experience, particularly working as in-house counsel in a technology company or advertising or marketing agency.
JD or LLM degree from top law school; admitted to a U.S. state bar. California State Bar membership is preferred. At a minimum, the candidate must be qualified for and quickly obtain Registered In-House Counsel status in California and the willingness to maintain that status during employment.
Experience managing marketing and advertising related commercial transactions.
Experience with marketing and advertising related regulatory issues.
