The consumer litigation team manages all retail customer-facing litigation risks throughout the world. The successful candidate will support the litigation teams international litigation docket, led by our Associate General Counsel, Litigation, based in London, and the US-based consumer litigation team, which focuses on consumer arbitrations and consumer class actions in the United States.  
What youll be doing (ie. job duties): 

Manage fast-paced docket of consumer litigation. 
Develop, execute, and continuously assess Coinbase global consumer litigation strategy and tactics, including providing thought leadership, strategic direction and proactive solutions.  
Identify and mitigate consumer protection issues and risks arising out of operating the most trusted crypto platforms in numerous jurisdictions on multiple continents.  
Advise a cross-functional team of internal and external stakeholders on Coinbases global consumer litigation matters and strategy.
Draft and file pleadings and briefs as needed.  
Engage and manage outside counsel to effectively and efficiently represent Coinbase.
Work collegially and supportively with other members of the legal team.

What we look for in you (ie. job requirements): 

Four years litigation experience at a top law firm.
Experience managing and successfully resolving strategic litigation in the U.S.
Experience with litigation andor arbitration outside of the US is highly desirable.
Experience with consumer protection law.
Strong negotiation skills.
Ability to manage expanding litigation practice, using outside counsel strategically and efficiently.
Excellent judgment, analytical rigor, clear communication, and business pragmatism.
Team player essential -- willing to tackle tasks large or small.
Ability to learn new areas of the law quickly and well.
Organized and detail oriented.
