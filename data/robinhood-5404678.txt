The Securities Lending Senior Trader will support Robinhoods ambitious growth plans for Securities Finance (Short Selling, Alternative Securities Lending, and Repo). They will serve as a domain expert, functional area advisor, and contribute to our business plans for measurable short to mid-term growth. The candidate will drive the scoping and planning of critical projects and manage their execution. Additionally, the candidate will be responsible for presenting new ideas and areas for growth and will influence senior leaders on the direction to take the desk.
It is preferred that this role is located in one of the office locations listed on this job description which will align with our in-office working environment. This position is only eligible for remote work in limited geographies within the US where we do not have physical office locations. Please connect with your recruiter for more information regarding our in-office philosophy and expectations.
What youll do

Be the face of RHS to the Street; acting as an account rep, salesperson and ambassador for the firm.
Constantly collaborate with the Director of Securities Finance, and other groups within the organization.
Adhere to, and remain compliant with Robinhood Securities Securities Lending Trading related Written Supervisory Procedures.
Process Robinhood Securities regulatory inquiries, reporting and audits.
Manage and resolve counterparty relationships and issues at the executive level.
Meet and exceed daily, weekly, monthly and annual revenue targets.
Manage trade blotter and optimize revenue stream by seeking out, identifying and selling special situation trades and deal stocks to our counterparties.
Mentor other traders on the desk and coach them to provide unique solutions to complex problems.
Collaborate with senior leaders, executives, legal, compliance, product managers and engineers.

What you bring 

15 years of experience and be recognized as an expert by the industry.
Aggressive approach to trading with the ability to operate in an extremely fast paced environment.
Engaging personality and excellent relationship management skills.
Ability to learn quickly, think critically and apply problem-solving skills to resolve issues and implement process improvements.
Trustworthy, self-motivated and able to thrive in an entrepreneurial environment.
A passion for Robinhoods product and our mission to democratize access to Our Financial System.
Previous Securities Lending experience and expertise.
FINRA Series 7 or attained within 90 days.
