About the job
This position is needed to play a pivotal role in shaping, defining and executing our product vision and strategy.  You will collaborate closely with cross-functional teams, including engineering, design, and GTM to bring to life groundbreaking solutions that will shape the future of the international supply domain.  You will lead the ideation, development, and launch of high-impact products, while also mentoring and guiding a team of product managers.  
Specifically, this role will lead the international supply route registry product for Business Communication.
Responsibilities
In this role, youll:

Develop, communicate, generate excitement for and obtain buy-in of the product vision, strategy, and roadmap.
Partner with cross functional peers to drive product planning, design, execution, and delivery of product investments against defined success metrics.
Partner with BD team on business development opportunities via alliances, partnerships, acquisitions and marketplaces.
Conduct regular data-driven business reviews that analyze product execution and revenuemarket accomplishments.
Continuously shape new global messaging supply strategies with our GTM team to effectively control and manage a highly volatile environment
Lead supporting product managers to ensure ecosystem is performing optimally at all times.

Required:

Bachelors degree in relevant domain; MBA or advanced degree preferred.
6 years of experience in product management, with a focus on messaging and CPAAS.
Proven track record of leading successful product launches and driving product strategies.
Exceptional analytical skills with the ability to synthesize complex information into actionable insights.
Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to influence and collaborate at all levels.
Use of quantitative and qualitative data to make product decisions, get executive buy-in, and drive towards measurable team, product, and financial performance.
Initiative, ownership, and accountability to decode vague priorities, manage conflicting tasks and provide the day-to-day blocking and tackling against ambiguous and complex challenges. 
The ability to collaborate and influence effectively across cross-functional teams.
The demeanor and maturity to present complex business and technical concepts, trade offs, and difficulties to internal and external audiences up to C-level executives and to know when and how to assert opposing viewpoints when necessary.

Desired:

 A strong balance of business acumen, technical insight, and user-centered design skills to make the right tradeoffs for optimizing costs, quality, and business value while standing up for your customers needs.
Strong understanding of the global messaging industry, trends, technologies and market dynamics.
Proficiency in agile development methodologies.
Results-oriented mindset with demonstrated ability to meet or exceed goals.
Experience with messaging supply chain operations.
