About the job
This person in this position will report directly to the Head of GTM Enablement working closely with their peers in the GTM Enablement organization as we continue to transform the business and state of Enablement. If you like adding value and seeing the direct impact of your work while thriving in highly dynamic environments and love rolling your sleeves up to get things done, this role is for you. This is a people leader role. 
Responsibilities
In this role, youll:

Partner with the Head of GTM Enablement to develop quarterly enablement plans and lead the Field Enablement Business Partners to execute the plans
Execute on a plan to establish and conduct Leadership Development programs
Customize and deliver content tailored by audience leveraging cutting edge enablement tools 
Help coach sellers to ensure skills are mastered   
Experience working independently and as part of a team, specifically coming with experience developing strong cross functional relationships
Provide thought leadership to the organization through contributions to knowledge bases, training sessions, knowledge development, weekly updates 
Maintain knowledge of Twilios competitors products, company direction, financial stability, etc  
Lead and develop team of GTM Field Enablement Business Partners
