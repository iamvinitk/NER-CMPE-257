Circle is looking for a transaction monitoring expert to join our Financial Crimes Compliance team. Reporting to the Global Head of Financial Crimes Compliance, the Director, Financial Crimes Transaction Monitoring, will be responsible for enhancing the design of our global AMLCTF transaction monitoring program, across both fiat and crypto. The ideal candidate will have extensive experience working with financial intelligence, data and engineering teams to design transaction monitoring systems and scenarios, and is looking to find creative approaches to detect financial crimes in the fast-changing crypto ecosystem.
What youll work on: 

Owning Circles global AMLCTF transaction monitoring program, including definition of scenarios, rules and thresholds.
Working closely with the Financial Intelligence Unit and Investigations Operations teams to enhance transaction monitoring based on SARs filed and broader ecosystem intelligence.
Partnering with Compliance Data  Technology, Product, and Engineering teams to implement enhancements to the transaction monitoring systems.
Driving scenario testing and tuning exercises to identify optimal transaction monitoring scenarios and thresholds.
Identifying opportunities to improve the overall efficacy of the transaction monitoring program, including but not limited to, use of machine learning and artificial intelligence to detect unusual activity.
Staying at the forefront of money laundering, terrorist financing and other financial crimes risks and typologies, and technologies to detect suspicious activity.
