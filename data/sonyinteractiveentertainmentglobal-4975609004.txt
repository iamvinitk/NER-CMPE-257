Santa Monica Studio is seeking a Production Intern to join our Santa Monica Studio for our Summer 2024 Internship Program!
Established in 1999, Santa Monica Studio became renowned for excellence in the actionadventure genre with the worldwide award-winning success of the 2005 release, God of War, and its subsequent sequels. Our most recent God of War (2018) became one of the fastest selling PlayStation 1st-party games of all-time and has since won over 190 Game of the Year awards including DICE, BAFTA, The Game Awards, Game Developers Choice Awards, IGN, Game Informer, EW, and Variety.
Responsibilities:

Help to facilitate scheduling, asset tracking, communication and assist in a variety of tasks to maintain the infrastructure for various disciplines
Assist production staff with day-to-day production issues for different departments
Work closely with various cross-departmental groups to ensure communication, delivery of assets and feedback
Help assemble presentations for teams, take screenshots and record footage
Help to take notes in various stand-ups and meetings, disseminate information, update schedule and bug databases, the wiki and track status

Currently enrolled in an accredited institution, pursuing a Bachelors or Advanced degree majoring in Production Management, Fine Arts, Visual Art, Studio Art, Computer Animation, Interactive Entertainment or related field
