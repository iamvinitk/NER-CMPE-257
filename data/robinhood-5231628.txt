Robinhood is looking for a Leader to oversee the Creative Production function that supports our Creative, Marketing, and Product teams. As the Senior Manager of Creative Production, your duties will include overseeing all aspects of Creative Production and Creative Operations, as well as leading a team of exceptional creative producers in developing campaigns for all of Robinhoods products and Brand Marketing initiatives.
This is a highly strategic role, owning the partnership with Marketing  Creative teams working directly with our leaders. The ideal candidate has proven experience managing and mentoring a growing team, leading large-scale production for integrated campaigns, and a deep passion and expertise in creative operations.

What youll do 

Oversee, mentor, and coach a team of Creative Producers. Maintain and enhance a culture of performance, delivery and continuous improvement.
Oversee the successful execution of all creative campaigns across product and marketing
Develop and maintain master creative production schedule, roadmap and budgets.
Define overall staffing requirements and balance workload to optimize the effectiveness of the team.
Partner with department leaders to establish creative and production standards, define working models with marketing, and help scale the creative production discipline and practice. 
Partner closely with Creative and Marketing leadership to drive alignment and desicion making
Drive operational improvements to reduce cost and increase overall impact of creative production campaigns.
Develop measurements to define progress and ultimate success; establish ongoing reporting requirements and key performance indicators for planning and recognizing adoption and success.

What you bring

Experience leading creative production teams in both a product and brand environment
Portfolio of previous work, that demonstrates an ability to lead complex campaigns across different channels and mediums
A strong operator, able to drive a clear production vision and bring along executive leadership, while still supporting your team
Ability to anticipate leadership and organizational needs and changes and course correct appropriately
Strong creative and design sensibility
Ability to analyze a business process, articulate, and implement recommended improvements
Experience working both in-house and at agencies a plus
