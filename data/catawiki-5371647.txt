The CX Coaching Specialist is motivated, enthusiastic, and customer oriented. We are passionate about people and performance excellence and put our customers at the centre of everything we do. The coaching specialist is committed to providing in-depth coaching to instil tool and process training concepts within the CX Ops teams. The CX Coach uses their knowledge of quality within a customer contact centre to guide CX specialists towards behaviours to increase customer satisfaction and enliven the voice of the customer. 
The CX Coach is responsible for extending new hire training into practice via the creation and maintenance of 1:1 coaching system. They are experts in the CX processes and ways of working and they coach to ensure alignment, consistency, and high quality of interactions. The Coaching Specialist is an important part of the CX innovation cycle, supporting adoption of improvements and collecting feedback for iteration. 
The CX Coach is an integral part of the feedback cycle and understands that being open underpin our collaboration efforts to move in sync as a team.  Working closely with CX Training Specialists to extend training into 1:1 and real world scenarios.
