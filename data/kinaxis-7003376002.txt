About the team
The Accounts Receivable (AR) Team Lead is responsible for managing the billing and collection of the global accounts receivable portfolio for Kinaxis. This role will be a trusted partner to colleagues within the Finance team and across the organization, ensuring the complete and accurate processing of all invoice-related activities and related journal entries for the Company, in accordance with International Financial Accounting Standards (IFRS).
The AR Team Lead is also responsible for managing the performance of a team of AR Administrators, understanding the needs of, and ensuring adequate support to the finance team.
What you will do

Collaborate with the Sales, Professional Services and Finance Operations teams to ensure timely receipt of customer purchase orders to enable billing and payment by contracted due dates and terms.
Review and approve monthly services billings and subscription billings per contracted terms.
Manage and maintain overdue accounts lists, ensuring timely follow-up and communication with vendors and customers to accelerate collection.
Expedite collection issues for the team by collaborating with other departments and escalating to the Controller, Financial Services when required.
Process incoming cash receipts in NetSuite and coordinate the month end process including managing the AR aging report and leading the bi-weekly AR review with the AR team and the Controller, Financial Services.
Lead the quarterly close process for the AR team and assist with the quarterly audit reviewsyear end audit.
Recommend and participate in the implementation of effective and efficient processes and practices.
Lead regular and ad-hoc information requests including but not limited to financial reporting and KPI metrics such as Days Sales Outstanding.
Provide leadership, coaching, development opportunities, and guidance to a team of AR administrators, supporting them through review of complex situations and ensuring that the team is structured for success.

What we are looking for

Post-secondary diploma or degree in Business Accounting or a related field, or equivalent experience.
5 years of progressive accounting experience, preferably in a high-tech environment.
2 years of direct or indirect people leadership experience, managing the performance of a team whose work may be directed by others.
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to apply sound judgement in situations that are ambiguous in nature.
Critical thinker when faced with challenges, and applies skills to work through complex issues that may require change in policy or practice.
Highly adaptable and able to pivot based on prioritization and needs of the business; proactively solicits and delivers feedback to ensure alignment.
A clear, concise, and professional communicator with the ability to present information to various audience.
