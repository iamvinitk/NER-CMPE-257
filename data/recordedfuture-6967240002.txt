Act as a trusted advisor to leaders in the Region and provide HR insights and recommendations.
Manage employee relations and performance-related discussions and coach managers and employees on effective performance management, addressing employee concerns, conflicts and grievances across the Region
Build strong relationships within the People function and collaborate on cross functional people projects
Effectively communicate and execute corporate initiatives, programs, and projects eg annual merit cycle, annual Talent Assessment etc 
Provide advice on relevant employment law, emerging trends, best practice and support on implementing new legislation in collaboration with People  Talent leadership
Champion and incorporate Recorded Future culture and values in all aspects of work
