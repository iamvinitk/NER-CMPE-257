The Business Intelligence (BI) Analyst will be a key member of the Go-To-Market Strategy, BI and Analytics team, leading the development of core business reporting and analytics to drive actionable insights to stakeholders. The analyst will work closely with Sales Operations, Field Sales, Finance, Marketing and Strategy teams to support key business initiatives. This role will require timely collaboration between multiple stakeholders from urgent, time-sensitive decisions to longer-term strategic initiatives.

Gather and document requirements from stakeholders, accurately translating business requirements into technical ones
Develop executive ready reporting and visualizations to provide easy to digest actions out of complex problems
Present work to functional leaders and executives on a regular basis. Anticipate business questions by proactively developing targeted analysis
Communicate regularly with stakeholders to set expectations and progress of current development work 
Responsible for prioritization of business requirements with development teams
Participate in data warehouse design and optimization
Create first interactions or models of business insights
Developing and maintaining key project governance documentation to ensure the proper amount of structure is applied for project success
Help define a roadmap and vision for Business Insights, data and related tools strategy
Assist in assessment and recommendation of the BI tool ecosystem and data tech stack

WHAT YOULL NEED TO BRING TO THIS ROLE...

8 years of experience inor supporting Sales or Revenue Operations
BA degree required (degrees in business, financial or analytical fields)
Ability to manage multiple priorities simultaneously, work well under pressure and adapt quickly to change.
Ability to partner cross-functionally, through strong relationship building, influencing, negotiating and communication skills, with all levels of management.
Self-motivated curiosity, sense of urgency, ability to pivot seamlessly between big picture and fine level granularity, and relentless focus on outcomes not activity
Excellent analytical, decision-making, project management, and financial modeling abilities.
Strong communication and story-telling skills, particularly to executive management.
Subject-matter expert in Excel and Tableau (or similar BI tools) used for business reporting and analytics
Experience developing with iPaaS tools a plus 
Must be eligible to work in the United States without visa sponsorship
