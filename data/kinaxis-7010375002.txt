Job Location: In-office in Ottawa, ON
Term length: 4, 8, or 12 months starting January 2024
About the Team
As a member of the Finance team reporting to the Accounting and Reporting Manager, the Accounting Analyst Co-opIntern will support tasks related to our public company reporting requirements, various technical accounting matters, our financial close process and our internal control requirements. This role assists with preparing the financial statements and MDA, and helping to test internal controls over financial reporting, among other duties.
What you will do

Assist in preparing the annual and quarterly financial statements and MDA
Support annual audit and quarterly review engagements
Support testing of the design and implementation and operating effectiveness of internal controls over financial reporting
Assist in compilation of other compliance documentation, financial reports, and presentation material as required
Prepare monthly reconciliations and supporting documents to ensure the completeness and accuracy of specific accounts as part of the monthly close
Participate in additional ad-hoc projects or provide support to other teams within Finance upon request
May perform additional projects as requested

What we are looking for

Currently enrolled in a Commerce or Business program with a specializationmajor in Accounting
Attention to detail
Ability to manage multiple priorities and meet deadlines
Excellent communication and interpersonal skills

Things that Would Definitely Help

Experience with Microsoft Office, specifically Microsoft Excel and Microsoft Word
Any accounting or audit related work experience
Any knowledge of IFRS accounting principles
